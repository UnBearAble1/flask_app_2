6 Points
Warrior; Hunt Prey
Prerequisites Double Prey; Warden's Boon; Master in Warrior
Hunting as a duo, you and your ally both single out your prey. When you use Hunt Prey and select only one prey, you can grant your Hunt Prey benefits and any feats you have that modify it (that don't grant abilities to other creatures) to an ally in addition to gaining them yourself. The ally retains these benefits until you use Hunt Prey again.
