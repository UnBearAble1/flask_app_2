3 Points
Arcana; School Spells
Prerequisites: Expert in Arcana
You gain the call of the grave school spell, and you increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Call of the Grave; Arcane, Attack, Necromancy
2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 living creature
You fire a ray of sickening energy. Make a spell attack roll.
  • Critical Success The target becomes sickened 2 and slowed 1 as long as it's sickened.
  • Success The target becomes sickened 1.
  • Failure The target is unaffected.
