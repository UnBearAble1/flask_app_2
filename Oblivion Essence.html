LEGENDARY
Alchemical, Consumable, Injury, Poison 
Price 100 gp Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk L 
Activate one action, Interact

Created from a daemon’s powdered soul gems or refined from the waters of Abaddon’s rivers, oblivion essence causes victims to rapidly age and decay.

Saving Throw DC 22 Fortitude; Maximum Duration 6 rounds; 
  • Stage 1 2d6 poison damage and slowed 1 (1 round); 
  • Stage 2 3d6 poison damage, enfeebled 2, and slowed 1 (1 round); 
  • Stage 3 4d6 poison damage, enfeebled 3, slowed 1 (1 round)
