6 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Nature
You gain the goodberry order spell, and you increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Goodberry; Healing, Necromancy
Special: This Focus Spell takes 2 Focus Points to Cast 
2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range touch; Targets 1 to 3 ripe berries; Duration 10 minutes
You imbue the target berry with the bounty of nature, turning it into a goodberry that can heal and sustain far beyond its normal capacity. A creature can eat the goodberry with an Interact action to regain 1d6+4 Hit Points (Expert-Tier effect).  A berry not consumed during the duration withers away. Every six goodberries consumed gives as much nourishment as one square meal for a typical human. A creature can consume any number of goodberries from the same casting with a single Interact action.

Special: You can cast this spell as a Master-Tier effect (which also enchants an extra berry). If you are Legendary tier, you can cast this as a Legendary-Tier effect (which enchants two extra berries), but it costs 3 Focus Points. If you use either of these options, you cannot use either again until you have completed 2d4 full rests.
