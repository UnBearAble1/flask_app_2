TRAINED

Evocation, Light
Traditions arcane, divine, occult, primal
Three Actions;  material, somatic, verbal; Cost 6 gp of ruby dust
Range touch; Targets 1 object; Duration unlimited

A magical flame springs up from the object, as bright as a torch. It doesn't need oxygen, react to water, or generate heat.

Heightened: You can heighten this ability to the tier of your choice for the purposes of resisting counterspell or darkness effects. The cost in ruby dust double each time (12, then 24, then 48.)
