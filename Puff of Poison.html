Cantrip, Evocation, Inhaled, Poison
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions somatic, verbal
Range 5 feet; Targets 1 creature; Saving Throw Fortitude

You exhale a shimmering cloud of toxic breath at an enemy's face. The target takes 3d8 + spellcasting modifier poison damage,  and 4 persistent poison damage, depending on its Fortitude save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The target takes half initial and persistent damage.
Failure The target takes full initial and persistent damage.
Critical Failure The target takes double initial and persistent damage.
