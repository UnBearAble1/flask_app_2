Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L 
Activate Single Action, Interact

This tingly solution increases the elasticity and contraction of your leg muscles. 

TRAINED: 
Cost: 5 gold 
For 1 minute after drinking this elixir, you can High Jump or Long Jump as a single action instead of 2 actions. If you do, you don’t perform the initial Stride (nor do you fail if you don’t Stride 10 feet).

EXPERT:
Cost: 20 gold
As Trained, but the maximum vertical distance you can jump with a High Jump is the same as you can jump horizontally with a Long Jump.
