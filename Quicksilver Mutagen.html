Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir, Mutagen, Polymorph
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L 
Activate Single Action, Interact

Your features become thin and angular. You become swifter and nimbler, but your body also becomes fragile.

Benefit You gain an item bonus to Acrobatics checks, Stealth checks, Thievery checks and Reflex saves, and you gain the listed status bonus to your Speed. When making attacks based on Dexterity, you can use one of the following sources (your choice): 
  • Your Warrior proficiency 
  • Your Alchemist proficiency 
  • The Tier of the Quicksilver Mutagen 

Drawback You take 10 points of damage; you can't recover Hit Points lost in this way by any means while the mutagen lasts. You take a –2 penalty to Fortitude saves
