MENTAL, LAWFUL
You take no pleasure from food, drink, sex, art, beauty or other such things. Maybe you were born without it, maybe it was taken from you, but you find it almost impossible to muster pleasure in this way. This doesn't mean you don't have drives and ambitions, but they may represent a more abstract sense of duty or accomplishment. 

Take a -3 to any roll where joie de vivre and passion are required, typically in performance or diplomacy. Further, take anywhere from a -1 to -3 to reaction rolls from people who notice and would care about your lack of appreciation.
