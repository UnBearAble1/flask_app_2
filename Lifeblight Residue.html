LEGENDARY
Alchemical, Consumable, Divine, injury, Poison 
Price 100 gp Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk L 
Activate two actions, Interact

This black slime is carefully culled from coffins and sarcophagi used by the undead, and is then distilled and combined with necromantic energy, creating a dangerous sludge that leeches life force as aggressively as it rots flesh.

Saving Throw DC 22 Fortitude; Maximum Duration 6 rounds; 
  • Stage 1 1d8 negative damage and 1d8 poison damage (1 round); 
  • Stage 2 2d8 negative damage and 1d8 poison damage (1 round); 
  • Stage 3 3d8 negative damage and 2d8 poison damage (1 round)
