4 Points
Environmental Acclimatization; Environment
Your people are particularly good at remaining hidden while moving in the wild. Pick any type of terrain (arctic, desert, forest, mountain, plains, sky, swamp, or underground)  If you perform your daily preparations in a terrain that is not your chosen terrain, you may switch your chosen terrain at that time to the one you are in. You gain a few benefits while in this terrain: 
  • If you are near an environmental feature that would allow you to Take Cover, you can Take Cover and then use that cover to Hide as a single action. 
  • A +2 circumstance bonus to Stealth checks to Hide , Sneak and your Survival DC to Cover Tracks.
  • You always gain the benefits of the Cover Tracks action without needing to use the activity or having to move half your Speed.
  • When you use the Acrobatics skill to Balance on narrow surfaces natural to that terrain you aren’t flat-footed, and if you roll a success on the Acrobatics check, you get a critical success instead
