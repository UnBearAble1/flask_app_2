Threat: 4 

These creatures look like roiling clouds of black smoke churning above a mound of burning refuse. Leering faces form and fade in the smoke above, while the burning trash writhes in spasmodic lurches, obviously alive.

Recall Knowledge - Elemental (Arcana, Nature)

NE, Medium, Elemental, Fire, Artillery 
Perception +9; darkvision, smoke vision
Smoke Vision The filth fire ignores the concealed condition from smoke.
Languages Ignan; can't speak any language
Skills Acrobatics +9, Athletics +5
Str +1, Dex +5, Con +4, Int -2, Wis +3, Cha +0
AC 16; Fort +5, Ref +9, Will +7
HP 32; Immunities bleed, fire, paralyzed, poison, sleep; Weaknesses cold 5
Speed 25 feet

Actions: 
  • Burning Lash; Melee Single Action; +7 (+2 / -3); Damage 2d6+4 bludgeoning plus 1d6 persistent fire
  • Ember Ball; Ranged Single Action; +9 [+4/-1] (fire, range increment 35 feet), Damage 2d8+4 bludgeoning plus 1d6 persistent fire
  • Noxious Burst; Two Actions; Toxic materials and churning rubbish within the filth fire's body explode in one of three ways. The filth fire chooses the effect, but it can't make the same choice twice in a row.
    • Fiery Beam (evocation, fire, primal) The filth fire expels a 30-foot line of flame that deals 4d6 fire damage with a DC 18 basic Reflex save.
    • Shrapnel Blast (evocation, primal) The filth fire shoots jagged rubbish out in a 5-foot emanation that deals 2d12 piercing damage with a DC 18 basic Reflex save.
    • Toxic Fumes (evocation, poison, primal) The filth fire belches a 15-foot cone of toxic smoke that deals 3d6 poison damage (DC 18 basic Fortitude save). A creature that fails is also sickened 1 (or sickened 2 on a critical failure).
