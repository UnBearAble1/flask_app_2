spellcaster; Heal Spell
[Healing, Positive]; Prerequisite: Trained in spellcaster;
You’re a conduit for positive energy, and as you channel it through you, it heals some of your minor injuries. When you cast the heal spell to heal a single creature other than yourself, you regain Hit Points equal to twice the spell tier of the heal spell. This is healing effect is considered one tier lower than the heal spell cast.
