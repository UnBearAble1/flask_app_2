Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir, Healing
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L 
Activate Single Action, Interact

This serum jolts the body to help shake off physical debilitations. When you drink this elixir, it attempts a counteract check with the listed counteract modifier to remove the clumsy or enfeebled condition from a single source, using the source of that condition to determine the counteract tier and DC.

The serum has a +6 counteract modifier.
