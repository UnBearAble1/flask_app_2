MASTER
Evocation, Water
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 500 feet; Area 50 feet long by 50 feet wide

By imposing your will upon the water, you can raise or lower the level of water in the chosen area by 10 feet. Water created this way isn't confined to the area once created, and the space of destroyed water would likely be quickly filled with other water if in the middle of a body water. Water creatures in the area are subjected to the effects of slow.
