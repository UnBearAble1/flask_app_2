EXPERT
Polymorph, Transmutation
Traditions primal
Two Actions somatic, verbal
Duration 1 minute

You channel the primal forces of nature to transform into an animal battle form, specifically that of a powerful and terrifying dinosaur. This spell works exactly like Animal Form, except you can assume these forms instead:

  • Ankylosaurus
  • Stegosaurus

Heightened: Master: You can assume these forms instead:

  • Triceratops
  • Tyrannosaurus
