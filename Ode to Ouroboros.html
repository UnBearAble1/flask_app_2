3 Points
Performance; Composition Spell
Prerequisites: Composition Spells
You learn the ode to ouroboros composition spell, which enables you to temporarily spare your allies from death. Your focus pool increases by 1.

Ode to ouroboros; Composition,Necromancy
Reaction verbal; Trigger: a creature's dying value would reach the value at which they would die; Range 60 feet; Targets the triggering creature
Your ode temporarily staves off death. The target's dying condition remains 1 below the value at which it dies. This doesn't help prevent death from effects that kill the target without increasing its dying condition, such as disintegrate and death effects.
