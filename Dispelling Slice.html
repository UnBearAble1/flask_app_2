7 Points
Warrior; Sneak Attack
2 Actions; Prerequisites: Sneak Attack; Master in Warrior; Legendary in Arcane, Nature, Religion or occultism
Your sneak attack slices through the threads binding magic to a target. Make a Strike against a flat-footed creature. If your Strike deals sneak attack damage, you attempt to counteract a single spell active on the target (your choice). If you fail your roll, the target becomes immune to your dispelling slice for 1 minute.
