LEGENDARY
Necromancy, Negative
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 500 feet; Targets any number of living creatures
Saving Throw basic Fortitude

You pull the moisture from the targets' bodies, dealing 6d10 negative damage. If you target only 4 or 5 creatures, you deal 7d10 damage. If you target only 2 or 3 creatures, you deal 8d10 damage. If you target a single creature, deal 11d10 damage. 

 Creatures made of water (such as water elementals) and plant creatures use the outcome for one degree of success worse than the result of their saving throw. Creatures whose bodies contain no significant moisture (such as earth elementals) are immune to horrid wilting.
