Threat: 4
Plague zombies are infested with hideous contagions.

Recall Knowledge - Undead (Religion)

NE, Medium, Mindless, Undead, Zombie, Skirmisher
Perception +8; darkvision
Skills Athletics +6
Str +4, Dex -2, Con +3, Int -5, Wis +0, Cha -2
Slow: A zombie is permanently slowed 1 and can’t use reactions.
AC 18; Fort +7, Ref +9, Will +5
HP 40 (negative healing); Immunities death effects, disease, mental, paralyzed, poison, unconscious; Weaknesses positive 10, slashing 10
Speed 25 feet

Actions: 
  • Fist: Melee Single Action fist +9 [+4/-1], Damage 2d8+4 bludgeoning plus Grab and zombie rot
    • Zombie Rot (disease, necromancy) An infected creature can’t heal damage it takes from zombie rot until it has been cured of the disease. Saving Throw DC 18 Fortitude; 
      • Stage 1 carrier with no ill effect (1 day); 
      • Stage 2 1d6 negative damage (1 day); 
      • Stage 3 1d6 negative damage (1 day); 
      • Stage 4 1d6 negative damage (1 day); 
      • Stage 5 dead, rising as a plague zombie immediately
  • Jaws: Single Action (attack) 
    • Requirement The plague zombie has a creature grabbed or restrained. 
    • Effect The plague zombie makes a jaws unarmed melee Strike against the grabbed or restrained creature with an attack modifier of +10 that deals 2d10+4 piercing damage. A creature damaged by the jaws is exposed to zombie rot.
