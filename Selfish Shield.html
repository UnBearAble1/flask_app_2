10 Point
Religion/Warrior; Champion's Reaction
Reaction; [Tactic] Trigger An enemy within 15 feet damages you. Prerequisites: Master in Religion, Expert in Warrior
Your self-interest keeps you safe. You gain 7 resistance against the triggering damage, regardless of damage type. In addition, your Strikes against the triggering creature deal 2 extra damage until the end of your next turn. You choose whether this extra damage is evil or negative each time you use this reaction.
