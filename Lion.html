Threat: 4 

Lions are cooperative hunters, ambushing dangerous prey in groups of lionesses that work in tandem to trap and kill their prey. Male lions are typically larger, with long manes, and when they hunt, they tend to do so on their own.

Recall Knowledge - Animal (Nature)

N, Large, Animal, Skirmisher 
Perception +8; low-light vision, scent (imprecise) 30 feet
Skills Acrobatics +7, Athletics +8, Stealth +7
Str +4, Dex +3, Con +2, Int -4, Wis +2, Cha -2
AC 18; Fort +7, Ref +9, Will +5
HP 50
Speed 30 feet

Features: 
  • Pack Attack: The lion deals 1d4 extra damage to any creature that's within reach of at least two of the lion's allies
  • Sneak Attack: The lion deals 1d6 extra precision damage to flat-footed creatures.

Actions: 
  • Jaws: Melee Single Action; +8 [+3/-2], Damage 2d8+4 piercing plus Grab
  • Claws: Melee Single Action; +8 [+4/+0] (agile), Damage 2d6+4 slashing
  • Pounce; Single Action; The lion Strides and makes a Strike at the end of that movement. If the lion began this action hidden, it remains hidden until after this ability's Strike.
