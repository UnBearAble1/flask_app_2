6 Points
Medicine; Revive the Dead
3 Actions; Healing, Manipulate: legendary in Medicine; Requirements You are holding healer's tools or are wearing them and have a hand free. Also, the target's body is mostly intact.
You can use Medicine to resuscitate the dead. Attempt a Legendary Medicine check to revive a dead creature that has been dead for no more than 3 rounds. If you succeed, the target returns to life with 1 Hit Point, no points in any pools or any other daily resources, and still with any long-term debilitations of the old body. It is also clumsy 2, drained 2, and enfeebled 2 for 1 week; these conditions can't be removed or reduced by any means until the week has passed. It also has the wounded condition it had before dying, increased by 1 (or wounded 1 if it wasn't wounded before dying). Whether you succeed or fail, the target is temporarily immune to Resuscitate for 1 day.
