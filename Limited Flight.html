4 Points
Physiology; Wings
One Action; Usable once per round; Requires: Winglets
You can fly a little! You have a fly speed equal to half your Speed, but it only lasts for this single action.  If you don't end your movement on solid ground, you fall at the end of your turn.

 If you didn't use your action to fly this turn, you can also use the arrest a fall action. If you are still falling past your turn, you may have to make the check again on subsequent turns.
