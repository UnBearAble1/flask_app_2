Threat: 4 

This wave of dirt takes the crude likeness of the melded forequarters of a charging warhorse and a rocky knight wielding a rudimentary lance of gray stone.

Recall Knowledge - Elemental (Arcana, Nature)

N, Large, Earth, Elemental, Skirmisher 
Perception +8; darkvision, tremorsense (imprecise) 60 feet
Languages Terran
Skills Athletics +8
Str +4, Dex +1, Con +4, Int -1, Wis +3, Cha +0
AC 18 ; Fort +7 , Ref +9 , Will +5
HP 50; Immunities bleed, paralyzed, poison, sleep
Speed 50 feet, burrow 30 feet; earth glide

Reactions: 
  • Tilting Strike
    • Trigger The earthen destrier tramples a creature; 
    • Effect The earthen destrier makes a lance arm Strike against the creature it's trampling at a –5 penalty.

Features: 
  • Earth Glide; An earthen destrier can Burrow through earthen matter, including rock. When it does so, it moves at its full burrow Speed, leaving no tunnels or signs of its passing.
  • Lancing Charge; If the destrier moved at least 10 feet directly before its lance arm Strike, it gains a +2 circumstance bonus to its damage roll.

Actions: 
  • Lance Arm; Melee Single Action: +9 (+4 / -1) (deadly d8, reach 10 feet), Damage 2d8+4 piercing and lancing charge
  • Hoof; Melee Single Action hoof +9 (+4 / -1), Damage 2d6+4 bludgeoning
  • Trample; Three Actions; Medium or smaller, hoof, DC 18
