Attack, Necromancy, Nonlethal
Traditions arcane, divine
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 60 feet; Targets 1 creature

A ray of energy bludgeons your target into submission without causing lasting harm. When you cast this spell, you choose whether the ray feels like a strong punch or slap. Make a spell attack roll. The ray deals 7d6 bludgeoning damage.
  • Critical Success The target takes double damage.
  • Success The target takes full damage.

Heightened:The damage increases by 4d6.
