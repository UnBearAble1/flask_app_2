5 Points
Physiology; Healing and HP
Increase your maximum Hit Points by 5. When you have the dying condition, the DC of your recovery checks is equal to 9 + your dying value (instead of 10 + your dying value). Additionally, You die from the dying condition at dying 5, rather than dying 4. 

If you also have the Toughness feat, the Hit Points gained from it and this feat are cumulative, and the DC of your recovery checks is equal to 6 + your dying value.
