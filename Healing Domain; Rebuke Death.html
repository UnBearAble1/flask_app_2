12 Points
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Religion
Your healing magic is particularly potent. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.

Rebuke Death; Healing, Necromancy, Positive
Special: This Focus Spell takes 2 Focus Points to Cast 
1-3 Actions (somatic); Area 20-foot emanation; Targets 1 living creature per action spent to cast this spell
You snatch creatures from the jaws of death. You can spend 1 to 3 actions Casting this Spell, and you can target a number of creatures equal to the actions spent. Each target regains 4d6 Hit Points (Expert Tier). 

Special: You can cast this spell as a Master-Tier effect (increasing it's healing to 5d6), but it costs 2 Focus Points. If you are Legendary tier, you can cast this as a Legendary-Tier effect (increasing it's healing to 6d6), but it costs 3 Focus Points. If you use either of these options, you cannot use either again until you have completed 2d4 full rests.
