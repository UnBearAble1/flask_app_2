EXPERT
Divination
Traditions arcane, divine, occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 text or remnants thereof

You learn one piece of information that was removed from the target text after its creation. You can instead learn one piece of information from a text that had been destroyed, but you must have a fragment of the text, such as a piece of the cover, a page, or even a clasp or metal fittings.

Heightened (Master) The spell reconstructs all missing information regarding a specific topic of your choice.
Heightened (Legendary) The spell also reveals information the writer self-censored and didn’t commit to paper.
