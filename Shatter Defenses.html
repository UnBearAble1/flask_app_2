5 Points
Warrior; Intimidation
1 Action; Press; Requirements A frightened creature is in your melee reach; Prerequisites: Expert Intimidation; Expert in Warrior
Your offense exploits your enemy’s fear. Make a melee Strike against a frightened creature. If you hit and deal damage, the target becomes flat-footed until its frightened condition ends. If the target was already flat-footed to you when you damaged it with this Strike, pick between these two options: 
  • Its flat-footed value increases. 
  • it can’t reduce its frightened value below 1 until the start of your next turn.
