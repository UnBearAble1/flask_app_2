TRAINED
Alchemical, Consumable, Injury, Poison
Price 5 gp
Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk L
Activate Two Actions,  Interact

Once injected, this synthetic toxin sinks into the extremities, numbing them nearly to paralysis.

Saving Throw DC 16 Fortitude; Maximum Duration 6 rounds; 
  • Stage 1 1d6 poison damage and –5-foot status penalty to all Speeds (1 round); 
  • Stage 2 1d8 poison damage and –10-foot status penalty to all Speeds (1 round); 
  • Stage 3 1d10 poison damage and –20-foot status penalty to all Speeds (1 round)
