Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir, Mutagen, Polymorph
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L 
Activate Single Action, Interact

Your features transform into something bestial and you take on muscle mass, but your lumbering form is clumsy.

Benefit You gain an item bonus to Athletics checks. You gain a claw unarmed attack with the agile trait and a jaws unarmed attack. Your proficiency tier in these unarmed attacks can be equal to one of the following (your choice): 
  • Your Warrior proficiency 
  • Your Alchemist proficiency 
  • The tier of the Bestial Mutagen 

Drawback You take a -1 penalty to AC and Reflex saves.

TRAINED: 
Cost: 5 gold 
You gain a +1 item bonus, your claw deals 2d4 slashing damage, your jaws deal 2d6 piercing damage, and the duration is 1 minute.

EXPERT:
Cost: 20 gold
You gain a +2 item bonus, your claw deals 2d6 slashing damage, your jaws deal 2d8 piercing damage, and the duration is 10 minutes.

MASTER:
Cost: 50 gold
You gain a +3 item bonus, your claw deals 2d8 slashing damage, your jaws deal 2d10 piercing damage, and the duration is 30 minutes. 

LEGENDARY:
Cost: 85 gold
You gain a +4 item bonus, your claw deals 2d8 slashing damage, your jaws deal 2d10 piercing damage, and the duration is 1 hour. You gain the "sword" critical specialization effect with the claws and the "pick" one for jaws.
