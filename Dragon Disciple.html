7 Points
Arcana; Dragon Disciple
Requires: Expert in Arcana
You choose to study or worship one type of dragon, and your focus grants you a measure of its power. Choose one color of dragon when you select this feat; if you have a draconic bloodline, this must be the same type as your bloodline. 

You gain 5 resistance equal to against one type of damage determined by the chosen dragon type. The GM may allow you to choose a dragon type not listed below, and will determine the damage type appropriate for that dragon.
  • Black or Copper: Acid
  • Blue or Bronze: Electricity
  • Brass, Gold, or Red: Fire
  • Green: Poison
  • Silver or White: Cold
You also gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saving throws against sleep effects and effects that would make you paralyzed.
