2 Points
Physiology; Perception

Your people are incredibly observant. You gain the following benefits: 
  • You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your Perception DC, though not to your Perception checks.
  • Each time you use the Seek action,  you can search for objects in your choice of a 10-foot emanation around you or an adjacent 15-foot-by-15-foot area, rather than a single adjacent 10-foot-by-10-foot area.
