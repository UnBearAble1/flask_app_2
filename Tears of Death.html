LEGENDARY
Alchemical, Consumable, Inhaled, Poison, Virulent
Price 150 gp Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk L 
Activate one action, Interact

Tears of death are among the most powerful of alchemical poisons, distilled from extracts of five other deadly poisons in just the right ratios.

Saving Throw DC 22 Fortitude; Onset 1 minute; Maximum Duration 10 minutes; 
  • Stage 1 2d8 poison damage and paralyzed (1 round); 
  • Stage 2 3d8 poison damage and paralyzed (1 minute); 
  • Stage 3 4d8 poison damage and paralyzed (1 minute)
