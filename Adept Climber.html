4 Points
Cultural Training; Climbing

Your people are used to climbing regularly, typically because of their home in the mountains or the trees. You gain the following benefits: 
  • You are not flat-footed while you Climb.
  • You can climb hands free if needed. 
  • If you succeed at an Athletics check to Climb, you critically succeed instead.
  • On a critical success to Climb, you climb half your land speed 
  • If you roll a success on an attempt to Grab an Edge, you get a critical success instead; if you roll a critical failure, you get a failure instead.
