Arcana; School Spells
Requires: Call of the Grave; Legendary in Arcana
You gain the Life Syphon school spell, and you increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Life Syphon; Healing, Necromancy
Reaction verbal; Trigger You cast a non-cantrip Arcane spell of the necromancy school.
You use some of the spell's magic to heal yourself, regaining 1d8 Hit Points per Tier of the spell. This healing effect has a Tier equal to one lower than the spell cast.
