5 Points
spellcaster; Harm Spell
1 Action; Concentrate, metamagic;  Prerequisites: Master in spellcaster
You pour negative energy into your undead subject to empower its attacks. If the next action you use is to cast harm to restore Hit Points to a single undead creature, the target then deals an additional 1d6 negative damage with its melee weapons and unarmed attacks until the end of its next turn. If the harm spell is at least Master level, this damage increases to 2d6, and if the harm spell is at least Legendary, the damage increases to 3d6.
