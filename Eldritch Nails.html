5 Points
occultism; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in occultism
Your nails are supernaturally long and sharp. You gain a nails unarmed attack that deals 2d6 slashing damage. Your nails are in the brawling group and have the agile and unarmed traits. You can deliver non-cantrip Focus spells with the Hex trait through your nails. When you successfully cast a non-cantrip hex Focus Spell that requires 2 actions or more to cast and that doesn't require a spell attack roll, if your target is within your reach, as part of the spellcasting activity you can make a nails Strike against the foe before applying any effects of the hex. If this Strike misses, the hex has no effect.
