SUPERNATURAL
When anything goes wrong in the group, it always happens to you first and to the worst possible results. If chance would have it where something good would happen, it never happens to you. The DM has carte blanche to screwing over your character with arbitrary bad chance happenings. If you want to buy this off, you’ll first have to deal with what caused you to be cursed in the first place, deal with it, and then spend the points to do so.
