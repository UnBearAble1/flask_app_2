EXPLORATION, HEALING, MANIPULATE
Requirements You are holding healer's tools, or you are wearing them and have a hand free

You spend 10 minutes administering medical care on a creature (including yourself, if you choose). The DC of Administer First Aid is 10. 

The result of your Medicine check determines how many Hit Points the target regains.
  • Critical Success The target regains 6d8 Hit Points, to a maximum of the amount of damage the creature has taken since the last time Treat Wound was used on them within the last 24 hours. 
  • Success The target regains 4d8 Hit Points. to a maximum of the amount of damage the creature has taken since the last time Treat Wound was used on them within the last 24 hours.
  • Critical Failure The target takes 1d8 damage.
