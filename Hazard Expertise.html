Cultural Training, Exploration

Your people are particularly skilled with dealing with hazards. You gain the following benefits: 
  • Whenever you hit with a Strike against a trap or an unattended object, you ignore the first 5 points of the object's Hardness.
  • You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC and saves against hazards.
  • You gain the Inspirit Hazard Reaction: 
    • Reaction; Requirements The hazard isn't broken; Trigger You Disable a hazard. Concentrate, Occult, Transmutation
      You awaken a spirit of creation within the hazard. You learn the hazard's effects. Once before your next daily preparations, when you observe a creature take an action that would trigger one of the hazard's reactions or free actions if you hadn't Disabled it, you can use a reaction to rearm the hazard, causing the observed creature to trigger it immediately.
