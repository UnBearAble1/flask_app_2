5 Points
Society; Archeologist
Prerequisites Magical Scholastics; Legendary in Society
You broaden your magical studies, allowing you to find the right path, detect objects, and conceal those objects from unscrupulous rivals. You can cast augury, locate, and nondetection as occultism innate spells, each once per day. You can cast this nondetection spell only on an object, and it is automatically heightened to your proficiency tier of society.
