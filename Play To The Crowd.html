1 Point
Performance; Gladiator
Reaction; Concentrate; Trigger You reduce an enemy to 0 Hit Points during a non-trivial combat encounter with spectators. Prerequisite: Gladiator
You show off for the crowd. Attempt a Performance check; the DC is determined by the GM but is typically the Expert DC or the DC to Make an Impression on the spectators, whichever is higher. On a success, choose one of the following benefits; on a critical success, choose two benefits:
  • 10 temporary hit points; these last for 1 minute.
  • A +1 circumstance bonus to AC until the end of your next turn.
  • A +1 circumstance bonus to your next attack roll before the end of your next turn.
