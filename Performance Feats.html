    • Directed Audience [M] -3 pts: Turn composition from emanation to cone 
    • Dirge of Doom [M] -5 pts: Cantrip that frightens 30 feet emanation of enemies 
    • Earworm [M] -4 pts: 10 minutes to set up composition that triggers on free action
    • Fatal Aria [M] -7 pts: Focus composition damages, stuns, maybe kills target 
    • Harmonize [M] -10 pts: Allows you to play two compositions at once 
    • House of Imaginary Walls [M] -3 pts: Create an illusory / real 10x10 foot wall 
    • Hymn of Healing [M] -6 pts: Focus composition gives fast healing 5 and temp HP 
    • Inspire Competence [M] -5 pts: Cantrip lets you aid with performance with limited failure
    • Inspire Courage [M] -5 pts: Cantrip gives you and allies +1 to attack, damage, fear
      • Courageous Advance [M] -1 pt: Inspire Courage targets can reaction stride 
      • Courageous Assault [M] -5 pts: Inspire Courage targets can reaction strike 
        • Courageous Onslaught [M] -5 pts: Targets can reaction stride and strike
        • Shared Assault [M] -3 pts: Inspired target crits, another gets reaction strike
      • Courageous Opportunity [M] -4 pts: If inspired, you can opportunity attack 
      • Discordant Voice [M] -8 pts: Inspired targets get +1d4 dmg on attacks 
      • Vigorous Inspiration [M] -9 pts: inspired courage targets gain at least 3 temp HP 
    • Inspire Defense [M] -5 pts: Cantrip gives allies +1 to AC, saves, and 2 DR 
      • Defensive Coordination [M] -3 pts: You and one ally raise shield when inspire def. 
    • Inspire Heroics [M] -6 pts Focus composition increases inspire defense/courage with a performance check
    • Lingering Composition [M] -4 pts: Focus composition extends composition cantrip up to 4 rounds with a check 
    • Ode to Ouroboros [M] -3 pts: Focus composition reduces a dying value by one 
    • Pied Piping [M] -10 pts: Focus composition disables maybe controls enemies 
    • Resounding Finale [M] -1 pt: End focus composition spell to give ally 10 sonic resist 
    • Silver's Refrain [M] -2 pts: Cantrip turns allies attacks silver, counteracts devil teleporting
    • Song of the Fallen [M] -15 pts: Focus composition animates dead or does large amount of damage to up to 4 targets 
    • Songs of Strength [M] -2 pts: Cantrip gives +1 to athletics things 
    • Soothing Ballad [M] -9 pts: Focus composition counteracts ailments / heals damage 
    • Symphony of the Unfettered Heart [M] -3 pts: Focus composition counteracts a wide variety of conditions 
    • Triple Time [M] -2 pts: Cantrip gives allies +10 feet to speed 
    • Triumphant Inspiration [M] -4 pts: Automatically perform comp cantrip on a crit 
    • Unusual Composition [M] -2 pts: Switch composition verbal and somatic components

GLADIATOR

  • Gladiator [E/E] -1 pt: Get 10 temp HP if fighting in front of spectators 
    • Play to the Crowd [E/E] -1 pt: If you drop an enemy in front of spectators, gain a bonus 
      • Call Your Shot [E/E] -1 pt: add frightening a random target to bonus list 

INFLICT CONDITIONS

  • Fascinating Performance [T] -3 pts: Fascinate one or more observer with music 
    • Focused Fascination [T] -4 pts: Can fascinate single target more simply in combat 
  • Flourishing Finish [E] -4 pts: Frighten a target after you drop another one 

JUGGLE

  • Juggle [T] -2 pts: You can juggle multiple items, keeping them in reach and wielded but also giving yourself a free, open hand 
  • Focused Juggler [T] -3 pts: Juggle as a free action each round, +1 item juggled 
  • Quick Juggler [E] -2 pts: add 2 items when you would add 1, +1 item juggled 
  • Reflexive Catch [T/E] -2 pts: Reaction to add thrown item / dropped item to juggle 

SPECIAL DEFENSES

  • Never Tire [M] -4 pts: If you have 3 observers, you can put off fatigued condition 1 min 
  • Reverberate [M] -4 pts: Reaction to possibly reflect up to 40 sonic damage 

SPELL-LIKE ABILITIES

  • Dance of Intercession [L] -12 pts: You stride half your speed and can cast up to a legendary-tier heal or harm spell with no spellcasting roll required 
  • Head of the Night Parade [L] -6 pts: Once per day, cast three Mad-Monkeys spells layered atop one another without needing to roll. 

VIRTUOSO
