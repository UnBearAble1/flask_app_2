5 Points
Warrior; Hunt Prey, General Attacks
Prerequisites: Flurry; Legendary in Warrior
You can blend your weapon mastery with skillful targeting to make a series of precise attacks. Your multiple attack penalty for attacks against your hunted prey is –2 (–1 with an agile weapon) on your second attack of the turn, and –4 (–2 with an agile weapon) on your third and subsequent attacks of the turn.
