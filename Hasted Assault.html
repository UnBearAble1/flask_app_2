Warrior/Spellcaster
Prerequisites: Expert Warrior, Expert Spellcaster 

You can speed yourself up to move and attack more swiftly. You learn the hasted assault conflux spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Hasted Assault, Transmutation
1 Action, somatic; Conflux Focus Spell; 
Duration 1 minute

You call upon your magic to speed up your attacks. You gain the quickened condition and can use the extra action each round for only Strike actions.
