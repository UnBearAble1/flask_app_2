7 Points
Warrior; Rage
Prerequisites: Rage; Master in Warrior
You have become a brutal, unstoppable force of nature, able to shrug off mortal wounds with ease. If you are reduced to 0 Hit Points while raging, you can end your rage as a reaction to stay at 1 Hit Point. If you do, you become wounded 2 (or increase your wounded condition by 2 if you are already wounded).
