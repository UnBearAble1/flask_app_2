3 Points 
2 Actions; Prerequisites: Inexorable Iron, Spellstrike; Requirements: You are in Arcane Cascade stance and your Spellstrike is charged.

Your Spellstrike sweeps are so wide and devastating, they splash onto nearby foes. Make a Spellstrike. Foes adjacent to the target take 4 splash damage. The damage type is the same as Arcane Cascade; if the spell already deals splash damage of the same type, combine the damage together before applying weaknesses or resistances.
