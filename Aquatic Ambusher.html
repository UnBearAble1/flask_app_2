1 Points
Physiology; Aquatic

Your people are incredibly good at ambushing from the water. You gain a number of benefits: 
  • While you are submerged just below the water's surface, you have cover from attacks made by creatures out of the water.
  • While fully submerged in water, as long as an observer is at least 10 feet away from you, you don't need cover from that observer to Hide or Sneak, and you have advantage on checks to do so. 

You also gain two special actions:

Drag Down; 1 Action
While swimming in water at least 10 feet deep, you grab an adjacent creature (on nearby land or in the water) and pull it below the surface. Attempt an Athletics check to Grapple the creature. On a success, if the creature is on land, in addition to the normal effects of Grapple, you pull the creature into the water in a space adjacent to you. If the creature is already in the water, on a success, in addition the normal effects of Grapple, you drag the creature 10 feet deeper into the water, moving 10 feet with the creature. Moving a creature using Drag Down is forced movement for the creature but not for you.

Riptide;  Reaction
Requirements:  You are close enough to  water at least 10 feet deep, and half your speed is sufficient to reach the water with one Stride. 
Trigger: You successfully Grapple a creature of the same size as you or smaller.
Effect: You Stride up to half your speed to enter the water, bringing the grabbed creature with you into the water.
