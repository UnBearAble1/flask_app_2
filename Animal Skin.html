4 Points
Warrior; Rage
Instinct, Morph, Primal, Transmutation; Prerequisites: animal instinct; Master in Warrior
When you are raging and unarmored, your skin transforms into a thick hide. You gain a +2 item bonus to AC. The thickness of your hide gives you a Dexterity modifier cap to your AC of +3. This item bonus to AC is cumulative with any type of magical clothing, at the GM's discretion
