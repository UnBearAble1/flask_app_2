MASTER
Uncommon, Clockwork, Consumable, Mechanical, Poison, Snare, Trap
Price 55 gp

This cute and unassuming dragon toy activates once a creature moves into its square. It then unleashes a rapid burst of toxic gas in a 10-foot emanation. Those within the emanation when the snare is activated must attempt a DC 20 Fortitude saving throw or take 3d6 poison damage.

  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature takes half damage.
  • Failure The creature takes full damage and is sickened 1.
  • Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and is sickened 2.
