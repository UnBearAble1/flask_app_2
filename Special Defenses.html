These effects increase your ability to avoid attacks, effects or damage. 

Ability Immunity


You are completely immune to a specific type of ability, such as a specific spell or special power. Work with your GM on specifics. 
  • Very rare or mostly inconsequential effect. Example: Risen as an undead (Mod: 3) 
  • Rare/Very Specific or relatively inconsequential effect. Example: The Fireball Spell (Base: 8, Mod +4) 
  • Common or consequential effect. Example: Attacks of Opportunity, Death Effects (Base 12, Mod +8) 
  • Very common or incredibly serious effect. Example: Detection Effects, Emotion Effects (Base: 16, Mod +4) 
Other Modifiers: 
  • The enemy knows automatically an ability would have no effect on you (Mod -2) 
  • Instead of being completely immune, a counteract check is automatically rolled against any attempt to affect you with the specific power (Mod -6) 

Damage Reduction

You can resist damage. 

Breadth of your reduction: 
  • All Damage [Base: 20] 
  • Broad Type of Damage (Physical, Non-Physical, Magical, Non-Magical) [Base: 16] 
  • Moderately Broad Type of Damage, or very common (Elemental, Melee Weapon, Ballistic, Extra Critical Damage) [Base: 12] 
  • Specific Common Type (Slashing, Bludgeoning, Piercing, Fire, Cold, Poison, Negative, Visual, Auditory) [Base: 8]
  • Specific Rare Type (Precision, Bleed, Lightning, Acid, Sonic, Mental, Alignment, Force, Olfactory/Inhaled) [Base: 4]

Degree of your reduction
  • DR 1d8 (Mod +4) 
  • DR 2d8 [Base +4) 
  • DR 3d8 [Base +4, Mod +10) 
  • You take minimum damage from the attack (Base +8) 
  • You reduce a critical hit to a normal hit (Base +4) 
Special Modification: 
  • Shared Burden: (Limit) Instead of granting yourself damage reduction, you grant it to an ally (activation, duration, default range: touch) - only you take the amount of damage reduced. If the damage is reduced to 0 this way, you take any additional affects as well instead of the target. [Mod: -8]

Delay Consequences


(Base: 12) 
Activation: Duration 
Action Type: Reaction 
Default Duration: End of your Next Turn (How long the effect is delayed. This can only be increased up to 1 minute). 
You delay the consequences of an attack you are hit by, receiving them at the end of the duration. 

Multiple Images


Activation, Duration
[Illusion]

You create a double of yourself that mirrors your movements. When targeted by a Guard or Reflex single target attack, roll a dice to see if the image is targeted or if you are. Once an image is hit, it is destroyed. If an attack roll fails to hit you  but doesn't critically fail, it destroys an image but has no additional effect (even if the attack would normally have an effect on a failure). If an attack roll is a critical success and hits one of the images, one of the images is destroyed and the attack roll becomes a success against you. 
  • 1 Image: The chances of hitting you are 1 in 2; (1-3 on a 1d6 hits you) (Base: 6) 
  • 2 Images: The chances of hitting you are 1 in 3; (1-2 on a 1d6 hits you) (Base: 10) 
  • 3 Images: The chances of hitting you are 1 in 4; (1 on a 1d4 hits you) (Base: 14)
Modifications: 
  • Applies against Area of Effect attacks [mod: +8] 
  • Applies against Willpower or Fortitude Defenses [Base: +4] 

Persistent Damage Recovery


Your flat checks for recovering from a type of persistent damage are easier. 

Type of Persistent Damage: 
  • Common: Bleed, Fire, Acid, Negative, Lightning, Poison [Base: 8]
  • Less Common: Piercing, Slashing, Bludgeoning, Cold, Precision, Sonic, Mental, Alignment, Force [Base: 4]
Degree of your recovery: 
  • -2 to Flat Checks and -3 to Assisted Recovery Checks (Mod: +4) 
  • -4 to Flat Checks and -6 to Assisted Recovery Checks (Mod: +8) 
  • -6 to Flat Checks and -9 to Assisted Recovery Checks (Base: +4) 
  • The effect ends automatically (Base: +8) 

Reduce Lethality


[Base: 12] 
Activation: Instantaneous 
You reduce the lethality of an incoming attack (either the very next attack after you activate, or one you are reacting to if you reduce the number of actions to a reaction), causing the attack to gain the nonlethal trait.

Sanctuary


Activation: Duration 
[Mental]

While this duration is active, any creature who attacks you must make a will save. The result will depend on which effect you pick: 
  • The creature can't attack you and wastes the action. It can't attempt further attacks against you this turn (Base: 16) 
  • The creature wastes the action and can't attempt to attack you for the rest of the duration. (Base: 20, mod +8)

Target Changing


Activation: Instantaneous 
Default Range: 30 feet 
Default Action Type: Reaction 

You change the target of non-area attack. Your new target must be a viable target (within range of the attack, within line of sight and effect, etc). 
Nature of change: 
  • You become the target of an attack (Base: 8) 
    • You must decide before the original target rolls a defense roll: (standard) 
    • You decide after you know the outcome, but make your own defense roll (Mod: +4)  
    • You decide after you know the outcome, but automatically get the same result that the original target (Mod: -4)  
  • Before defense is rolled, you select a new target of an attack as a reaction. Both target and attacker must be within range. (Base: 12) 
    • If this can redirect a portion of an area attack (Mod: +4) 
    • If this can redirect an entire area attack (Base: +4, Mod +8) 

Type of Attack: 
  • Strike (Mod -2) 
  • Any Guard (base) 
  • Any Reflex (Mod +2) 
  • Any Fortitude (Mod +4) 
  • Any Willpower (Mod +6) 
  • Any Attack (Base +4, Mod +4) 

Temporary Hit Points


Activation: Duration 
You gain temporary hit points. These last until they are removed, or until the duration has passed.  You cannot benefit from new temporary hit points until your old temporary hit points are removed. New applications of temporary hit points do not replenish or replace your current temporary hit points. 
  • 5 Temp HP (Base: 4) 
  • 10 Temp HP (Base: 8) 
  • 15 Temp HP (Base: 12) 
  • 20 Temp HP (Base: 16)

Affliction Recovery Effects


For any of the following, you must select Poison, Disease, or Curses when you create the ability. 

Reduce Affliction Category 
[Base: 8, Mod +2] 
Activation: Instantaneous
You reduce an affliction type by one category. 


Affliction Resistance
[Base: 8]
When you would normally reduce an affliction by a category (typically on a successful save) you reduce it by two categories instead 


Resist Virulent Afflictions 
[Base: 4] 
Virulent afflictions are normal to you. 

Concealment Effects


Gain Concealment
[Base: 12] 
Activation: Duration
You gain the concealed condition. 


Increase Concealment Check
[Base: 8]
Activation: Duration
You increase the flat check to hit you when concealed by 1 

Concealing Cloud 
[Base: 8]
Activation: Duration
Default Area: 10 foot emanation
You create a cloud of particulates of some kind, granting concealment to all creatures being targeted through the cloud in any way

Parry Effects


Parry with any weapon 
[Mod: 4]
You add the Parry trait to any melee weapon you wield 


Enhanced Parry 
[Base: 4] 
Weapons in your hands with the Parry Trait add an additional +1 to AC for you

Rase a Shield Effects

These effects modify the Raise a Shield move 


Better Bucklers
[Mod: 5]
Bucklers give you an additional +1 to AC when you raise them


Shields Give Reflex Bonus 
[Base: 8] 
Get your guard bonus when you Raise a Shield to Reflex Saves 


Shields Give All Saves Bonus 
[Base: 12] 
Get your guard bonus when you Raise a Shield to All Saves 


Alternate Item As Shield 
[Mod: 1] 
You can use an alternate item (not a weapon) as a shield, such as a tome or frying pan (specified when this feat is selected.) It gives +2 to AC if Raised, and has at least Hardness 3, HP 12, BT 6 if you Shield Block with it. 
  • Infuse Shield: Activation: Duration; You can also fuse a normal shield with your alternate item, giving your alternate item the Hardness, HP and BT of the shield. The default duration of this effect is Until your next daily preparations (10 points) 

Shield Block Effects

These effects modify your use of the Shield Block maneuver 


Hardened Shields 
Shields in your hands have extra hardness 
  • +1 Hardness (Mod: 2) 
  • +2 Hardness (Base: 4) 
  • +3 Hardness (Base: 8) 


Tougher Shields 
Shields in your hands have extra hit points. If you put the shield down, and pick it up again later, it still has the same remaining bonus hit points. 
  • +5 Hit Points  (Mod: 2) 
  • +10 Hit Points (Base: 4) 
  • +15 Hit Points (Base: 8) 


Shield Block for Ally 
[Mod: 4]
Can Shield Block for an adjacent ally 
  • You can evenly split the remaining damage after the Shield Block between the ally and yourself. (Mod +4) 


Shield Block Additional Types
You can Shield Block additional types of damage
  • Physical Reflex Attacks: (Mod: 4) 
  • All Defense Attacks, even Non-Physical Damage  (Base: 8)


Group Shield Block 
[Base: 4] 
When you use Shield Block against damage from an area of effect, adjacent allies who would take damage due to defenses against the same effect also benefit from the damage reduction.


Shield Absorbs Extra Damage
[Mod: 2] 
When you Shield Block, you can reduce the damage to yourself by double the shield's Hardness, but if you do, the shield takes double the normal amount of damage it would have taken (before applying its Hardness). You can't use if your shield can't be broken or destroyed 


Save Shield 
[Base: 12]
If a shield would be destroyed due to damage taken during your Shield Block, the shield remains intact at 1 Hit Point instead. 

Take Cover Effects


Improved Cover 
[Base: 12] 
When have cover, you improve it by one category (taking cover improves it based on its new category.)

Provide Improved Cover 
[Mod: 2] 
If you would provide lesser cover, you instead provide standard cover


Don't Provide Lesser Cover 
[Mod: 3] 
You don't provide lesser cover to enemies against your allies' attacks
