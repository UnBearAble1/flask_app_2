When you attack with a melee or ranged weapon (not a spell or magical special ability), and you roll a critical failure, you put yourself out of position. Roll 1d6 to see what penalty you gain. The penalty lasts only until it is applied once on a roll by you or against you, or until the end of your next round, whichever happens first: 

  1. Flat-Footed to anyone’s next attack 
  2. Flat-Footed to your target’s next attack 
  3. You trigger any possible reaction attacks (such as Attack of Opportunity) 
  4. Enfeebled on your next roll that it’s applicable 
  5. -10 to speed on next move action 
  6. Clumsy on the next roll that it’s applicable
