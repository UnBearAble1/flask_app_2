4 Points
Warrior; Hunt Prey. Specific Creatures
Prerequisites: Hunt Prey, Favored Enemy; Master in Survival
By adopting resonant reflections, you have learned to copy the abilities of your favored enemy as well. When you use Hunt Prey on a creature type named by your favored enemy  within 100 feet, you gain resistance 10 to all damage types that target has resistance or immunity to-  for as long as the foe remains within 100 feet.
