4 Points
Knowledge Skills; Trick Magic Item
Requires: Trick Magic Item Prerequisites trained in Arcana, Nature, Occultism, or Religion
You've studied scrolls in depth. This might have been a comprehensive education in formal setting, or the sort of education where you somehow obtain a number of scrolls and try not to explode anything you didn't mean to explode. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to skill checks to Trick scrolls. If you roll a critical failure to Trick a Magic Item that's a scroll, you get a failure instead.
