5 Points
Ancestral Relationships,  Enemies / Allies
Reaction; Usable Once Per day; Duration: Until the next time you use this ability 
Your people really know how to hold a grudge. If you take damage or take any negative effect from a harmful ability from a creature, you can utter a vow against all creatures like it. Until the next time you use this ability, you gain the following benefits when interacting with creatures of that type: 
  • +1 to Perception, Survival, and Intimidation checks against them.
  • +1 to all saving throws against them. 
  • if you roll a success on an Intimidation check to Coerce a member of your ancestral enemy's species, you get a critical success instead; if you roll a critical failure, you get a failure instead.
  • If you roll a success on a saving throw against a curse ability or a spell cast by them, you get a critical success instead.
  • If they dealt damage to you or any ally since your last turn, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to damage with weapons and unarmed attacks against them. 
    • If they successfully affect you or one of your allies within 60 feet with an enchantment or mind altering ability, you gain a +4 circumstance bonus to damage with weapons and unarmed attacks against them for a minute.
