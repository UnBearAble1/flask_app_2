30 Points
Religion; Spell-Like Ability
Prerequisite: Legendary in Religion
A life of evil has made you a nexus for your deity’s vile power. You’re continuously surrounded by a bane spell with an expert level. The radius is 15 feet, and you can’t increase it. You can Dismiss the spell; if you do, or if it is dispelled for any reason, it returns automatically after 1 minute.
