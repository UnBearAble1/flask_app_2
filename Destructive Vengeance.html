8 Points
Religion/Warrior; Champion's Reaction
Reaction; [Tactic]; Trigger An enemy within 15 feet damages you; Prerequisites: Master in Religion, Expert in Warrior
Bloodshed begets bloodshed as you drag your enemy toward oblivion. You increase the amount of damage you take by 3d6, and you deal 3d6 damage to the triggering enemy, choosing evil or negative damage to deal to the enemy each time you use this reaction. In addition, until the end of your next turn, your Strikes against the triggering creature deal 4 extra damage of the type you chose.
