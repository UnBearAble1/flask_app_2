3 Points
Survival; Favored Terrain
Requires: Favored Terrain
Your time spent in the hostile parts of the world has inured you to extreme climates. In your favored terrain, you gain resistance 5 to all environmental damage and are affected by temperature effects as though they were one step less severe (incredible heat or cold becomes extreme, extreme heat or cold becomes severe, and so on). When other creatures Follow the Expert with you as a guide in your favored terrain, they too treat temperature effects as one step less severe.
