1 Point
Professional; Teamwork
Prerequisites: Expert in Professional
Reaction; Trigger An ally you can see succeeds or critically succeeds at a Strength- or Dexterity-based skill check, using a skill in which you are untrained.
You are practiced at following the example of a more skilled ally to accomplish a wide variety of tasks. You are +0 to proficiency instead of -4 if you are untrained. This does not allow you to attempt trained actions using that skill unless you would otherwise be able to do so.

You retain this benefit until you use this ability again on a different skill.
