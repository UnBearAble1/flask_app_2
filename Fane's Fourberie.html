3 Points
Warrior; Improvised Weapons
1 Action; Stance; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior
Stella Fane has perfected a technique for using playing cards as weapons that's one part sleight of hand and one part magic. When you enter this stance, choose whether to treat playing cards in your possession as daggers, shuriken, or darts (note, this excludes them from being treated as improvised weapons for the purposes of other feats, but you do treat them as daggers, shuriken or darts for the purposes of other feats); you can wield a playing card in all ways as the chosen weapon until the stance ends. As long as the majority of the deck remains in your possession, any cards that are lost or thrown can be found after 1 minute. Otherwise, the remaining cards are likely lost or destroyed. A character who has this feat can enchant their deck of cards in all the same fashions available to other weapons.
