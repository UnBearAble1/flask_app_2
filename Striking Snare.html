MASTER
Consumable, Mechanical, Snare, Trap
Price 55 gp

You affix a trip line or other trigger to a group of either stones or wooden stakes to strike a creature that enters the snare’s square. The creature must attempt a DC 20 basic Reflex saving throw. If you choose stones, the snare deals 7d8 bludgeoning damage; if you choose spikes, it deals 7d8 piercing damage.
