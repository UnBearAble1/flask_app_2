MASTER
Alchemical, Consumable, Ingested, Poison, Sleep
Price 200 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate Single Action, Interact

Slumber wine sees its greatest use in games of intrigue, where an absence can be more devastating than injury. Due to its powerful Icreatures unconscious from slumber wine can't wake up by any means while the poison lasts, don't need to eat or drink while unconscious in this way, and appear to be recently dead unless an examiner succeeds at a DC 30 Medicine check.
Saving Throw DC 20 Fortitude; Onset 1 hour; Maximum Duration 7 days; 
  • Stage 1 unconscious (1 day); 
  • Stage 2 unconscious (2 days); 
  • Stage 3 unconscious (3 days).
