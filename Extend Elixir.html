5 Points
Alchemist; Elixers
Prerequisite: Trained in Aclhemist
When you consume one of your own alchemical elixirs created with Quick Alchemy or Advanced Alchemy, and a duration of 1 minute or longer, that elixir’s duration is doubled.

Boost: (+7 Points) Eternal Elixir (requires: Legendary in Alchemist). Your body readily accepts and retains minor changes. When you drink one of your trained-tier alchemical items made with Quick or Advanced Alchemy with a duration of 1 minute or more, you can make the elixir’s duration indefinite. If you later consume a different elixir and make it indefinite, the effect of the previous indefinite elixir ends.
