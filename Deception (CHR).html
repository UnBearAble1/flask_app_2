You can trick and mislead others using disguises, lies, and other forms of subterfuge.

Untrained Actions

  • Create a Diversion: With a gesture, a trick, or some distracting words, you can create a diversion that draws creatures' attention elsewhere.
  • Impersonate: You create a disguise to pass yourself off as someone or something you are not.
  • Lie: You try to fool someone with an untruth.
  • Persuade: Deception is one of the three approaches when you attempt to persuade someone of a truth or to taking your side.

Trained Actions

  • Feint: With a misleading flourish, you leave an opponent unprepared for your real attack.
