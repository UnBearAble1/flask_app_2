Warrior; Teamwork
Reaction; Trigger An enemy critically succeeds at a Strike against an adjacent ally; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior
You’ve trained to protect your allies by diverting the worst attacks to yourself. The enemy doesn’t double the damage from the triggering Strike but instead applies the normal damage to both you and your ally. Both of you take any effects that would happen on a hit that dealt damage, such as poison, but neither of you are subject to any effects that would happen only on a critical success.

Boost (+2 Points) Stave Off Catastrophe: When you use the Shoulder Catastrophe reaction, you can choose to take the full critical damage and effects from the attack, including effects that apply on a critical hit, and have your ally take no damage and avoid the attack's effects.
