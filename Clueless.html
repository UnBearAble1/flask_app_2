MENTAL
You don’t quite “get it” when it comes to social interactions. Maybe you are alien to common cultural interactions, or maybe you are the aloof genius type.  The good news is you are +4 to resist mundane attempts to level you with wit (like the Bon Mot feat) or charm you with sex appeal. The bad news is that you are -4 to any roll that requires sophisticated manners, and after a minute of interacting with you, you are -1 on reaction rolls with NPCs unless they are exceptionally charitable toward the clueless. Additionally, you get -4 to sense motive to understand if you’re the butt of someone’s jokes.
