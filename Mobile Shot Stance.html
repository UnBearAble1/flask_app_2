4 Points
Warrior; Ranged Weapons
1 Action; Stance ;Prerequisites Master in Warrior
Your shots become nimble and deadly. While you’re in this stance, You can use a reaction that normally allows you to make a melee weapon Strike to instead make a ranged weapon Strike. You must be Striking an adjacent target. If necessary for the reaction’s trigger, you treat your ranged weapon as if it had a reach of 5 feet. If the reaction has other requirements, such as wielding a specific kind of weapon, Mobile Shot Stance doesn’t allow you to ignore them The triggering creature must be within 5 feet of you for you to do so.
