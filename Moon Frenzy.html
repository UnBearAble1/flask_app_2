MASTER
Morph, Transmutation
Traditions primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets up to 5 willing creatures
Duration 1 minute

A feral aspect overcomes the targets, making them tough and savage. Targets gain 15 temporary Hit Points, a +15-foot status bonus to their Speeds, and weakness 10 to silver. They also grow vicious fangs and claws, which are unarmed attacks. The fangs deal 2d10 piercing damage; the claws deal 2d8 slashing damage and have the agile and finesse traits. The targets use the warrior proficiency with these attacks. On a critical hit with one of these unarmed attacks, the creature struck takes 1d6 persistent bleed damage.

The targets can't use concentrate actions unless those actions also have the rage trait, with the exception of Seek. A creature can attempt to end the spell's effect on itself by using a single action, which has the rage trait, to attempt a Will save against your spell DC; on a success, it ends the spell's effect on itself.

If a target is in the light of a full moon, it also grows by one size if it were Medium or smaller. This increases the reach of a Medium or Tiny creature by 5 feet.
