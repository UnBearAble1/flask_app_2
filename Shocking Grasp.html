TRAINED

Attack, Electricity, Evocation
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature

You shroud your hands in a crackling field of lightning. Make a melee spell attack roll. On a hit, the target takes 4d12 electricity damage. If the target is wearing metal armor or is made of metal, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your attack roll with shocking grasp, and the target also takes 1d12 persistent electricity damage on a hit. On a critical hit, double the initial damage, but not the persistent damage.

Heightened: The damage increases by 2d12, and the persistent electricity damage increases by 1d12.
