2 Points
Cultural Training; Aquatic

Your people are extremely gifted swimmers, though not quite amphibious or finned. You gain a number of benefits: 
  • When you roll a success to Swim, you get a critical success instead.
  • If you end your turn in water without having succeeded at a Swim action that round, you don't sink (but you might still be moved by the current).
  • When you fall into water at least 10 feet deep, you take no falling damage regardless of the distance.
  • You can hold your breath for 25 times as long as usual before suffocating. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saving throws against inhaled threats, such as inhaled poisons, and if you roll a success on such a saving throw, you get a critical success instead.
