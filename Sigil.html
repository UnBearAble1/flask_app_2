Cantrip, Transmutation
Traditions arcane, divine, occult, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature or object; Duration One year (see below)

You harmlessly place your unique magical sigil, which is about 1 square inch in size, on the targeted creature or object. The mark can be visible or invisible, and you can change it from one state to another by using an Interact action to touch the target. The mark can be scrubbed or scraped off with 5 minutes of work. If it's on a creature, it fades naturally over the course of a week, on an object it fades after one year.
