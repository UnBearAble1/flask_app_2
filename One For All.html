5 Points
Diplomacy; Teamwork
Prerequisites Master in Diplomacy;
1 Action; Auditory, Concentrate, Emotion, Linguistic, Mental
With precisely the right words of encouragement, you bolster an ally's efforts. Designate an ally within 30 feet; this action counts as sufficient preparation to Aid that ally. When you use the Aid reaction to help that ally, you can roll Diplomacy in place of the usual check.
