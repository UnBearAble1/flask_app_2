MASTER
Necromancy, Poison
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Saving Throw Fortitude

You replicate the attack of a deadly purple worm. You deal 4d6 piercing damage to the touched creature and afflict it with purple worm venom. The target must attempt a Fortitude save.
  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target takes 3d6 poison damage.
  • Failure The target is afflicted with purple worm venom at stage 1.
  • Critical Failure The target is afflicted with purple worm venom at stage 2.

Purple Worm Venom (poison); Maximum Duration 6 rounds. 
  • Stage 1 3d6 poison damage and enfeebled 2 (1 round); 
  • Stage 2 4d6 poison damage and enfeebled 2 (1 round); 
  • Stage 3 6d6 poison damage and enfeebled 2 (1 round).
