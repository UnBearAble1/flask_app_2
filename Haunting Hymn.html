Auditory, Cantrip, Evocation, Sonic
Traditions divine, occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Area 15-foot cone; Saving Throw Fortitude

You echo a jarring hymn that only creatures in the area can hear. The hymn deals 3d6 + spellcasting ability modifier sonic damage; with a basic Fortitude save. If a target critically fails the save, it's also deafened for 1 minute.
