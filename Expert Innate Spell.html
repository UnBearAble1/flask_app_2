12 Points
Natural Abilities; Spell-Like Ability
Pick an expert-tier spell, you can cast it as an innate spell once per day. It has a tradition of your choice. The DC or spellpower checks are based on the higher of your Weapon Proficiency or Spellcasting Proficiency. If you have an extremely related Basic Innate Spell.  reduce the cost of this feat by 3 pts (for example, if your basic innate spell is burning hands, and your expert innate spell is fireball, the DM may allow you to take 3 points off the cost of the Expert Innate Spell.)
