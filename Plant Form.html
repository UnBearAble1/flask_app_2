MASTER
Polymorph, Transmutation
Traditions primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Duration 1 minute

Taking inspiration from verdant creatures, you transform into a plant battle form. This spell works exactly like Animal Form, except you can assume these forms instead: 

  • Counteflora
  • Dezullon

Heightened: Legendary:  You can assume these forms instead: 

  • Drakauthix
  • Giant Flytrap
