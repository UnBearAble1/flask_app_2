MASTER
Polymorph, Transmutation
Traditions occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Duration 1 minute

Harnessing the power of vile entities, you transform yourself into an aberration battle form. This spell works exactly like Animal Form, except you can assume these forms instead: 

  • Gug
  • Ofalth

Heightened: Legendary:  You can assume these forms instead: 

  • Gogiteth
  • Quoppopak
