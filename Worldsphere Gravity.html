9 Points
Spellcasting; Metamagic 
One Action; Metamagic, Wizard; Frequency once per hour; Prerequisites: Legendary in Spellcaster 

Though creating demiplanes usually requires lengthy rituals, you know how to establish minor planar effects.  If your next action is to Cast a Spell of at least Master-Tier from your repetoire that affects an area, you can apply one of the following planar effects to a 20-foot burst centered within the spell's area. The effect persists for 1 minute.
  • Gravitational Morass: The pull of gravity is amplified here, creating greater difficult terrain. Flying creatures that pass through the area must succeed at a Reflex save against your spell DC or fall to the ground, taking bludgeoning damage equal to half the distance fallen.
  • Flowing Time:  Erratic timestreams aid you within this space. A creature that starts its turn in the burst is affected by haste on that turn.
