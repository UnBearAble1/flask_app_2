You are trained in a particular set of skills favored by thieves and miscreants.

Untrained Actions

  • Palm an Object: You palm a small, unattended object. 
  • Steal: You try to take a small object from another creature without being noticed. 

Trained Actions

  • Disable Device: This action allows you to disarm a trap or another complex device.
  • Pick a Lock: You attempt to open a lock without a key i
