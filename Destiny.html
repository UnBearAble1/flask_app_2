-1 or -2 points

SUPERNATURAL
You are destined to take part in a story, and it will not go well for you. As soon as your destiny turns out true, you have a few options: 
  • You buy it off with points. 
  • You permanently lose the value of your Destiny in Class Feats 
  • Gain the Unlucky Complication equal to the value of your Destiny. 

The DM should be prepared to give you enough time to bank up the points you got in Destiny before thrusting your ill fate upon you. It’s only fair, given that things aren’t going to be good for you. 

Minor Disadvantage: [-1] You probably won’t die in your part of the story, but you will very likely suffer a setback or a failure. It’s likely to be embarrassing and not fun. 

Major Disadvantage: [-2] You are threatened to play a terrible role in a terrible turn of events. Maybe you’ll be the one who is late with a message that would have saved the day, maybe you assassinate the wrong person and cause the collapse of a society. But you’ll survive.
