Price 3 sp; Damage 2d4 P; Bulk L
Hands 1
Category Simple
Group Knife; Traits Agile, Deadly d6, Monk, Uncommon

Also known as punching daggers, katars are characterized by their H-shaped hand grip that allows the blade to jut out from the knuckles.

Agile:
The multiple attack penalty you take with this weapon on the second attack on your turn is –4 instead of –5, and –8 instead of –10 on the third and subsequent attacks in the turn.

Deadly d6:
On a critical hit, the weapon adds a weapon damage die of the listed size. Roll this after doubling the weapon's damage. 

Monk: 
This weapon is favored by those who use ki arts.
