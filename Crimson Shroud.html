9 Points
Warrior; Red Mantis
1 Action; Frequency: A number of times per day equal to your number of Red Mantis feats; Prerequisites: Red Mantis Assassin, Master in Warrior
You swathe yourself in a veil of red mist for 1 minute. While the shroud is active, you gain fast healing 5 (Expert-Tier effect). You can Interact with your shroud, swirling it around you, to gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC until the beginning of your next turn. Finally, if you die while the shroud is active, you can choose to have your entire body vanish into red mist, leaving behind only your gear; you make this choice when you activate Crimson Shroud.
