3 Points
Acrobatics; Combat Mobility
Reaction; Prerequisite: Expert in Acrobatics; Requirements You're aware of the attack and aren't flat-footed; Trigger You are the target of a melee attack.
You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC against the triggering attack. If the attack misses you, you can Step after the Strike. If you're a master in Acrobatics, you can move 10 feet on this Step instead of 5 feet.
