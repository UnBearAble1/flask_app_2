You are educated in the fields of magic, religion, the planes, and spellcasting, and so can identify magic items and spells and make magic items yourself.

Trained Actions

  • Decipher Writing about arcane theory.
  • Recall Knowledge about alchemical theory, arcane symbols, deities, magic traditions, the planes, religious traditions and symbols, and related topics. Includes constructs with the magical subtype, dragons, fey, magical beasts, outsiders, and undead.
  • Identify Magic, You can identify spells being cast, magical effects, or magical items.  
  • Disable Device on magical traps and arcane locks. 
  • Craft Magic Item: You can craft magical items using the crafting rules. 
  • Repair: You can repair magical items.
