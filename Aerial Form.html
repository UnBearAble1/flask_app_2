Polymorph, Transmutation
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions somatic, verbal
Duration 1 minute

You call upon primal energy to transform yourself into an aerial animal battle form. This spell works exactly like Animal Form, except you can assume these forms instead: 

  • Griffon 
  • Giant Dragonfly 

Heightened: Master:  You can assume these forms instead: 

  • Giant Mosquito 
  • Giant Eagle
