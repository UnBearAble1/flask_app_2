MASTER
Conjuration
Traditions divine
Three Actions; material, somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

You conjure a chaotic celestial, monitor, or fiend to fight on your behalf. You can't summon a creature unless its alignment is one of your deity's preferred alignments (or, if you don't have a deity, is within one step of your alignment). This works like summon animal, except you summon one of the creatures below, instead: 

  • Lilend (CG) 
  • Naunet (CN) 
  • Nabasu (CE) 

Heightened (legendary): You may instead summon one of the following:

  • Einherjar (CN) 
  • Garuda (CG) 
  • Vrock (CE)
