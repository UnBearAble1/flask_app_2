1 Point
Physiology; Senses

Your people have a long history of group ambush tactics. You gain the following benefits: 
  • As long as you're aware of a creature, once per round, you can Point Out the creature to all allies as a free action. 
  • Additionally, Willing allies that you are aware of within 60 feet that would otherwise be undetected by you are instead hidden from you. 
  • The flat check for you to target willing allies within 60 feet that are hidden from you is 5 instead of 11.
