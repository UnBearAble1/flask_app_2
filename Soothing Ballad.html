9 Points
Performance; Composition Spell
Prerequisites: Composition Spells
You soothe your allies’ wounds with the power of your performance. You learn the soothing ballad composition spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Soothing Ballad; Composition, Emotion, Enchantment, Healing, Mental
Special: The healing option of this spell costs 2 focus points to use.
2 Actions ; somatic, verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets you and up to 3 allies
You draw upon your muse to soothe your allies. Choose one of the following three effects:
  • The spell attempts to counteract fear effects on the targets.
  • The spell attempts to counteract effects imposing paralysis on the targets.
  • The spell restores 5d8 Hit Points to the targets. (Expert-Tier effect) 

Special: You can increase this spell to a Master-Tier effect. If you are legendary in Performance, you can instead increase it to a Legendary-Tier effect, increasing it's healing power to 6d8, but it costs 3 focus points to cast. After using either of these effects, you are unable to use either again until you have had 2d4 full rests.
