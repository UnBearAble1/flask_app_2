Threat: 5

Tigers are solitary and territorial hunters, using their striped hides to blend into the forests and jungles they call home and preferring to attack with surprise.

Recall Knowledge - Animal (Nature)

N, Large, Animal, Skirmisher
Perception +9; low-light vision, scent (imprecise) 30 feet
Skills Acrobatics +8, Athletics +10, Stealth +8
Str +5, Dex +3, Con +3, Int -4, Wis +2, Cha -2
AC 19; Fort +8, Ref +10, Will +6
HP: 60
Speed 30 feet

Features: 
Sneak Attack: The tiger deals 1d6 extra precision damage to flat-footed creatures.

Actions: 
  • Jaws; Melee Single Action; +10 [+5/+0], Damage 2d8+5 piercing plus Grab
  • Claws; Melee Single Action; +10 [+6/+2] (agile), Damage 2d6+5 slashing
  • Pounce; Single Action; The tiger Strides and makes a Strike at the end of that movement. If the tiger began this action hidden, it remains hidden until after this ability's Strike.
  • Wrestle; Single Action; The tiger makes a claw Strike against a creature it is grabbing. If the attack hits, that creature is knocked prone.
