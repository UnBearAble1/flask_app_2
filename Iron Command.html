12 Points
Religion/Warrior; Champion's Reaction
Reaction; [Divine, Emotion, Enchantment, Mental, Tactic];  Trigger An enemy within 15 feet damages you; Prerequisites: Master in Religion, Expert in Warrior
You put an impertinent foe who dared harm you in their proper place. You command your enemy to kneel before you in obedience. If they dare to refuse, they must pay the price in pain and anguish. The foe must choose one of the following options.
  • The enemy kneels, dropping prone as a free action.
  • The enemy refuses, and you deal 3d6 mental damage to it.

In addition, your Strikes against the triggering creature deal 2 extra damage until the end of your next turn. You choose whether the damage type is evil or negative each time you use this reaction.
