4 points 
Warrior/Spellcaster
Reaction; Prerequisites: Spell Parry
Trigger: You're targeted by a Strike or a spell attack roll, or you're about to roll a saving throw against a spell effect;  Requirements: You have one or more hands free.

You snap your weapon or limb into place to ward off harm at the last second. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to AC and saving throws against the triggering effect until the start of your next turn.
