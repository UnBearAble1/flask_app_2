MASTER
Consumable, Contact, Divine, Poison
Price 50 gp
Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk L
Activate one action, Interact

Cultivated by mashing and fermenting toxic weeds found in lonely graveyards, fearweed is a greenish paste magically infused with psychic horror. The frightened condition from fearweed can't be removed while the poison lasts.

Saving Throw DC 20 Fortitude; Onset 1 minute; Maximum Duration 6 minutes; 
  • Stage 1 2d6 poison damage and frightened 1 (1 minute); 
  • Stage 2 3d6 poison damage and frightened 2 (1 minute); 
  • Stage 3 4d6 poison damage and frightened 3 (1 minute)
