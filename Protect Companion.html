Abjuration, Cantrip
Traditions arcane, divine, occult, primal
One Action; verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets A creature with the minion trait under your control
Duration until the start of your next turn

You extend your aura, as a magical shield that protects your  minion. The target gains a +1 circumstance bonus to AC until the start of your next turn. You gain the following reaction; after using the reaction, the spell ends and you can't cast protect companion again for 10 minutes.

Life Block
Reaction; Trigger The spell's target would take damage; 
Effect Reduce the triggering damage by up to 30, but you lose half as many hit points. Even if this reduces the damage to 0, the target still takes any effects that would come with the damage, such as the poison on a viper's fangs Strike.
