Athletics allows you to perform deeds of physical prowess. When you use the Escape basic action, you can use your Athletics modifier instead of your unarmed attack modifier.

Untrained Actions: 

  • Leap: You make a short, careful jump. 
  • Climb: You move up, down, or across an incline.
  • Grapple: You attempt to grab a creature or object with your free hand. 
  • High Jump: You Stride, then make a vertical Leap.
  • Long Jump: You Stride, then make a horizontal Leap.
  • Swim: You propel yourself through water.
