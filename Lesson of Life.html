You temporarily bolster the target's vitality. The target gains fast healing 5. 

Special: If you are Master tier in Occultism, you can cast this spell as a Master-Tier effect (increasing its fast healing to 6). If you are Legendary tier, you can cast this as a Legendary-Tier effect (increasing its fast healing to 7), but it costs 3 Focus Points. If you use either of these options, you cannot use either again until you have completed 2d4 full rests.
