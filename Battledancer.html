Free with the Panache feat
Warrior; Panache
Prerequisites: Trained in Performance;  Fascinating Performance 
To you, a fight is a kind of performance art, and you command your foes' attention with mesmerizing motions. You gain panache during an encounter when the result of your Performance check to Perform exceeds the Will DC of an observing foe, even if the foe isn't fascinated. If you successfully hit with a finisher: You can Step as a free action immediately after the finisher.
