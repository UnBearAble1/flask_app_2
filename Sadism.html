-3 points

MENTAL, CHAOTIC
You take immense pleasure in cruelty, and inflicting pain and punishment. This could be mental or physical, or both. You must make a self control roll when you have a chance to inflict pain but it would be better not to (such as when you're holding a captive that must be returned unharmed) or you inflict it anyway. You aren't necessarily a bully, nor evil, but those people you would otherwise harm  you really want to harm in extra ways. 

Anyone who witnesses your sadism and is opposed to that sort of thing will have a -3 reaction roll penalty.
