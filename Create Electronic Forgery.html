DOWNTIME, SECRET
You can use Computers to forge official electronic documents (like official electronic mail, secured document files, or deepfakes) or credentials (like passwords, key cards or biometric data). This process usually takes from a day to a week, and will likely require something to copy or base your work off of. You don't make a check at the time. Instead, whenever someone (or a system) reads the forgery, you make a check versus their Computers or Investigation skill DC. 

Success: The observer does not detect the forgery.
Failure: The observer knows your document is a forgery.
