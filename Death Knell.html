TRAINED

Death, Necromancy
Traditions: divine, occult
Cast Two Actions;  somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 living creature that has 0 HP; Saving Throw Will

You snuff the life out of a creature on the brink of death. The target must attempt a Will save. If this kills it, you gain 10 temporary HP and a +1 status bonus to attack and damage rolls for 10 minutes.

  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target's dying value increases by 1d3
  • Failure The target dies.
  • Critical Failure: The target dies, and cannot be restored to life unless the creature restoring it also succeeds on a counteract check versus your DC
