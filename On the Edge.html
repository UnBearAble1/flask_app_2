MENTAL, CHAOTIC
You have no regard for your own safety when it comes to facing danger. In fact, you prefer to face down overwhelming odds when the opportunity strikes. Make a self control roll whenever you face a life threatening situation, such as piloting a burning vehicle or facing down a gang or bloodthirsty dwarves when you are wholly unprepared. If you fail, you may not back down from the challenge, though you can make your self-control check after each critical success you make in the challenge. 

In combat, make a self-control roll every time you take your turn. If you fail, you must spend all your actions recklessly attacking or performing some other obviously suicidal actions. 

Most people who witness this behavior are -2 on reaction rolls. Individuals who value bravery over self-preservation (GM’s decision) will react at +2.
