1 Point
Warrior; Armor
Prerequisite: Expert in Warrior; Trained in Athletics
Reaction; Requirements You are wearing medium armor or heavier. Trigger An adjacent foe critically fails an attack roll to Strike you with a melee weapon or unarmed attack.
You rebuff puny attacks with your armor, knocking back your foe. Attempt an Athletics check to Shove the triggering foe, even if you don't have a hand free. If you succeed, you can't Stride to follow the foe as normal with shove, as you're knocking the foe back with the rebounded attack, not by physically moving towards them.
