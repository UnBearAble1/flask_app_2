Reaction;  Trigger A creature you are aware of critically succeeds on a Strike against you and would deal damage to you. [ Emotion, Mental, Visual ]
The attacking creature takes a -12 circumstance penalty to the damage of the triggering Strike. This penalty applies after doubling the damage for a critical hit. The attacker is then immune to your Cause Hesitation for 24 hours.
