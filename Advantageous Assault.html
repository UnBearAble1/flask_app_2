3 Points
Warrior; Special Attacks
1 Action, Press; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior
When an enemy’s movement is compromised, you deliver a more deadly blow. Make a Strike against a creature that is grabbed, prone, or restrained. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to damage on this Strike, or +4 if you’re wielding the weapon in two hands. The Strike gains the following failure effect.
  • Failure You deal 2 damage to the target or 4 damage if you’re wielding the weapon in two hands. This damage has the same damage type as the weapon.
