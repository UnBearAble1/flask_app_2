4 Points
Professional; Skills
Prerequisites: Master at Professional
Reaction; Trigger You gain no information from a Recall Knowledge check (usually because you failed the secret check) using one of the two skills you chose for this feat.
You immediately reattempt the triggering check using the other chosen skill. Special: When you gain this feat, choose two of the following skills: Arcana, Crafting, Lore (any one), Medicine, Nature, occultism, Religion, or Society.
