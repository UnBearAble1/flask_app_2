MASTER
Conjuration
Traditions occult
Three Actions; material, somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

You conjure an aberration to fight on your behalf. This works like summon animal, except you summon one of the creatures below, instead: 

  • Chuul 
  • Destrachan
Heightened (legendary): You may instead summon one of the following:

  • Gug 
  • Ofalth
