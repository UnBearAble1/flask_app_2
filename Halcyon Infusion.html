MASTER
Divination, Mental
Traditions arcane, primal
Cast 10 minutes (material, somatic, verbal)
Range touch; Targets one willing creature 
Duration: Special

You infuse magical energy into your target, opening up their mind to the pathways of magic and granting them the ability to use magic to affect the world around them. Choose a trained tier spell you that's in your repertoire, of the same tradition you used to cast halcyon infusion. The spell must be one that has a listed range. If the target has your appropriate spellcasting attribute at at least 14, the spell can be expert tier. The spell lasts for one day per point of your spellcasting attribute they have above 8, or until they successfully cast the spell, whichever comes first. 

They cast the spell in the same way you do, including rolling a spellcasting check and gathering strain, but they use your numbers for all purposes in the spell (including saving throw DC or spell attack.)
