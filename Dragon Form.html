MASTER
Polymorph, Transmutation
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Duration 1 minute

Calling upon powerful transformative magic, you gain a dragon battle form. This spell works exactly like Animal Form, except you can assume these forms instead: 

  • Frost Drake 
  • Desert Drake

Heightened: Legendary:  You can assume these forms instead: 

  • Young Copper Dragon 
  • Young Red Dragon 
  • Young Blue Dragon
