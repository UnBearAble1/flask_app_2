4 Points
Warrior; Armor
Prerequisite: Expert in Warrior
When wearing light or medium armor, you can rest normally, rather than receiving poor rest that leaves you fatigued. If you are a master in warrior, you can rest in heavy armor this way.

Additionally, if you are trained in either Athletics or Warfare Lore, You can attempt an Athletics or Warfare Lore check with a DC determined by the GM (but usually Trained for common armor, Expert for uncommon armor, and Master for rare armor) to halve the time you take to don armor. You can halve an ally's time to don armor by working with them to don the armor and succeeding at an Athletics or Warfare Lore check against the same DC.
