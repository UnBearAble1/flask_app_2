Price 4 gp; AC Bonus +3; Dex Cap +2; Check Penalty -2; Speed Penalty -5 ft.
Strength 14; Bulk 2; Group Composite; Traits —

Scale mail consists of many metal scales sewn onto a reinforced leather backing, often in the form of a long shirt that protects the torso, arms, and legs.

Armor Specialization Effect (Granted by some feats and other abilities when wearing medium or heavy armor)
Composite: The numerous overlapping pieces of this armor protect you from piercing attacks. You gain resistance to piercing damage 1 for medium armor, or 2 for heavy armor.
