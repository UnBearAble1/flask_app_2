Warrior; Combat Mobility
2 Actions; Flourish; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior, Expert in Acrobatics
Either Stride twice or attempt an Acrobatics check (DC determined by the GM, but usually expert) to swing on a rope up to twice your Speed. If you boarded or disembarked from a boat or similar vehicle during this movement, you can make a melee Strike that deals one additional weapon damage die.
