These effects are all connected to fooling someone or appearing to be a different creature. 

Alter Memories


Activation: Duration 
[Mental] 
Default Duration: 1 minute / 24 Hours 
Default Range: Touch 

You alter or suppress the memories of a target creature 

  • The creature always gets a Willpower defense, though it can choose to fail.
  •  It isn't forced to react to the new memories in a particular way, and it's likely to question them if they contradict other information it knows or are implausible for the situation. 
  • This effect doesn’t include the ability to read the memories of the target, but you can specify a time range, and you can get more abstract than this if you work with the “includes unknown information” boost. 
  • This effect cannot be used in combat

The default duration of the memory altered is 1 minute, and the default duration the memories remain altered is 24 hours - both can be changed with Limits and Boosts.
The price is based on the amount of time since the memory occurred:  
  • Up to one minute ago (Base: 8) 
  • Up to one hour ago (Base: 8, Mod +2) 
  • Up to one day ago (Base: 8, Mod +6) 
  • Up to one week ago (Base: 8, Mod +12) 
  • Up to one month ago (Base: 12, Mod +8) 
  • Up to one year ago (Base: 16, Mod +8) 
  • Any time during the creature’s life (Base: 16, Mod +14).

Creature Impersonation


[Base: 8]
Linked Skill: Deception

You can use Impersonate to disguise yourself as creatures that otherwise you wouldn’t logically be able to (they must be no larger than one size bigger than you), enough to even convince creatures of that type. If the creatures are mindless, they don’t get a chance to see through the disguise. 

Fool Magical Detection Effects


Divination Countermeasures 
[Base: 8, Mod +2]
Linked Skill: Deception
Detection, revelation, or scrying effects detect nothing from you or your possessions and auras. 


Cover Identity 
[Base: 4, Mod +3]
Linked Skill: Deception
Activation: Duration
You can create a type of persona, such as ‘nonspecific civilian’, ‘dangerous assassin’, or the like - getting as specific or general as you like. When a creature attempts to use a detection, revelation or scrying effect on you, your possessions or auras, assuming it has not actually affected you, they receive information to lead them to believe you match your specified persona. 


Feign Mental Malady 
[Base: 4, Mod -2] 
Linked Skill: Deception 
Default Action: Reaction 
When a creature attempts to use a mental effect on you, as a reaction, you can falsify whether or not it has worked on you.

Pickpocketing Effects


Incredible Pickpocketing 
[Base: 4] 
Linked Skill: Thievery 
You can Steal or Palm an Object that’s closely guarded, such as in a pocket, using the same DC as stealing something worn on a belt. 

Undetected Pickpocketing 
[Base: 4] 
Linked Skill: Thievery 
Taking a single Palm an Object or Steal action doesn’t end your undetected condition. 

Unbelievable Pickpocketing 
[Base: 12, Mod -4] 
Linked Skill: Thievery 
You can attempt to Steal something that is actively wielded or that would be extremely noticeable or time consuming to remove (like worn shoes or armor) using the same DC as stealing something on a belt. if the item is extremely prominent—like a suit of full plate armor—onlookers will quickly notice it’s gone after you steal it. 

Spycraft Effects


Disguised Language
[Mod: 2] 
Linked Skill: Deception
You can convey a simple message consisting of three basic words (such as “Danger assassin flee” or “Meet me moonrise”) hidden in your normal dialogue. Your intended listener, if they know you have this ability and have practiced with you, can discern these messages. 


Clandestine Decipher Writing 
[Mod: 4] 
Linked Skill: Society
You are able to Decipher Writing on a message that is only partially glimpsed, upside down or reversed from your perspective, or even sealed. When using this effect to decipher sealed letters, your attempt to Decipher Writing gains the manipulate trait. This effect doesn't prevent witnesses from noticing your efforts, particularly with sealed letters which you must manipulate directly in order to read; so you might need to attempt Deception or Stealth checks to avoid being noticed. 


Tricking Identifying Magic Effects


Deceptive Spellcasting
[Base: 8, Mod -2]
Linked Skill: Deception
When you cast spells, you can make it seem like you are casting different spells of your choice. 


Charlatan 
[Base: 8, Mod -4] 
Linked skill: Deception 
When you use magic items, you can make it seem like you are generating the effect yourself (including the specifics of how, such as making it seem like you are casting a spell).
