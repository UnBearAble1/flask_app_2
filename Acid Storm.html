MASTER
Acid, Evocation
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 120 feet; Area 20-foot-radius burst
Duration 1 minute

You evoke a storm of acid rain that pelts the area for the spell’s duration. A creature that begins its turn in the area takes 5d8 acid damage (basic Reflex save).

Heightened: The damage increases by 2d8.
