LEGENDARY
Alchemical, Consumable, Contact, Poison 
Price 100 gp Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L 
Activate one action, Interact

This oil, which is pressed from vrock spores and mixed with a combination of acacia ash and thistle seeds, seeps into the skin of living creatures, initially inspiring irritability that unpredictably manifests as berserk rage.

Saving Throw DC 22 Fortitude; Onset 1d6 minutes; Maximum Duration 6 rounds; 
  • Stage 1 2d6 mental damage, quickened 1, attack nearby creatures as if confused (1 round); 
  • Stage 2 3d6 mental damage, attack nearby creatures as if confused (1 round); 
  • Stage 3 4d6 mental damage, fatigued, attack nearby creatures as if confused (1 round)
