9 Points
Warrior; Ranged Weapons
3 Actions; Frequency once per day; Prerequisites Eldritch Archer ; Legendary in Warrior
You modify an arrow or bolt to bring death to your target in a single potent hit. Make a bow Strike. On a hit, you deal double damage. On a critical hit, the target must also succeed at a Fortitude saving throw or be immediately slain; this save has the death and incapacitation traits.
