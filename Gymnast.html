Free with the Panache Feat
Warrior; Panache
Prerequisites: trained in Athletics.
You reposition, maneuver, and bewilder your foes with daring feats of physical prowess. You gain panache during an encounter whenever you successfully Grapple, Shove, or Trip a foe. 

If you successfully hit with a finisher: If the foe is grabbed, restrained, or prone, you gain a +4 circumstance bonus to the damage roll.
