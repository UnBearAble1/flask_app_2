MASTER
Earth, Evocation, Fire
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Area 120-foot line; Saving Throw Reflex

A thin crack rips through the earth, erupting with magma, then swiftly seals up. Each creature along the line and on solid ground takes 5d6 bludgeoning damage and 6d6 fire damage with a Reflex save.

  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature takes half damage.
  • Failure The creature takes full damage, and it falls prone.
  • Critical Failure The target takes double damage, and it falls prone.

Heightened: The damage increases by 2d6 bludgeoning and 2d6 fire.
