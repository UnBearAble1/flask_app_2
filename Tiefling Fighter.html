Combat Abilities:
  • Tiefling Fire Resistance: [Magical]  If you take fire damage, you can use a reaction to reduce it by 1d8 points.
  • Trip of Opportunity: [Mechanical] If any creature within reach use a move or manipulate action, you can attempt to trip them.
  • Power Attack: [Mechanical] For 2 actions, you can make a strike with a weapon that deals normal damage +2d6 additional damage of the weapon's type, or +4d6 additional damage on a critical hit.  
  • Defensive Stance: [Mechanical, Stance, Open]: Focus Spell: Spend an action and a focus point to enter this stance, and for the rest of combat shields in your hand are +2 Hardness and +10 Hit Points. You also immediately Raise your Shield as part of entering this stance.
  • Come Get Some: [Mechanical] (1 action): Once per minute, If you are in Defensive Stance, you can spend an action and target three creatures within 30 feet of you. Those creatures automatically marked by you, meaning they are -2 to any attacks that don't target you. The duration of this effect is 1 minute.
  • Quick Draw: [Mechanical] Once per day, you can draw a weapon as a free action.
  • Respiratory Smash: [Mechanical, Flourish] (1 action) Target: Any marked creature. Requires: You have your shield raised. 
    • You lash out with your shield, slamming into a creature in such a way to limit their ability to breathe and shove them backward. Make a shove attempt.
      • Success: You push your target back 5 feet. You can Stride after it, but you must move the same distance and in the same direction. The target also begins to suffocate for 1d6 rounds. At any time they can spend an action to try and clear the obstruction. If they do so, you must make a check versus their athletics - on a success, they continue to suffocate, on a failure, they can breathe normally again.
      • Critical Success: You push your target up to 10 feet away from you. You can Stride after it, but you must move the same distance and in the same direction. The target also begins to suffocate for 1d10 rounds. At any time they can spend an action to try and clear the obstruction. If they do so, you must make a check versus their athletics - on a success, they continue to suffocate, on a failure, they can breathe normally again. Additionally, they take 2d6 persistent poison damage as they bleed internally (this is cleared separately from the suffocation.) 
      • Critical Failure: You lose your balance, fall, and land prone.
  • Leave It In: [Mechanical, Press] (1 action):  Usable Once Per Hour. Requires: You have successfully hit an adjacent creature with a strike this turn, and you must release the weapon you are wielding. For the duration of this effect, you cannot reclaim your weapon. Make a Reflex Attack. 
    • Success: The creature takes 1d8+10 piercing damage, and is Clumsy 2 for 1d6 rounds. 
      • The creature can attempt to pull the weapon out by using an action. If they do so, make a check versus their Athletics. If you succeed, the weapon stays in. If you fail, they pull the weapon out and it drops to the ground. 
    • Critical Success: The creature takes 1d12+10 piercing damage, and is Clumsy 4 for 1d6 rounds. 
      • Same rules as success regarding pulling the weapon out.
  • Resist Sweeps: [Mechanical] You are +1 to defend against any effect that knocks you prone
  • Nonlethal Expert: [Mechanical] You do not take the -2 to attack when making nonlethal strikes with a lethal weapon.
  • Limit Tactics: [Mechanical, Mental, Auditory] Range: 120 feet. Defense: Will 
    • You should a targeted battle cry at a foe, shaking them into inaction. Make a Will attack: 
      • Success: The target cannot take cover for 1d4 rounds
      • Critical Success: The target cannot take cover for 1d10 rounds
  • Deafening Blow: [Mechanical] Range: Touch, Defense: Fortitude 
    • You smash a foe in the ear, damaging their ability to hear. Make a Fortitude Attack. 
      • Success: The target is deafened for one minute. They can spend an action to attempt to end the effect, forcing you to attempt a check versus their Medicine DC. If you fail, they are no longer deafened. 
      • Critical Success: As Success, but they must spend two actions to attempt to end the effect. 


Utility Abilities:
  • Quick Armor: [Magical] (Free Action): Requires: A focus item- a smalls statue of an armored figure. 
    • You instantly don your armor, which must be adjacent to you.
  • Living Compass: [Mechanical] You always know which way is north.
  • Quick Climb: [Mechanical] You climb 5 additional feet on a success, 10 on a critical, to a maximum of your speed.
  • Stick the Landing: [Mechanical] When you fall, you don't land prone.
  • Superior Balance: [Mechanical] When balancing, you aren't flat-footed. Further, you treat Critical Failures on Balance checks as Failures.
  • Superior Jumping: [Mechanical] On long and high jump checks, you treat Critical Failures as Failures. Additionally, once per minute [Encounter] or once per 2 hours [Exploration], you can either: 
    • leap and long jump 10 additional feet horizontally
    • leap and high jump 5 feet up vertically
