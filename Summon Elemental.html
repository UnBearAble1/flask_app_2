TRAINED

Conjuration
Traditions arcane, primal
Three Actions material, somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

You conjure an elemental to fight for you. This works like summon animal, except you summon one of the creatures below, instead: 

  • Mist Stalker 
  • Filth Fire
  • Earthen Destrier 
  • Living Thunderclap 
 
Heightened (expert): You may instead summon one of the following: 

  • Living Landslide 
  • Living Waterfall 
  • Living Whirlwind 
  • Living Wildfire 

Heightened (master): You may instead summon one of the following:

  • Belker 
  • Blizzardborn
  • Sand Sentry
  • Striding Fire

Heightened (legendary): You may instead summon one of the following:

  • Invisible Stalker
  • Quatoid 
  • Salamander
  • Xorn
