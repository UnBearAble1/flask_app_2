TRAINED

Conjuration
Traditions primal
Three Actions; material, somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

You conjure a plant or fungus to fight for you.  This works like summon animal, except you summon one of the creatures below, instead: 

  • Arboreal Warden
  • Calathgar
  • Myceloid
 
Heightened (expert): You may instead summon one of the following: 

  • Awakened Tree
  • Basidirond
  • Wizard Sponge

Heightened (master): You may instead summon one of the following:

  • Arboreal Reaper
  • Tendriculos
  • Scythe Tree

Heightened (legendary): You may instead summon one of the following:

  • Counteflora
  • Dezullon
  • Drakauthix
