TRAINED
Uncommon, Consumable, Mechanical, Snare, Trap
Price 9 gp

You weave plant matter among loose stones in an area with trees or a ceiling overhead. When a creature enters the square, the snare's square and each adjacent square becomes difficult terrain, and the triggering creature must attempt a DC 16 Reflex saving throw.

  • Success: The target is unaffected.
  • Failure: The target takes 1d6 bludgeoning damage.
  • Critical Failure: The target takes 2d6 bludgeoning damage.
