PHYSICAL
You suffer from some kind of neurological disorder, resulting in tremors, facial ticks, or other such phenomena. The point value depends on the severity of your disorder: 
  • Mild [-3 pts]: You are -2 to tasks that require fine motor skills, and they take twice as long to perform. You are also -2 to social checks (such as impersonation) where this disorder would be a factor. 
  • Severe [-6 pts]: You find it difficult to operate in society. You are -4 to tasks that require fine motor skills, and they take twice as long to perform. You are also -4 to social checks where this disorder would be a factor. Your speed cannot be greater than 25.
