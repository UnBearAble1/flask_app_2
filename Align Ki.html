5 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts
Reaction; Trigger You Cast a Ki Arts Focus Spell; Prerequisites Any Ki Arts focus spell, Master in Warrior
The power of your ki soothes your wounds and energizes you for battle. You regain 10 Hit Points plus your Wisdom modifier. The tier of this healing is equal to the focus spell's that triggered it. Each time you use this ability again before your next daily preparations, reduce the amount healed by 5 and the tier of the effect by one tier (cumulative.)
