    • Know Weakness [E/E] -4 pts: Can recall knowledge as part of action, if you crit, gain +1 to strategem attack (and give allies +1 to their next attack as well)

    • Takedown Expert [E] -2 pts: Add 1 handed clubs to strategem weapons

PANACHE, BASE FEATS

  • Panache [E] -17 pts: Perform daring actions to gain a status called "Panache," which gives you a +5 bonus to speed, +1 on some skill checks, +4 precision damage on agile and finesse melee strikes (or +4d6 precision damage with a finisher) 
    • Confident Finisher [Free with Panache]: Finisher move deals 2d6 damage on a failure. 

PANACHE STYLES (select one for free with your Panache feat, must meet prerequisites) 
  • Battledancer: Gain panache with fascinating performance. Step for free after a finisher. 
  • Braggart: Gain panache when you demoralize foes. Finishers end temporary demoralize immunity
  • Fencer: Gain panache when you feint or create a diversion. Finishers cause foe to be flat footed 
  • Gymnast: Gain panache when you grapple, shove or trip. Gain +4 damage with finishers if foes is grabbed, restrained or prone. 
  • Wit: Gain panache when you Bon Mot. Finishers cause -2 to attack rolls.
