TRAINED

Polymorph, Transmutation
Traditions primal
Two Actions somatic, verbal
Duration 1 minute

You call upon primal energy to transform yourself into an animal battle form. You can Dismiss the spell. While in this form, you gain the animal trait. Additionally, you so fully adopt the spirit of the creature you are taking the form of that, aside from your intelligence score, you replace all aspects (skills, hit points, abilities, actions, saving throws, perception, reactions, statistics, senses, movement, and everything else) of your character with those listed in the creature's entry. 

If you are ever reduced to 0 hit points in your battle form (remember, your hit point total is based on the form you are morphed into), you immediately shift back to your normal form, and all wounds you took in while in your battle form transfer to your normal form. Additionally, any leftover damage from the attack or effect that reduced your battle form to 0 is applied as a new wound on your normal form. 

Each time you take more than 10 damage while in your battle form, you must make a DC 15 flat check or have this spell disrupted. 

When you cast this spell, you can pick from the following animal battle forms: 
  • Tiger 
  • Rhinoceros 
  • Emperor Cobra 
  • Cave Bear 
  • Dire Wolf 
  • Giant Moray Eel 

Heightened, Expert. You can also pick one of these forms: 

  • Owlbear 
  • Giant Tarantula 
  • Great White Shark
  • Elephant 
  • Megalania 
  • Blood Boar 
 
Heightened, Master. You can also pick one of these forms:

  • Giant Squid 
  • Deadly Mantis 
  • MegaPrimatus
  • Aurumvorax 
  • Bulette
  • Krooth
