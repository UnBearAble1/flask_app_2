10 Points
Warrior; Two Weapon Fighting
3 Actions; Flourish, Open Requirements You are wielding two melee weapons, each in a different hand; Prerequisites: Legendary in Warrior
You forgo precision to attack at an impossible speed. Make three melee Strikes with each of the required weapons. All of these Strikes take the maximum multiple attack penalty, as if you had already made two or more attacks this turn. No matter what combination of feats and abilities, this penalty cannot be reduced further than -6.
