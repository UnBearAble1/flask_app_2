MENTAL, LAWFUL
You are singularly obsessed with a goal. You must always rationalize every decision through the lens of your obsession, and whenever you have to do something that would lead you astray from that goal, you have to make a self-control roll, otherwise you ignore it and continue on your path. The point value of this complication depends on how long the goal will take to accomplish: 
  • Short term [-1 point], such as assassinating the king 
  • Long term [-2 point], such as becoming the king

Note, if you ever accomplish your goal, you must either take a new one of equal value or buy off this complication.
