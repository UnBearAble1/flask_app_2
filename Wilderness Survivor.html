4 Points
Environmental Acclimatization; Environment

Your people are particularly good at surviving in the wild. Pick any type of terrain (arctic, desert, forest, mountain, plains, sky, swamp, or underground)  If you perform your daily preparations in a terrain that is not your chosen terrain, you may switch your chosen terrain at that time to the one you are in. You gain several benefits while in this terrain: 
  • A +2 circumstance bonus to Survival checks to Subsist and Sense Direction. 
  • You can use the Seek action to sense undetected creatures within a 30-foot burst instead of a 15-foot burst.
  • As a free action, you can make a DC 20 athletics, acrobatics, or survival check to ignore natural difficult terrain or to treat natural greater difficult terrain as ordinary difficult terrain until the end of your turn (if in combat) or for one hour (outside of combat.)
