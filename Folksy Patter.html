1 Point
Cultural Training; Sneakiness
You are adept at disguising coded messages as folksy idioms. Using slang, jokes, loanwords, and the like, you convey a simple message consisting of three basic words (such as “Danger assassin flee” or “Meet me moonrise”). Your intended listener can attempt a Perception check to discern the message (Trained Hard DC if an ally, Trained Moderate DC if a same-ancestry ally, Trained Easy if a same-ancestry ally with Folksy Patter). Eavesdroppers can also attempt a Perception check against your Deception DC to discern your meaning. Any bonuses or penalties to Perception checks to Sense Motive apply.

Additionally, you can attempt to recover critical failures with deception using this folksy patter. If you critically fail, you can re-roll at -2. Any creatures who hear this attempt are immune to it for one month afterward.
