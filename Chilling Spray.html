TRAINED

Cold, Evocation
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions;  somatic, verbal
Area 15-foot cone; Saving Throw Reflex

A cone of icy shards bursts from your spread hands and coats the targets in a layer of frost. You deal 6d6 cold damage to creatures in the area; they must each attempt a Reflex save.

  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature takes half damage.
  • Failure The creature takes full damage and takes a –5-foot status penalty to its Speeds for 2 rounds.
  • Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and takes a –10-foot status penalty to its Speeds for 2 rounds.

Heightened:  The damage increases by 4d6.
