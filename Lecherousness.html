-3 points

MENTAL, CHAOTIC
You have an extreme desire for romance, which lands you in trouble more often than not. When you encounter anyone, even in passing, that you would be attracted to you must make a Self-Control Roll or make a pass at them, no matter the consequences. If the person is exceptionally attractive, the DM may give you up to a -10 to this roll. Unless the person is extraordinarily attractive, you won't have to make this roll more than once per encounter with them. 

Note that, if there are no truly attractive people for you around, you may quickly change your standards of beauty!
