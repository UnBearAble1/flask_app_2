Threat: 8 

Tidal masters use their power over waves and water to drown their enemies.

Recall Knowledge - Elemental (Arcana, Nature)

N, Large, Aquatic, Elemental, Water, Brute 
Perception +10; darkvision
Languages Aquan
Skills Athletics +13, Stealth +10
Str +5, Dex +5, Con +5, Int -1, Wis +3, Cha +0
AC 21; Fort +13 , Ref +9 , Will +11
HP 130; Immunities bleed, paralyzed, poison, sleep; Resistances fire 10
Speed 30 feet, swim 80 feet

Features: 
  • Water-Bound: When not touching water, the tidal master is slowed 1 and can’t use reactions.
  • Vortex (aura, water) 40 feet. Water in the area that is in the same body of water as the tidal master is difficult terrain for Swimming creatures that don’t have the water trait.

Actions: 
  • Wave; Melee Single Action: +11 (+6 /+1) (reach 15 feet), Damage 2d12+8 bludgeoning plus Push or Pull 10 feet 
  • Drench; Single Action (abjuration, primal, water) The elemental puts out all fires in a 10-foot emanation. It extinguishes all non-magical fires automatically and attempts to counteract  magical fires (+11 counteract modifier).
