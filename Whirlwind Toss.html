5 Points 
Warrior; General 
Requirements You have a foe grabbed; Prerequisites: Master in Warrior;  Collateral Thrash

You whirl a foe to smash into all nearby creatures before throwing them far away.  During this Thrash, your Collateral Thrash feat applies to all foes adjacent to you. You can then throw the grabbed creature 10 feet, where they fall prone.

If the foe you choose for Collateral Thrash is also adjacent to you, they attempt only one save and take the damage only once.
