Arcana; School Spells
Prerequisites: Expert in Arcana
You gain the physical boost school spell, and you increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Physical Boost: Transmutation
1 Action verbal; Range touch; Targets 1 living creature; Duration until the end of the target's next turn
You temporarily improve the target's physique. The target gains a +2 status bonus to the next Acrobatics check, Athletics check, Fortitude save, or Reflex save it attempts.
