8 Points
Professional; Fortune
Prerequisites: Master in Professional
Reaction; Fortune, Linguistic; Requirements You spent 10 minutes during your daily preparations reviewing methodologies for the day's adventures with up to four allies.
Trigger One of the required allies is about to attempt an attack roll or skill check.
You quickly advise your ally, using your earlier planning to make sure luck can't throw a wrench in the cogs of your schemes. The ally rolls the triggering check with advantage. That ally then becomes temporarily immune to your Inspired Methodology until your next daily preparations.
