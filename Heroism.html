EXPERT
Enchantment, Mental
Traditions divine, occult
Two Actions;  somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Duration 10 minutes

You tap into the target's inner heroism, granting it a +1 status bonus to attack rolls, Perception checks, saving throws, and skill checks.

Heightened (Master) The status bonus increases to +2.
Heightened (Legendary) The status bonus increases to +3.
