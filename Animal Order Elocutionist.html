3 Points
Natural Abilities; Animal Kinsmanship
You can ask questions of, receive answers from, and use the Diplomacy skill with narrow type of animals, such as cats, canines, bovines, burrowing creatures, rodents, or flying insects. You also gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Make an Impression on these animals (which usually uses the Diplomacy Skill.) A small subsect of this type (you pick) also have an indifferent or friendly starting attitude toward you and give you time to make your case, though the others react to you like any other adventurer.

Boost: (+5 points) :  it instead works on all animals, and the small subsect of animals with improved starting attitude instead can be a broad category of animals (such as cats, canines, bovines, etc.)
