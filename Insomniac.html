-2 or -3 points

PHYSICAL
You suffer from bouts of sleeplessness. Whenever such a bout occurs, roll a DC 8 flat check. On a failure, you lose two hours of sleep that night, and must either make it up (make another check to see if you can) or suffer the consequences (see Rest.) Then the bout continues another night, until you make the flat check. If you roll a 1 on the flat check, you get no sleep that night. 

The value of this complication is based on the regularity of your episodes: 
  • Mild: [-2 pts] The DM rolls a secret 1d20 check for how many days until your next bout. 
  • Severe: [-3 pts] The DM rolls a secret 1d10 check for how many days until your next bout. 

Regardless of the severity, during times of prolonged stress the DM may require a flat check to see if your bout begins immediately.
