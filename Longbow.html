Price 6 gp; Damage 2d8 P; Bulk 2
Hands 1+; Range 100 ft.; Reload 0
Category Martial
Ammunition Arrows
Group Bow; Traits Deadly d10, Volley 30 ft.

This 5-foot-tall bow, usually made of a single piece of elm, hickory, or yew, has a powerful draw and is excellent at propelling arrows with great force and at an extreme distance. You must use two hands to fire a longbow, and it can’t be used while mounted.

Traits

Deadly d10:
On a critical hit, the weapon adds a weapon damage die of the listed size. Roll this after doubling the weapon's damage. 

Volley 30 ft.:
This ranged weapon is less effective at close distances. Your attacks against targets that are at a distance within the range listed take a –2 penalty.
