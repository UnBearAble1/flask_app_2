MENTAL, CHAOTIC
This is the serious, dangerous type of curiosity (beyond simple “Adventurer spirt” curiosity). Think “I have to figure out what happens when you push this big red button.” Make a self control check to avoid examining interesting items, even if doing so could put you at risk. In general, you do everything in your power to investigate any situation in which you aren’t completely familiar - and the more genuinely mysterious, the greater the penalty to the self control check.
