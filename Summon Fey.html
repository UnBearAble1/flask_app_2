TRAINED

Conjuration
Traditions occult, primal
Three Actions; material, somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

You conjure a fey to fight for you. This works like summon animal, except you summon one of the creatures below, instead: 

  • Dryad 
  • Tooth Fairy Swarm
  • Unicorn
 
Heightened (expert): You may instead summon one of the following: 

  • Pixie
  • Satyr
  • Kelpie

Heightened (master): You may instead summon one of the following:

  • Lurker In Light
  • Redcap
  • Grimstalker

Heightened (legendary): You may instead summon one of the following:

  • Eloko
  • Elananx
  • Lampad
