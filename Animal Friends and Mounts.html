These effects are related to training animals and mounting them. 

Training Animals


Bonded Animal 
[Base: 12, Mod -4]
Activation: Duration 
Default Duration: 24 hours 
You cause an indifferent or friendly animal to be helpful to you, unless you do something egregious to break your bond. A helpful animal is easier to direct, as described under Command an Animal. (Consider adding limits and boosts to make this a natural training method that takes time yet is permanent.) 


Advanced Animal Training 
[Base: 8, Mod -4] 
Activation: Duration 
Default Duration: 24 hours 
When interacting with a target animal, you can add new actions to the list of basic actions in Command an Animal, though it cannot be a trick that uses critical thinking (GM decides.) The type of actions are limited by the animal’s intelligence, just like training animals to do tricks in real life. Consider adding limits and boosts to make this a natural training method that takes time yet is permanent. 

Riding Mounts


Superior Riding 
[Mod: 1] 
Any animal you’re mounted on acts on your turn, like a minion. If you Mount an animal in the middle of an encounter, it skips its next turn and then acts on your next turn.


Call Mount 
[Mod: 1] 
Any mount you've ridden for at least two days will automatically return to you upon your specified call (a whistle, horn, shout, etc) as long as it can hear you.
