Threat: 6 

These horrifically enlarged versions of the common mosquito often prey upon megafauna like dinosaurs and other large creatures, but they won't turn down a chance to drink the blood of a smaller target—such as a humanoid.

Recall Knowledge - Animal (Nature)

N, Medium, Animal, Lurker 
Perception +10; darkvision, scent (imprecise) 60 feet
Skills Acrobatics +11, Stealth +11
Str +4, Dex +5, Con +2, Int -5, Wis +2, Cha -5
AC 19; Fort +7, Ref +11, Will +9
HP 72
Speed 20 feet, fly 50 feet

Actions: 
  • Melee Single Action proboscis +10 [+5/+0] (finesse), Damage 2d8+6 piercing plus Grab and septic malaria
    • Septic Malaria (disease) The victim can't reduce its sickened condition while it's affected by septic malaria; Saving Throw DC 20 Fortitude; Onset 1 day; 
      • Stage 1 sickened 1 (1 day); 
      • Stage 2 drained 1 and sickened 1 (1 day); 
      • Stage 3 as stage 2 (1 day); 
      • Stage 4 unconscious (1 day); 
      • Stage 5 dead
  • Blood Drain; Single Action; 
    • Requirements The giant mosquito has a creature grabbed; 
    • Effect The giant mosquito uses its proboscis to drain blood from the grabbed creature. This deals 3d6 damage, and the giant mosquito gains temporary Hit Points equal to the damage dealt. A creature that has its blood drained by a giant mosquito is drained 1 until it receives healing of any kind or amount. The grabbed creature is then immune to blood drain for one hour.
