7 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts
Prerequisites: Master in Warrior
You gain the Unfolding Wind Rush ki spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Unfolding Wind Rush; Evocation
1 Action verbal
You travel on a rushing wind. Move two times: two Strides, two Steps, or one Stride and one Step (in either order). You can Stride or Step into the air during this movement, moving upward at a 45-degree angle at most; if you end your movement in the air, you fall after taking one action or when your turn ends, whichever comes first. A wall of wind appears in all the spaces you left during this movement, lasting for 3 rounds
