3 Points
Spellcaster; Counterspell
Prerequisites Counterspell; Expert in Spellcaster
When you successfully Counterspell a spell of the tradition that matches your tradition, you consume it, replenishing yourself with its energy. When you do, you are nourished as if you had eaten a meal and regain Hit Points equal to twice the tier of the counteracted spell. This healing effect is equal to the tier of the spell countered.
