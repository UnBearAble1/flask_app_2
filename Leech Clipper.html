1 Point
Cultural Training; Weapons

You are trained to capture deserters, or “leeches.” You gain the critical specialization effect with flails. Additionally,  If you critically hit a foe with a weapon from the flail weapon group, you can wrap the weapon around the target’s legs and then drop it, causing the foe to take a –10-foot circumstance penalty to their Speeds until they or their allies disentangle the weapon, which takes a total of 2 Interact actions.
