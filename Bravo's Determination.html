12 Points
Deception; Special Defenses
Prerequisites: Legendary in Deception;
Reaction; Trigger A foe’s Strike reduces you to 0 Hit Points; you were not at 1 Hit Point and you would not be killed.
Your confidence borders on self-delusion, but it keeps you going against overwhelming odds. Attempt a Deception check against the attack roll of the triggering Strike. You take a penalty to this check equal to twice your wounded value, if any. If the triggering attack was a critical hit, use the result one degree of success worse than what you rolled.
  • Critical Success You avoid being knocked out and remain at 1 Hit Point.
  • Success You avoid being knocked out and remain at 1 Hit Point, but you increase your wounded value by 1.
