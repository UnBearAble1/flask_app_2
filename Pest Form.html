TRAINED

Polymorph, Transmutation
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Duration 10 minutes

You transform into the battle form of a Tiny animal, such as a cat, insect, lizard, or rat. You can decide the specific type of animal (such as a rat or praying mantis), but this has no effect on the form's Size or statistics. While in this form, you gain the animal trait. You can Dismiss the spell.

You gain the following statistics and abilities:
  • AC = 17. Ignore your armor's check penalty and Speed reduction.
  • 5 Hit Points (when reduced to 0 hit points, you return to your normal form - and any further damage that would have been applied to your pest form is applied as a wound to your normal form) 
  • Speed 10 feet.
  • Weakness 5 to physical damage. (If you take physical damage in this form, you take 5 additional damage. This applies even if the total damage would reduce you to 0 in your pest form)
  • Low-light vision and imprecise scent 30 feet.
  • Acrobatics and Stealth modifiers of +10, unless your own modifier is higher; Athletics modifier –4.

Particularly observant or paranoid creatures may (if you are not hidden or unobserved) attempt a Perception, Nature, Arcane, or Survival check against your Spellcasting DC to realize something isn't quite right about your form. 

Heightened (Master) You can turn into a flying creature, such as a bird, which grants you a fly Speed of 20 feet.
