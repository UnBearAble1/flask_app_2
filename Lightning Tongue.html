1 Point
Physiology; Tongue
Your tongue darts out to retrieve loose objects. You Interact to pick up a single unattended object of light Bulk or less within 15 feet of you. If you don't have enough hands free to hold the object, it falls to the ground in your space.

Boost (+1 Point) Tongue Disarm: You can attempt to Disarm (as an action, per normal) a creature within 15 feet, ignoring the requirement that you must have at least one hand free.
