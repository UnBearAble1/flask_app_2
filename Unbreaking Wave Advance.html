5 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts
Prerequisites: Master in Warrior
You gain the Unbreaking Wave Advance ki spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Unbreaking Wave Advance; Evocation, Water
1 Action verbal; Area 15-foot cone; Saving Throw Fortitude
You unleash a mighty wave from your hand that buffets back your foes. Each creature in the area must attempt a Fortitude saving throw. If a creature pushed by unbreaking wave advance would be pushed into a solid barrier or another creature, it stops at that point and takes 5d6 damage.
  • Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Failure The creature is pushed 10 feet.
  • Critical Failure The creature is pushed 20 feet.
