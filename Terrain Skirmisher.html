4 Points
Environmental Acclimatization; Environment
Your people are particularly good at battling in the wild. Pick any type of terrain (arctic, desert, forest, mountain, plains, sky, swamp, or underground)  If you perform your daily preparations in a terrain that is not your chosen terrain, you may switch your chosen terrain at that time to the one you are in. You gain two benefits while in this terrain: 
  • You ignore concealment caused by natural conditions in that terrain type. 
  • You can always use the Take Cover action when in that terrain to gain cover, even if you're not next to an obstacle you would normally be able to Take Cover behind.
