10 Points
Physiology; Healing and HP
If you rest for 10 minutes, you recover a number of hit points equal to your Constitution modifier. 

This is an expert-tier effect.
Options (chosen at selection): 
  • Solar Rejuvination: Reduce this ability by 2 points if it requires you to be in full sunlight
  • Replenishing Hydration: Reduce this ability by 4 points if it requires you to be in fully submerged in water
