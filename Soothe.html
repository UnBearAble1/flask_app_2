EXPERT

Emotion, Enchantment, Healing, Mental
Traditions occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 willing living creature
Duration 1 minute

You grace the target's mind, boosting its mental defenses and healing its wounds. The target regains 3d10+12 Hit Points when you Cast the Spell and gains a +2 status bonus to saves against mental effects for the duration.

Heightened: The amount of healing increases by 1d10+4
