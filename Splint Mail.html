Price 13 gp; AC Bonus +5; Dex Cap +1; Check Penalty -3; Speed Penalty -10 ft.
Strength 16; Bulk 3; Group Composite; Traits —

This type of armor is chain mail reinforced with flexible, interlocking metal plates, typically located on the wearer’s torso, upper arms, and legs. A suit of this armor comes with an undercoat of padded armor and a pair of gauntlets.

Armor Specialization Effect (Granted by some feats and other abilities when wearing medium or heavy armor)
Composite: The numerous overlapping pieces of this armor protect you from piercing attacks. You gain resistance to piercing damage 1 for medium armor, or 2 for heavy armor.
