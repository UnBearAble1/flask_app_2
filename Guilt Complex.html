MENTAL
Whenever you let down people who are significant to you, you are riddled by anxiety and guilt. These people include your friends, adventuring companions, dependents, those you have duty or a sense of duty toward, and the like. 

If anything bad happens to these people, and it wasn't your fault, make a Self-Control check. On a failure, you find yourself depressed 1 for 24 hours, after which you make the check again (and again each day until you make it.) 

If the bad thing was your fault, you are -4 to the Self Control check and you are depressed 3.
