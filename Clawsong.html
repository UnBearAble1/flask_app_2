TRAINED

Uncommon, Transmutation
Traditions divine, primal
Cast Two Actions; verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 willing creature with an unarmed claw Strike
Duration: 1 minute

You sing a fierce prayer, low and growling, and awaken the ancient spirit of a Terwa beast to guide and strengthen the target's unarmed blows. The target's claw attack gains the versatile (piercing) trait, and increases the damage by a die category (so 2d6 become 2d8, or 2d10 becomes 2d12). If its claw attack already had the versatile (piercing) trait, it gains the deadly 1d8 trait.

Heightened (Expert) The spell can affect up to 6 willing creatures with unarmed claw Strikes.
