-1 to -3 points

MENTAL, LAWFUL
You live by a strict set of rules in order to properly express and understand your faith. These are often seen as excessive by most members of your faith, but they are critical to your beliefs. Here are some examples: 

 Asceticism: [-3 pts] You try to forego all worldly positions, living a minimalist and extremely spartan lifestyle. You may use tools and items only to succeed on missions of your faith, and do all you can to avoid wealth and reputation. 

Monasticism: [-2 pts] You must spend most of your time (75% of it, even) in silent contemplation and study, seeking connection to your God through study. You also avoid excess wealth and secularism. 

Mysticism: [-2 pts] You spend most of your time (75% of it) in chanting meditation. You are often chanting, humming, and focusing your energy on the mystic energy of your connection to your God. Except for similar disciples of your faith, you suffer a -1 to reaction rolls from NPCs. 

Ritualism: [-1 pts] You have strict and elaborate rituals for all aspects of life, from eating to bathing to sex. Each ritual is complex, having its own wording, trappings and ceremony. Only through these perfect performances can you find your connection to your God.
