9 Points
Warrior; Panache
1 Action; Finisher, Death; Prerequisites: Panache; Legendary in Warrior
You stab your foe in a vital organ, possibly killing them outright. Make a Strike. On a success, you forego your normal 4d6 precision damage from the finisher. Instead, your target takes additional precision damage based on a Fortitude save. If your Strike was a critical hit, the target's saving throw outcome is one degree worse.
  • Critical Success You deal 6 precision damage.
  • Success You deal 3d6 precision damage.
  • Failure You deal 6d6 precision damage.
  • Critical Failure You deal 12d6 precision damage.
