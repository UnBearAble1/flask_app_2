LEGENDARY
Alchemical, Consumable, Contact, Poison 
Price 100 gp Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk L 
Activate one action, Interact

A mix of digestive juices and green dragon poison glands nauseates the victim as its flesh is digested from within.

Saving Throw DC 22 Fortitude; Onset 1 minute; Maximum Duration 6 rounds; 
  • Stage 1 2d10 poison damage and sickened 2 (1 round); 
  • Stage 2 3d10 poison damage and sickened 3 (1 round); 
  • Stage 3 4d10 poison damage and sickened 4 (1 round)
