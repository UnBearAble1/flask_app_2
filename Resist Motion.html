Abjuration, Cantrip
Traditions arcane, divine, occult, primal
One Action; verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature; Duration 1 minute 

You ward a creature against attempts to move them. While under this spell's effect, the target treats all attempts to resist forced movement or being knocked prone as one degree better. If an effect automatically does this as the result of an attack or tag-on as a secondary effect, this spell automatically attempts to counteract the movement using your spellcaster roll.
