Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L 
Activate Single Action, Interact

This elixir is made from salamander scales to withstand heat. For 24 hours, you are protected from the effects of heat. The severity is based on the tier of the Elixir. 

TRAINED: 
Cost: 5 gold 
you are protected from the effects of severe heat.

EXPERT:
Cost: 20 gold
You’re also protected from extreme heat.

MASTER:
Cost: 50 gold
You’re also protected from extreme and incredible heat.
