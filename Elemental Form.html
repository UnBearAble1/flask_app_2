MASTER
Polymorph, Transmutation
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Duration 1 minute

You call upon the power of the planes to transform into an elemental battle form. 

This spell works exactly like Animal Form, except you can assume these forms instead: 

  • Belker 
  • Quatoid 
  • Salamander
  • Sand Sentry

Heightened: Legendary:  You can assume these forms instead: 

  • Invisible Stalker
  • Xorn
  • Magma Scorpion 
  • Tidal Master
