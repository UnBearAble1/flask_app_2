5 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts
2 Actions; Prerequisites Tiger Stance; Requirements You are in Tiger Stance; Prerequisites Tiger stance; Master in Warrior
You make a fierce swipe with both hands. Make a tiger claw Strike. It deals 2d8 extra damage and you can push the target 5 feet away as if you had successfully Shoved them. If the attack is a critical success and deals damage, add your Strength modifier to the persistent bleed damage from your tiger claw.
