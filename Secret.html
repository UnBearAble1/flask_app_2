-1 to -6 points

RELATIONSHIPS
You have a secret that, if revealed, could cause you troublesome repercussions. The point value of this complication is based on exactly how troublesome they are. 
  • Serious Embarrassment: [-1 pt]  Either this attracts unwelcome public attention or besmirches your reputation in some way. 
  • Utter Rejection: [-2 pts] This will change your whole life, possibly calling rejection by everyone you know and love. Perhaps you'll start getting harassed by journalists, cultists, fans or the like. 
  • Imprisonment or Exile: [-4 pts] If the secret is revealed, the authorities will no doubt imprison or exile you. 
  • Possible Death: [-6 pts] The revelation could lead to your death, possibly by a government or possibly for supernatural reasons. 

Frequency of Appearance: 
At the beginning of a session, the DM will roll a DC16 flat check. On a success, your secret will crop up. It won't necessarily be revealed, and you'll have a chance to do something about it in the session. 

When a Secret is Revealed: 
If your secret is made public, you either gain new disadvantages or lose feats equal to DOUBLE the point value of your secret - typically enemies or social stigmas. The DM picks these new disadvantages based on the nature of your secret.
