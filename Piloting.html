You know how to drive vehicles, pilot hexdrive ships, and navigate.

Trained Actions: 

  • Navigate: You can use Piloting to navigate or astrogate. This lets you direct your vehicle or ship in your desired heading and to plot longer courses.
  • Pilot a Hexdrive ship: you use your Piloting skill to maneuver, attempt stunts, and otherwise fly the Hexdrive ship.
  • Drive a Vehicle: you attempt Piloting checks to safely race at full speed, pull off maneuvers, engage in vehicular combat, and maneuver in a chase.
