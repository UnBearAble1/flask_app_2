4 Points
Spellcaster; Halcyon Spells
1 Action; Metamagic; Prerequisites Halcyon Speaker
If your next action is to Cast a non-cantrip spell, you gain a benefit. If the spell is arcane, you can attempt a skill check to Recall Knowledge with a +1 status bonus to the check after casting the spell, or a +2 status bonus if the spell is legendary. If the spell is primal, you gain a number of temporary Hit Points equal to the spell's tier. If the spell is a halcyon spell, you gain both benefits.
