PHYSICAL, CHAOTIC
Like the mental complication drug addiction,  alcoholism is a drug addiction that is cheap, incapacitating, and usually legal. Normally this is a 2 point disadvantage, but this is 3 due to the following additional complications: 
  • Alcohol is prolific and common. Any time you are exposed to it, you must make a Self Control check to avoid binging, which is followed by a hangover. 
    • A binge lasts 2d6 hours, during which your character must make regular self control checks to avoid social complications (such as getting in brawls, talking too freely, insulting the wrong people, etc) 
  • Even if you manage to completely withdraw from the addiction you must still make a self-control check to avoid binging. Three binges, and you reinstate the alcoholism complication. 
  • Hangover: When your binge is over (usually because you fell asleep) make self-control check. The degree of failure is how many hours you are hungover (this begins when you wake up, or immediately if you don’t sleep.) While hungover, you are dazzled. Taking damage or making athletics checks also makes you sickened 1.
