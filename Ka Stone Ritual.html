5 Points
Crafting; Ka Stone
Prerequisites: Living Monolith
You inscribe your true name on your ka stone and embed it in your forehead in a special ritual.. The ka stone is an invested magic item that you automatically invest each day. It can’t be removed without your permission, nor can it be dispelled, and you can never lose investiture in the ka stone. Its Bulk is negligible.

The ka stone grants you a +2 item bonus to saves against death effects and negative effects. You also gain the following special action:

Ka Stone Ritual
2 Actions (envision), or a different activation if otherwise specified; Frequency: a number of times each day equal to the number of Ka Stone feats you have;
You cast the enlarge spell on yourself or use one of the other activated abilities granted by a later Living Monolith feat.
