-1 to -3 points

MENTAL
You have a behavior that is generally repugnant to others. When noticed (which is almost immediately) you will suffer reaction roll penalties. 
  • Minor: [1 pt] (-1 to reaction rolls)  Constant scratching or tuneless humming, for example. 
  • Major: [2 pts] (-3 to reaction rolls) Constant bad puns or spitting on the floor. 
  • Severe: [3 pts] (-5 to reaction rolls)... the really bad stuff.
