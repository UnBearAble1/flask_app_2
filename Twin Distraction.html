2 Points
Warrior; Two Weapon Fighting
Requirements You are wielding two melee weapons, each in a different hand; Prerequisites Twin Feint;
Your movements with two weapons are so swift and disorienting that they befuddle your opponent. When using Twin Feint, if both your Strikes deal damage to a targeted creature, the target must succeed at a Will saving throw or become stupefied 1 (or 2, if both were critical hits) until the end of your next turn.
