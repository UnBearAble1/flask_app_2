A ritual is an esoteric and complex spell that anyone can cast. It takes much longer to cast a ritual than a normal spell, but rituals can have more powerful effects. Rituals are either learned in world or purchased at character creation.

TRAINED

  • Consecrate: You consecrate a site to your deity, chanting praises and creating a sacred space.
  • Elemental Sentinel: You place a tiny elemental wisp within a single object, usually a mirror, statue, or other mundane-looking item, to serve as an alarm.
  • Fey Abeyance: You ward an area with cold iron bells, protecting it from the influence of the first world.
  • Heartbond: You create a magical bond between two willing creatures, who are secondary casters of the ritual and must share genuine affection for one another.
  • Inveigle: You win over the target's mind, causing it to see you as a close and trusted friend and look upon your every suggestion as reasonable.

EXPERT

  • Arcane Weaving: You and the secondary casters weave together spells in a complex ritual that combines magical learning with art, allowing all the participants to share spells they know with one another.
  • Atone: You attempt to help a truly penitent creature atone for its misdeeds, typically actions contrary to your deity's alignment or anathema to your deity.
  • Awaken Portal: You attempt to reactivate a dormant gate, portal, or teleportation circle by infusing it with magical energies.
  • Blight: You twist and stunt plants in the area, causing them to wither.
  • Concealment's Curtain: Curse the target such that they are unable to see you in any way.
  • Geas: You enforce a magic rule on a willing target, forcing it to either perform or refrain from carrying out a certain act.
  • Guardian's Aegis: The guardian's aegis is a ritual used across numerous cultures to bind a chosen guardian and ward together so that they can complete a quest of great import.
  • Mystic Carriage: This ritual allows you and the other casters to conjure a magical carriage that transports you to a destination of your choice.
  • Plant Growth: You cause the plants within the area to be healthier and more fruitful.
  • Repair Public Work: You lead your community in repairing a public work, such as a bridge, well, or mural, through the power of memory and art.
  • Rest Eternal: You call upon gods, spirits, and stranger beings to bar a creature's spirit from ever returning.
  • Simulacrum: You create an illusory duplicate of the target creature by animating ice or snow sculpted in its shape.
  • Tattoo Whispers: You carefully tattoo the same design upon each secondary caster, which grants the ability to communicate over distances
  • Unseen Custodians: You create a site-bound, long-lasting unseen servant spell effect, forming entities of pure force to carry out basic tasks at a fixed location.

MASTER

  • Asmodean Wager: You facilitate a magically binding agreement between yourself and one or more additional secondary casters, wherein each participant stakes something of value on the outcome of an event that has yet to be determined.
  • Astral Projection: You project the targets' spiritual essences into the Astral Plane, leaving their inanimate physical forms behind.
  • Awaken Animal: You grant intelligence to the target, transforming it into a beast.
  • Awaken Object: You imbue a single Small or smaller object with rudimentary awareness and consciousness, and it gains mental ability modifiers depending on the results of the ritual.
  • Call Spirit: You tear the veil to the afterlife and call a spirit from its final resting place.
  • Commune: You call upon an unknown planar entity to answer questions.
  • Commune with Nature: You call upon the primal spirits of nature to answer questions.
  • Dread Ambience: In some places, it always feels like something is observing you, as if the very land doesn't want you there.
  • Heroes' Feast: You conjure otherworldly beings to serve you and your companions a feast with restorative properties.
  • Ideal Mimicry: You craft a doll with care and quiet meditation on the target, and can damage the target through the doll.
  • Mind Swap: This ritual allows two subjects to exchange minds, fully inhabiting one another's bodies.
  • Mosquito Blight: You infest the area with tenacious, biting insects that disease and drain life from creatures in the area.
  • Planar Ally: You call upon your deity to grant you aid in the form of a divine servitor.
  • Primal Call: You craft a faerie circle to call a nearby creature of nature.
  • Statuette: This ritual is a variant of the shrink item spell that allows for the transformation of significantly larger items
  • Terminate Bloodline: Bestow the target with a wasting disease that is contagious to their family members
  • The World's a Stage: This famous ritual is a sophisticated example of symbolic magic, binding fate and fortune to follow a prepared script.
  • Ward Domain: This ritual has long been used to guard the private sanctums of powerful rulers, spellcasters, and other figures of import.
  • Waters of Prediction: You and the secondary casters peer into the still waters of a natural pool or lake to divine your collective future.

LEGENDARY

  • Bathe in Blood: You bathe in the freshly spilled blood of your people, whose lives and years you steal in the process.
  • Control Weather: You alter the weather.
  • Create Demiplane: A demiplane created with this ritual exists on the Astral or Ethereal Plane.
  • Freedom: You perform a ritual to free a creature imprisoned, petrified, or otherwise put into stasis.
  • Imprisonment: You perform a ritual to imprison a creature in one of several forms.
  • Legend Lore: You attempt to learn useful legends about a particular subject.
  • Reincarnate: You call forth the target's soul and attempt to incarnate it into a brand-new body.
  • Remove Greater Curse: You attempt to remove a powerful curse or effect
  • Word of Recall: You bind yourself and your allies to the specific safe location where you perform the ritual.
