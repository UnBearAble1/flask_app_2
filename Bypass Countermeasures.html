REACTION
Trigger: You are targeted by a Security system's countermeasures
Whether it's a static trap or an active countermeasure, you can use the Computers skill to avoid the consequences. 

  • Critical Success: You avoid the consequences of the countermeasures entirely. 
  • Success: You stop the countermeasures from targeting you for 1d4 rounds, and may attempt to avoid detection and targeting or completing your infiltration to avoid it entirely. 
  • Failure: You suffer the consequences of the countermeasure. 
  • Critical Failure: You suffer the critical consequences of the countermeasure. 

Example Countermeasure: 

Tracing Program: This countermeasure attempts to identify and track the intruder
  • Consequence: The countermeasure detects your location within 1200 feet of accuracy. It identifies your public profile and information. 
  • Critical Consequence: The countermeasure detects your location exactly. It identifies your true identity, and infects your computer with tracking virus (must be removed separately as a downtime activity, removed with a special Daemon, or the computer must be destroyed.)
