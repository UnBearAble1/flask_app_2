Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir, Healing
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L 
Activate Single Action, Interact

Focus briefly calms your neurons, helping ease mental effects. When you drink this elixir, the elixir attempts a counteract check with the listed counteract modifier to remove the confused or stupefied condition from a single source, using the source of that condition to determine the counteract tier and DC.
