Needle of Vengeance; Enchantment, Hex, Mental
1 Action somatic; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 enemy; Saving Throw basic Will; Duration sustained up to 1 minute
A long, jagged needle jabs into the target foe's psyche whenever it tries to attack a specifically forbidden creature. Name yourself or one of your allies. The target takes 10 mental damage any time it uses a hostile action against the named creature
