6 Points
Knowledge Skills; Saves and Resistances
Prerequisites: Legendary in Religion, Nature, Occultism, or Arcana
Your experience with a specific type of harmful magic makes you more resistant to it. Choose a magical tradition (arcane, divine, occult, or primal) based on the skill you selected as the prerequisite. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saving throws against spells and effects with that tradition’s trait and resistance 5 to all damage from spells and effects with that tradition’s trait. The effect must have actually been cast or created with that tradition, rather than simply being on that tradition’s spell list.
