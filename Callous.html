MENTAL, LAWFUL
Though you have no problems understanding people’s emotions, you can’t seem to bring yourself to care about their feelings or pain. You are automatically -2 on any rolls that require you to sympathize with someone, such as teaching or counseling. You also take a -2 to reaction rolls with anyone who has witnessed (or heard of) your callousness in the past (or anyone who is exceptionally empathetic.)
