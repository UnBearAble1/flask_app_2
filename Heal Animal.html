2 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Expert in Nature
You gain the heal animal order spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Heal Animal; Healing, Necromancy, Positive
1-2 Actions somatic; Range touch or 30 feet (see text); Targets 1 willing living animal creature (that is neither an animal companion nor a familiar)
You heal an animal's wounds, restoring 5d8 Hit Points to the target. The number of actions spent Casting this Spell determines its effect
  • Single Action somatic The spell has a range of touch.
  • Two Actions somatic, verbal The spell has a range of 30 feet and restores an additional 8 Hit Points to the target.

Special: If you are a Master in Nature, you can make this a Master-Tier effect that heals 6d8, but it costs 2 focus points to cast. If you are Legendary you can make it a Legendary-Tier effect that heals 7d8 but costs 3 focus points to cast.
