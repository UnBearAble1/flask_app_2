These effects covers any way in which your character can gain information or clues through investigation or oracular means. 

Area Knowledge


Activation: Instantaneous 

You gain knowledge of an area you are in. The cost is based on how detailed the knowledge you gain is, how broad the domain knowledge of the answers are,  and how big the area is. You never gain knowledge about something intentionally hidden, disguised, or exceptionally hard to find. 

Detail: 
  • The GM will answer three simple questions about the environment, but the answers are almost always a single word. (Base: 8) 
  • You learn as much information as you could by spending fifteen minutes talking with someone who lives in the area. (Base: 12) 
  • You learn as much information as you could by spending three days living in the area. (Base: 16, Mod +8)
Domain: The answers you get can only be about the domain you choose when you create this ability: 
  • Narrow: Battle, The Dead, High Emotion, Commerce, Police, High Society, Ores, etc (Mod -4) 
  • Moderate: Settlements, Flora and Fauna, Geographical Features, Magic, Monsters, etc (default) 
  • Broad: People, Exact Structural Layouts, Valuables, etc  (Base +4) 
  • Anything. (Base +8, Mod +12) 

Radius: 
  • 100 feet (base) 
  • 500 feet (Mod +4) 
  • 1 mile (Mod +20) 
  • 10 miles (Base +4, Mod +30) 
  • 25 miles (Base +8, Mod +60)

Assess Personal Details

Activation: Instantaneous
Default Range: 30 feet

You learn personal details about a target creature. 
  • Their profession, their specialty within that profession [Base: 4] 
  • Their profession, their specialty within that profession, and a major accomplishment or controversy from their career. [Base: 8] 
  • The nation and settlement where they live, as well as the district in a city large enough to have districts. [Base: 4] 
  • The nation and settlement where they live, as well as the district in a city large enough to have districts. You also learn the nation or settlement where they spent their formative years. [Base: 8]


Know the Past


Activation: Instantaneous
Linked Skill: Investigation
Default Range: Touch. 

You can learn information about an event that occurred within the past month involving an object you are touching or place you are in. 

Specificity of Information: 
  • Vaguely what was happening, with no personally identifiable information. You get more of a 'sense' of what was happening than any sensory information. Example: A human shot an orc with this arrow (Base: 8) 
  • Approximately what was happening, with some clues as to who may have been involved. You sense some elements, but they are muffled or fuzzy, and you are unable to narrow in on explicitly identifiable information. Example: A middle aged female, human ranger with a red oak bow shot a fleeing orc barbarian in the back with this arrow. (Base: 12) 
  • Exactly what was happening. Here you perceive with all your senses plain as day what was happening. Example: A dark haired, middle aged, bronze skinned female human ranger with a red oak bow from the Northlands, wounded and retaliating, shoots a fleeing orc barbarian in the back with this arrow after he and his war party attempted to ambush her on the trail. You recognize her as Jetra, the Ranger from the last town you were in. (Base: 16) 

Type of Event: 
  • The most emotionally charged event within your designated time range: (Default) 
  • A specific event you have knowledge about or direct evidence of (such as touching at bloody arrow, and finding the event that bloodied the arrow, or finding a door smashed in and finding an event where the door was smashed in.) (Mod +10) 

Time Frame. You can always pinpoint when in the time frame the event occurred : 
  • You can learn about an event that occurred within the last year. (Mod +6)
  • You can learn about an event that occurred within the last decade. (Mod +12) 
  • You can learn about an event that occurred within the entirety of the object’s history. (Mod +20)

Target: 
  • One Object (Mod -4) 
  • One Object or a 10 foot radius (default) 
  • One Object or a 20 foot radius (Mod +4) 
  • One Object or a 50 foot radius (Mod +10) 
Modifications: 
  • You cannot pinpoint when an event occurred (Mod -6)

Photographic Memory


[Mod: 4] 

You never forget information. You may ask the GM to provide you with any details you may have forgotten that were previously known to your character.

Predicting the Future


Activation: Instantaneous 

You can predict future events enough to provide some useful information for a decision you are making. 
  • You learn which of two courses of action are more likely to help you succeed in your plans. (Base: 16, Mod: +24) 
  • You learn whether a single course of action will most likely yield a good, bad or truly mixed result (Base: 16, Mod: +24) 
  • You specify a course of action and possible outcome. You then learn whether that course of action is highly likely, somewhat likely, somewhat unlikely, or highly unlikely to have the specified outcome.  (Base: 16, Mod: +24) 

Question The Dead


[Base: 12, Mod +2]
Activation: Duration
Default Duration: 1 round. 
Range: 30 feet.

You grant a target corpse a semblance of life, though it is unable to perform any actions other than answering questions. The corpse isn't required to be truthful (though you could add additional effects to make them helpful,) but it must answer one question per round that you pose to it. 

The corpse won't volunteer additional information beyond answering your questions fully and directly, but it won't avoid adding details either. 

Modifications: 
  • Speak with Spirit: You don’t even need the remains, as long as you have an item that belonged to them, and are aware of their existence.  (Base +4, Mod +6) 

Creature Knowledge Effects


Spirit or Haunt Insight
[Base: 4] 
Linked Skill: Religion or Spirit or Haunt Lore
Activation: Instantaneous
Default Range: 30 feet. 
You instantly gain a flash of insight into how spirit or haunt can be permanently put to rest.

Habitat Study 
[Mod: 2]
Activation: Instantaneous
Default Range: 30 feet.
You can make a recall knowledge check about a creature by examining an environment in which they habitat or you know they have visited within the past 72 hours using the same DC as simply seeing the creature directly. 
 

Investigating Clue Effects


Detect Red Herring
[Base: 8, Mod -4]
Linked Skill: Investigation
Activation: Instantaneous
Default Range: 30 feet.
The GM tells you if a clue is inconsequential. For example, if you found a splatter of gray mud on the wall, and thought it was suspicious, the GM would tell you if there was no greater mystery related to it.


Deeper Clue Analysis 
[Base: 8, Mod -4]
Linked Skill: Investigation
Activation: Instantaneous
Default Range: Touch
You are able to examine clues for details normally hidden to the naked eye. You can ask the GM a 'yes or no' question about the clue that normally would fall outside the possibility of mundane examination. The clue cannot indicate a specific person, place, or object - but can provide categories: 
  • Was the clue left by a [creature trait]? (Choose a creature trait such as humanoid, undead, or dwarf; this trait must be accurate as of the time the clue was left.)
  • Was the clue left within the last hour? 
  • Was the clue left within the last day? 
  • Was the creature that left the clue in a heightened emotional state when it left the clue?
  • Was the clue intended to be hidden? 


Mind Reading Effects


Read Surface Thoughts
[Base: 8, Mod +2]
Linked Skill: Empathy
[Mental], Activation: Duration
Range: 30 feet.
You perceive vague surface thoughts from the target. These are typically streams of consciousness things, fragments, images, but can be used to piece together information if done correctly. A creature who is intentionally being deceptive will require a check versus their deception. 

Read Emotions
[Base: 8]
Linked Skill: Empathy
[Mental], Activation: Duration
Range: 30 feet.
You perceive the emotional state from the creature.A creature who is intentionally being deceptive will require a check versus their deception.

Mind Probe
[Base: 12, Mod +8], Linked Skill: Empathy
[Mental], Activation: Instantaneous
Range: 30 feet.
You can telepathically probe a creature. Ask a question you want to have answered by the creature’s thoughts and memories, and make a check versus their Deception DC.If they have the answer, you receive it.  You know the degree of certainty the creature has over the knowledge, but not whether or not the knowledge is true (meaning that they could be completely wrong about the knowledge, they cannot by default actively deceive you.)  


Glean Interesting information
[Base: 8, Mod +6], Linked Skill: Empathy
[Mental], Activation: Instantaneous
Range: 30 feet.
Make a check versus the creature's willpower defense. On a success, you can telepathically pull random interesting information, usually in the form of a random memory, from a creature’s mind. The details and extent of this information are at the GM's discretion, and you have no control over the type of information gleaned… though it is always interesting. 


Modifications to Read Surface Thoughts, Mind Probe, and Glean Interesting Information: 
  • Read the Dead: You can use a mind reading ability on a corpse, as long as you have at least a head and throat. (Mod +10) 
    • You can ONLY use the mind reading ability this way (Mod +0 instead of Mod +10) 
    • Speak with Spirit: You don’t even need the remains, as long as you have an item that belonged to them and aware of their existence. (Mod +20 instead of Mod +10, unless this is the only way you can use the mind reading ability.)
