3 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts
Prerequisites: Master in Warrior
You gain the Unblinking Flame Revelation ki spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Unblinking Flame Revelation; Abjuration
1 Action verbal; Requirements Your previous action was a successful unarmed Strike; Targets the creature you hit; Duration 2 rounds
You light your foe with revealing flame. The GM attempts a secret counteract check against each illusion affecting the creature; on a success, you suppress the illusion for the duration, rather than end the effect.
