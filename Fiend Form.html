MASTER
Evil, Polymorph, Transmutation
Traditions arcane, divine
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Duration 1 minute

You infuse yourself with the evil of hellscapes, assuming a mighty Fiend battle form. This spell works exactly like Animal Form, except you can assume these forms instead: 

  • Nessian Warhound 
  • Hellwasp Swarm 

Heightened: Legendary:  You can assume these forms instead: 

  • Kalavakus
  • Piscodaemon
