5 Points
Arcana; Dragon Disciple
Requires: Dragon Disciple
Your fingernails grow into razor-sharp claws, and scales speckle your fingers, hands, and wrists. Your claws are agile, finesse unarmed attacks that deal 2d6 slashing damage and are in the brawling weapon group. If you have the dragon claws focus spell, when casting it increase the spell's slashing damage die from d4 to d6 and increase the resistance to 20.
