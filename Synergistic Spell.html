12 Points
Spellcaster; Halcyon Spells
1 Action; Metamagic; Usable Once Per Day; Prerequisites: Fulminating Synergy; legendary in Arcana or Nature, Master in the other.
You combine your synergy and spells into a fantastic web of magic, allowing you to cast two spells at once. If the next action you use is to Cast a Spell with a Halcyon tag, after that spell resolves, you can Cast another Spell as a free action. This spell must also be a Halcyon spell.
