Depressed has a number value associated with it, usually appearing after it such as "Depressed 2." While depressed, you take a penalty to willpower saving throws and self control checks equal to the value of your Depressed, and you find it difficult to muster the energy to do anything other than stay in bed or remain otherwise isolated.

Others can attempt to motivate you for short bursts to get out the door by making Expert DC request or coerce rolls. Checks made to do so take a penalty equal to the value of your Depressed condition. 

The duration for Depressed is based on the effect that caused it.
