5 Points
Warrior; Two-Handed Weapon
1 Action; Press; Requirements You are wielding a melee weapon in two hands; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior
Your final blow can make an impact even if it rebounds off a foe’s defenses. Make a Strike with the required weapon. After the Strike, your turn ends. The Strike deals one extra weapon damage die. The Strike also gains the following failure effect. 
  • Failure You deal damage equal to one weapon damage die of the required weapon.
