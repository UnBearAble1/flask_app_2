LEGENDARY
Healing, Necromancy, Positive
Traditions divine, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 willing living creature
Duration 1 minute

An infusion of positive energy grants a creature continuous healing. The target temporarily gains fast healing 10. If the target takes acid or fire damage, it's fast healing deactivates until after the end of its next turn.

Each time the creature regains Hit Points from fast healing, You can heal a Grievous Wound once during the duration, and a Serious wound once during the duration. These wounds do not need the limb to regrow.
