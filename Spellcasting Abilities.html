These effects modify your spellcasting abilities. 

Advanced Spellcasting


You can make spellcasting rolls with certain numerical advantages: 
  • +1 to the Spellcasting Roll (Base: 12) 
  • +2 to the Spellcasting Roll (Base: 16, Mod +8) 
  • Rolled with Advantage (Base: 20, Mod +20) 
  • Turn Critical Failure to a normal failure (Base: 16)

Dynamic Spell


Activation: Duration 
Default Duration: 30 minutes (Maximum 24 hours)
Requires: You are a prepared magic user. 
Special: You can only take this effect once.
You are able to temporarily add a spell to your repertoire. The spell must be a spell you could normally learn and cast. (Base: 12, Mod -4)  
  • Spell can be of any Tradition (Mod +12) 

Prevent Spellcasting


[Base: 4, Mod +3] 
Activation, Duration
Default Range: 30 feet,  Default Duration: 3/1d6 rounds. 

You prevent a spellcaster from casting a single spell in their repertoire. They get a Will defense to avoid the effect. 

Resist Spell Disruption


[Base: 8]
If an effect would disrupt your spell, including taking damage while performing a “continue casting” action, your spell is not disrupted.

Alternate Tradition Effects


Shift Tradition 
[Base: 8, Mod -4] 
Though you still have a single tradition, and can only cast spells available to that tradition, when you cast your spells you can turn your spell from its normal tradition to another specific tradition chosen when you pick this effect. 

Managing Strain Effects


Reduce Strain Cost 
[Base: 16]
Activation: Instantaneous 
You reduce your strain cost from casting your next spell this turn by 1d6 points. If you reduce the action cost of  this effect to a reaction, you reduce it from a spell you are currently casting. 


Take Strain for Ally
[Base: 8] 
Activation: Instantaneous 
Default Duration: End of your next turn, Requires you are a spellcaster. 
The next time a target spellcaster would accrue strain, you accrue the strain on their behalf. (they still make the rolls).
