MENTAL
You cannot understand the emotions of other people. You have your own emotions, of course, and you understand that other people have them - you just can't make sense of them. As a result you have difficulty in most social interactions. 

You take a -3 to social skill rolls, and sense motive. The GM may ask you to make Sense Motive rolls to understand someone's expression even if others would automatically understand it.
