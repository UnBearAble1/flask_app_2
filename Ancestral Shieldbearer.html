You gain the Shield Block reaction, and when you use it, your shield gains an additional 2 points of hardness.
Shield Block
Reaction; Trigger While you have your shield raised, you would take damage from a physical attack.
You snap your shield in place to ward off a blow. Your shield prevents you from taking an amount of damage up to the shield’s Hardness. You and the shield each take any remaining damage, possibly breaking or destroying the shield.
