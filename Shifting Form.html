3 Points
Arcana; School Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Arcana; Physical Boost
You gain the Shifting Form school spell, and you increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Shifting Form: Morph, Transmutation
1 Action somatic; Duration 1 minute
You gain one of the following abilities of your choice. You can Dismiss this spell.
  • You gain a 20-foot status bonus to your Speed.
  • You gain a climb or swim Speed equal to half your Speed.
  • You gain darkvision.
  • You gain a pair of claws. These are agile finesse unarmed attacks that deal 2d8 slashing damage.
  • You gain scent 60 feet (imprecise).
