LEGENDARY
Enchantment, Mental
Traditions divine
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 willing creature other than yourself

You infuse a target with spiritual energy, refreshing its magic. It immediately removes 3d4 strain.  If it has a focus pool, it regains its Focus Points, as if it had Refocused. The creature is then immune to Divine Inspiration for 24 hours.
