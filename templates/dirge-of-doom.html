Dirge of Doom


5 Points
Performance; Composition Spell
Prerequisites: Composition Spells

You learn the dirge of doom composition cantrip, which frightens your enemies.



Dirge of Doom; Cantrip, Composition, Emotion, Enchantment, Fear, Mental

1 Action verbal; Area 30-foot emanation; Duration 1 round

Foes within the area are frightened 1. They can't reduce their frightened value below 1 while they remain in the area.
