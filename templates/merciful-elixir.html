Merciful Elixir


6 Points
Alchemist; Healing

Free Action; Additive 2; Prerequisite: Expert in Alchemist; Frequency once per round; Trigger You craft an elixir of life using Quick Alchemy

You mix a special additive into your elixir that calms the drinker's body and mind. The elixir of life attempts to counteract one fear effect or one effect imposing the paralyzed condition on the drinker using your Alchemist Skill.

Boost (+6 points) Greater Merciful Elixir: Your additives contain panaceas that can remedy a plethora of maladies. When you use Merciful Elixir, your elixir can instead attempt to counteract the
blinded,
deafened,
sickened, or
slowed
condition.
