Flowing Strike


MASTER
Evocation, Water
Traditions arcane, primal
Three Actions; material, somatic, verbal

A wave of flowing water rushes behind you like an elegant river, pushing you across the battlefield with fluid grace. You Stride up to 50 feet. This movement doesn't trigger reactions. You can make a Strike at any point during your Stride. If your Strike hits, the wave flows after you to batter the target of your Strike as well, dealing 4d10 bludgeoning damage and pushing it 20 feet in a direction of your choice.
