Battle Medicine


4 Points
Medicine; Treat Wounds
1 Action; healing, Manipulate; Prerequisites trained in Medicine; Requirements You're holding or wearing healer's tools.



You can patch up wounds, even in combat. Attempt a Medicine check with the same DC as for Administer First Aid and restore the corresponding amount of HP; the maximum of the amount of damage that can be healed this way is still the amount the creature has taken since the last time Administer First Aid or Battle Medicine was used on them within the last 24 hours.
