Freedom Domain: Unimpeded Stride


4 pts
Acrobatics; Remove Conditions
Prerequisites: Master in Acrobatics; Trained in Religion

You liberate yourself and others from shackles and constraints. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Unimpeded Stride; Transmutation

1 Action; somatic

Nothing can hold you in place. You immediately escape from every magical effect that has you immobilized or grabbed unless the effect is of legendary proficiency. If the effect is legendary, but so is your acrobatics, you can use this spell to immediately attempt a counteract check on the effect. You then Stride. During this movement, you ignore difficult terrain and any circumstance or status penalties to your Speed.
