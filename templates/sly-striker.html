Sly Striker


5 Points
Warrior; Sneak Attack
Prerequisites: Master in Warrior; Greater Sneak Attack Boost

Your attacks deal more damage, even against creatures that aren't flat-footed. When you succeed or critically succeed at a Strike against a creature that isn't flat-footed, you also deal 1d6 precision damage. This applies only if you're using a weapon or unarmed attack you could deal sneak attack damage with.
