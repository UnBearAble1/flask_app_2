Imp Sting


TRAINED

Uncommon, Evil, Necromancy, Poison
Traditions divine, occult
Cast Two Actions: somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature; Saving Throw Fortitude

You reproduce an imp's evil, poisonous sting, stabbing at an enemy and possibly affecting them with the devil's benumbing venom. Make a spell attack roll. On a hit, you deal 4d6 piercing damage to the touched creature and afflict the creature with imp venom. If the target takes piercing damage, they also take 2d6 evil damage and must attempt a Fortitude save.


  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target takes 1d6 poison damage.
  • Failure The target is afflicted with imp venom at stage 1.
  • Critical Failure The target is afflicted with imp venom at stage 2.

Imp Venom (poison); Maximum Duration 6 rounds;

  • Stage 1 1d6 poison and clumsy 1;
  • Stage 2 1d6 poison, clumsy 1, and slowed 1 (1 round).
