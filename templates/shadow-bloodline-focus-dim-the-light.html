Shadow Bloodline Focus: Dim the Light


3 Points
occultism; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Expert at occultism

You gain the focus spell, Dim the Light, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Dim The Light; Darkness, Evocation

Reaction; somatic; Trigger You cast a darkness or shadow spell.

You magnify the darkness from the triggering effect, causing the ambient light level to flicker briefly so you can slip from sight. You can immediately attempt a Stealth check to Hide, and you can become hidden to a creature even if you don't have cover and aren't otherwise concealed from that creature. You cease being hidden at the end of your turn unless you move into cover or become concealed before then. Additionally, Shadows grow deeper around you, granting a +1 status bonus to Stealth until the end of your next turn.
