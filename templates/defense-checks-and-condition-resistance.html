Defense Checks and Condition Resistance




You have a particular advantage when resisting certain effects or making defense rolls.
The Tier of this effect is based on how common the check is - with these as the defaults:


  • [Category A] The Guard Defense
  • [Category B] One specific non Guard Defense Type (Fortitude, Reflex, Willpower)
  • [Category C] Defenses against a very broad type: Magic, Area attacks
  • [Category D]
    A broad type: Mental, Specific Tradition, Alchemy, The Energy or Life Magic Spheres
  • [Category E]
    A narrow type: Any Specific Sphere other than Energy or Life, Any of the Four Elements, Poison, Any restrictions to free movement, Emotion
  • [Category F]
    One of the following: Disease, Inhaled Threats, Visual, Auditory, Prone, Enfeebled, Clumsy, Drained, Stupefied, Sickened, Fatigued, Frightened, Flat-Footed, Grabbed, Immobilized, Restrained, Slowed, Stunned,
    Dazzled, Blinded, Fleeing, Paralyzed, Unconscious, Confused, Controlled
  • [Category G]
    One of the following: Doomed, Deafened, Marked, Forced Movement, Speed Penalties, Disarmed, Fascinated, Inability to perform a maneuver (such as step or flank), Petrified

When you purchase this ability, you select one type of scope type from the list above, and one benefit from the list below.
