Radiant Blade Spirit


10 Points
Religion/Warrior; Blade Ally
Requires: Blade Ally, Legendary in Religion; Master in Warrior

Your divine ally radiates power, enhancing your chosen weapon. When you choose the weapon for your blade ally during your daily preparations, it deals additional alignment damage based on your alignment:

  • Holy: deals an additional 1d6 good damage against evil targets, Also, you gain this ability:

    • Reaction; command; Frequency once per day; Trigger You critically succeed at an attack roll against an evil creature with the weapon; Effect You regain HP equal to 4x the creature's highest proficiency tier (the tier of this healing effect is equal to the creature's highest proficiency tier). This is a good, positive, healing effect.
  • Unholy: deals an additional 1d6 evil damage when it hits a good target. And you gain the following ability:


    • Activate Reaction command; Frequency once per day; Trigger You critically succeed at an attack roll against a good creature with the weapon; Effect The target takes persistent bleed damage equal to 1d8 per weapon damage die
  • Axiomatic: deals an additional 1d6 lawful damage against chaotic targets,


    • When you critically succeed at an attack roll with this weapon against a chaotic creature, instead of rolling, count each weapon damage die as average damage rounded up (3 for d4, 4 for d6, 5 for d8, 6 for d10, 7 for d12).
  • Anarchic: deals an additional 1d6 chaotic damage against lawful targets,


    • When you critically succeed at a Strike with this weapon against a lawful creature, roll 1d6. On a 1 or 2, you deal double minimum damage; on a 3 or 4, double your damage normally; on a 5 or 6, you deal double maximum damage.
