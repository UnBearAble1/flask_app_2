Warden's Boon


6 Points
Warrior; Hunt Prey
1 Action; Prerequisites: Hunt Prey

By pointing out vulnerabilities, you grant the benefits listed in Hunt Prey and any feats you have that modify it (that don't grant abilities to other creatures) to an ally until the end of their next turn. Depending on whether you call out or use gestures, this action gains either the auditory or visual trait.
