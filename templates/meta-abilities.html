Meta Abilities


These effects modify other effects or modify the rules of other effects in unique ways.

Contingency


Activation: Instantaneous



You set a contingency. This is a special type of trigger that automatically activates another
Effect when certain conditions are met. Each time you activate this effect, you set the trigger condition and the effect that is triggered.



Additional Rules:

  • The Effect must match the source of your Contingency (mechanical, magical, or psionic)
  • The special ability cannot be one that takes more than two actions to activate.

  • Because this is dynamically chosen each time, it doesn't qualify as a Situational Limit (unless your ability to use the Contingency power itself has a situational limit.)
  • Any individual person can only have up to three contingencies set upon their person at a time.

The cost is based on the type of activation of the contingent ability and how long it remains in effect, and how many contingencies you can have active at one time.

Contingency Duration:

  • Your Contingency lasts 24 hours (Base: 8)
  • Your Contingency lasts 1 week (Base: 12)
  • Your Contingency lasts indefinitely (Base: 16)

Activation Type:

  • Your Contingency activates using a reaction (Mod +4)
  • Your Contingency activates using a triggered free action (Mod +16)
  • Your Contingency activates automatically, even if you are unconscious or dead. (Mod +32)


Number of Contingencies You Can Have in Existence at One Time:

  • One (Default)
  • Two (Base +4)
  • Three (Base +8)

Create Alternate Point of Origin



Activation: Duration
Default Duration: 2 / 1d4 rounds
Default Range: 30 feet (maximum: 120 feet)


When you activate this effect, you designate an alternate point of origin for your effects that applies until the duration expires.
Like all abilities, you must have line of effect and sight to the new point of origin, but from then the point of origin is considered "you" for line of effect.




The maximum range you can be from your point of origin at any time you activate an effect to apply to it is the range of this ability.

Origin must be:

  • An ally (Base: 8)
  • An enemy (Base: 8, Mod +4)
  • Any square (Base: 12, Mod +8)
  • A specific item, specified at creation (Base: 8, Mod +6)
  • Another effect (made from creation, illusion, alteration, etc, specified at creation) (Base: 8, Mod +2)

Duplicate Effect



Activation: Instantaneous

[Base: 16, Mod +4]



When you activate this effect, you duplicate an ongoing existing effect you have in place, but in a different location or with a new target. This can be combined with the temporary augmentation above to change other aspects of the effect as well.

Focus Point Effects



Instantly Recover Focus Point
[Base: 16, Mod -6]
Activation: Instantaneous
You instantly recover a focus point.



Regain Additional Focus Point
[Base: 8]

Activation: Instantaneous

Usable directly after you refocus. If you've spent 2 focus points since your last refocus, you regain 2 focus points instead of 1 from your refocus.





Use Focus Point For Ally
[Base: 12, Mod -4]

Activation: Instantaneous

Default Action: Reaction

You can pay for the entirety of a focus point abilities activation on behalf of an ally (you must have the focus points).



Modify Ongoing Effect Perimeter



Activation: Instantaneous


When you activate this effect, you can change a decision you made with another activated ongoing effect. The decision must one that had to have been made
when the effect was initially activated.

  • You can change one such type of decision (specified when designing the this effect, such as location, or target of the benefit) (Base: 8)
  • You can change one decision per activation of this effect, but you can pick different decision points each time. (Base: 12)
  • You can change any and all decisions that were selectable when the target effect was activated. (Base: 16, Mod +8)


Type of Effects that can be modified by this augmentation:

  • Any Spells (Default)
  • Abilities wherein the most expensive effect has one of the following traits: Acid, Cold, Electricity, Force, Negative, Positive, Sonic, Any Alignment, Fear, Light, Shadow (Mod +6)
  • Abilities wherein the most expensive effect has the Emotion, Fire, Water, Air or Earth traits (Mod +10)


Note: This ability will not let you double up the amount of damage you deal in a round or targets for a damaging effect, even if you reposition a damaging effect in a way that it would make sense. You could move a zone that damages creatures non-immediately, however.

Temporary Effect Augmentation (aka: Meta-magic)



Activation: Instantaneous

This complex effect is an augmentation to other effects. Once activated, this ability modifies the very next action you take.


Degree of Augmentation:

  • Added Specific Boost: Pay for the boost as normal, then add Base +8 for this boost.
  • Reduced Specific Limit: Pay off the boost as normal, then add Base +8 for this boost.
  • Alter existing Choices: This alters a single choice (chosen when you design this ability) made when the modified ability was originally selected, such as changing the type of damage on a damaging effect to a specified new type. (Base: 8, Mod +2).
  • Added Effects:
    You add entirely new effects to the activated effect. To calculate the base cost of this "augmentation", build the group of effects you want to add as normal, then add Mod +6 to each of them from the boost of this effect (you cannot select a situational limit reflecting that they can only be used in this added way, that is already calculated in this boost's cost). Add the number of actions this ability cost to the actions to activate the "Temporary Ability Augmentations" effect itself (2 reactions aren't possible, so in that case it uses an action and a reaction).

Type of Abilities that can be modified by this augmentation:

  • Any Spells (Default)
  • Abilities wherein the most expensive effect has one of the following traits: Acid, Cold, Electricity, Force, Negative, Positive, Sonic, Any Alignment, Fear, Light, Shadow (Mod +6)
  • Abilities wherein the most expensive effect has the Emotion, Fire, Water, Air or Earth traits (Mod +10)


Special Modifications:

  • Persistent Change: This effect creates a single modifier that lasts for a duration (default duration = Until the End of your Next Turn). During the duration, all qualifying abilities that you activate are modified by this effect. (Mod +20)
