Ferocity


4 Points
Behavioral Trait, Healing and HP
Reaction; Usable once per day; Trigger You would be reduced to 0 Hit Points but not immediately killed.
Fierceness in battle runs through your blood, and you refuse to fall from your injuries. You avoid being knocked out and remain at 1 Hit Point, though you still gain a serious wound (or a grievous wound if the damage was high enough.)

Boost: (+3 points) Incredible Ferocity: Given time to collect yourself after a near-death scrape, you can rebuild your ferocity and withstand additional finishing blows. You can use Ferocity with a frequency of once per hour, rather than once per day.

Boost: (+1 point) Rampaging Ferocity: You lash out viciously even as you fend off death. As a free action, when you use Ferocity, make a single melee
Strike. If this Strike brings a foe to 0 Hit Points, this activation of Ferocity doesn't count against its frequency.



Boost: (+2 points) Undying Ferocity: You resist death's clutches with supernatural vigor. When you use Ferocity, you gain 10 temporary Hit Points

Boost: (+1 point) Ferocious Beasts: Animal companions or bonded animals you have gain the Ferocity feat, and they gain a reaction they can use only for Ferocity. If you have the Undying Ferocity
boost, all animal companions or bonded animals you have also gain the benefits of that boost when using the Ferocity reaction.
