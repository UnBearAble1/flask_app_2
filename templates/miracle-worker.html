Miracle Worker


15 Points
Alchemist; Healing
Frequency once every 10 minutes; Prerequisite: Legendary in Alchemist

Your alchemical mastery can resuscitate the recently slain. You can administer a legendary elixir of life to a creature who has been dead for no more than 2 rounds. When you do, that creature is immediately returned to life with 1 Hit Point and increases their wounded value by 1.
