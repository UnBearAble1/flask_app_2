Wet Shock Snare


EXPERT
Uncommon, Consumable, Electricity, Mechanical, Snare, Trap
Price 22 gp

A hidden copper plate conceals a bag of electric eels. When a creature steps on the plate, the eels become agitated, and the creature takes 4d8 electricity damage (DC 18 basic Reflex save). On a critical failure, the current causes the creature to become stunned 2. The eels are unharmed after use, but the mechanism is broken as usual for snares.
