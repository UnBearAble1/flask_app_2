Electric Arc


Cantrip, Electricity, Evocation
Traditions: arcane, primal
Two Actions;
somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 or 2 creatures
Saving Throw: basic Reflex

An arc of lightning leaps from one target to another. You deal electricity damage equal to 5d4 plus your spellcasting ability modifier.
