Dragon Transformation


10 Points
Warrior; Rage

1 Action; Usable Once Per Day; Concentrate, Instinct, Polymorph, Primal, Rage, Transmutation; Prerequisites: dragon instinct; Dragon's Rage Wings; Legendary in Warrior

You transform into a ferocious dragon, gaining the effects of the Dragon Form spell except that you can use your own AC and attack modifier, you apply your extra damage from rage, and the Breath Weapon uses your Warior DC. The action to Dismiss the transformation gains the rage trait.
