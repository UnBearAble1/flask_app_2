Deadly Aim


3 Points
Warrior; Hunt Prey, Ranged
1 Action; Prerequisites: Hunt Prey; Master in Warrior

You aim for your prey's weak spots, making your shot more challenging but dealing more damage if you hit. Make a ranged Strike against your hunted prey at a -2 penalty. You gain a +4 circumstance bonus to damage on that Strike.
