Snares


Full rules on snares.

TRAINED


  • Alarm Snare (3 gp) Creates a sound if triggered
  • Appetizing Flavor Snare (3 gp) Cover target with scent attractive to local predators
  • Caltrop Snare (3 gp) Drops caltrops on the target or adjacent square
  • Dust pods (3 gp) Dazzles and blinds the target
  • Hampering Snare (3 gp) creates difficult terrain
  • Marking Snare (3 gp) Makes the target easier to track
  • Signaling Snare (3 gp) Signals the target has been there
  • Spike Snare (3 gp) Deals 2d8 damage
  • Flare Snare (4 gp) Fires a flare up when triggered
  • Deadweight Snare (6 gp) Penalizes attack rolls
  • Expulsion Snare (2 gp) Moves target to adjacent square
  • Noisemaker's Snare (6 gp) 2d6 fire damage and maybe deafens
  • Static Snare (6 gp) Deals 1 persistent electricity, penalizes saves against electricity
  • Thunder Snare (6 gp) Booming thunder deafens target
  • Clockwork Chirper (7 gp) Chirps an alert and follows the target
  • Grasping Tree (7 gp) Makes flat-footed, maybe immobilizes
  • Rock Ripper Snare (9 gp) Makes difficult terrain, deals bludgeoning damage
  • Torrent Snare (8 gp) Makes difficult terrain, knocks prone, maybe pushes
  • Firebox (10 gp) fires a cone of fire (4d6) in a random direction
  • Detonating Gears Snare (12 gp) Deals 3d8 piercing, 3 persistent

EXPERT


  • Acid Spitter (15 gp) 3d6 acid and 5 persistent
  • Biting Snare (15 gp) 5d6 slashing damage
  • Fang Snare (15 gp) 2d8 poison damage
  • Hobbling Snare (15 gp) Penalizes speed by 10 or more
  • Stalker Bane Snare (15 gp) Foils invisibility
  • Tar Rocket Snare (15 gp) Renders target flat-footed and clumsy
  • Trip Snare (15 gp) Knocks target prone and flat-footed
  • Warning Snare (15 gp) Like alarm snare, but sound is disguised
  • Boom Snare (18 gp) 2d6 fire and 2d6 sonic, deafened
  • Glittering Snare (18 gp) Foils stealth
  • Clinging Ooze Snare (18 gp) 2d6 acid, 2d6 bludgeoning, flat footed
  • Tin Cobra (18 gp) 3d6 poison and sickened
  • Flame Drake Snare (19 gp) 6d6 fire cone
  • Pummeling Snare (22 gp) 5d8 bludgeoning
  • Wet Shock Snare (22 gp) 2d8 electricity, maybe stunned 2
  • Mirror Ball Snare (22 gp) 10 foot radius dazzled
  • Nauseating Snare (30 gp) sickened 2 or 3
  • Piercing Whistle Snare (30 gp) 4d6 sonic, 10 foot area, deafens

MASTER


  • Envonomed Snare (50 gp) 4d6 piercing and purple worm venom
  • Bomb Snare (50 gp) Ignites three bombs on target and adjacents
  • Grasping Snare (50 gp) Immobilizes and penalized speed
  • Rusting Snare (50 gp) Persistent damage to a metal object
  • Striking Snare (55 gp) 7d8 bludgeoning or piercing damage
  • Spirit Snare (55 gp) immobilizes incorporeal creatures
  • Frost Worm Snare (55 gp) 8d6 cold to 30 foot line
  • Puff Dragon (55 gp) 3d6 poison and sickened to 10 foot emanation
  • Binding Snare (55 gp) 2d6 piercing, prone and immobilized
  • Burning Badger Guts Snare (65 gp) 5d8 fire, 1d8 persistent fire, sickened
  • Snagging Hook Snare (65 gp) 3d10 piercing, 3d10 slashing

LEGENDARY


  • Mudrock Snare (100 gp) Stuns, immobilizes
  • Bleeding Spines Snare (100 gp) 6d8 piercing, 2d8 bleed, repeats in sq each round
  • Scything Blade Snare (100 gp) 9d8 slashing damage
  • Stunning Snare (100 gp) 6d6 bludgeoning, flat-footed and stunned
  • Shrapnel Snare (100 gp) 8d6 piercing in a 10 foot radius, deafened
