Fanaticism


-3 points

MENTAL, LAWFUL
You are completely, utterly, and overwhelmingly fanatical about a deity, country, ideology or similar thing. You will put it above all else, and possibly even die for it. This can, and probably will, be used against you by a clever opponent.

Like with Code of Honor, there are no self-control rolls. This is what you want to do, and you MUST do it.
