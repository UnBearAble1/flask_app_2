Formation Training


2 Points
Cultural Training; Combat Teamwork
Prerequisites: trained in all martial weapons

When you are adjacent to at least two allies that have this feat, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC and saving throws. This bonus increases to +2 on Reflex saves against area effects.

Boost: (+2 points) the allies don't need to have this feat in order for you to gain this benefit
