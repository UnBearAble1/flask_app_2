Mending


TRAINED

Transmutation
Traditions arcane, divine, occult, primal
Cast 10 minutes (somatic, verbal)
Range touch; Targets non-magical object of 1 Bulk or less

You repair the target item. You restore 10 Hit Points per spell tier to the target, potentially removing the broken condition if this repairs it past the item's Broken Threshold. You can't replace lost pieces or repair an object that's been completely destroyed.

Heightened (Expert) You can target a non-magical object of 2 Bulk or less, or a magical object of 1 Bulk or less.
