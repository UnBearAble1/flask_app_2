Group Bow; Traits Deadly d10, Propulsive, Volley 30 ft.

This projectile weapon is made from horn, wood, and sinew laminated together to increase the power of its pull and the force of its projectile. Like all longbows, its great size also increases the bow's range and power. You must use two hands to fire it, and it cannot be used while mounted. Any time an ability is specifically restricted to a longbow, such as Erastil's favored weapon, it also applies to composite longbows unless otherwise stated.

Deadly d10:
On a critical hit, the weapon adds a weapon damage die of the listed size. Roll this after doubling the weapon's damage.

Propulsive:
You add half your Strength modifier (if positive) to damage rolls with a propulsive ranged weapon. If you have a negative Strength modifier, you add your full Strength modifier instead.

Volley 30 ft.:
This ranged weapon is less effective at close distances. Your attacks against targets that are at a distance within the range listed take a -2 penalty.
