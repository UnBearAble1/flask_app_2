Heal Mount


2 Points
Religion; Lay on Hands
Requires: Lay on Hands

Your devotion to your mount manifests as a surge of positive energy. When you cast lay on hands on your mount, you can restore an additional 20 hit points if it is a normal animal, even one trained through specialized training feats, or 1d8 if it is a specialized mount gained through special means, such as an animal companion.
