Eternal Blessing


30 Points
Religion; Spell-Like Ability
Prerequisite: Legendary in Religion

Your good deeds have brought your deity's grace to you for all of eternity. You're continuously surrounded by a bless spell with an expert level. The radius is 15 feet, and you can't increase it. You can Dismiss the spell; if you do, or if it is dispelled for any reason, it returns automatically after 1 minute.
