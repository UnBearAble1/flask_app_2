Nearsighted


-4 points

PHYSICAL
You cannot read normal writing from further than a foot away, nor make out most signs at more than about 30 feet away. Creatures more than 30 feet away from you are concealed to you, you are -4 to perception checks to detect things more than 3 feet away, and you half all range increments for your ranged weapons and attacks.

If you can wear special glasses to eliminate this complication while wearing them, it is only worth 1 point instead of 4.
