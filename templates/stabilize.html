Stabilize


Cantrip, Healing, Necromancy, Positive
Traditions divine, primal
Two Actions, somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 dying creature

Positive energy shuts death's door. The target loses the dying condition, though it remains unconscious at 0 Hit Points. It does not gain the wounded condition.
