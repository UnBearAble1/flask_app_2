Revulsion


-1 to -6 points

SUPERNATURAL
You have a supernatural weakness toward a particular material. You have Weakness 5 against the material, which also applies if you are merely touched by it. Ingesting the material makes you sickened 3, and you take 5 persistent damage until you remove the sickened condition.

The value of this complication is based on how common the material is:

  • Occasional (leather, soap): [-1 point]
  • Common (smoke, wood): [-3 points]
  • Very Common (grass, metal): [-6 points]
