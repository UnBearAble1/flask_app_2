Lightning Storm


MASTER
Electricity, Evocation
Traditions primal
Three Actions;
material, somatic, verbal
Range 120 feet; Area 5-foot burst
Saving Throw basic Reflex; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

You create a black, rumbling storm cloud (the cloud cover the space of a 30 foot burst within range) and call down one lightning bolt. The bolt is a vertical line from any point in the the storm cloud to the ground below, dealing 4d12 electricity damage to creatures in the spell's area (basic Reflex save). The first time you Sustain the Spell each round, you can call another lightning bolt from the cloud. If you Cast this Spell outdoors, you can create two non-overlapping clouds instead of one, though you can still call down only one bolt per turn.

Heightened: The damage of each bolt increases by 2d12.
