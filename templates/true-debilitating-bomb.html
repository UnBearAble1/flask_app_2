True Debilitating Bomb


12 Points
Alchemist; Bombs
Prerequisites Greater Debilitating Bomb; Legendary in Alchemist

Ever inventive, you have discovered increasingly devastating ways for your bombs to impede and hamper your foes. When you use Debilitating Bomb, add the following to the list of effects you can choose from: enfeebled 2, stupefied 2, or a -15-foot status penalty to Speeds.
