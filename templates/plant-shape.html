Plant Shape


7 Points
Natural Abilities; Shapechanging
2 Actions; Polymorph, Primal, Transmutation

You take the form of an ordinary specimen of a specific type of plant or fungus (meaning immobile, as well) chosen when you take this feat. This has the effects of tree shape, except that your size remains the same, and if someone is suspicious or paranoid, they could attempt a perception check to detect something is off about the plant.
