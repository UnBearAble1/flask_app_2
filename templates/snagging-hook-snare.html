Snagging Hook Snare


MASTER
Consumable, Mechanical, Snare, Trap
Price 65 gp

This snare snags a creature with its wicked metal hooks. The first creature to enter the square takes 3d10 piercing damage and 3d10 slashing damage, with a DC 20 basic Reflex save. On a critical failure, the hooks piercing its flesh make the creature immobilized until it successfully Escapes (DC 20).
