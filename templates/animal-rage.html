Animal Rage


7 Points
Warrior; Rage

1 Action; Concentrate, Instinct, Polymorph, Primal, Rage, Transmutation; Prerequisites animal instinct; Master in Warrior

You transform into your animal. You gain the effects of an expert level animal form spell except you use your own statistics, Hit Points, and unarmed attacks instead of those granted by animal form. You also retain the constant abilities of your gear. Dismissing the transformation gains the rage trait.
