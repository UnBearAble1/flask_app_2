Warrior, Weapon Style Feats


IMPROVISED WEAPONS


  • Fane's Fourberie [E] -3 pts: Stance to use playing cards as daggers/shuriken/darts
  • Improvised Weapon Fighter [E] -1 pt: Don't take the -2 improvised weapon penalty


    • Improvised Critical [E] -1 pt: Apply crit specialization effects to improvised weapons


      • Improvised Pummel [E] -1 pt: Get +1 to improvised attack, weapon breaks


        • Shattering Strike [E] -3 pts: Reaction if improv weapon breaks, deal +3d6 dmg
        • Makeshift Strike [E] -1 pt: Pick up improvised weapon & Strike for 1 action
    • Surprise Strike [E] -1 pt: Creature flat footed against first improvised weapon strike

  • Oversized Throw [E] -3 pts: Throw big object, 2d10 dmg, 2 actions

RANGED WEAPONS


  • Archer's Aim [E} -3 pts: Get +2 to hit, ignore concealed, lessen hidden (2 actions)
  • Assisting Shot [E] -4 pts: Strike and give ally +1 or +2 to do the same
  • Bullseye [E] -2 pts: Spend an action gives next one +1 to hit, ignore concealed / cover
  • Crossbow Terror [E] -3 pts: Get +2 dmg with xbows (or +1 dice step if simple)
  • Debilitating Shot [M] -3 pts: Strike that slows (2 actions)
  • Double Shot [E] -3 pts: Strike twice at -2 each (2 actions)


    • Triple Shot [M] -7 pts: Can do three shots at -4 each (3 actions)
  • Far Shot [E] -3 pts: Double your ranged increments
  • Far Throw [M] -3 pts: Only -1 per each range increment beyond 1st
  • Forceful Shot [M] -4 pts: Strike pushes up to 10 feet, flat footed even on failure
  • Impossible Volley [L] -7 pts: Attack 20 foot area, -2 to hit, 3 actions
  • Incredible Ricochet [E] -3 pts: Press attack that ignores concealed and cover
  • Lobbed Attack [M] -3 pts: Attack with ranged while juggling, doesn't provoke reactions, foe possibly flat footed
  • Mobile Shot Stance [M] -4 pts: Stance lets you use melee reaction feats with ranged
  • Opportune Throw [M/E] -3 pts: Reaction attack using juggled thrown weapon
  • Parting Shot [E] -4 pts: Step and Strike, foe flat-footed (2 actions)
  • Penetrating Projectile [L] -8 pts: Attack foes in a 30 foot line (2 actions)
  • Perfect Shot [M] -4 pts: Strike for max damage (3 actions)
  • Point Blank Shot [E] -3 pts: Stance ignores volley trait, or +2 dmg if non-volley
  • Quick Shot [E] -2 pts: Draw a bow and strike for 1 action
  • Rebounding Toss [M] -4 pts: Strike with throw weapon bounces to 2nd target (2 actions)
  • Reloading Trick [M] -3 pts: Strike and Reload a crossbow for 1 action (1x a round)
  • Ricochet Stance [E] -3 pts: Stance bounces weapons back to your hand


    • Ricochet Feint [E] -2 pts: Feint with a thrown weapon while in the stance
  • Running Reload [E] -3 pts: Stride, Step, or Sneak and Reload for 1 action
  • Shootist's Draw [E] -3 pts: Draw xbow and strike, or strike and stow, 1 action

    • Repeating Hand Crossbow [M] -3 pts: Trained in Repeating Xbow and gain crit specialization Effect
  • Strong Arm [E] -3 pts: Thrown weapon range +10 feet

SHIELDS

  • Buckler Expertise [E] -2 pts: Get +2 to AC when raising a buckler
  • Divine Shield Wall [M] -4 pts: All your adjacent squares are difficult terrain for your foes

    • Rescuer's Press [M] -3 pts: Shield gains shove, can move to either side of shoved foe, and shove further
  • Mirror Shield [M] -3 pts: Reaction possibly lets you bounce spells back to caster
  • Nimble Shield Hand [M] -4 pts: Shield hand counts as free for interact actions
  • Paragon's Guard [M] -6 pts: Stance to always treat shield as raised
  • Practiced Defender [E] -1 pt: full speed with Defend exploration activity
  • Reactive Shield [E] -2 pts: Reaction to raise a shield
  • Reflexive Shield [M] -4 pts: Get raise a shield bonus, shield block, to reflex save

  • Shield Block [E] -1 pt: Reaction to let shield take damage for you


    • Aggressive Block [M] -2 pts: Shove or flat-foot the foe you shield block
    • Destructive Block [M] -3 pts: Double damage blocked/taken by shield
    • Disarming Block [M/T] -3 pts: Disarm the foe you block
    • Quick Block [M] -4 pts: Get additional reaction for shield block
    • Second Shield [E] -3 pts: Draw second shield if 1st destroyed by shield block
    • Shield Warden [E] -2 pts: Can protect adjacent ally with shield block

  • Shield Salvation [M] -5 pts: 1st time shield would be destroyed, goes to 1 instead
