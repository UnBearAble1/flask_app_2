Skilled Surge


2 Points
Behavioral Trait, Knowledge
Requires: Surge

You can choose to activate Surge when you attempt a skill check, instead of when you attempt an attack roll. If you do, you gain a +2 status bonus to the triggering skill check. If you get a success or critical success, you cannot use Surge again until your next daily preparations.
