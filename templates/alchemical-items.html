Alchemical Items


Alchemical Items can be crafted using the Alchemical Crafting feat, created instantly (in short lived format) using the Alchemist skill, found in the game world, or bought from vendors in some game worlds (ask your GM).

There are five types of Alchemical Items:

  • Alchemical Bombs: Consumable weapons that deal damage or produce special effects, and they sometimes deal splash damage.
  • Alchemical Drugs: Drugs offer short-term benefits with harmful side effects and long-term consequences.
  • Alchemical Elixirs:
    Alchemical liquids that are used by drinking them. These potent concoctions grant the drinker some alchemical benefits.
  • Alchemical Poisons: Potent toxins distilled or extracted from natural sources and made either stronger or easier to administer.
  • Alchemical Tools: Consumable items you don't imbibe, smoke, etc.
