Group Polearm; Traits Deadly d8, Forceful, Reach

This polearm consists of a long, single-edged blade on the end of a 7-foot pole. It is extremely effective at delivering lethal cuts at a distance.

Deadly d8:
On a critical hit, the weapon adds a weapon damage die of the listed size. Roll this after doubling the weapon's damage.

Forceful:
This weapon becomes more dangerous as you build momentum. When you attack with it more than once on your turn, the second attack gains a +2 circumstance bonus to damage, and each subsequent attack gains a circumstance +4 bonus to damage

Reach:
Natural attacks with this trait can be used to attack creatures up to the listed distance away instead of only adjacent creatures. Weapons with this trait are long and can be used to attack creatures up to 10 feet away instead of only adjacent creatures. For creatures that already have reach with the limb or limbs that wield the weapon, the weapon increases their reach by 5 feet.
