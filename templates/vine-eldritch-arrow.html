Vine Eldritch Arrow


3 Points
Warrior; Ranged Weapons

1 Action; Activate, Interact; You can only use one Eldritch Arrow per round; Prerequisites Eldritch Archer; Master in Warrior

You transform a non-magical arrow or bolt into a Vine Eldritch Arrow, which you can then fire with a later action. You must shoot the ammunition before the end of your turn or the magic dissipates. The DC is your tradition skill DC (occultism, arcane, nature, or religion) or spellcasting DC. The arrows revert back to normal arrows after a minute once fired.



Leafy stalks protrude from the shaft of this rustic arrow. When an activated vine arrow hits a target, the arrow's shaft splits and grows, wrapping the target in vines. The target takes a -10-foot circumstance penalty to its Speeds for 2d4 rounds, or until it Escapes. On a critical hit, the target is also immobilized until it Escapes.
