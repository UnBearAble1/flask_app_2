Power Attack


3 Points
Warrior; Powerful Blows

2 Actions; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior

You unleash a particularly powerful attack that clobbers your foe but leaves you a bit unsteady. Make a melee Strike. This counts as two attacks when calculating your multiple attack penalty. If this Strike hits, you deal an two extra die of weapon damage.

Furious Focus (+4 points) [Requires: Master in Warrior] You've learned to maintain your balance even when swinging furiously. When you make a Power Attack with a melee weapon you're wielding in two hands, it counts as one attack toward your multiple attack penalty instead of two.
