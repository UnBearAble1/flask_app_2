Toll Domain: Tireless Worker


5 Points
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Religion

You work constantly and refuse to let anything stand in your way. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Tireless Worker; Healing, Necromancy

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature; Duration sustained for up to 1 minute

You suppress your choice of one of the following conditions that's affecting the target: clumsy, encumbered, enfeebled, or fatigued. If you don't remove the effect that caused the condition, the condition returns after the spell's duration expires, assuming it hasn't been removed by then.
