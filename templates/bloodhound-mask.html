Bloodhound Mask


Alchemical, Consumable
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate Single Action Interact

This wide, single-use mask is designed to be slipped over your mouth and nose and its alchemical filter activated all with one hand. Once activated, the mask sharpens odors, giving you imprecise scent with the listed range. You can't wear other masks while you're wearing a bloodhound mask. When you use Survival to Track a creature by its scent, you gain an untrained bonus of +0 instead of -4, and the mask grants you the listed item bonus to your Survival check. The GM sets the Survival DC based on the area's ability to hold scent rather than on visual clues, as normal for using scent.
