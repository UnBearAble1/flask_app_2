Brutal Bully


3 Points
Warrior; Combat Maneuvers
Prerequisite: expert in warrior, expert in Athletics

You push your foes around and leave bruises. When you successfully Disarm, Grapple, Shove, or Trip a foe, you deal that foe bludgeoning damage equal to your Strength modifier; add this to the damage from a critical success to Trip. If your maneuver was already part of a special attack that did damage, do not add the additional damage from this ability.
