Toxicologist


5 Points
Alchemist; Poisons
Prerequisite: Trained in Alchemist

You specialize in toxins and venoms of all types. You start with the formulas for two alchemical poisons per tier that you can make, in addition to your other formulas. You can apply an injury poison you're holding to a weapon you're wielding as a single action, rather than as a 2-action activity.

Boost (+2 points) Perpetual Toxicologist (requires: Expert in Alchemist) When you craft an alchemical poison with Quick Alchemy, you don't automatically critically fail or fail on a natural 1.

Boost (+4 points) Toxicology Prodigy (requires: Master in Alchemist, Perpetual Toxicologist boost) When using
advanced alchemy
to make poisons during your daily preparations, you craft an extra one for each one you make.
