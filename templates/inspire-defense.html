Inspire Defense


5 Points
Performance; Composition Spell
Prerequisites: Composition Spells

You learn the Inspire Defense composition cantrip, which protects you and allies.



Inspire Defense: Cantrip, Composition, Emotion, Enchantment, Mental

1 Action; verbal; Area 60-foot emanation; Duration 1 round
