Death Domain: Eradicate Undeath


4 Points
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Religion

You have the power to end lives and destroy undead.. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Eradicate Undeath; Necromancy; Positive

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Area 30-foot cone; Saving Throw basic Fortitude

A massive deluge of life energy causes the undead to fall apart. Each undead creature in the area takes 5d12 positive damage.
