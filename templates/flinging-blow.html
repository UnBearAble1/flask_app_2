Flinging Blow


3 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts

2 Actions; Prerequisites: Master in Warrior; Powerful Fist

You take a moment to center yourself, then unleash a powerful blow that sends your target tumbling away. Make an unarmed Strike. If you hit, the target must succeed at a Fortitude save or be pushed up to 10 feet directly away from you (or 20 feet, on a critical failure) and fall prone. If another creature or physical object would prevent this movement, both your target and the blocking creature or object take 1d6 bludgeoning damage per 5 feet of movement that was prevented.
