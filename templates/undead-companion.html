Undead Companion


7 Points
Cultural Training; Necromancy
Requirement: an animal companion or a familiar

Your companion gains the undead trait instead of the animal trait and gains negative healing. It otherwise uses the normal rules for an animal companion or familiar; that means it can still be affected by many conditions or effects to which most undead are immune.
