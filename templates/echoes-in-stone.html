Echoes in Stone


4 Points
Natural Abilities; Stone Affinity
1 Action; Concentrate Requirements: You are standing on Stone

Until the start of your next turn, you gain a new sense: imprecise tremorsense with a range of 20 feet. A creature that moves more than 5 feet per action, or one that casts a spell or makes an attack while on the same ground, can be picked up by the sense.
