Far Lobber


3 Points
Alchemist; Bombs
Prerequisite: Expert in Alchemist

You've learned how to throw a longer distance. When you throw an alchemical bomb, it has a range increment of 30 feet instead of the usual 20 feet.

Boost (+7 points) Master Hurler (requires: Master in Alchemist) You lob bombs unerringly, despite obstructions or distance. When you throw an alchemical item with the bomb trait, its range increment increases to 60 feet.

Boost (+4 points) Uncanny Bombs (requires: Master in Alchemist) When you throw an alchemical item with the bomb trait, you reduce any circumstance bonus to the target's AC from cover by 1, and you automatically succeed at the flat check when targeting a concealed creature.
