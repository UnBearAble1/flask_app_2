Debilitating Bomb


5 Points
Alchemist; Bombs
Prerequisite: Bomber; Expert in Alchemist

Free Action; Additive 1; Frequency once per round; Trigger You use Quick Alchemy to craft an alchemical bomb

Your bombs impose additional effects on your enemies. You mix a substance into the bomb that causes one of the following: dazzled, deafened, flat-footed, or a -5-foot status penalty to Speeds. If the attack with that bomb hits, the target must succeed at a Fortitude saving throw or suffer that effect until the start of your next turn. If you craft two bombs due to a critical success on your Quick Alchemy, pick only one to apply the effect to.

Boost (+4 Points) Greater Debilitating Bomb (requires: Master in Alchemist): When you use Debilitating Bomb, add the following to the list you can choose from:
clumsy
1,
enfeebled
1,
stupefied
1, or -10-foot status penalty to Speeds.

Boost (+3 Points) True Debilitating Bomb (requires: Legendary in Alchemist, Greater Debilitating Bomb Boost): When you use Debilitating Bomb, add the following to the list of effects you can choose from:
enfeebled
2,
stupefied
2,
or a -15-foot status penalty to Speeds.

Boost (+2 Points) Expert Debilitation (requires: Legendary in Alchemist): If you craft two bombs due to a critical success on your Quick Alchemy, you can apply the same or different effect from your list of Debilitating Bomb effects to each bomb.

Boost (+4 Points) Perfect Debilitating Bomb (requires: Master in Alchemist): When you use Debilitating Bomb, your target avoids the condition the bomb imposes only if it critically succeeds at its saving throw.
