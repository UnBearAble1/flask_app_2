Baba Yaga's Patronage


2 Points
Spellcaster; Familiar
Prerequisites: Familiar

Some spellcasters can choose an inanimate object as a familiar. If they do, they still can gain master abilities and some familiar abilities that don't require movement. The object familiar has no Speeds and must select a Speed familiar ability before it can move, coming to life in a way appropriate to the chosen Speed and using the statistics of a normal familiar for that day.
