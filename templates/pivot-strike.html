Pivot Strike


4 Points
Warrior; Weapon Training

2 Actions; Attack, Open; Requirements You are wielding your staff or polearm; Prerequisites: Master in Warrior; Staff Acrobat

You Leap and then Strike using your staff or polearm. You gain a +4 circumstance bonus to damage for the Strike. Regardless of whether your Strike hit, you can then attempt to Shove or Trip the target.
