Mood Swings


-4 points

MENTAL, CHAOTIC
Your moods are on a see-saw, swinging from heightened enthusiasm and morose withdrawal. At the beginning of each session roll a 1d6. On a 1-3, you are in the manic phase,
a 4-6 indicates depression. Every five hours of in-game time, roll a DC 11 Flat Check. On a failure, your mood begins to swing, and moves to the other side over the course of an hour.



While manic, you have the Overconfidence and Workaholic complications. While depressed, you are depressed 1.
