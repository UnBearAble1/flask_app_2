Mountain Stance


6 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts

1 Action; Stance; Requirements You are unarmored and touching the ground; Prerequisites: Powerful Fist

You enter the stance of an implacable mountain--a technique first discovered by dwarven monks--allowing you to strike with the weight of an avalanche. The only Strikes you can make are falling stone unarmed attacks. These deal 2d8 bludgeoning damage; are in the brawling group; and have the forceful, nonlethal, and unarmed traits. While in Mountain Stance, you gain a +4 item bonus to AC and a +2 circumstance bonus to any defenses against being Shoved or Tripped. However, you have a Dexterity modifier cap to your AC of +0, meaning you don't add your Dexterity to your AC, and your Speeds are all reduced by 5 feet. The item bonus to AC from Mountain Stance is cumulative with armor bonuses you get from magical clothing.
