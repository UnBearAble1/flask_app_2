Light Sleeper


-1 point

PHYSICAL
You have a difficult time getting to sleep and staying asleep, especially if your sleep is disturbed. In order to get to sleep in an uncomfortable area (like camping) or someplace noisy, you must make a DC 8 flat check. If you fail, you lose an hour of sleep.
You get to make that check again each hour until you fall asleep. This may mean you don't get your full 8 hours of rest.

You automatically succeed on any checks to regain consciousness from noise or being jostled, but you must again make the check to fall back to sleep.
