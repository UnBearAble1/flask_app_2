Channel Smite


5 Points
spellcaster; Harm or Heal
2 Action; Necromancy; Cost Cast a harm or heal spell; Prerequisites: Expert in Spellcaster; Expert in Warrior

You siphon the destructive energies of positive or negative energy through a melee attack and into your foe. Make a melee Strike and add the spell's damage to the Strike's damage. You have to roll both the attack AND the spell roll. This is negative damage if you expended a harm spell or positive damage if you expended a heal spell. The spell is cast with no effect if your Strike fails or hits a creature that isn't damaged by that energy type (such as if you hit a non-undead creature with a heal spell).
