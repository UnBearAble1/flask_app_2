Corgi Mount


6 Points
Cultural Training; Small Stature
Requires: Tiny

You gain a Corgi Familiar. It's Small rather than Tiny, and it's appropriate for use as your mount, unlike most familiars. It has the scent ability, which counts against your limit for familiar and master abilities as normal. Furthermore, it can never gain a familiar ability that grants it any Speeds other than a land Speed.
