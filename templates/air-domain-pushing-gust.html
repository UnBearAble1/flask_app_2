Air Domain: Pushing Gust


3 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Nature; Trained in Religion

You can control winds and the weather. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Pushing Gust: Air, Conjuration

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 500 feet; Targets 1 creature; Saving Throw Fortitude

Giving the air a push, you buffet the target with a powerful gust of wind; it must attempt a Fortitude save.

  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target is pushed 5 feet away from you.
  • Failure The target is pushed 10 feet away from you.
  • Critical Failure The target is pushed 10 feet away from you and knocked prone.
