Nauseating Snare


EXPERT
Consumable, Mechanical, Poison, Snare, Trap
Price 30 gp

You position particularly foul substances to splash over a creature. The first creature to enter the square must attempt a DC 18 Fortitude saving throw.


  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature becomes sickened 1.
  • Failure The creature becomes sickened 2.
  • Critical Failure The creature becomes sickened 3.
