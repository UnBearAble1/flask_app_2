Diplomacy (CHR)


You influence others through negotiation and flattery.

Untrained Actions


  • Gather Information: You canvass local markets, taverns, and gathering places in an attempt to learn about a specific individual or topic.
  • Make an Impression: You seek to make a good impression on someone to make them temporarily agreeable.
  • Persuade: Diplomacy is one of the three approaches when you
    attempt to persuade someone of a truth or to taking your side.
  • Request: You can make a request of a creature that's friendly or helpful to you.
Useful reference: Disposition and Attitude
