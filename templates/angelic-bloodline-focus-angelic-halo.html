Angelic Bloodline Focus: Angelic Halo


1 Point
Religion; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Expert in Religion

You gain the focus spell, Angelic Halo, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Angelic Halo; Abjuration, Good

1 Action; verbal; Area 15-foot emanation; Duration 1 minute

You gain an angelic halo with an aura that increases allies' healing from the heal spell. Allies in your halo's emanation who are healed by a heal spell gain a status bonus to Hit Points regained equal to double the heal spell's tier.



Additionally, immediately upon casting this spell, until the end of your next turn, an angelic aura grants you a +1 status bonus to saving throws for 1 round.
