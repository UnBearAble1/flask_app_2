Hymn of Healing


6 Points
Performance; Composition Spell
Prerequisites: Composition Spells
You learn the Hymn of Healing composition spell, which mends wounds.

Hymn of Healing; Composition, Positive, Necromancy, Healing
Special: This focus spell costs 2 focus points to cast.
2 Actions; verbal; Range 60 feet;
Targets you or 1 ally; Duration: Sustained up to 5 rounds.

Your divine singing mends wounds and provides a temporary respite from harm. The target gains fast healing 4 (expert-tier). When you Cast the Spell and each time you Sustain the Spell, the target gains 4 temporary Hit Points, which last for 1 round.

Special: If you are Master in Performance, you can cast this spell as a Master-Tier effect (increasing its fast healing and temporary hit points to 5). If you are Legendary, you can cast this as a Legendary-Tier effect (increasing its fast healing and temporary hit points to 6), but it costs 3 Focus Points.
If you use either of these effects, you cannot use either again until you've had 2d4 full rests.
