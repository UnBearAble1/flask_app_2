Smoked Goggles


Price
20 gp

Usage
worn eyepiece

These goggles use lenses made out of smoked glass to protect against creatures with gaze attacks. While wearing smoked goggles, you're always considered to be
Averting your Gaze, but all creatures have
concealment
from you. The fact that the goggles conceal creatures is part of what grants the wearer the item's benefits. If you have a way to negate the concealment from the smoked goggles, you no longer gain the benefit, either.
