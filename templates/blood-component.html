Blood Component


8 Points
Spellcaster; Improved Spellcasting
Prerequisites: Expert in Spellcaster
You can bypass the need for incantations and gestures by drawing energy directly from your blood, causing you to visibly glow the color of your blood and crackle with magical energy. When you Cast a Spell, you can replace all verbal, material, or somatic spellcaster components with a blood component. To use a blood component, you lose Hit Points equal to twice the spell's tier as the energy in your blood is depleted, and you can't decrease the Hit Points lost in any way, nor can mundane uses of the Medicine skill heal this damage. As usual for altering components, this has no effect on the number of actions required to Cast the Spell, and your Cast a Spell activity gains the concentrate trait but not the manipulate trait. You can't use blood components to replace any required part of a spell's cost.
