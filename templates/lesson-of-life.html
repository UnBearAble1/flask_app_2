Lesson of Life


6 Points
occultism; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Expert in occultism

You learn the Life Boost hex. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Life Boost; Healing, Hex, Necromancy
Special: This Focus Spell takes 2 Focus Points to Cast


1 Action somatic; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature; Duration 4 rounds

You temporarily bolster the target's vitality. The target gains fast healing 5.

Special: If you are Master tier in Occultism, you can cast this spell as a Master-Tier effect (increasing its fast healing to 6). If you are Legendary tier, you can cast this as a Legendary-Tier effect (increasing its fast healing to 7), but it costs 3 Focus Points.
If you use either of these options, you cannot use either again until you have completed 2d4 full rests.
