Stealth Feats


ARMOR


  • Armored Stealth [E] -2 pts: Ignore some stealth penalties while wearing armor

CROWDS


  • Lost in the Crowd [T] -1 pt: Full speed stealth in crowd, gain +2 to +4 to stealth there


    • Crowd Mastery [T] -1 pt: Treat crowd as flankers against enemy

FOCUS SPELLS

Darkness Domain

  • Cloak of Shadow [M] -3 pts: Focus spell: Drape target in droppable dim light cloak
  • Darkened Eyes [M] -3 pts: Focus spell: Suppress targets darkvision, maybe blind it
Secrecy Domain

  • Forced Quiet [M] -2 pts: Focus spell: prevent target form being heard over 10 feet
  • Safeguard Secret [M] -3 pts: Focus spell: Give targets +4 to keep secrets for an hour
Shadow Magic:

  • Shadow Illusion [M/T] -10 pts: Focus spell: Create illusion in dim light or darkness
  • Dance of Darkness [M/M] -5 pts: Focus spell: Stride, create 10ft burst magic darkness
  • Shadow Jump [L] -5 pts: Focus spell: Teleport up to 120 feet in darkness

IMPROVED STEALTH


  • Fane's Escape [E] -2 pts: Use playing cards to create cover enough to hide
  • Foil Senses [M] -4 pts: You are always taking precautions against special senses
  • Shadow Mark [E] -2 pts: Target gets perception penalties if you follow them
  • Sneak Savant [M] -7 pts: You get improved success on sneak actions

MOVEMENT


  • Fleeting Shadow [E] -2 pts: Hide then sneak twice for 2 actions
  • Swift Sneak [M] -6 pts: You can move full speed while sneaking


    • Legendary Sneak [L] -16 pts: Hide/Sneak without cover on your turn, get free avoid notice in exploration mode
  • Tactical Entry [M] -1 pt: When you roll stealth for initiative and go first, stride without triggering reactions as a free action

SPELL-LIKE ABILITIES


  • Hidden Paragon [L] -14 pts: Once a day, become invisible when you hide for 1 minute, and no magic can find you.
  • Nameless Anonymity [E] -8 pts: Once a day, cast nondetection on yourself
  • Shadow Dancer [L/L] -10 pts: You gain greater darkvision, +2 to stealth in dim/darkness

SMUGGLING

Experienced Smuggler [T] -1 pt: Much higher chance of success to conceal items

SURPRISE ATTACK


  • Ambushing Knockdown [E/E] -5 pts: For 2 actions: Stride 1/2 speed from hiding, strike creature, knock them prone
  • Scout's Pounce [E] -2 pts: For 2 actions: Stride from hiding, strike twice, foes are maybe considered flat footed
  • Spring from Shadows [E] -3 pts: For 1 action: Stride from hiding, strike while hidden
  • Startling Appearance [M] -4 pts: Strike unaware creature, remains flat footed for rest of turn and is frightened 1 or 2.


    • Frightening Appearance [M/E] -1 pt: Also attempt to demoralize multiple opponents
    • Stunning Appearance [M] -3 pts: Target also stunned 1 or 2
  • Subtle Shank [M] -4 pts: Draw concealed weapon, strike, and conceal weapon for 2 actions, possibly without anyone noticing at all.

TEAMWORK


  • Quiet Allies [E] -1 pt: When Avoiding notice, allies Follow the Expert, all roll as one
  • Underhanded Assault [T] -2 pts: If ally is flanking foe, sneak up to foe even if not possible and strike them

TERRAIN


  • Camouflage [M/E] -5 pts: Spend 1 minute blending in, can sneak even if observed


    • Shadow Hunter [M/M] -6 pts: Always concealed if you use camouflage
  • Terrain Stalker [T] -2 pts: Can remain undetected in favored terrain without roll if move slowly


    • Terrain Scout [T] -1 pt: Allies Follow the Expert when you Avoid Notice, give one the benefit of any of your Terrain feats
  • Warden's Step [M/E] -3 pts: Avoid Notice, and let any number of allies do so for free
