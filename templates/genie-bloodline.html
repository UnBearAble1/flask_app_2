Genie Bloodline


10 Points
Spellcaster; Magical Bloodine
Prerequisites: Trained in Spellcaster (Arcane)

Through lineage, magic, or wishes made real, the blood of a noble genie flows through your veins. Add the following spells to your repertoire, even if they aren't normally part of the arcane tradition (note you must still have the appropriate level of spellcaster proficiency to cast the spell):

  • cantrip: detect magic,
  • Trained: illusory disguise, variable*
  • Expert: enthrall, creation,
  • Master: true seeing, variable*
  • Legendary: energy aegis, variable*

Choose the type of genie that influenced your bloodline: janni, djinni, efreeti, marid, or shaitan. You can't change your genie type later. This determines some of your granted spells:

  • Janni: Trained: create food; Master: banishment; Legendary: scintillating pattern
  • Djinni: Trained: invisibility; Master: illusory scene; Legendary: punishing winds
  • Efreet: Trained: enlarge; Master: elemental form (fire only); Legendary: maze
  • Marid: Trained: water walk; Master: control water; Legendary: horrid wilting
  • Shaitan: Trained: glitterdust; Master: wall of stone; Legendary: earthquake

Whenever you cast a spell from the list of spells above, gain the following Blood Magic ability: Your spellcasting warps reality and distracts your foes. Either you gain a +1 status bonus to Deception checks for 1 round, or a target takes a -1 status penalty to Perception for 1 round.
