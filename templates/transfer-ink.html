Transfer Ink


Cantrip, Conjuration, Teleportation
Traditions
arcane, occult
Casting time: 10 minutes ; somatic, verbal
Range: 5 feet

You teleport ink from one object or willing person to another. This cantrip can move up to 3 square feet of ink to another viable surface. The ink retains its shape and position, allowing you to transfer a tattoo in its original form to a scroll, for example, or the letters of a page to a new piece of parchment.
