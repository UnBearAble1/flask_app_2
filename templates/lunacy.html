Lunacy


-2 points

MENTAL, CHAOTIC
The moon has a significant effect on your personality. Exactly how depends on the phase of the moon:

  • Full: Emotional and violent (you have the bad temper complication, -2 to willpower saves and self control rolls for your other complications)
  • New Moon: Passive (you suffer from the Laziness complication)
  • Waxing: You are focused and pleasant
  • Waning: You are apathetic and a little touchy (roleplay it!)

Full (4 Days) > Waning (10 Days) > New Moon (4 Days) > Waxing (10 Days) >
