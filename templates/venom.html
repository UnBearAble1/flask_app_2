Venom


4 Points
Physiology; Augment Natural Weapon
1 Action; Usable once per day; Requirements Any ancestry feat providing a natural unarmed attack that deals slashing, piercing or poison damage.

Your body produces a natural poison. If you attack with a natural unarmed attack that deal slashing, piercing or poison damage, before the end of your next turn and it hits and deals damage, you deal persistent 10 poison damage to the target.

Boost (+3 points) Venomous: You can use Venom twice per day.
