Glimpse of Redemption


17 Points
Religion/Warrior; Champion's Reaction
Prerequisites: Master in Religion, Expert in Warrior

Reaction; [Tactic]; Trigger An enemy damages your ally, and both are within 15 feet of you.

Your foe hesitates under the weight of sin as visions of redemption play in their mind's eye. The foe must choose one of the following options:

  • The ally is unharmed by the triggering damage.
  • The ally gains 12 resistance to all damage against the triggering damage. After the damaging effect is applied, the enemy becomes enfeebled 2 until the end of its next turn.
