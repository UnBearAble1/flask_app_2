Crafting Feats


AUGMENT GEAR


  • Tweak Appearances [E] -5 pts: Modify someone's clothing to give them various bonuses

CONSUMABLES


  • Bless Tonic [M] -2 pts: Channel positive to give elixir negative resistance properties
  • Bless Toxin [M] -5 pts: Channel negative to cause poison to prevent healing
  • Seasoned [T] -1 pts: Get +1 or +2 in crafting food, drink, potions

CRAFT SPECIFIC ITEMS


  • Alchemical Crafting [T] -1 pts: You can craft alchemical items and gain formulas
  • Cauldron [E] -2 pts: You can craft oils and potions, and craft extra


    • Temporary Potions [E] -7 pts: You can brew free potions each day
  • Lethargy Poison [T] -7 pts: You can craft lethary / stupor poisons
  • Magical Crafting [E] -5 pts: You can craft magic items
  • Specialty Crafting [T] -3 pts: Get +2 crafting in your specialty


    • Impeccable Crafting [M] -2 pts: Improved successes crafting your specialty
  • Witch's Hut [L] -15 pts: You can craft a huge mobile hut that follows you

FOCUS SPELLS


  • Creation Domain: Artistic Flourish [M] -4 pts: Focus Spell boosts item effectiveness
  • Creation Domain: Splash of Art [M] -3 pts: Focus Spell with area random debuff
  • Indulgence Domain: Overstuff [M] -2 pts: Focus Spell sickens a target
  • Indulgence Domain: Take Its Course [M] -5 pts: Focus Spell speeds up removing certain effects

FORMULAS


  • Reverse Engineering [E] -3 pts: You reverse engineer more effectively

IDENTIFY ITEMS


  • Alchemical Savant [T] -1 pts: You can quickly identify alchemical items
  • Crafter's Appraisal [T] -3 pts: You can identify magic items with Crafting

KA STONE


  • Living Monolith [E] -10 pts: Your dying level doesn't increase on a failed recovery

    • Ka Stone Ritual [E] -5 pts: Gain the Enlarge Spell a multiple times a day


      • Attunement to Stone [E] -5 pts: Gain limited tremorsense and Meld into Stone spell
      • Fortified Flesh [E] -5 pts: Gain physical resistance based on ka stone feats
      • Judgement of the Monolith [E] -7 pts: Cast zone of truth and talking corpse
      • Stone Blood [E] -5 pts: More quickly recover from persistent bleed damage
      • Stone Communion [E] -10 pts: Gain stone tell spell

REPAIR


  • Improvised Crafting [T] -3 pts: Repair without a kit, craft basic gear
  • Improvised Repair [E] -1 pt: Can quickly repair an item, but it is shoddy
  • Quick Repair [T] -5 pts: Repair an item much more quickly

SCROUNGING


  • Craft Facsimile [E] -1 pt: You can create a quick evenly weighted shoddy replica
  • Scrounger [T] -3 pts: Can easily craft with scraps without tools


    • High Quality Scrounging [E] -3 pts: Can now craft expert tier items

SIGNIFIER MASK


  • Signifier Mask [E] -4 pts: Eyeless mask gives you +1 to some social actions

    • Masked Castring [E] -3 pts: Avert gaze as a free action when spellcasting
    • Signifier's Sight [L] -5 pts: Gain lowlight or darkvision and other bonuses

SKILL SUBSTITUTION


  • Expert Disassembler [M] -8 pts: Can use crafting to pick locks / disable device

SNARES


  • Snare Crafting [T] -5 pts: You can craft snares, gain some formula


    • Remote Trigger [T] -2 pts: You can trigger snares from a distance
    • Snare Hopping [T] -5 pts: Focus Spell can teleport a snare 50 feet
  • Snare Specialist [E] -12 pts: You can quick deploy some snares a day


    • Giant Snare [E] -4 pts: Your quick deployed snares cover 10x10 area
    • Quick Snares [M] -5 pts: Can craft snares much more quickly


      • Lightning Snares [M] -5 pts: Can craft them even faster
    • Surprise Snare [E] -4 pts: Can attack an adjacent foe with your snare
    • Ubiquitous Snares [L] -6 pts: Double the number of prepared snares


      • Impossible Snares [L] -4 pts: Can prepare 1 snare a minute for free
