Death Domain: Death's Call


7 Points
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Legendary in Religion

You have the power to end lives and destroy undead.. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Death's Call; Necromancy

Reaction; verbal; Trigger A living creature within 20 feet of you dies, or an undead creature within 20 feet of you is destroyed; Duration 1 minute

Seeing another pass from this world to the next invigorates you. You gain temporary Hit Points equal to the creatures highest proficiency tier x 5 (trained = 5, Expert = 10, Master = 15, Legendary = 20) If the triggering creature was undead, double the number of temporary Hit Points you gain. These last for the duration of the spell, and the spell ends if all the temporary Hit Points are depleted earlier.
