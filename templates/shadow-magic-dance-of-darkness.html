Shadow Magic: Dance of Darkness


5 Points
Stealth; Focus Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Stealth, Master in Performance

You gain the Dance of Darkness focus spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Dance of Darkness; Darkness, Evocation

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Duration varies

You dance, Striding up to half your Speed. At either the start or end of your Stride, you create an area of magical darkness in a 10-foot burst centered on your location. This area is filled with magical darkness that works the same way as that created by an expert level darkness spell. When you create the darkness, attempt a Performance check to dance, with a master DC, to determine the duration of the darkness.

  • Critical Success 1 minute
  • Success 2 rounds
  • Failure 1 round
