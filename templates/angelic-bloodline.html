Angelic Bloodline


10 Points
Spellcaster; Magical Bloodine
Requires: Trained in Spellcaster (Divine)

One of your forebears hailed from a celestial realm, or your ancestors' devotion led to their lineage being blessed. Add the following spells to your repertoire, even if they aren't normally part of the divine tradition (note you must still have the appropriate level of spellcaster proficiency to cast the spell):

  • cantrip: light,
  • Trained: heal and spiritual weapon,
  • Expert: searing light and divine wrath,
  • Master: flame strike and blade barrier,
  • Legendary: divine decree and divine aura,

Whenever you cast a spell from the list of spells above, gain the following Blood Magic ability: An angelic aura protects you or one target, granting a +1 status bonus to saving throws for 1 round.
