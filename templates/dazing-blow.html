Dazing Blow


6 Points
Warrior; Grappling

1 Action, Press; Requirements You have a creature grabbed; Prerequisites: Master in Warrior

You pummel a held foe, hoping to stagger them. Make a melee Strike against a creature you have grabbed. The weapon damage from this Strike is bludgeoning damage. If the Strike hits, the creature must attempt a Fortitude save against your Warrior DC; this is an incapacitation effect.

  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature is stunned 1.
  • Failure The creature is stunned 2.
  • Critical Failure The creature is stunned 3.
