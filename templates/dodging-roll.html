Dodging Roll


6 Points
Acrobatics; Saves and Resistances
Prerequisites: master in Acrobatics

Reaction; Trigger You take damage from an area effect that allows a Reflex save.

You dodge a blow at the last possible moment. You Step and gain 10 resistance to all damage against the triggering effect. If your Step moves you out of the triggering effect's area, this resistance is instead equal to 10 plus your Dexterity modifier.
