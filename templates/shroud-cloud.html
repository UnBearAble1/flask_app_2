Shroud Cloud


5 Points
Natural Abilities; Elemental
Requires: The Shroud

When you use The Shroud, you can spend one additional action to disperse it as a 10-foot-radius cloud centered on you instead of as haze; the activity gains the concentrate trait. All creatures within the cloud become concealed, and all creatures outside the cloud become concealed to creatures within it.
