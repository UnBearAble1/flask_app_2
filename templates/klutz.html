Klutz


-1 or -3 points

PHYSICAL
You are prone to physical blunders. This doesn't mean you have a low Dexterity, just that, for whatever reason you are exceptionally awkward. This comes in two levels:

  • Klutz: [-1 pt] When in a situation where being a klutz would be troublesome, such as in a shop, around explosives, in a lab, or the like, the DM makes a secret DC 8 flat check. On a failure, you land yourself in trouble.
  • Total Klutz: [-3 pts] As above, but anytime you fail a Dexterity based roll, it is considered a critical failure.
