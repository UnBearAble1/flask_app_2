Cloudkill


MASTER
Death, Necromancy, Poison
Traditions arcane, primal
Three Actions ; material, somatic, verbal
Range 120 feet; Area 20-foot burst
Saving Throw basic Fortitude; Duration 1 minute

You conjure a poisonous fog. This functions as obscuring mist, except the area moves 10 feet away from you each round. You deal 5d8 poison damage to each breathing creature that starts its turn in the spell's area. You can Dismiss the spell.

Heightened: The damage increases by 2d8.
