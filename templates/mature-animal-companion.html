Mature Animal Companion


12 Points
Nature; Animal Companion
Prerequisites Animal Companion; Expert in Nature or Spellcasting (Primal Tradition)

Your animal companion grows up, becoming a mature animal companion and gaining additional capabilities:


  • +1 to attack and damage rolls
  • +1 to AC, Perception and Saving Throws
  • +5 Hit Points
  • Advance any three skills to Expert
  • If your companion is medium or smaller, it grows by one size.
