Angelic Bloodline Focus: Angelic Wings


10 Points
Religion; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Angelic Bloodline Focus: Angelic Halo; Master in Religion

You gain the focus spell, Angelic Wings, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Angelic Wings; Evocation, Light

2 Actions; somatic, verbal; Duration: 1 minute

Wings of pure light spread out from your back, allowing you to make long, flighted jumps. Each round this is in effect, you gain a fly speed equal to your speed, but it ends at the end of the round and you begin to fall - gliding down 60 feet (you take no damage if you hit the ground.) Your wings cast bright light in a 30-foot radius. When this spell's duration would end, if you're still flying, you float to the ground, as feather fall.



Additionally, immediately upon casting this spell, until the end of your next turn, an angelic aura grants you a +1 status bonus to saving throws for 1 round.
