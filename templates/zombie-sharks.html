Zombie Sharks

These undead sharks haunt areas of death, like shipwrecks.

N, Huge, Undead, Aquatic, Skirmisher
Perception +9; blood scent, scent (imprecise) 100 feet

  • Blood Scent The shark can smell blood in the water from up to 1 mile away.
Skills Athletics +10, Stealth +8, Survival +5
Str +6, Dex +2, Con +4, Int -4, Wis +1, Cha -4
AC 19 ; Fort +8 , Ref +10 , Will +6
HP 80, Negative Healing;
Immunities
death effects, disease, mental, paralyzed, poison, unconscious;
Weaknesses
positive 10, slashing 5

    jaws +10 [+5/+0], Damage 2d8+5 piercing ( Deadly d12, and on a Crit, the sharks Jaws attacks deal 1d6 persistent necrotic damage and 1d6 bleed damage.)

After the Strike, the shark splashes back down into the water.
  • 1 Action: Savage:

    Requirement The shark hit with a jaws Strike on its most recent action this turn. Effect The creature the shark hit takes 2d8 slashing damage.
