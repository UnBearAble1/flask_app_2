Nature Domain: Vibrant Thorns


6 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Nature; Trained in Religion

You hold power over animals and plants.You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Vibrant Thorns; Morph, Plant, Transmutation

1 Action; somatic; Duration 1 minute

Your body sprouts a coat of brambly thorns that harm those that strike you and thrive on life magic. Adjacent creatures that hit you with a melee attack, as well as creatures that hit you with unarmed attacks, take 5 piercing damage each time they do. Anytime you cast a positive spell, the damage from your thorns increases to 4d6 until the start of your next turn.
