Rejuvenation


6 Points
Natural Abilities; Healing and HP
2 Actions; Usable once per day; Healing, Necromancy, Positive

The life-giving energy that flows in your blood revitalizes you. You recover 4d6 Hit Points instantly and gain 10 temporary Hit Points for 1 minute. This is an expert-tier effect. You can concentrate on this ability using 1 additional action to increase the effect to master-tier (and increasing the healing to 6d6). Doing so prevents you from using this ability again until you've taken 2d4 full rests.
