Great White Shark


Threat: 6

An apex predator among the coastal surface waters where it hunts, the great white shark is one of the largest shark species. These silent killers glide gracefully through the ocean, always in search of their next meal.

Recall Knowledge - Animal (Nature)

N, Huge, Animal, Aquatic, Skirmisher
Perception +10; blood scent, scent (imprecise) 100 feet
Blood Scent: The shark can smell blood in the water from up to 1 mile away.
Skills Athletics +12, Stealth +8, Survival +7
Str +6, Dex +2, Con +4, Int -4, Wis +1, Cha -4
AC 20; Fort +9, Ref +11, Will +7
HP 90
Speed swim 40 feet

Actions:

  • Jaws; Melee Single Action;
    +11 [+6/+1], Damage 2d8+6 piercing
  • Breach; Two Actions; The shark Swims up to its swim Speed, then Leaps vertically out of the water up to 25 feet high, making a Strike against a creature at any point during the jump (this lets it attack a creature within 30 feet of the water's surface). After the Strike, the shark splashes back down into the water.
  • Savagery; Single Action


    • Requirement The shark hit with a jaws Strike on its most recent action this turn.
    • Effect The creature the shark hit takes 2d8+6 slashing damage, which is applied to the same wound the Shark delivered for the requirement.
  • Strafing Chomp; Single Action; The shark Swims up to half its swim Speed, makes a jaws Strike, and then Swims up to half its Speed further. The Strike deals half damage.
