Second Chance Enchantment


10 Points
Spellcaster; Extra Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Spellcaster;
[Enchantment]


When your target proves resilient to your magical deceptions, you can try them again on someone else. When you cast an enchantment spell that targets one creature and that spell has no effect (likely because the creature is immune or that creature critically succeeds at its Will save) you can Cast the Spell again before the end of your next turn on a different creature without having to re-roll your casting check- use the same one.
