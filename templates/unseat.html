Unseat


2 Points
Nature; Mounted Combat
Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior; Expert in Nature

1 Action; Requirements You are riding your mount and wielding a jousting weapon.

You attempt to knock an opponent off of their mount. Make a melee Strike against a mounted opponent. If your attack hits, attempt an Athletics check against the opponent's Fortitude DC. If you succeed, the foe is knocked off its mount into a space of its choice adjacent to its mount. If you critically succeed, it lands prone.
