Grappling Hook


Price

Hands

You can throw a grappling hook with a rope tied to it to make a climb easier. To anchor a grappling hook, make an attack roll with the
secret
trait against a DC depending on the target, typically at least DC 16. On a success, your hook has a firm hold, but on a critical failure, the hook seems like it will hold but actually falls when you're partway up.
