Projected Clarity


4 Points
Willpower; Saves and Resistances
Prerequisites Pure Clarity; Legendary in Willpower

You can project your pure clarity to your allies. When you use Pure Clarity, allies within 15 feet of you who failed the same saving throw against the same mental effect can also spend their reactions to re-roll the failed saving throw with a +2 circumstance bonus.
