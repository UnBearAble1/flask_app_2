Wholeness of Body


8 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts
Prerequisites: Master in Warrior
You can restore your health by tapping into your ki. You gain the wholeness of body ki spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Wholeness of Body; Healing, Necromancy, Positive
Special: This Focus Spell takes 2 Focus Points to Cast


1 Action verbal

You heal yourself in one of the following ways, chosen by you when you cast the spell.

  • You regain 5d10 Hit Points (expert tier.)
  • You attempt to cure one poison or disease afflicting you; attempt to counteract the affliction.

Special: You can use a Master-Tier version of this spell, restoring 6d10. If you are a Legendary warrior, you can use a Legendary-Tier version, restoring 7d10, but it costs 3 focus points to use. If you use either of these options, you cannot use either again until you have completed 2d4 full rests.
