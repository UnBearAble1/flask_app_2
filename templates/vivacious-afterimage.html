Vivacious Afterimage


5 Points
Warrior; Tactic Feats
Prerequisites: Master in Warrior,
vivacious speed; Requirements You have panache.
You gain the focus spell, Vivacious Afterimage, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.

Vivacious Afterimage; Illusion, Occult, Visual
One Action; Somatic
You move so quickly that you create a short-lived afterimage, looking exactly like you, in front of you or behind you. You Stride. At any point along your movement, you leave an afterimage of yourself behind. Alternatively, you can stop moving and send your afterimage forward instead, up to the remainder of your Speed. A creature that spends actions to engage with your afterimage can attempt to disbelieve it with a Will save. Once an enemy has disbelieved any of your afterimages, they are temporarily immune to all of your Vivacious Afterimages for 10 minutes. As long as a creature has not disbelieved the afterimage, the afterimage counts as a creature for flanking. The afterimage lasts until the beginning of your next turn or until you use Vivacious Afterimage again, whichever comes first.
