Dangerous Sorcery


10 Points
Spellcaster; Metamagic
1 action; [metamagic]; Prerequisites: Master in Spellcaster

Your legacy grants you great destructive power. If the next action you use is to cast a non cantrip spell, and the spell deals damage and doesn't have a duration, you gain a status bonus to that spell's damage equal to 2 x the spell's tier.
