Bleeding Finisher


8 Points
Warrior; Panache

1 Action; Finisher; Prerequisites: Panache; Master in Warrior

Your blow inflicts profuse bleeding. Make a slashing or piercing Strike with a weapon or unarmed attack that allows you to add your panache precision damage. If you hit, the target also takes 4d6 persistent bleed damage.
