Tyranny Domain: Commanding Lash


3 Points
Intimidation; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Intimidation; Trained in Religion

You wield power to rule and enslave others.You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Commanding Lash; Enchantment, Incapacitation, Mental

1 Action verbal; Requirements Your most recent action dealt damage to a target; Range 100 feet; Targets A creature you dealt damage to on your most recent action.; Saving Throw Will; Duration until the end of your target's next turn

With the threat of more pain, you compel a creature you've recently harmed. You issue a command to the target, with the effects of the spell command.
