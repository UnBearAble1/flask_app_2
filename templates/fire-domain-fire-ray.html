Fire Domain: Fire Ray


4 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Nature; Trained in Religion

You control flame. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Fire Ray: Attack, Cleric, Evocation, Fire

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 60 feet; Targets 1 creature

A blazing band of fire arcs through the air. Make a spell attack roll. The ray deals 8d6 fire damage.

  • Critical Success The ray deals double damage and 4d4 persistent fire damage.
  • Success The ray deals full damage.
