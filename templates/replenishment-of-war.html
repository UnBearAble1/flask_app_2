Replenishment of War


7 Points
Spellcaster; Martial Combat
Free Action; Trigger: You successfully deal damage with your diety's favored weapon. Limit:
once per round; Prerequisites: Expert in Spellcaster (Divine)

Striking out against your enemies draws praise and protection from your deity. You gain temporary hit points equal to 2 x your tier of spellcaster. Add 1d6 if you crit. These temporary Hit Points last until the start of your next turn.
