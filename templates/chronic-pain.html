Chronic Pain


-1 to -10 points

PHYSICAL
For some reason, you are constantly in some type of pain. This could be arthritis, migraines, an old physical injury, or some sort of supernatural affliction. When you wake up, and each time you are in a stressful situation, you must make a pain frequency check (DC selected below) or suffer conditions based on the severity of your pain(also selected below). The consequences continue until you finally pass a pain check, but how regularly you get to make that check is also selected below. Note that the maximum for any combination you make
is -10 pts.

Severity:

  • Mild: [-1 pts] -2 to self control rolls, clumsy 1, stupefied 1.
  • Severe: [-2 pts] -4 to self control rolls, clumsy 1, drained 1, stupefied 1.
  • Agonizing: [-3 pts] -6 to self control rolls, clumsy 2, drained 1, Stupefied 2

  • DC 10: [x 1.5]
  • DC 13: [x 2]

Interval:

  • 4 hours: [x 1.5]
  • 8 hours [x 2]
