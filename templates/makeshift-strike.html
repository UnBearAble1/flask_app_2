Makeshift Strike


1 Point
Warrior; Improvised Weapons

1 Action; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior; Improvised Pummel

You quickly snatch up whatever's nearby and then attack with it as an improvised weapon. You Interact to pick up a nearby object you could use as an improvised weapon. You then either Strike or make an Improvised Pummel with the improvised weapon.
