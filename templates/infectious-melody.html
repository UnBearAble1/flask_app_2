Infectious Melody


EXPERT
Auditory, Enchantment, Mental
Traditions occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Area 15-foot emanation; Targets enemies in the area
Saving Throw Will; Duration varies

You call forth the chords of a tune so catchy that foes hearing it can't help but sing along, making it harder for them to communicate. Each target must attempt a Will save.


  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success For 1 round, the creature can communicate only by singing along to the melody. It can speak only on its turn, and it must take the Perform action when it does. A single Perform check is sufficient for any amount of speaking on the creature's turn. The DC to properly communicate at all is 6. This effect might interfere with verbal components of spells or other linguistic abilities that rely on precise speech. In addition, the creature takes a -2 status penalty on linguistic Intimidation checks, as singing its threats saps away some of the menace. The creature's constant humming of the tune also makes it nearly impossible for the creature to remain undetected.
  • Failure As success, but the duration is 2 rounds, and the DC is 8
  • Critical Failure As success, but the duration is 3 rounds, and the DC is 12
