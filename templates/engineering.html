Engineering


You can identify, build, repair, or disable technological devices; assess the stability of structures and machinery; and properly arm and disarm explosives.

Trained Actions:


  • Configure Explosives: You can use Engineering to configure an explosive device.
  • Craft Tech Item: You can craft technological items using the crafting rules.
  • Disable Tech Device: You can use Engineering to disable a lock, a trap, or a mechanical or technological device, or to disarm an explosive.
  • Identify Technology: You can use Engineering to identify the nature of technological devices.
  • Recall Knowledge: About technology or structures, including assessing their structural integrity or weak spots.
  • Repair: You can repair equipment, technology, or structures.
  • Jury Rig: You can cause technology to behave in a way it wasn't intended to.
