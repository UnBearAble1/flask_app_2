Nature Domain: Nature's Bounty


3 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Legendary in Nature; Trained in Religion

You hold power plants.You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.


Nature's Bounty: Conjuration, Plant, Positive

1 Action somatic; Requirements You have a free hand.

You can cause a plant, no matter the season, to suddenly produce a copious amount of fruit or vegetables that the plant normally produces. This produce is nutrient rich, and assuming it is edible, each one one able to sustain a fully grown person for a single meal. The plant produces enough of these for 15 meals. The produce goes bad after four days.
