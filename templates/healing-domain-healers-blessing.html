Healing Domain; Healer's Blessing


2 Points
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Religion

Your healing magic is particularly potent. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Healer's Blessing; Necromancy

1 Action ; verbal

Range 30 feet; Targets 1 willing living creature; Duration 1 minute

Your words bless a creature with an enhanced connection to positive energy. When the target regains Hit Points from a healing spell, it regains 8 additional Hit Points.



The target regains additional Hit Points from healer's blessing only the first time it regains HP from a given healing spell, so a spell that heals the creature repeatedly over a duration would restore additional Hit Points only once.
