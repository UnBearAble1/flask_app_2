Luck Domain: Lucky Break


6 Points
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Religion

You're unnaturally lucky and keep out of harm's way. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Lucky Break; Divination, Fortune

Reaction; verbal; Trigger You fail (but don't critically fail) a saving throw.

Reroll the saving throw and use the better result. You then become temporarily immune for 10 minutes.
