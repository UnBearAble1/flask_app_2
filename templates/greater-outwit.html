Greater Outwit


7 Points
Warrior; Hunt Prey
Prerequisites: outwit; Legendary in Warrior

Your mastery of skills allows you to overwhelm your prey. If you have master proficiency in Deception, Intimidation, Stealth, or the skill you use to Recall Knowledge about your prey, increase the circumstance bonus against the prey with that skill from +2 to +4. Increase the circumstance bonus to AC against the prey from +1 to +2.
