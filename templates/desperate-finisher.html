Desperate Finisher


3 Points
Warrior; Reaction Attack

Reaction; Requirements You meet the requirements to use an action with the press trait; Trigger You complete the last action on your turn, and your turn has not ended yet;
Prerequisites: You have a feat with the Press Trait, Master in Warrior

You throw everything into one last press. Use a single action that you know with the press trait as part of Desperate Finisher. You forgo the ability to use reactions until the start of your next turn.
