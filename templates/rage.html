Rage


6 Points
Warrior; Rage

1 action; Tactic, Concentrate, Emotion, Mental; Requirements You aren't fatigued or raging; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior

You tap into your inner fury and begin raging. You gain temporary Hit Points equal to your 5 plus your Constitution modifier. This frenzy lasts for 1 minute, until there are no enemies you can perceive, or until you fall unconscious, whichever comes first. You can't voluntarily stop raging. While you are raging:

  • You deal 2 additional damage with melee Strikes. This additional damage is halved if your weapon or unarmed attack is agile.
  • You take a -1 penalty to AC.
  • You can't use actions with the concentrate trait unless they also have the rage trait. You can Seek while raging.
  • After you stop raging, you lose any remaining temporary Hit Points from Rage, and you can't Rage again for 1 minute.

Optional Rule: You can add the "Rage" trait to any Warrior feat (that makes sense, work with the GM.) Doing so means you can only use the feat while raging, but reduces it's cost by 20% (round up)
