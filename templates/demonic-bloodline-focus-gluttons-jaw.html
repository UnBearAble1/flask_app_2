Demonic Bloodline Focus: Glutton's Jaw


6 Points
Religion; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Expert in Religion

You gain the focus spell, Glutton's Jaw, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Glutton's Jaw; Morph, Necromancy

Bloodline demonic

1 Action; somatic, verbal; Duration 1 minute

Your mouth transforms into a shadowy maw bristling with pointed teeth. These jaws are an unarmed attack with the forceful trait dealing 2d8 piercing damage. If you hit with your jaws and deal damage, you gain 2d4 temporary Hit Points.



Additionally, immediately upon casting this spell, until the end of your next turn, The corruption of sin weakens a target's defenses or makes you more imposing. You gain a +1 status bonus to Intimidation checks.
