Acid Splash


Acid, Attack, Cantrip, Evocation
Traditions: arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature

You splash a glob of acid that splatters your target and nearby creatures. Make a spell attack. If you hit, you deal 3d6 + your spellcasting modifier acid damage plus 3 splash acid damage. On a critical success, the target also takes 4 persistent acid damage.
