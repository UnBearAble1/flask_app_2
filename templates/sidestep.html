Sidestep


8 Points
Warrior; Crowd Fighting

Reaction; Trigger The attack roll for a Strike targeting you fails or critically fails;
Prerequisites: Master in Warrior

You deftly step out of the way of an attack, letting the blow continue to the creature next to you. You redirect the attack to a creature of your choice that is adjacent to you and within the reach of the triggering attack. The attacker re-rolls the Strike's attack roll against the new target.
