Theoretical Acumen


3 Points
Cultural Training; Knowledge
Reaction; Trigger: You succeed at a skill check to Recall Knowledge to identify a creature.

Once before the end of your next turn, you can use the total roll result from the triggering check in place of:

  • Your total rolled saving throw against one of the creature's abilities
  • Your total rolled Perception to Seek the creature
  • Your total rolled Deception to Feint against the creature
  • Your total rolled Intimidation to Demoralize the creature.
  • your AC against one of it's attacks

Once you've used this feat on a creature, you can no longer use it on that type of creature until your next daily preparations.
