Alchemical Healer


6 Points
Alchemist; Healing
Prerequisite: Trained in Alchemist

You concentrate on healing others with alchemy. You start with the formulas each tier of the following items that you can make,
in addition to your other formulas: antidote, antiplague, and elixir of life.

Boost (+3 points) Perpetual Surgeon (Expert in Alchemist):
When using
quick alchemy
to make trained-tier antidotes and antiplagues, you don't automatically critical fail on a natural one.
Additionally, As long as your proficiency rank in Medicine is trained or better, you can attempt a Crafting check instead of a Medicine check for any of Medicine's untrained and trained uses.

Boost (+4 points) Surgical Prodigy (Master in Alchemist): When using
advanced alchemy
to make elixers of life, antiplagues or antitoxins during your daily preparations, you craft an extra one for each one you make.
