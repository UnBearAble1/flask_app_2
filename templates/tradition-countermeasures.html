Tradition Countermeasures


2 Points
Physiology; Resistance
Pick a magical tradition (Arcane, Divine, Primal or Occult.) You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to all saving throws against spells cast using that tradition. This feat can only be selected once.

Boost (+1 Point) Tradition Bane: You can use a reaction to increase your circumstance bonus from this feat from +1 to +3.

Boost (+3 Points) Kneel before no man or god: When you use your Tradition Bane boost reaction (above) against a spell and roll a critical failure on the saving throw against that spell, you get a failure instead
