Attack of Opportunity


6 Points
Warrior; Reaction Attack
Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior

Reaction; Trigger A creature within your reach uses a manipulate action or a move action, makes a ranged attack, or leaves a square during a move action it's using.

You lash out at a foe that leaves an opening. Make a melee Strike against the triggering creature. If your attack is a critical hit and the trigger was a manipulate action, you disrupt that action. This Strike doesn't count toward your multiple attack penalty, and your multiple attack penalty doesn't apply to this Strike.

Boost (+10 Points) Combat Reflexes [Requires: Legendary in Warrior] You are particularly swift at punishing foes who leave you openings. At the start of each of your turns when you regain your actions, you gain an additional reaction that can be used only to make an Attack of Opportunity.

Boost (+3 Points) Disorienting Opening [Requires: Master in Warrior]: You use your foes' openings to expose even greater weaknesses. When you hit a creature as part of an Attack of Opportunity, that creature becomes flat-footed until the start of your next turn.

Boost (+8 Points) Disruptive Stance [Requires Master in Warrior]: You gain the Disruptive Stance:

  • 1 Action; Stance: As long as you are in this stance, you can use Attack of Opportunity when a creature within your reach uses a concentrate action, in addition to manipulate and move actions. Furthermore, you disrupt a triggering concentrate or manipulate action if your Strike hits (not only if it's a critical hit).

Boost (+4 Points) Impassible Wall Stance [Requires Master in Warrior]: You gain the Impassible Wall Stance:

  • 1 Action; Stance; You refuse to let foes past your guard. As long as you are in this stance, when you critically hit with an Attack of Opportunity triggered by a move action, you disrupt that move action.
