Sorrow Domain: Lament


8 Points
occultism; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in occultism; Trained in Religion

You have a painful connection to melancholy and sadness.. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Lament; Emotion, Enchantment, Mental

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 30-foot cone; Targets 1 or more creatures; Saving Throw basic Will

You let out your negative emotions in a guttural wail that shakes your enemies' hearts and deals 5d8 mental damage (basic Will save). If you have any harmful conditions imposed by emotion effects, lament deals additional 2 mental damage equal to the proficiency tier of the highest-level effect.
