Knowledge Domain: Scholarly Recollection


6 Points
Knowledge Skills; Recall Knowledge
Prerequisites: Legendary in Any Knowledge Skill, Trained in Religion

You receive Divine Insights. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Scholarly Recollection; Divination, Fortune

Reaction; verbal; Trigger You attempt a Perception check to Seek, or you attempt a skill check to Recall Knowledge with a skill you're trained in.

Speaking a short prayer as you gather your thoughts, you're blessed to find that your deity gave you just the right bit of information for your situation. Roll the triggering check twice and use the better result.
