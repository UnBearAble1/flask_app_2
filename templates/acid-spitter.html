Acid Spitter


EXPERT
Uncommon, Acid, Clockwork, Consumable, Mechanical, Snare, Trap
Price 15 gp

This tin clockwork lizard is activated when a creature moves adjacent to it, at which point it spits out a glob of acid. The target must succeed at a DC 18 Reflex saving throw or take 3d6 acid damage.


  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature takes full damage.
  • Failure The creature takes full damage and 5 persistent acid damage.
  • Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and 10 persistent acid damage.
