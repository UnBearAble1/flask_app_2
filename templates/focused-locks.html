Focused Locks


4 points
Occultism; Biology
Prerequisites: Expert in Occultism, Living Hair

Your hair unarmed attack gains the reach trait. You can deliver focus spells through your hair. When you successfully cast a focus spell that requires 2 or more actions to cast and that doesn't require a spell attack roll, if your target is within your reach, as part of the spellcasting activity you can make a hair Strike against the foe before applying any effects of the spell. If this Strike misses, the spell has no effect.
