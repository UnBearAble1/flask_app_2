Survival


These effects help you survive the environment and the basics of being a biological life form.

Hold Breath Longer


Linked Skill: Athletics

You can hold your breath longer.

  • You can hold your breath 10x longer
    (Mod: 4)
  • You can hold your breath 25x longer
    (Mod: 7)
  • You don't need to breathe (Base: 8)

Living Compass


[Mod: 1]
Linked Skill: Survival

You always know which way is north.

Long Lived



Your age much more slowly

  • You can live to be up to 350 years old (Base: 2)
  • You cease aging (Mod: 7)

Reduced Temperature Penalties


Linked Skill: Survival

You take lessened penalties for being in extreme weather.

  • You treat environmental cold effects as if they were one step less extreme (incredible cold becomes extreme, extreme cold becomes severe, and so on). (Mod: 4)
  • You are immune to the effects of environmental cold (but not cold damage): (Base: 8)
  • You treat environmental heat effects as if they were one step less extreme (incredible heat becomes extreme, extreme heat becomes severe, and so on). (Mod: 4)
  • You are immune to the effects of environmental heat (but not heat damage): (Base: 8)

Food and Water Effects


Reduced Starvation / Thirst Effects
Linked Skill: Survival

  • You can go 10x longer before starvation/thirst (Mod: 7)
  • You take damage only every 2 hours from thirst and every 2 days from starvation, rather than every hour and every day. (Mod: 2)




Alternate Food Source
Linked Skill: Survival
You can survive on something other than or in addition to traditional food.

  • Easy to Obtain: Dirt and Ash, (Base 8, Mod +2)
  • Typically Easy to Obtain: Spoiled Food, Sunshine (Base 8, Mod -2)
  • As Hard to Obtain as Food and Water: Tree Bark, Paper (Base: 3)
  • Harder to Obtain
    (Note, you should also pick a Complication to reflect the difficulty)


Don't need to eat or drink
[Base: 12]
Activation: Duration, Linked Skill: Survival
Default Duration: 8 Hours
You don't need to eat or drink at all


Produce Fruit and Vegetables
[Tier D, 10 points]
Activation: Instantaneous
Linked Skill: Survival

You can cause a plant that normally produces edible fruits or vegetables to suddenly produce them. This produces enough food for one meal (Base: 2)

  • Per additional meal: (Mod +2, additional Per six meals: Base +1)


Plant Growth
Activation: Instantaneous

Default Area: 60 foot burst.
You cause the plants within the area to be healthier and more fruitful. In addition to other benefits of healthy plants, this increases the crop yield for farms over the course of their normal time.

  • 50% bonus yield (Base: 4)
  • Double yield (Base: 8)
  • Triple yield (Base: 12)


Better Foraging
Linked Skill: Survival
You can forage for food even if the area lacks food that could normally sustain you.

  • In other planes (Mod: 2)
  • In hostile environments on the prime material plane (Mod: 4)


Purify Food and Drink
[Base: 4]
Default Area: 1 cubic foot (about 8 gallons)

You remove toxins and contaminants from food and drink, making them safe to consume.

Sleep and Rest Effects



Reduced Rest Time
[Mod: 3]
You can rest in 6 hours, instead of 8


Conditional Waking
Activation Duration
Default duration 8 hours
You can set a condition from which to wake up from sleep.

  • A specific time (Mod: 1)
  • Triggered by an event (requires the Trap boosts) - when combined with trap, you can be the target of the effect rather than it requiring it be the person who triggered the trap. (Base: 3)



Don't Need Sleep
You don't sleep.

  • Instead of sleeping, you enter another state that is similar to sleeping, except you are aware of your surroundings and don't take penalties for being unconscious. Much like with sleeping, if you go too long without entering your state, you become fatigued and can't recover until you enter the state.
    (Base: 4, Mod +4)
  • You don't need to sleep at all, but must still rest for 8 hours. During this rest you can perform light non adventuring activity (Base: 4, Mod +12)
