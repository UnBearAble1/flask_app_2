Blood Vendetta


TRAINED

Curse, Necromancy
Traditions arcane, divine, occult
Reaction; verbal; Trigger A creature deals piercing, slashing, or persistent bleed damage to you Requirements You can bleed; Range 30 feet; Targets the triggering creature
Saving Throw Will; Duration varies

You curse the target, punishing it for having the audacity to spill your blood. The target takes 2d6 persistent bleed damage and must attempt a Will save.


  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target takes half the persistent bleed damage.
  • Failure The target takes the full persistent bleed damage. Until the bleeding stops, the target has weakness 2 to piercing and slashing damage.
  • Critical Failure As failure, but the target takes double the persistent bleed damage.

Heightened:
The persistent bleed damage increases by 2d6.
