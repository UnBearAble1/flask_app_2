Student of the Dueling Arts


5 Points
Warrior; Single One Handed
Prerequisites: Legendary in Warrior

You have studied a great many combat techniques, which you can review each day. During your daily preparations, you can swap out any number of your dueling feats for other dueling feats of the same or lesser value for which you are qualified. You can't swap out Student of the Dueling Arts in this way.
