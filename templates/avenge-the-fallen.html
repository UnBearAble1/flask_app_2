Avenge the Fallen


2 Points
Behavioral Trait, Combat Teamwork
Reaction; Usable once per hour
Trigger: An ally within 30 feet gains the dying condition.

You immediately either make a ranged attack (if you have a weapon ready) or stride and make a melee strike (if you can reach them) against the creature who felled your ally. Either version of the strike is made with advantage, and deals +2d6 precision damage.

Additionally, whether you were able to make the attack or not, you gain 10 temporary hit points for 1 minute. As long as you have these temporary Hit Points, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to attack and damage rolls.
