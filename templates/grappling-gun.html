Grappling Gun


Hands
2;
Usage
held in 2 hands;
Price
3 gp
Bulk
1

This wooden, pistol-like device features a large reel coiled with 100 feet of thin metal cord and can fire a
grappling hook
up to 100 feet. To reload the grappling gun, you must manually recoil the cord by turning the reel's crank, and then lock in the grappling hook. Reloading a grappling gun takes 1 minute.
