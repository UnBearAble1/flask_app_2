Flame Wisp


TRAINED

Evocation, Fire
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions somatic, verbal
Duration 1 minute

Three faintly glowing wisps of fire float around your head. Each time you hit a creature with a Strike, one of the wisps goes hurtling towards that creature, dealing 1d6 fire damage. If you Cast a Spell with the fire trait while you have fewer than three wisps, a new wisp appears.

Heightened: The damage increases by 1d6.
