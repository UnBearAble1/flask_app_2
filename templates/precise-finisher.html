Precise Finisher


7 Points
Warrior; Panache
Prerequisites: Confident Finisher; Master in Warrior

Even when your foe avoids your Confident Finisher, you can still hit a vital spot. On a failure with Confident Finisher, you deal your full 4d6 precise strike damage instead of halving it (you still halve the rest of your damage.)
