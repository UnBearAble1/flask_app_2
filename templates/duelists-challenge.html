Duelist's Challenge


5 points

Warrior; Single One Handed
1 Action; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior, you are wielding a single one handed weapon

Select one foe that you can see and proclaim a challenge. That foe is your dueling opponent until they are defeated, flee, or the encounter ends. Any time you hit that enemy using a single one-handed melee weapon while your other hand or hands are free, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to the Strike's damage. If you attack a creature other than your dueling opponent, you take a -2 circumstance penalty to damage.
