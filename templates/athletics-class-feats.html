Athletics Class Feats


CLIMBER


  • Combat Climber [T] -1 pt: You aren't flat-footed and have a free hand while climbing
  • Impossible Climber [L] -10 pts: Gain a climb speed equal to your speed
  • Lead Climber [E] -1 pt: When others Follow you as the Expert, they gain bonuses
  • Quick Climber [M] -1 pt: Gain extra speed when succeeding or critting at climbing
  • Rapid Mantel [E] -1 pt: Use athletics to Grab an Edge and climb up simultaneously

COMBAT MANEUVERS


  • Rope Master [E] -1 pt: Climb faster on rope, use combat maneuvers with rope at reach.
  • Titan Wrestler [T] -2 pts: Size is less of a problem for your combat maneuvers

DOMAIN SPELLS


  • Might Domain: Athletic Rush [M] -2 pts: Focus spell gives you bonus on a movement
  • Might Domain: Enduring Might [L] -5 pts: Focus spell gives reaction damage resistance

LEAPER


  • Cloud Jump [L] -14 pts: Triple your long jump, greatly improve high jump
  • Powerful Leap [E] -1 pt: You leap further
  • Quick Jump [T] -2 pts: High Jump and Long Jump as single action
  • Wall Jump [M] -5 pts: You can jump off of vertical surfaces, maybe multiple times

MIGHTY


  • Forced Entry [E] -2 pts: Force open without a crowbar, improved successes
  • Adrenaline Rush [T] -1 pt: Increased carry and athletics bonuses in combat
  • Bashing Charge [T] -2 pts: 2 actions to stride twice and force open in move
  • Hefty Hauler [T] -1 pt: Bulk limits are +2

STRIDE


  • Incredible Movement [E/L] -9 pts: Get +10 or more foot to speed when unarmored
  • Wall Run [L] -6 pts: You can stride along vertical surfaces
  • Water Sprint [M] -4 pts: You can stride over liquid surfaces

SWIMMER


  • Breath Control [T] -4 pts: Hold your breath 25x longer, bonus to inhalation saves
  • Impossible Swimmer [L] -8 pts: Gain a swim speed
  • Quick Swim [M] -1 pt: Swim further on success/crit success
  • Underwater Marauder [T] -1 pt: Fewer penalties while underwater

TEAMWORK


  • Pick Up the Pace [T] -1 pt: Your group can hustle longer
  • Caravan Leader [T] -1 pt: Can hustle even longer
  • Friendly Toss [M] -3 pts: You can hurl an ally up to 30 feet with no roll / reactions
