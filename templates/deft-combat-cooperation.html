Deft Combat Cooperation


2 Points
Warrior; Teamwork
Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior

When you set things up to help your allies, you also gain some of the fruits of your labors. When you successfully Aid an attack roll or AC, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to attack rolls or AC against that enemy until the end of your next turn, whichever you granted to your ally.
