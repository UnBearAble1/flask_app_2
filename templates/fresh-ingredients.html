Fresh Ingredients


4 Points
Nature; Treat Wounds
Prerequisites; Natural Medicine
; expert in Nature

During your daily preparations, you can assemble a small batch of herbs for each point of wisdom modifier you have. When using Natural Medicine to Treat Wounds, you can spend a batch of herbs to gain the +2 circumstance bonus from having fresh ingredients, even if not in wilderness. If you spend a batch of herbs in wilderness, you gain a +4 circumstance bonus instead.
