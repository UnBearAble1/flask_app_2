Earth Domain: Hurtling Stone


3 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Nature; Trained in Religion

You control Soil and Stone. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.


Hurtling Stone; Attack, Earth, Evocation

1 Action somatic; Range 60 feet; Targets 1 creature

You evoke a magical stone and throw it, with your god's presence guiding your aim. Make a spell attack roll against the target. The stone deals bludgeoning damage equal to 6d6 plus your Strength modifier.

  • Critical Success The stone deals double damage.
  • Success The stone deals full damage
