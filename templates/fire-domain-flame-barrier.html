Fire Domain: Flame Barrier


4 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Nature; Trained in Religion

You control flame. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Flame Barrier; Abjuration

Reaction ; verbal; Trigger An effect would deal fire damage to either you or an ally within range.; Range 60 feet; Targets the creature that would take fire damage

You swiftly deflect incoming flames. The target gains fire resistance 15 against the triggering effect.
