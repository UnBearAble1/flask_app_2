Agitate


TRAINED

Enchantment, Mental, Nonlethal
Traditions arcane, occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Saving Throw Will; Duration varies

You send the target's mind and body into overdrive, forcing it to become restless and hyperactive. During the duration, the target must Stride, Fly, or Swim at least once each turn or take 4d10 mental damage that turn; The GM might decide to add additional move actions to the list for creatures who possess only a more unusual form of movement. The duration of this effect depends on the target's Will save.


  • Critical Success The spell has no effect.
  • Success The duration is 1 round.
  • Failure The duration is 2 rounds.
  • Critical Failure The duration is 4 rounds.

Heightened:
The damage increases by 2d10.
