Liberating Step


10 Points
Religion/Warrior; Champion's Reaction
Reaction; Trigger An enemy damages, Grabs, or Grapples your ally, and both are within 15 feet of you. [Tactic]; Prerequisites: Master in Religion, Expert in Warrior

You free an ally from restraint. If the trigger was an ally taking damage, the ally gains 12 resistance to all damage against the triggering damage.The ally can attempt to break free of effects grabbing, restraining, immobilizing, or paralyzing them. They either attempt a new save against one such effect that allows a save, or attempt to Escape from one effect as a free action. If they can move, the ally can Step as a free action, even if they didn't need to escape.
