Incredible Companion


10 Points
Nature; Animal Companion
Prerequisites Mature Animal Companion; Master in Nature or Spellcasting (Primal Tradition)

Your animal companion continues to grow and develop. It becomes a savage or nimble gaining additional capabilities determined by the type of companion:

Incredible Animal Companion: Savage (upgrade to the Brute type)


  • +2 to Attack, Damage, and Fortitude Saving Throws
  • +1 to AC, Perception, Reflex and Willpower Saving Throws
  • +10 Hit Points
  • Advance athletics to master (or legendary, if it was already master)
  • Learns an Advanced Maneuver
  • If your companion is Medium or smaller, it grows by one size
  • Its attacks become magical for the purpose of ignoring resistances.

Incredible Animal Companion: Nimble (upgrade to the Skirmisher type)


  • +2 to Attack, AC, and Reflex Saving Throws
  • +1 to Damage, Perception, Fortitude and Willpower Saving Throws
  • +5 Hit Points
  • Advance Acrobatics to expert (or master, if it was already expert)
  • Learns an Advanced Maneuver
  • If your companion is Medium or smaller, it grows by one size
  • Its attacks become magical for the purpose of ignoring resistances.
