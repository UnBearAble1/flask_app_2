Living Waterfall


Living waterfalls are humanoid-shaped columns of churning water.

Recall Knowledge - Elemental (Arcana, Nature)

N, Large, Aquatic, Elemental, Water, Brute
Perception +7; darkvision
Languages Aquan
Skills Athletics +9, Stealth +8
Str +4, Dex +3, Con +3, Int -2, Wis +1, Cha +0
AC 18; Fort +10 , Ref +6 , Will +8
HP 85; Immunities bleed, paralyzed, poison, sleep; Resistances fire 5
Speed 20 feet, swim 60 feet

Features:

  • Water-Bound When not touching water, the living waterfall is slowed 1 and can't use reactions.
  • Vortex (aura, water) 30 feet. Water in the area that is in the same body of water as the living waterfall is difficult terrain for Swimming creatures that don't have the water trait.

Actions:

  • Wave: Melee Single Action;
    +8 (+3 /-2)(reach 10 feet), Damage 2d12+5 bludgeoning plus Push or Pull 5 feet
  • Drench; Single Action (abjuration, primal, water) The elemental puts out all fires in a 5-foot emanation. It extinguishes all non-magical fires automatically and attempts to counteract magical fires (+14 counteract modifier).
