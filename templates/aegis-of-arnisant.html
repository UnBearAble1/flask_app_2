Aegis of Arnisant


3 Points
Religion/Warrior; Shields
Prerequisites: Shield Block; master in Religion, Master in Warrior;

2 Actions; Requirements You are wielding a shield.

Inspired by the tale of Arnisant, you transform your shield into a magic ward. Until the beginning of your next turn, you gain the following reaction:



Absorb into the Aegis:

Reaction; Trigger An enemy casts a spell that targets you or an ally within 15 feet of you

You interpose your shield's ward against the spell. Attempt a counteract check, using your Religion modifier as your counteract modifier. On a success, the spell is counteracted and your shield takes damage equal to 8 times the spell tier as it absorbs and disperses the magical energy. On a failure, the shield takes damage equal to four times the spell's tier.
