Ardent Nature


4 Points
Willpower; Saves and Resistances
Prerequisites: Expert in Willpower

You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saves against mental effects that specifically improve your attitude and to your Will DC against Demoralize. If a mental effect would compel you to act in a way that violates your tenets (as determined by the GM), you can attempt to break free from the effect as a reaction triggered by receiving the violating order, and you gain a new Will save against the effect. You can attempt this new save only once for a given effect, even if you are compelled to violate your order's tenets multiple times. If an effect confuses you into believing something false to be true, such as attacking an ally thanks to a confusion condition, it may not be considered a violation because of the false interpretation of your mind.
