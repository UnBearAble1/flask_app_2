Surge


4 Points
Behavioral Trait, Fighting Style
Reaction; Usable once per minute; Trigger: You attempt an attack roll
You gain a +1 to the attack roll.

Boost (+2 Points) Protective Surge: You can choose to activate Surge when you attempt a saving throw, instead of when you make an attack roll. If you do, you gain a +2 status bonus to the triggering saving throw.

Boost (+2 Points) Skilled Surge: You can choose to activate Surge when you attempt a skill check, instead of when you attempt an attack roll. If you do, you gain a +2 status bonus to the triggering skill check. If you get a success or critical success, you cannot use Surge again until your next daily preparations.
