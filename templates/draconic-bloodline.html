Draconic Bloodline


10 Points
Spellcaster; Magical Bloodine
Prerequisites: Trained in Spellcaster (Arcane)

The blood of dragons flows through your veins. These beasts are both fearsome in combat and skilled at magic.. Add the following spells to your repertoire, even if they aren't normally part of the arcane tradition (note you must still have the appropriate level of spellcaster proficiency to cast the spell):

  • cantrip: shield,
  • Trained: true strike and resist energy,
  • Expert: haste and spell immunity,
  • Master: chromatic wall and dragon form,
  • Legendary: mask of terror and prismatic wall,

Whenever you cast a spell from the list of spells above, gain the following Blood Magic ability: Blood Magic Draconic scales grow briefly on you or one target, granting a +1 status bonus to AC for 1 round.
