Leg Disability


-6 points

PHYSICAL
You either have no legs, or your legs are disabled. Your land speed is reduced to 0 (though you can still crawl). If you have no legs, you cannot be targeted in the legs nor randomly struck there.
If you do, then you ignore the penalties for being struck there (though still take the damage.)

Characters with this disability can get around using wheelchairs, which take 2 hands to fully operate, but grant a land speed equal to the character's original land speed. Wheelchairs may treat additional types of terrain as difficult terrain, or be completely inoperable in some situations.
Relevant: Consider including a variant of the awesome combat wheelchair in your game!
