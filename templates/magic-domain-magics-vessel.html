Magic Domain: Magic's Vessel


4 Points
arcana; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Arcana; Trained in Religion

You perform the unexpected and inexplicable. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Magic's Vessel; Enchantment

1 Action; somatic; Range touch; Targets 1 creature; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

A creature becomes a receptacle for pure magical energy.. The target gains a +1 status bonus to saving throws. Each time you Cast a Spell, you automatically Sustain this Spell and grant its target resistance to damage from spells until the start of your next turn. This resistance is equal to the tier of the spell you cast x 3.
