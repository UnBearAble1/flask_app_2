Shield Paragon


3 Point
Religion/Warrior; Shields
Prerequisites: Shield Ally, Shield Block, Legendary in Religion

Free Action; Usable Once Per Day; Requires: Shield Ally, Shield Block; Trigger: You use Shield Block

Your shield is a vessel of divine protection. Against this attack, Your chosen shield doubles its HP and BT, rather than increasing them by half. If it would be destroyed, it vanishes to your deity's realm instead, where your divine ally repairs it. During your next daily preparations, the shield returns to you fully repaired.
