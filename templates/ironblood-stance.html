Ironblood Stance


10 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts

1 Action; Requirements You are unarmored; Prerequisites: Powerful Fist

You enter the stance of impenetrable iron, refusing to yield to any blow. You can make iron sweep unarmed attacks. These deal 2d8 bludgeoning damage; are in the brawling group; and have the nonlethal, parry, sweep, and unarmed traits. While in Ironblood Stance, you gain resistance 3 to all damage.
