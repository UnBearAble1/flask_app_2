Blackfinger's Blades


MASTER

Poison, Transmutation
Traditions divine, primal
Two Actions ; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets up to 4 weapons, each wielded by a separate willing creature within range
Duration up to 1 minute

You pray for swift and painful death to your enemies and find your and your allies' weapons blessed to deliver just that. The target weapons are coated with giant scorpion venom with a DC equal to your spell DC. The effects remain on a weapon for 1 minute or until the weapon deals damage, whichever comes first.

Giant Scorpion Venom (poison)
Fortitude Save; Maximum Duration 6 rounds;
Stage 1: 1d10 poison damage and enfeebled 1 (1 round);
Stage 2:
2d10 poison damage and enfeebled 1 (1 round);
Stage 3:
2d10 poison damage and enfeebled 2 (1 round)
