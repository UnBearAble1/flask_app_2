Reflect Blow


Abjuration, Cantrip, Force
Traditions
arcane, divine, occult
Reaction;
verbal; Trigger: You take damage from a melee attack

You redirect some of the kinetic energy of a melee attack back onto the attacker. Make a Spellcaster check against a DC equal to the total attack roll that hit you.

  • Critical Success: You reduce the damage you take by 2d10 points, and the attacker takes the same damage (of the same type as the attack.)
  • Success: You reduce the damage you take by 1d10 points, and the attacker takes the same damage (of the same type as the attack.)
  • Failure: You fail to redirect any damage.
  • Critical Failure: You accidentally direct more force into the blow. You take an extra 1d10 damage from the attack.
