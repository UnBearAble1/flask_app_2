Angelic Bloodline Focus: Celestial Brand


4 Points
Religion; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Angelic Bloodline Focus: Angelic Wings

You gain the focus spell, Celestial Brand, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Celestial Brand; Curse, Necromancy

1 Action; somatic; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 evil creature; Duration 1 round

A blazing symbol appears on the target, cursing it to face divine justice. You and your allies receive a +1 status bonus to your attack rolls and skill checks against it. Anytime a good creature damages it, the good creature deals an additional 1d4 good damage. After the duration is up, the target is temporarily immune for 1 minute.



Additionally, immediately upon casting this spell, until the end of your next turn, an angelic aura grants you a +1 status bonus to saving throws for 1 round.
