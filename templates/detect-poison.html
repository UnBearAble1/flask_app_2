Detect Poison


TRAINED

Detection, Divination
Traditions divine, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 object or creature

You detect whether a creature is venomous or poisonous, or if an object is poison or has been poisoned. You also learn the number and types of poison.You do not ascertain whether the target is poisonous in multiple ways. Certain substances, like lead and alcohol, are poisons and so mask other poisons.
