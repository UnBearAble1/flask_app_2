Ranger's Bramble


4 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Nature
You can cause plants to grow and trap your foes with thorns. You gain the ranger's bramble warden spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Ranger's Bramble; Plant, Transmutation

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 100 feet; Area all squares that contain plants in a 5-foot burst; Duration 1 minute

You cause plants in the area to entangle your foes, with the effects of the entangle spell. A creature that critically fails the save takes 4d4 persistent bleed damage in addition to being immobilized. Escaping your bramble doesn't end the bleed damage.
