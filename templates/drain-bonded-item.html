Drain Bonded Item


6 Points
Spellcaster; Extra Spells
Requires: Trained in Spellcaster

You place some of your magical power in a bonded item. Each day when you prepare your spells, you can designate a single item you own as your bonded item. This is typically an item associated with spellcasting, such as a wand, ring, or staff, but you are free to designate a weapon or other item. You gain the Drain Bonded Item free action



Free Action; Once Per Day

You expend the power stored in your bonded item. During your turn, you gain the ability to cast one spell from your repertoire and already successfully cast during the last four hours, gaining advantage on the spellcasting roll to cast it again.
Boost (+3 Points) Bond Conservation: You gain the Bond Conservation metamagic action:

  • 1 Action; Metamagic, Manipulate; Requirements The last action you used was Drain Bonded Item.

    By carefully manipulating the arcane energies stored in your bonded item as you drain it, you can conserve just enough power to cast another, slightly weaker spell. If the next action you use is to Cast a Spell using the energy from Drain Bonded Item, you gain an extra use of Drain Bonded Item. You must use this extra use of Drain Bonded Item before the end of your next turn or you lose it, and you can use this additional use only to cast a spell of a lower tier than the first spell cast with Drain Bonded Item.

Boost (+1 Point) Call Bonded Item: You gain the Call Bonded Item action:

  • 1 Action; Concentrate, Conjuration, Teleportation; Prerequisites
    Drain Bonded Item

    You're mystically connected to your bonded item and can call it to your hand. If your bonded item is 1 Bulk or less, you teleport your bonded item into your hand from up to 1 mile away.

Boost:
(+3 Points) Linked Focus:
Frequency once per day: (Requires:
School Specialist)

You have linked your bonded item to the well of energy that powers your school spells. When you Drain your Bonded Item to cast a spell of your Specialist school, you also regain 1 Focus Point.

Boost:
(+2 Points) Superior Bond: (Requires: Master in Spellcaster): When you draw upon your bonded item, you can leave a bit of energy within it for later use. You can use Drain Bonded Item one additional time per day, but only to cast a spell 2 or more tiers below your highest possible tier.
