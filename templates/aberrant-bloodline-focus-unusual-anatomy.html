Aberrant Bloodline Focus: Unusual Anatomy


3 Points
Occultism; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Aberrant Bloodline Focus: Aberrant Whispers

You gain the focus spell, Unusual Anatomy, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Unusual Anatomy; Polymorph, Transmutation

1 Action; somatic; Duration 1 minute

You transform your flesh into an abhorrent monstrosity. This has three effects:

  • You gain resistance 10 to precision damage and resistance 10 to extra damage from critical hits. If the resistance is greater than the extra damage, it reduces the extra damage to 0 but doesn't reduce the attack's normal damage.
  • You gain darkvision.
  • Acid oozes from your skin. Any creature that hits you with an unarmed attack or with a non-reach melee weapon takes 1d6 acid damage.



    Additionally, immediately upon casting this spell, until the end of your next turn, Aberrant whispers shield your mind, granting a +2 status bonus to Will saving throws.
