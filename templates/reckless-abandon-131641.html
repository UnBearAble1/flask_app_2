Reckless Abandon


7 Points
Warrior; Rage

Free Action: Trigger: Your turn begins, and you are at half or fewer Hit Points; Prerequisites: Rage; Master in Warrior

Your blood boils when you take a beating, and you throw caution to the wind to finish the fight. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to attack rolls, a -2 penalty to AC, and a -1 penalty to saves. These bonuses and penalties last until your Rage ends or until you are above half Hit Points (including temporary hit points), whichever comes first.
