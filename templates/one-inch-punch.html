One Inch Punch


5 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts
2 or 3 Actions; Prerequisites: Master in Warrior; Powerful Fist

You put all your force into a single mighty, carefully controlled blow. Make an unarmed Strike. If you spend two actions and this Strike hits, you deal 2 extra die of weapon damage. You can instead spend 3 actions to perform an even more powerful attack, dealing 4 additional die of weapon damage on a hit.
