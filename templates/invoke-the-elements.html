Invoke the Elements


3 Points
Natural Abilities; Elemental
1 Action; Usable once per day; Evocation, Primal

You summon an aura of shimmering heat, flurrying snow, crashing waves, or swirling wind and lightning, shadow, or other elemental effect (choose at creation). For 1 minute, you gain concealment against creatures more than 5 feet away.

When you first activate this ability, you can choose to add this effect: any creature that ends its turn within 5 feet of you suffers 4d4 damage of a matching elemental type (basic Reflex save).

Boost: (1 point) When you use Invoke the Elements, you can spend one additional action to disperse it as a 10-foot-radius cloud centered on you instead of as haze; the activity gains the concentrate trait. All creatures within the cloud become
concealed, and all creatures outside the cloud become concealed to creatures within it. The additional damaging effect applies to any creature within cloud.

Boost: (2 points) You can use Invoke the Elements one additional time per day
