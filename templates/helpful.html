Helpful


8 Points
Behavioral Trait, Cooperation

Your people are extraordinarily helpful. This gives you a number of benefits:

  • On a critical success to Aid, you grant your ally a +3 circumstance bonus if you have expert proficiency in the skill, a +4 if you have master proficiency.
  • If you roll a critical failure on a check to Aid, you don't give your ally a -1 penalty to their check.
  • When you roll a check to aid, you get +4 to the roll, and others get +2 to Aid you.
  • If you are an expert in the skill you are Aiding, you treat critical failures and failures as successes.
  • You can use the Aid reaction to grant a bonus to an ally's saving throw (pick Fortitude, Reflex or Will when you pick this feat.) As usual for Aid, you need to prepare by using an action on your turn to encourage the ally.
