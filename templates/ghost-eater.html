Ghost Eater


7 Points
Warrior/Religion; Undead Hunter
Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior, trained in Occultism or Religion

You have trained as a ghost eater, a hunter specialized in the destruction of spirits. You know how to channel your own spiritual energy to harm spirits. Your weapon and unarmed attack Strikes become magical. Against incorporeal creatures, they also gain the effects of a ghost touch property.
