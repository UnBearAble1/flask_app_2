Retributive Strike


15 Points
Religion/Warrior; Champion's Reaction

Reaction; [Tactic] Trigger An enemy damages your ally, and both are within 15 feet of you. Prerequisites: Master in Religion, Expert in Warrior

You protect your ally and strike your foe. The ally gains 12 resistance to all damage against the triggering damage If the foe is within reach, make a melee Strike against it.
