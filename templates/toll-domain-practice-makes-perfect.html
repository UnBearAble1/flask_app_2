Toll Domain: Practice Makes Perfect


7 Domain
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Religion

You work constantly and refuse to let anything stand in your way. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Practice Makes Perfect; Divination

Reaction; somatic; Trigger You or an ally within range fails a skill check for a skill in which you or they are trained.; Range 30 feet

The target gains a +2 status bonus to the triggering check, or a +3 bonus if the target has master or better proficiency in the skill. This bonus can change the outcome of the target's check.
