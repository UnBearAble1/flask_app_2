Nessian Warhound


Threat: 7

Believed to be bred by the Prince of Darkness himself within vast kennels in the infernal realm of Nessus, Nessian warhounds are the favored guards and hunting hounds of powerful fiends and, rarely, those mortals who worship them and have earned their favor.

Recall Knowledge - Beast (Arcana, Nature)
Recall Knowledge - Fiend (Religion)

LE, Large, Beast. Fiend, Fire, Skirmisher
Perception +11; darkvision, scent (imprecise) 120 feet
Languages Infernal; (can't speak any language)
Skills Acrobatics +9, Athletics +12, Stealth +9, Survival +11 (+13to Track)
Str +6, Dex +5, Con +5, Int -2, Wis +4, Cha -2
AC 21 ; Fort +10 , Ref +12 , Will +8
HP 80; Immunities fire; Weaknesses cold 10
Speed 40 feet

Reactions:

  • Hellish Revenge;


    • Trigger The Nessian warhound is critically hit by any Strike.
    • Effect The Nessian warhound's Breath Weapon recharges. It can immediately use it as part of this reaction.

Actions:

  • Melee Single Action jaws +12 [+7/+2] (magical), Damage 2d6+7 piercing plus 1d6 evil and 2d6 fire
  • Breath Weapon; Single Action (divine, evocation, fire) The warhound breathes flames that deal 10d6 fire damage to all creatures in a 15-foot cone (DC 21 basic Reflex save.) The warhound can't use Breath Weapon again for 1d4 rounds. If the Nessian warhound would take fire damage or be targeted by a fire effect, its Breath Weapon recharges.
