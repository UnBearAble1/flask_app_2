Legendary Negotiation


6 Points
Diplomacy; Make an Impression / Request
3 Actions; Prerequisites legendary in Diplomacy

You can negotiate incredibly quickly in adverse situations. You attempt to Make an Impression and then Request your opponent cease their current activity and engage in negotiations. You take a -5 penalty to your Diplomacy check. The GM sets the DC of the Request based on the circumstances--it's generally at least a Master DC -- Some creatures might simply refuse, and even those who agree to parley might ultimately find your arguments lacking and return to violence.
