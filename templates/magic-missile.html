Magic Missile


TRAINED

Evocation, Force
Traditions arcane, occult
One Action to Three Actions (somatic, verbal)
Range 120 feet; Targets 1 or more creatures

You send two darts of force streaking toward a creature (or one dart at two different creatures) that you can see. They automatically hit and deal 1d4+1 force damage each. For each additional action you use when Casting the Spell, increase the number of missiles you shoot by one, to a maximum of four missiles for 3 actions. You choose the target for each missile individually. If you shoot more than one missile at the same target, combine the damage before applying bonuses or penalties to damage, resistances, weaknesses, and so forth.

Heightened: You shoot one additional missile with each action you spend (so up to three extra darts per spell tier)
