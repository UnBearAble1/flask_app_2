Water Walk


TRAINED

Transmutation
Traditions arcane, divine, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature; Duration 30 minutes

The target can walk on the surface of water and other liquids without falling through. It can go underwater if it wishes, but in that case it must Swim normally. This spell doesn't grant the ability to breathe underwater.

Heightened (Expert) The spell's range increases to 30 feet, the duration increases to 1 hour, and you can target up to 10 creatures.
