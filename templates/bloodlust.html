Bloodlust


-3 points

MENTAL, CHAOTIC
You want to see your enemies die. You must make a self-control roll to use any non-lethal attacks against your enemies, whenever you need to accept a surrender, avoid a foe, take a prisoner or the like. If you fail that self control roll, you attempt to kill them instead, complications be damned.
This only applies to actual enemies, which includes any group that wants to bring you or your allies real harm as well as anyone else that seems to obviously fall into the "enemy" category. If you were in a simple tavern brawl, for example, this wouldn't apply, but if you were in a fistfight with someone from your rival clan, however...
