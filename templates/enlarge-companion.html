Enlarge Companion


3 Points
Nature; Animal Companion
Requires: Animal Companion

You make your companion enormous. You gain the enlarge companion warden spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Enlarge Companion; Polymorph,

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets your animal companion; Duration 1 minute

Your animal companion grows much larger, towering over its foes in battle. Your animal companion becomes Huge. Its equipment grows with it but returns to natural size if removed. The creature is clumsy 1. Its reach increases by 10 feet (or 15 feet if the creature started out Tiny) and it gains a +4 status bonus to melee damage. This spell has no effect on a Large or larger creature.
