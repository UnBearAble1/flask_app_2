Energy Ward


2 Points
Spellcaster; Metamagic

One Action; [Abjuration, Metamagic]; Prerequisites: Expert in spellcaster

If your next action is to cast a non-cantrip spell that deals energy damage.You trap energy from the spell you cast within your body, coating your flesh in a protective ward. Until the start of your next turn, you gain resistance 5 to one type of energy damage dealt by the spell you just cast.
