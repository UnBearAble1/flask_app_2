Mixed Maneuver


3 Points
Warrior; Combat Maneuvers
1 Action; Flourish; Prerequisites master in Warrior and Master in Athletics

You combine two different maneuvers together into a single flowing whole. Choose any two of Grapple, Shove, and Trip. Attempt both against the same or different creatures. Note you can use only 1 action with the flourish trait per turn.
