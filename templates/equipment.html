Equipment


These effects are all related to creating items or managing your inventory.

Prescient Planning


[Base: 8, Mod -4]
Activation: Instantaneous

You can spend time digging through your equipment and pull out "just the thing" that you "had on you all along," effectively allowing you to purchase mundane equipment retroactively when you need it. The item must fit within your pack. Once you draw the item, you immediately lose personal wealth equal to the value of the item, starting with any coin your character has (the GM has final say if you have no coin). If you lack the wealth to use this ability, or if the wealth would disappear in a non-discreet fashion or in a way that provides some other tactical benefit of any kind, this ability cannot be used. The spirit of this ability is a representation of your character's preplanning.

  • This can include Tier F consumable magic items, if they were purchasable in a settlement you have been in within the past month (or the very last settlement you were in, if you haven't been in one more than a month) (Base +4)
  • As above, but Tier D consumable magic items (Base +4, Mod +4)

Repair Item


[Base: 3, Mod +1]
Activation: Instantaneous, Linked Skill: crafting
Default Range: Touch
Target: 1 non-magical item of 1 bulk or less, that could otherwise be repaired with the "Repair" activity.

You restore 10 hit points to a broken object, possibly removing the broken condition. You can only use this ability once per instance of damage on an object during a 24 hour period (meaning if an object has taken 33 points of damage, and you heal that object 10 hit points, you cannot use this power to heal those other 23 points of damage until 24 hours have passed. If something else damages the item later, you can use this power to heal that damage. You can reduce this time using boosts.)

  • Heal +10 HP (mod +2)
  • Heal +20 HP (mod +4)
  • Heal +30 HP (base +1, Mod +4)
  • Heal +40 HP (base +5)
  • Per +1 Bulk (Mod +2)
  • The object can be magical. Especially powerful objects may require a counteract check, which you can only attempt once. (Base +5)
  • The object can be one that normally requires the "Perform Repairs" downtime activity.
    (Base +9, Mod +20)
  • The object can be a destroyed one (repairing a destroyed object restores it to its optimal state, including it's original paint, assuming it reaches full hit points.)
    (Mod +10).

Trick Magic Device


[Base: 8]

You can activate magic items that you typically don't meet the requirements to activate (such as a ring only usable by followers of a certain religion, that you are not.)


Crafting


Scrap Crafting
[Base: 8]
Linked Skill: Crafting
When you craft mundane equipment, you can use scrap or similar (roughly same value) but incorrect materials (GM discretion, but the spirit of this ability is allowing characters to 'McGuyver' tools and items together).
In this case, you can use more bulk in materials than usual to equal the similar value. Consider adding limits to give these supplies limited duration.
