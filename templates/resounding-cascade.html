Resounding Cascade



5 Points
Warrior/Spellcaster
Free Action; Frequency: Once per minute; Prerequisites Arcane Cascade, Expert Spellcaster; Trigger You use Arcane Cascade.

When you enter your Arcane Cascade, you disperse the magical reinforcement to nearby allies. You grant the extra damage of Arcane Cascade in an aura affecting all allies in a 5-foot emanation until you are no longer in the stance.
This uses only the base damage of the stance; it doesn't use any increase you get from bonuses, the laughing shadow hybrid study, or the like. As normal for duplicate effects, multiple Arcane Cascades affecting the same creature aren't cumulative.
