Megaprimatus


Threat: 7

The megaprimatus is among the mightiest of apes, quick to confront any perceived intrusions into its domain.

Recall Knowledge - Animal (Nature)

N, Huge, Animal, Brute
Perception +9; low-light vision, scent (imprecise) 30 feet
Skills Acrobatics +10, Athletics +15
Str +7, Dex +2, Con +5, Int -4, Wis +1, Cha +2
AC 20; Fort +12, Ref +10, Will +8
HP 135
Speed 30 feet, climb 30 feet

Actions:

  • Fist: Melee Single Action;
    +10 [+6/+2] (agile, reach 15 feet), Damage 2d12+7 bludgeoning
  • Jaws: Melee Single Action; +10 [+5/+0] (reach 10 feet), Damage 2d12+7 piercing
  • Mangling Rend; Two Actions; A megaprimatus makes two fist Strikes against the same target. If both hit, the attack deals an additional 2d6 bludgeoning damage, the target is flat-footed, and the target takes a -20-foot status penalty to all Speeds until the end of its next turn.
  • Terrifying Display;Two Actions (auditory, emotion, fear, mental) The megaprimatus beats its chest in a terrifying display. Creatures within 50 feet must attempt a DC 20 Will save. While a creature is frightened by this ability, it is flat-footed to the megaprimatus and to gorillas.

    • Critical Success No effect and temporarily immune for 1 minute.
    • Success The creature is unaffected.
    • Failure The creature is frightened 1.
    • Critical Failure The creature is frightened 2 and fleeing until the end of its next turn.
