Plague Domain: Divine Plagues


7 Points
Medicine; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Medicine; Trained in Religion

You wield disease and pestilence like a weapon. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Divine Plagues: Disease, Necromancy

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 15 feet; Saving Throw Fortitude

Your deity sends wracking plagues. The target must attempt a Fortitude save. When you Cast the Spell, decide whether the disease inflicts the clumsy, enfeebled, or stupefied condition.

  • Success The target is unaffected.
  • Failure The target is afflicted with divine plague (Virulent) at stage 1.
  • Critical Failure The target is afflicted with divine plague (Virulent) at stage 2.



    Divine Plague (disease)

    Level 1; Stage 1 clumsy 2, enfeebled 2, or stupefied 2 (1 round);

    Stage 2 slowed 1 and clumsy 2, enfeebled 2, or stupefied 2 (1 round);

    Stage 3 clumsy 2, enfeebled 2, or stupefied 2 and the target can't recover from the condition until they are cured (1 day)
