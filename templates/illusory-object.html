Illusory Object


TRAINED

Illusion, Visual, auditory, olfactory
Traditions: arcane, occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 500 feet; Area 20-foot burst; Duration 1 hour

You create an illusory visual image of a stationary object. The entire image must fit within the spell's area. The object appears to animate naturally, and makes appropriate sounds, generates normal smells, and feels right to the touch.

Heightened (Master) the duration is unlimited.
