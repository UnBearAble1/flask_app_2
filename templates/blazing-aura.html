Blazing Aura


11 Points
Natural Abilities; Elemental
Reaction; Usable once per day; Trigger: Your turn begins; [Concentrate, Evocation, Fire, Primal]

You explode in flame. Enemies in a 20-foot emanation take 7d6 fire damage (basic Reflex save.) Allies in the area are quickened for 1 round and can use the additional action to Strike or Stride.
