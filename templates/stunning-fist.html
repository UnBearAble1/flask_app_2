Stunning Fist


10 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts
Prerequisites Flurry of Blows; Master in Warrior

The focused power of your flurry threatens to overwhelm your opponent. When you target the same creature with two Strikes from your Flurry of Blows, you can try to stun the creature. If either Strike hits and deals damage, the target must succeed at a Fortitude or be stunned 1 (or stunned 3 on a critical failure). This is an incapacitation effect.
