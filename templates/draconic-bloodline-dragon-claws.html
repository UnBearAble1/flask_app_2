Draconic Bloodline: Dragon Claws


4 Points
Arcana; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Expert in Arcana

You gain the focus spell, Dragon Claws, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Dragon Claws; Morph,Transmutation;
1 Action verbal; Duration 1 minute

Vicious claws grow from your fingers. They are finesse unarmed attacks that deal 2d4 slashing damage and 2d6 extra damage of a type determined by the dragon in your bloodline. (Acid, Electricity, Poison, Cold, or Fire) Your scales from blood magic glow with faint energy, giving you resistance 5 to the same damage type.



Additionally, immediately upon casting this spell, until the end of your next turn, Draconic scales grow briefly on you, granting a +1 status bonus to AC for 1 round.
