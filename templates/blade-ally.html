Blade Ally


5 Points
Religion/Warrior; Blade Ally
Prerequisites: Master in Religion, Expert in warrior

A spirit of battle dwells within your armaments. Select one weapon or unarmed blows when you make your daily preparations. In your hands, the item gains one of the following effects: and you also gain the weapon's critical specialization effect.

  • Disrupting: It deals an additional 1d6 positive damage to undead.
  • Ghost Touch: It has the "ghost touch" Property
  • Returning: When you throw it, it returns to your hand after the attack
  • Shifting: If you spend an interact action, The weapon takes the shape of another melee weapon that requires the same number of hands to wield.
  • Fearsome: When you critically hit with this weapon, the target becomes frightened 1.
