Lifeblood's Call


1 Point
Behavioral Trait, Fighting Style
Prerequisites: Expert in Fortitude Saves
You gain a circumstance bonus to damage rolls on your melee Strikes equal to twice the sum of your number of injuries and doomed conditions (to a maximum of a +8 circumstance bonus to damage) whenever you have one or both conditions.
