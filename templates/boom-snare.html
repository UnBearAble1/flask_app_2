Boom Snare


EXPERT
Uncommon, Consumable, Mechanical, Snare, Trap
Price 18 gp

By combining a lesser alchemist's fire, a lesser thunderstone, and other catalysts with a pressure sensitive trigger, you create a snare that creates a fiery and thunderous explosion when triggered. The target and all creatures in adjacent squares take 2d6 fire damage and 2d6 sonic damage with a basic DC 18 Reflex save. On a failure, they are deafened for 1d4 rounds and take 1d4 persistent fire damage.

Craft Requirements Supply a lesser alchemist's fire and a lesser thunderstone.
