Survival Feats


COMBAT OPTIONS


  • Lumberjack [T] -3 pts: Gain lore, cover based advantages in the forest
  • Nature's Edge [E] -2 pts: Natural or Snare Diff Terrain makes enemies flat footed to you

EXPLORATION


  • Dead Reckoning [T] -2 pts: You always know which way is north
  • Survey Wildlife [T] -2 pts: Assess area check will allow recall knowledge about life there

FAVORED TERRAIN


  • Favored Terrain [E] -3 pts: Ignore difficult terrain in your favored environment. Gain incredible benefits if you also have Wild Stride (movement category below)


    • Acclimatization [E] -3 pts: Also resist environmental damage and temperatures there
    • Horizon Walker [E] -3 pts: Also gain +10 travel speed there
    • Perpetual Scout [M] -3 pts: Also always scout in exploration mode there
    • Sure Foot [M] -2 pts: Also always get +2 to skills to movement there
    • Terrain Expertise [E] -1 pt: Also always get +1 to survival there
    • Terrain Mastery [M] -4 pts: Spend 1 hour to change type of favored terrain

FOCUS SPELLS


  • Ephemeral Tracking [M] -1 pt: Focus spell lets you track through air and water
  • Terrain Transposition [M] -4 pts: Focus spell lets you teleport 90 feet in wilderness

HOSTILE ENVIRONMENTS


  • Environmental Explorer [T] -2 pts: Can find natural hazards even when not searching
  • Environmental Grace [M/T] -4 pts: Spend 1hr in dangerous temperature, become protected
  • Planar Survival [M] -4 pts: You can subsist in planes that normally don't sustain you
  • Survival of Desolation [E] -1 pt: Get +2 saves against blighted/marred regions
  • Wandering Oasis [M] -10 pts: You/Allies protected from extreme temperatures

MOVEMENT


  • Axe Climber [T] -2 pts: Use axes to climb wooden surfaces, or picks for ice
  • Legendary Guide [L] -5 pts: Party gets +10 to travel speed, ignores difficult terrain
  • Pirate [T] -1 pt: Gain lore, ignore ship movement difficult terrain
  • Rope Runner [T] -2 pts: Gain climb speed 15 ft, improved success on ship's rigging
  • Wild Stride [L] -8 pts: Ignore non-magical difficult terrain


    • Ultimate Skirmisher [L] -15 pts: Also greater/hazardous terrain, and don't trigger movement based hazards
  • Woodland Stride [E] -3 pts: Ignore difficult terrain caused by plants

SENSES


  • Storm Born [M] -5 pts: Ignore weather penalties/concealment with spell attacks
  • Wilderness Spotter [E] -3 pts: In favored terrain, use survival in place of many skills
  • Wortwitch [E] -6 pts: You can see through foliage of all kinds

SUSTENANCE


  • Forager [T] -3 pts: Feed more creatures with subsist
  • Rain-Scribe Sustenance [L] -13 pts: Don't need to eat/drink, less rest. Can rest without needing sleep if you abandon benefits for a week
  • Rugged Survivalist [M] -4 pts: Ignore starvation/thirst for much longer

TRACKING


  • Experienced Tracker [T] -3 pts: You can track at full speed with -5 penalty

    • Swift Tracker [E] -5 pts: Track at full speed, don't need to make new check each hour, can use another exploration activity while tracking
  • Trackless Step [M] -3 pts: You always gain benefits of cover tracks
