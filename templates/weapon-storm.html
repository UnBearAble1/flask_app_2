Weapon Storm


EXPERT
Evocation, Attack
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Area 30-foot cone or 10-foot emanation
Saving Throw Reflex

You swing a weapon you're holding, and the weapon magically multiplies into duplicates that swipe at all enemies in either a cone or an emanation. This flurry deals 4d10 damage to targets you choose in the area. This damage has the same type as the weapon.


  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The target takes half damage.
  • Failure The target takes full damage.
  • Critical Failure The target takes double damage and is subject to the weapon's critical specialization effect.

Heightened: +2d10
