Eloko


Threat: 7

If a biloko is lucky enough to consume several spellcasters or other creatures with innate magical abilities, they undergo a subtle transformation over the course of 1 week. Such a fey is known as an eloko, and in addition to their incredible appetite (even by biloko standards), they have the supernatural ability to grow to immense size.

Recall Knowledge - Fey (Nature)

NE, Small, Fey, Skirmisher
Perception +11; low-light vision, scent (imprecise) 30 feet
Languages Aklo, Mwangi, Sylvan
Skills Athletics +11, Crafting +6, Deception +8, Nature +6, Stealth +11, Survival +6
Str +6, Dex +5, Con +1, Int +0, Wis +4, Cha +1
AC 21 ; Fort +10 , Ref +12 , Will +8
HP 70
Speed 20 feet

Features:

  • Sneak Attack The eloko deals 2d6 extra precision damage to flat-footed creatures.
  • Inspired Feast (emotion, mental) If a biloko feasts on a human corpse for 1 minute or more, they gain a +1 status bonus to attack rolls and a +2 status bonus to damage rolls for 1 hour.

Actions:

  • Jaws; Melee Single Action: +11 [+6/+1], Damage 2d8+7 piercing
  • Dagger; Melee Single Action:
    +11 [+7/+3] (agile, magical, versatile S), Damage 2d6+7 piercing
  • Blowgun; Ranged Single Action:
    +11 [+7/+3] (agile, nonlethal, range increment 20 feet, reload 1), Effect 1 piercing plus 2d6 persistent poison
  • Thrown Dagger; Ranged Single Action; +11 [+7/+3] (agile, magical, thrown 10 feet, versatile S), Damage 2d6+7 piercing
  • Primal Innate Spells DC 21: Charm
  • Size Alteration: Two Actions (polymorph, primal, transmutation) The eloko is affected by an Expert enlarge spell. This lasts for 1 minute, and the eloko can Dismiss the Spell. While in this huge form, the Eloko's maximum hit points increase to 100 (and their current hit points increase by 30, accordingly)
