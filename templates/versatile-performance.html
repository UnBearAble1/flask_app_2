Versatile Performance


5 Points
Performance; Skill Substitution
Prerequisites: Acting!, Impressive Performance, or Fancy Moves

You can use your proficiency rank in Performance to meet the requirements of feats that require a particular rank in Deception, Diplomacy, or Intimidation if those feats specifically only give a related benefit to the corresponding action:

Acting = Impersonate
Impressive Performance = Make an Impression
Fancy Moves = Demoralize

For example, if you had the Fancy Moves feat (allowing you to use performance to demoralize) you could use your rank in performance to qualify for an intimidation feat, as long as that feat were specifically related to the demoralize action, such as Antagonize.
