Graveknight


Threat: 7

Graveknights are undead warriors granted unlife by a cursed suit of armor.

Recall Knowledge - Undead (Religion)

LE, Medium, Undead, Soldier
Perception +10; darkvision
Languages Common, Necril
Skills Athletics +11, Intimidation +12, Religion +9, Warfare Lore +10
Str +7, Dex +4, Con +4, Int +2, Wis +3, Cha +5
AC 22; Fort +12, Ref +10, Will +8
HP 94 (negative healing, rejuvenation); Immunities cold, death, disease, paralyzed, poison, unconscious
Speed 25 feet

Features:

  • Sacrilegious Aura (abjuration, aura, divine, evil) 30 feet. When a creature in the aura uses a positive spell or ability, the graveknight automatically attempts to counteract it, with a +11 counteract modifier.
  • Weapon Master The graveknight has access to the critical specialization effects of any weapons it wields.

Reactions:

  • Attack of Opportunity

Actions:

  • Greatsword: Melee Single Action +11 [+6/+1] (magical, versatile P), Damage 2d10+5 slashing
  • Fist: Melee Single Action
    +11 [+7/+3] (agile, cold), Damage 2d6+7 bludgeoning plus 1d6 cold
  • Ranged Single Action frost composite longbow +10 [+5/+0] (deadly 1d10, magical, range increment 100 feet, reload 0, volley 30 feet), Damage 2d8+7
  • Devastating Blast: Two Actions (arcane, cold, evocation) The graveknight unleashes a 30-foot cone of energy. Creatures in the area take 6d10 cold damage (DC 20 basic Reflex save). The graveknight can use this ability once every 1d4 rounds.
  • Phantom Mount: Three Actions (arcane, conjuration) The graveknight summons a supernatural mount as per phantom steed. Unlike phantom steed, the steed's AC and saving throw bonuses are all 4 lower than the graveknight's, and the steed has one-third the graveknight's Hit Points (rounded down). If the steed is destroyed, the graveknight must wait 1 hour before using this ability again.
