Tyranny Domain: Touch of Obedience


3 Points
Intimidation; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Intimidation; Trained in Religion

You wield power to rule and enslave others..You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Touch of Obedience; Enchantment, Mental

1 Action somatic; Range touch; Targets 1 living creature; Saving Throw Will; Duration varies

Your imperious touch erodes the target's willpower, making it easier to control. The target attempts a Will save.

  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target is stupefied 1 until the end of your current turn.
  • Failure The target is stupefied 1 until the end of your next turn.
  • Critical Failure The target is stupefied 1 for 1 minute.
