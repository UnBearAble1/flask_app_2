Perfect Distraction


8 Points
Deception; Spell-Like Ability
Prerequisites legendary in Deception.

1 Action; Requires: You spent time during your daily preparations to create a decoy.

You use clever tactics to mislead your foes as you sneak away. You Sneak while leaving a decoy behind. The decoy acts as the spell mislead, though you aren't invisible, just undetected. You can continue to concentrate to move your decoy, as with the spell, whether or not you remain hidden throughout the duration. Your decoy, if left behind, is a physical item with bulk 1. You can't use this ability again until your next daily preparations.
