Demonic Bloodline


14 Points
Spellcaster; Magical Bloodine
Prerequisites: Trained in Spellcaster (Divine)

Demons debase all they touch. One of your ancestors fell victim to their corruption, and you are burdened by that sin. Add the following spells to your repertoire, even if they aren't normally part of the divine tradition (note you must still have the appropriate level of spellcaster proficiency to cast the spell):

  • cantrip: acid splash,
  • Trained: fear and enlarge,
  • Expert: slow and divine wrath,
  • Master: abyssal plague and disintegrate,
  • Legendary: divine decree and divine aura,

Whenever you cast a spell from the list of spells above, gain the following Blood Magic ability: The corruption of sin weakens a target's defenses or makes you more imposing. Either a target takes a -1 status penalty to AC for 1 round, or you gain a +1 status bonus to Intimidation checks for 1 round.
