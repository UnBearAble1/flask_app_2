Community and Society


These effects are related to your interactions with the community.

Fame and Followers

Famous
[Mod: 9]
Linked skill: Lore or Performance
You are famous for something. When you meet an NPC, make a flat check. The DC is typically 10, but this is easier the more skill the NPC has in Society or relevant lores. On a success, they have heard of you. What this means will depend on your specific reputation and the NPC's disposition, but can include a friendlier disposition level than normal. For instance, if you're well-known for cruel and demanding behavior, creatures might be intimidated by you, rather than be friendly toward you.


Field Expert
[Mod: 2]
Linked Skill: Lore or Earn Income Skill
When you Earn Income with the linked skill or lore, you attract higher-level jobs than your location would allow, as employers will seek you out for your expertise.


Entourage
[Mod: 3]
You have a small group of admirers who tend to follow you around while you're in civilized settlements. Your admirers are helpful to you, allowing you to make simple Requests of them such as purchasing basic equipment with your funds or finding a room at an inn. These admirers do not travel with you to places that are obviously dangerous, including most adventure locations, but they'll wait for you at the nearest settlement. Your admirers never use actions in encounter mode other than to retreat, and they abandon you if you intentionally bring them harm or at the GM's discretion.



Settlements


Room and Board
[Mod: 1]
Linked Skill: Diplomacy
While in a settlement, you can locate a resident willing to provide lodging for you and up to six allies for up to 24 hours at no charge. The standard of living within the acquired lodging is comfortable, and square meals are provided at no cost. After 24 hours, you must pay standard prices for further lodging and meals or use this feat again to find a new resident willing to host you.


Connections
Linked Skill: Society or Relevant Lore
While in a settlement, You can arrange meetings with important figures with two hours of footwork

  • Minor: Artists, Niche professionals, Mercenaries, etc (Mod: 1)
  • Major: Politicians, Criminals, Merchants, etc (Base: 3)



Arrange Event Entry
[Base: 3]
Linked Skill: Society or Diplomacy
Any time you encounter a social event you would ordinarily be denied access to, such as a coronation, royal gala, or other society function, you can secure entry anyway. You find invitations, invitees looking for fashionable dates, temporary jobs with the caterers, event staff willing to look the other way, or some other mode of access for yourself and your allies. This ability doesn't apply to secret events or other small private gatherings with no staff, dates, or outsiders involved.
