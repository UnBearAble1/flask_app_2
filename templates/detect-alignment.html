Detect Alignment


TRAINED

Detection. Divination
Traditions divine, occult
Two Actions;
somatic, verbal
Area 30-foot emanation

Your eyes glow as you sense aligned auras. Choose chaotic, evil, good, or lawful. You detect auras of that alignment. You learn each aura's location and strength. You can choose not to detect creatures or effects you're aware have that alignment.

Only creatures with at least one master level proficiency--unless divine spellcasters, undead, or beings from the Outer Sphere--have alignment auras.

Aura strengths:

  • None
  • Faint
  • Moderate
  • Powerful
  • Overwhelming

The strength of a creature's alignment aura is based on how deeply they commit to the actions of their alignment. For example: a good creature who does good only when the opportunity presents itself (they see a creature suffering, so they do what they can to help alleviate it) would have a faint aura, where as one who dedicates their life to seeking out good to do would have a powerful aura. The auras of undead, clerics and other divine spellcasters with a patron deity, and creatures from the Outer Sphere are one step stronger than normal.

Objects and spells use the alignment of the creature responsible for them, and are often matched with the power of the item or spell.
