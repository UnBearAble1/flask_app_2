Investigation (V2)


Investigation is the ability to examine a location or item for clues, and the ability to connect clues and unravel mysteries.
If you are specialized in Investigation and declare you are investigating a scene for clues, the GM will automatically give you any core clues that are relevant to keep the story going. Optional clues may require you to make checks.

Untrained Actions:



  • Reconstruct the Scene: You study a scene or an item to gather clues and get a sense of what happened recently involving it.
  • Examine Clue: You study a clue to determine if it is important, and possibly why.
  • Connect the Dots: You compare two or more clues to understand if they are related.
