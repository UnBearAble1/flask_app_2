Twist the Knife


4 Points
Warrior; Sneak Attack

1 Action; Prerequisites: Your last action was a melee Strike that dealt sneak attack damage to a flat-footed target; Prerequisites: Greater Sneak Attack Boost; Master in Warrior

After stabbing your opponent in a weak spot, you tear the wound open. You deal persistent bleed damage to the target equal to your number of sneak attack damage dice.
