Power Word Blind


LEGENDARY
Auditory, Enchantment, Mental
Traditions arcane
Single Action; verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Duration varies

You utter an arcane word of power that can make the target blinded upon hearing it. Once targeted, the target is then temporarily immune for 10 minutes. The effect of the spell depends on the target's threat.

Minor or Trivial: The target is blinded permanently.
Moderate:
The target is blinded for 1d4 minutes.
Serious or Higher: The target is dazzled for 1 minute.
