Divine Armageddon


LEGENDARY
Necromancy, Negative, Positive
Traditions divine
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 120 feet; Area 60-foot burst
Saving Throw basic Fortitude

You call forth a divine cataclysm from your deity, destroying living and undead creatures in the area alike. Creatures in the area take 6d8 negative damage and 6d8 alignment damage (good, evil, lawful, or chaotic), chosen from among the alignments your deity has. If your deity is true neutral, increase the negative damage by 4d8 instead of dealing alignment damage. A creature harmed by positive damage, such as one with negative healing, takes positive damage instead of negative damage from this spell.

You can't cast this spell if you don't have a deity. This spell gains the trait corresponding to the alignment damage dealt.
