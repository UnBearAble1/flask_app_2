Holy Cascade


EXPERT
Evocation, Good, Positive, Water
Traditions divine
Two Actions; somatic, verbal; Cost one vial of holy water
Range 500 feet; Area 20-foot burst
Saving Throw basic Reflex

You call upon sacred energy to amplify a vial of holy water, tossing it an incredible distance. It explodes in an enormous burst that deals 5d6 bludgeoning damage to creatures in the area from the cascade of water. The water deals an additional 5d6 positive damage to undead and 5d6 good damage to fiends.

Heightened: The bludgeoning damage increases by 3d6, and the additional positive and good damage each increase by 3d6.
