Ruffian


3 Points
Warrior; Sneak Attack
Prerequisites: Sneak Attack

You can deal sneak attack damage with any simple weapon, in addition to the weapons listed in the sneak attack class feat. When you critically succeed at an attack roll using a simple weapon and the target has the flat-footed condition (unable to focus on defending itself), you also apply the critical specialization effect for the weapon you're wielding. You don't gain these benefits if the weapon has a damage die larger than d8 (after applying any abilities that alter its damage die size).
