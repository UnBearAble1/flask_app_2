Scroll Savant


12 Points
Spellcaster; Extra Spells
Prerequisites expert in Crafting; Expert in Spellcaster

During your daily preparations, you can create two temporary scrolls containing spells you know. These scrolls follow the normal rules for scrolls, with some additional restrictions. The spells cost nothing to create, but you DO have to cast the spell when you create them. Each scroll must be of a different spell Tier, and both spell levels must be at least 1 tier lower than your highest-level spell tier. Any scrolls you create this way become non-magical the next time you make your daily preparations. A temporary scroll has no value.
