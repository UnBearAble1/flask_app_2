Repeating Hand Crossbow


3 Points
Warrior; Ranged Weapons

Requires: Shootist's Draw; Prerequisites: Master in Warrior

You become trained in the repeating hand crossbow and gain access to repeating hand crossbows and shootist bandoliers. You also gain access to its critical specialization effect. You can perform the Shootist's Draw action with repeating hand crossbows.
