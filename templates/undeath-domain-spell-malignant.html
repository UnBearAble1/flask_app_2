Undeath Domain: Spell Malignant


12 Points
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Religion

Your magic carries close ties to the undead. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Spell Malignant; Necromancy, Negative

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range touch; Targets 1 willing undead creature; Duration 1 minute

You embed a seed of negative energy in an undead creature, restoring its unnatural vigor. The target gains fast healing 7. This healing comes from negative energy, so it heals the undead rather than damaging it.
