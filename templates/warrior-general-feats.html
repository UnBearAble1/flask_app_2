Warrior, General Feats


ARMOR


  • Armiger's Mobility [E] -6 pts: Reduce movement penalty in heavy armor
  • Armor Proficiency [T] -1 pt: Gain proficiency in medium or heavy armor
  • Armor Specialist [E} -3 pts: Gain the armor specialization effects of your armor
  • Armored Rebuff [E/T] -1 pt: Reaction to shove foe who crit fails attacking you
  • Mighty Bulwark [M] -4 pts: Your bulwark trait improves to +4, applies to all reflex saves
  • Sacrifice Armor [E] -5+ pts: Reduce damage taken by 20, but armor breaks
  • Second Skin [E] -4 pts: You can rest armor without becoming fatigued, don armor faster

BACKSTAB


  • Expert Backstabber [E/E] -3 pts: Backstabber weapons deal 1 extra damage
  • Mark for Death [E/E] -5+ pts: Mark a creature, get numerous benefits toward killing them

COMBAT MANEUVERS


  • Brutal Bully [E] -3 pts: Deal STR mod damage with combat maneuvers
  • Mixed Maneuver [E] -4 pts: Combine two combat maneuvers into a 1 action

COMBAT MOBILITY


  • Barreling charge [E/T] -1+ pts: You stride and strike for 2 actions, through enemy spaces
  • Determined Dash [E] -8 pts: Stride twice, ignore difficult terrain, for 2 actions
  • Farabellus Flip [E/T] -2 pts: Reaction against strike to get +2 AC, and step if you are hit
  • Guarded Movement [E] -2 pts: Get +4 AC against movement triggered reactions
  • Mobility [M] -10 pts: Stride at half speed and don't provoked triggered reactions
  • No Escape [E] -2 pts: Reaction to stride and follow a moving target
  • Ship to Ship
    [E/E] -1 pt: Stride twice / swing on rope / strike with one additional dice damage if disembarking a vehicle - for 2 actions
  • Sudden Charge [E] -3 pts: You stride twice and attack for 2 actions
  • Sudden Leap [E/E] -2 pts: Jump or leap and strike for 2 actions
  • Winding Flow [E/T] - 3 pts: Use 2 Stand/Step/Stride in combination for 1 action

CRITICAL HITS


  • Brutal Critical [M] -5 pts: Once per 10 min, Crit does extra damage and persistent bleed
  • Savage Critical [L] -8 pts: You automatically critical on a 19 as well as 20
  • Unbalancing Blow [M] -7 pts: If you crit, target is flat-footed against you until end of next turn

CROWD FIGHTING


  • Sidestep [M] -8 pts: Reaction to redirect foe's failed attack to adjacent creature
  • Spring Attack [E] -3 pts: Stride and attack away from an enemy to another, 1 action
  • Unbalancing Sweep [E] -4 pts: Shove or trip up to 3 enemies at once for 2 actions
  • Cleave [E] -3+ pts: Defeat one enemy, reaction attack against a second
  • Swipe [M] -4 pts: Attack 2 enemies for 2 actions but no penalty
  • Whirlwind Strike [M] -6 pts: Attack all enemies within reach for 3 actions

DEBILITATING STRIKE


  • Debilitating Strike
    [E] -8 pts: Hit a flat footed creature, penalize speed or make enfeebled 1

    • Critical Debilitations
      [E] -7 pts:
      Crit with DS, add option that may slow or paralyze target
    • Double Debilitation
      [M] -8 pts: You can apply two debilitating effects simultaneously
    • Eldritch Debilitation
      [E] -5 pts: Add stupefied and inability to step to list
    • Enduring Debilitation
      [E] -5 pts: Once a minute, make your debilitation last 1 minute
    • Methodical Debilitation
      [E] -5 pts: Add 'can't provide flanking' and 'can't use cover' to list
    • Precise Debilitation
      [E] -11 pts: Add 2d6 bleed and flat footed to your list
    • Tactical Debilitation
      [E] -6 pts: Add 'can't use reactions' and 'can't provide flanking' to list
    • Vicious Debilitation
      [E] -8 pts: Add weakness 5 to bludgeoning, slashing or piercing, and clumsy 1 to your list

DISARM


  • Disarming Assault [E/T] -3 pts: Strike + Disarm for 1 action
  • Disarming Flair [E] -3 pts: Disarm penalties you inflict last longer
  • Relentless Disarm [E/T] -4 pts: Better disarm bonus, demoralize when you crit disarm

FEAT VERSATILITY


  • Combat Flexibility [M] -12+ pts: Pick any warrior feat each day of 10 pts / one lower tier

FEINT


  • Deny Support [E] -2 pts: Your feint stops foes from flanking
  • Overextending Feint [E/T] -3 pts: Your feint can penalize attack rolls instead
  • Scoundrel [T/T] -3 pts: Your feints make them flat-footed longer than normal
  • Distracting Feint [M/T] -5 pts: Your feints also penalize reflex/perception
  • Scout's Charge [E/T] -5 pts: Stride, Feint (with stealth), Strike for 2 actions

FOCUS SPELL


  • Gravity Weapon [M] -4 pts: Focus Spell: +4 to damage on first strike each round
  • Sever Space [M] -8 pts: Focus Spell: Strike up to 80 feet, then either teleport to target or force them to teleport to you.
  • Weapon Surge [M/T] -5 pts: Focus Spell: Strike with +1 hit,+2 Dice Damage
  • Zeal Domain: Zeal for Battle [M/T] -5 pts: Focus Spell: You and ally roll Initiative w/ Advantage

FORCED MOVEMENT


  • Leading Dance [E/T] -3 pts: Check to move opponent and self up to 10 feet, 1 action
  • Dragging Strike [E] -1 pts: Strike, and move target 5ft toward you. Fail: they are flat footed.
  • Felling Strike [E] -3 pts: Strike airborne foe, they fall (no damage) 120 feet. 2 Actions.

GRAPPLING


  • Combat Grab [E] -3 pts: Strike, grab for 1 action.
  • Crushing Grab [E] -5 pts: When you grapple, deal STR damage
  • Dazing Blow [M] -6 pts: Strike, possibly stun, a creature you have grabbed - 1 action.
  • Embrace the Pain [E] -2 pts: Take damage, reaction to grapple or disarm
  • Furious Grab [M] -5 pts: Grapple with no check if following strike, 1 action
  • Impaling Thrust [M] -6 pts: Strike with piercing weapon, grapple with no check, 2 actions
  • Sleeper Hold [M] -6 pts: Possibly knock unconscious foe you have grappled
  • Tangle of Battle [E] -2 pts: You crit an enemy, reaction to grapple them
  • Thrash [E] -2+ pts: Deal 3+STR against grabbed foe (basic fort save)
  • Whirling Throw [M/M] -6 pts: Throw a foe up to 30 feet, do up 3d6 damage, maybe knock them prone

INITIATIVE


  • Battle Planner [E/E] -1 pt: If you Scout enemies, can use warfare lore for initiative
  • Quick Draw [E] -2 pts: Draw a weapon and strike in one action
  • Stance Savant [M] -3 pts: Enter a stance as a free action on initiative
  • Surprise Attack [E/E] -2 pts: Foes are possibly flat footed against you first round of combat
  • Swaggering Initiative [M] -5 pts: Free action, +2 initiative and draw a weapon

INTIMIDATION


  • Dread Striker [E/M] -6 pts: Frightened creatures are flat-footed against you
  • Fearsome Brute [M] -7 pts: Against frightened foes, gain double value as dmg bonus
  • Intimidating Strike [E/E] -4 pts: Strike inflicts frightened 1 or 2, for 2 actions
  • Shatter Defenses [E/E] -5 pts: Inflict flat-footed against a frightened foe, or extend duration

POWERFUL BLOWS


  • Deadly Grace [M] -7 pts: Crit with light weapon, double deadly dice (or gain 1d8)
  • Devastator [L] -7 pts: Take -2 to hit, then ignore 10 points of a creature's resistance
  • Overwhelming Blow [L] -7 pts: Upgrade hits and crits as a single 3 action attack
  • Power Attack [E] -3+ pts: For 2 actions, make strike with +2 dice damage

PRECISE ATTACKS


  • Certain Strike [E] -3 pts:
    Strike that deals some damage on a failure
  • Exacting Strike [E] -3 pts: Strike that doesn't count toward multi-attack-penalty on miss
  • Follow Up Assault [M] -3 pts: Follow up a miss with a strike with Backswing/Forceful
  • Perfected Form [L] -7 pts: On 1st strike against enemy, treat <10 roll as a 10.

REACH


  • Lunge [E] -4 pts: Make a strike (maybe even combat maneuver) 5 feet further


    • Lunging Stance [E] -3 pts: Can make lunge strikes as attacks of opportunity

REACTION ATTACKS


  • Attack of Opportunity [E] -6+ pts: Reaction attack against foe moving, manipulating, or using ranged attack
  • Deflect Arrow [E] -2+ pts: Reaction to gain +4 AC against ranged attack
  • Desperate Finisher [M] -3 pts: Reaction to use press action after you are out of actions
  • Opportune Backstab [M] -6 pts: Reaction to attack foe struck by nearby ally in melee
  • Opportune Riposte [E] -2+ pts: Reaction to attack a foe who crit misses you
  • Preparation [L] -5 pts: Spend 1 action to gain an extra reaction for warrior feats
  • Reactive Interference [M] -6 pts: Disrupt a reaction with your reaction
  • Stand Still [E] -2 pts: Reaction to strike foe who is moving, crits disrupt the move
  • Stay Down [E/M] -3 pts: Reaction to make athletics roll to force foe to remain prone

RED MANTIS ASSASSIN


  • Red Mantis Assassin [E/E] -11 pts: Extended feint benefits, including no-roll and 2d6 persistent bleed damage


    • Achaekek's Grip [M] -10 pts: Focus Spell: First time target takes persistent bleed damage each round they also take the same amount in slashing. Also harder to end bleed.
    • Achaekek's Sense [M/M] -1 pt: You can sense if a foe has been restored to life


      • Vernai Training [M/M] -3 pts: Your kills require counteract checks to restore to life
    • Crimson Shroud [M] -9 pts: A few times a day, gain expert-tier fast healing 5, gain minor shield, and possibly disappear when slain.


      • Fading [M] -5 pts: End your shroud to have flat 11 check to resist all damage
    • Mantis Form: [E/E]: -7 pts: Focus spell to turn into a normal mantis.

SENSES


  • Blind Fight [M/M] -7 pts: You ignore concealed, other benefits against hidden
  • Revealing Stab [M/E] -3 pts: Reveal concealed / hidden by stabbing weapon in them

SHOVE


  • Improved Knockback [M] -3 pts: Shove creatures further, into obstacles for damage
  • Knockback [E] -2+ pts: You can shove with no check for 1 action after a strike
  • Shove Down [E/T] -5 pts: Attempt a free trip against a target you shoved

SPECIAL ATTACKS


  • Advantageous Assault [E] -3 pts: Strike against grabbed, prone, restrained creature deals extra damage - even on failure
  • Annihilating Swing [L] -10 pts: Strike and destroy objects or surfaces with ease 1x hour
  • Boaster's Challenge [M/E] -5+ pts: Social challenge against foe's will adds up to +6 damage on your attacks, 1action, lasts possibly 3 rounds
  • Brutal Beating [E] -3 pts: Your crits cause targets to be frightened 1
  • Harrying Strike [M] -5 pts: Strike that imposes up to -15 foot speed penalty
  • Head Stomp [E] -3 pts: Unarmed against prone, impose flat-footed for a turn
  • Instant Opening [M/M] -10 pts: Make one target flat footed against your attacks with 1 action and no checks
  • Master Strike [L] -12 pts: Strike flat footed creature, impose enfeebled or paralyzed
  • Murderer's Circle [M/M] -5 pts: Strike, Step, foe is flat-footed against your next attack
  • Pin to the Spot [M] -7 pts: Strike that restrains target as if crit on grapple
  • Resounding Blow [M] -2 pts: Strike also deafens target for 1 round or a minute
  • Silencing Strike [M] -7 pts: Strike causes fort save or stun, possibly limit verbal ability
  • Snagging Strike [E] -4 pts: Strike renders flat-footed if you are within hand's reach
  • Time Dilation Cascade [L] 5 pts: Once per minute, attack six times.
  • Vivacious Evisceration [L] -7 pts: Once per 10 mins, strike can render drained 1 or 2

SPECIAL DEFENSES


  • Death's Door [L] -12 pts: Once per 10 minutesl gain 20 temp HP when would reach 0 HP
  • Deny Advantage [M] -8 pts: You aren't flat footed to hidden, undetected for flanking
  • Nimble Dodge [E] -1+ pt: Reaction to add +2 to AC
  • Reflexive Grip [E] -1 pt: When you fall unconscious, don't drop items. +2 against disarm

STANCES


  • Master of Many Styles [M] -4 pts: Enter a stance at beginning of each turn for free
  • Prevailing Position [E] -2 pts: Reaction to leave a stance and gain +4 to AC

TEAMWORK


  • Back to Back [M] -4 pts: If you and one or more allies are adjacent, you are usually not flat footed when flanked
  • Coordinated Charge [E] -3 pts: Stride, Strike, for 2 actions, allies can stride for a reaction
  • Coordinated Distraction [E] -3 pts: Make one enemy flat-footed to one ally (cost: 1 action)
  • Deft Combat Cooperation [E] -2 pts: If you Aid attack/AC, you also get +1 until end of turn
  • Gang Up [E] -4 pts: Gain benefits of flanking just by being within reach of enemy with ally
  • Knight Vigilant [M] -2 pts: You provide standard cover by being between ally and enemy
  • Leave an Opening [M] -2 pts: Crits on flat footed foes can trigger Attacks of Opportunity
  • Protect Ally [E] -2 pts: Adjacent Ally gains +1 to AC (cost: 1 action)
  • Rallying Charge [E] -4 pts: Stride, Strike, 2 actions. Allies gain temp HP= your CHR mod.
  • Scout's Warning [E/E] -3 pts: Allies get +1 to initiative with perception/survival.
  • Shoulder Catastrophe [E] - 2+ pts: Reaction: If ally is crit, you can take normal damage from attack to reduce the crit to a normal hit on the ally
  • Topple Foe [E/T] -2 pts: Reaction to trip target adjacent ally strikes
  • Vigil's Walls Rise Anew [E] -2 pts: For 2 actions, you and all allies you pre-train with can raise a shield

TRIP


  • Knockdown [E/T] -2+ pts: Strike and Trip for a single action, boost lets you strike and trip without an athletics roll but costs two actions.
