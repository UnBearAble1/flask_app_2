Freedom of Movement


EXPERT
Abjuration
Traditions arcane, divine, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature touched
Duration 10 minutes

You repel effects that would hinder a creature or slow its movement. While under this spell's effect, the target ignores effects that would give them a circumstance penalty to Speed. When they attempt to Escape an effect that has them immobilized, grabbed, or restrained, they automatically succeed unless the effect is magical and of a higher tier than the freedom of movement spell.
