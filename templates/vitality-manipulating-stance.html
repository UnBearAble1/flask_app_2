Vitality-Manipulating Stance


12 Points
Warrior / Ki-Arts
Prerequisites: Legendary in Warrior; Stunning Fist
You have learned to attack meridians and pressure points. You gain the Vitality-manipulating stance ki focus spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1. While entering the stance uses a ki focus spell, the Stunning Fist attack option
it grants is not, so you can use it as often as you like while in the stance.

Vitality-Manipulating Stance: Necromancy, Stance
1 Action somatic; Duration until you leave the stance

You gain a vitality blast ranged unarmed Strike that deals 2d4 negative damage, has a range of 30 feet, and has the unarmed and versatile positive traits.

Additionally, when you use Stunning Fist, instead of saving against being stunned, you can choose to have the target make a Fortitude save with the following results. You must choose to do this before the target rolls its save.

  • Critical Success: The target is unaffected.
  • Success: The target's muscles become temporarily weaker. For 1 round, the target is enfeebled 2, and any time it attempts to take an action with the manipulate trait, the action is disrupted unless it succeeds at a flat check with a DC equal to 5 + its enfeebled value.
  • Failure: As success, but the duration is 1 minute.
  • Critical Failure: As success, but the duration is 1 minute, the flat check is DC 11, and the target takes -10-foot status penalty to all Speeds for the duration.
