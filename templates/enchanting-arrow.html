Enchanting Arrow


7 Points
Warrior; Ranged Weapons

2 Actions; Emotion, Enchantment, Magical, Mental; Prerequisites: Eldritch Archer; Master in Warrior

With a single whisper carried on the wind, you enchant your ammunition to make a foe more vulnerable to your attacks. Make a bow Strike. On a hit, the target takes an additional 2d6 mental damage. On a critical hit, the target also becomes stunned 1.
