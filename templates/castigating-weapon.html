Castigating Weapon


4 Points
spellcaster; Heal Spell
Prerequisites Holy Castigation

The force of your deity's castigation strengthens your body so you can strike down the wicked. After you damage a fiend using a heal spell, your weapon or unarmed Strikes deal extra good damage to fiends equal to the tier of the heal spell until the end of your next turn. This is cumulative with any good damage the weapon already deals.
