Chaotic Elemental Resistance


3 Points
Physiology; Elemental
You gain resistance to a single damage type.

At the beginning of the day roll 1d6:


1- You gain Fire resistance 5 for the day

2- You gain Cold resistance 5 for the day

3- You gain Electricity resistance 5 for the day

4- You gain Sonic resistance 5 for the day

5- You gain negative resistance 5 for the day

6- You gain acid resistance 5 for the day

Boost: (+3 points) You gain the Alter Resistance Reaction:

  • Reaction; Usable once per hour; Requires:
    Chaotic Elemental Resistance;
    Trigger You take one of the other 5 types of damage other than the one you rolled for the day

    The energy resistance you gain from your Chaotic Elemental Resistance changes to match the triggering damage. This change occurs after the damage is dealt, and lasts until you use this ability again or your next daily preparations.
