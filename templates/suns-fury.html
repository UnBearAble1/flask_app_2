Sun's Fury


6 Points
Religion/Warrior; Devotion Spell
Prerequisites: Expert in Religion; Expert in Warrior

You gain the sun's fury focus spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Sun's Fury; Evocation, Good

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range touch; Targets 1 non evil weapon that is unattended or wielded by you or a willing ally; Duration 1 minute

The target weapon becomes wreathed in a glowing flame. The weapon deals an additional 1d4 fire and 1 good damage on a successful Strike. In addition, the flame causes the weapon to glow as bright as a torch. You can Dismiss the spell.
