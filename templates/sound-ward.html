Sound Ward


Cantrip, Abjuration
Traditions:
arcane, occult
Three Actions; material, somatic, verbal
Range:
10 feet, Target: One object no larger than six feet on a side
Duration:
Sustained, up to 10 minutes

You ward an object from generating sound. This could be an item like a crossbow, a creaky door, or a glass bottle you intend to break.
