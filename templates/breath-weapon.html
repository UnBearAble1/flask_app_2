Breath Weapon


6 Points
Natural Abilities; Elemental
3 Actions; Usable Every 1d4 rounds; Arcane, Evocation

You gain a breath weapon. Pick one of the following: a 30-foot line or a 15-foot cone, For damage type, pick: Fire, Cold, Lightning, Poison or Acid upon creation. Your breath weapon does 5d4 damage. Each creature in the area must attempt a basic reflex saving throw. You can't use this ability again for 1d4 rounds.

Boost: (+3 points) Creatures that critically fail their save against your Breath Weapon take 3d4 persistent damage of the type dealt

Boost: (+3 points) Once per day, you can boost your breath weapon's damage to 5d8.
