Disturbing Defense


5 Points
Professional; Oozemorph
Requires: Oozemorph, Legendary in Professional

Your internal organs and blood vessels have shifted and changed, and even you don't know precisely where in your body they are. You gain resistance to precision damage equal to 3 + your number of oozemorph feats and boosts. The DC of your flat checks to recover from persistent bleed damage is also reduced by this number.

Boost (+2 points) Rubbery Skin: Your skin develops a malleable, rubbery quality. Your Disturbing Defense also applies against bludgeoning damage

Boost (+5 Points) Spongey: Your Disturbing Defense resistance applies against extra damage from critical hits, and is even doubled in that case; if the resistance is greater than the extra damage, it reduces the extra damage to 0 but doesn't reduce the attack's normal damage.

Boost (+4 Points) Clot: For a reaction, you can apply your Disturbing Defense resistance against Slashing or Piercing Damage against an attack.
