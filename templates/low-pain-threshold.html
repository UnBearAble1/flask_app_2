Low Pain Threshold


-2 points

PHYSICAL
You are highly susceptible to pain. Anytime you are critically hit for damage, or critically fail a check that results into damage, you must also make a DC 6 Flat Check or be Stunned 1 and Sickened 1 on your next turn due to the pain.

Any time you take damage at all, you must make a DC 8 Flat Check or shout out in pain, possibly disrupting your plans at stealth, and gaining a -1 reaction roll from "Macho" types.
