Avoid System Detection


3 Actions
ENCOUNTER
If a system is on alert, you can avoid detection using the Computers skill.



  • Critical Success: You reduce the alert level by 2.
  • Success: You reduce the alert level by 1.
  • Failure: You increase the alert level by 1.
  • Critical Failure: You increase the alert level by 2.

Alert levels in a system:


  • Passive Alert: The system detects that something is wrong, and depending on the system, may alert an admin or try to detect further automatically.
  • Active Alert: The system detects the intruder, but still must attempt to identify their location to deploy countermeasures.
  • Deploying Countermeasures: The system has identified and located the intruder, and is actively deploying countermeasures.
