Phoenix Bloodline Focus: Shroud of Flame


7 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Expert in Nature; Rejuvenating Flames
You gain the focus spell, Shroud of Flame, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.


Shroud of Flame; Fire, Evocation
2 Actions; somatic, verbal
Area 10-foot emanation; Duration sustained up to 1 minute
You encircle yourself in an aura of protective flames. Each creature that hits you with an unarmed attack, Grapples you, or otherwise touches you takes 5 fire damage. When you Cast the Spell and the first time you sustain it each round, each creature in the area takes 5d6 fire damage (basic Reflex save).

Additionally, when you cast this spell, The primal fire of life and death flows through you or one target. Choose to have either you or a target of the spell gain 4 temporary Hit Points
for 1 round, or to have a target of the spell take 4 fire damage.
(if the spell already deals initial fire damage, combine this with the spell's initial damage before determining weaknesses and resistances).
