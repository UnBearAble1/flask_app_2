Hag Bloodline Focus: You're Mine


5 Points
occultism; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Hag Bloodline Focus: Horrific Visage

You gain the focus spell, You're Mine, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



You're Mine; Emotion, Enchantment, Incapacitation, Mental

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature; Saving Throw Will; Duration 1 round

You manipulate the target's emotions, potentially allowing you to control it for a brief instant. The target must attempt a Will save.

  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target is stunned 1.
  • Failure On the target's next turn, it's stunned 1 and you partially control it, causing it to take a single action of your choice. If it has actions left, it can act normally.
  • Critical Failure The target is controlled for 1 round.



    Additionally, once you cast this spell, The first creature that deals damage to you before the end of your next turn takes 6 mental damage (basic Will save.)
