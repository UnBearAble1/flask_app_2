Silver's Refrain


2 Points
Performance; Composition Spell
Prerequisites: Composition Spells

You learn the silver's refrain composition cantrip, which aids you when fighting devils.



Silver's refrain ; Abjuration, Cantrip, Composition

1 Action; verbal; Area 60-foot emanation; Duration 1 round

The "Song of Silver" was an enchanted song used by the Silver Ravens to battle diabolic forces. You pull from select verses to temporarily replicate some of the legendary song's benefits, albeit on a much smaller scale.



Weapon and unarmed attacks by allies in the area are treated as silver. In addition, this spell automatically attempts to counteract any teleportation spell or ability cast by a devil within the area or cast by a devil attempting to enter. If a devil attempts to enter the area using a teleportation effect and the spell is counteracted, the devil remains where it started.
