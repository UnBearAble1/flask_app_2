Tempest Surge


8 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Expert in Nature

You gain the tempest surge order spell, and you increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1



Tempest Surge; Air, Electricity, Evocation

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature; Saving Throw Reflex

You surround a foe in a swirling storm of violent winds, roiling clouds, and crackling lightning. The storm deals 5d12 electricity damage. The target must attempt a basic Reflex save. On a failure, the target also is clumsy 2 for 1 round and takes 5 persistent electricity damage.
