Zombie Hulk


Threat: 6

These towering horrors are animated from the corpses of monstrosities.

Recall Knowledge - Undead (Religion)

NE, Huge, Mindless, Undead, Zombie, Brute
Perception +8; darkvision
Skills: Athletics +12
Str +7, Dex -1, Con +4, Int -5, Wis +0, Cha -2
Slow: A zombie is permanently slowed 1 and can't use reactions.
AC 19; Fort +11, Ref +7, Will +9
HP 120 (negative healing); Immunities death effects, disease, mental, paralyzed, poison, unconscious; Weaknesses positive 10, slashing 10
Speed 25 feet

Actions

  • Hunk of Meat: Melee Single Action +9 [+4/-1] (reach 15 feet), Damage 2d12+6 bludgeoning
  • Wide Swing: Single Action; The zombie hulk makes a hunk of meat Strike and compares the attack roll result to the AC of up to two foes, each of whom must be within the Strike's reach. This attack counts as two attacks for the zombie hulk's multiple attack penalty.
  • Thrown Corpse: Ranged Single Action 9 [+4/-1] (brutal, range increment 30 feet), Damage 2d8+6 bludgeoning
  • Corpse Throwing: A zombie hulk can throw bodies at foes. If the zombie hulk has a creature grabbed from a grapple, it can use the Thrown Corpse ranged action to hurl the creature at another creature. The thrown creature makes a reflex save, regardless of the result of the Thrown Corpse attack roll. On a failure, the thrown creature takes half the Thrown Corpse damage and falls prone.
