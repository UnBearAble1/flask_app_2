Ancestral Friend


1 Point
Ancestral Relationships, Enemies / Allies
When you arrive at a new settlement, you automatically find the nearest enclave for your ancestry by spending 1 day of downtime searching, provided one exists and its members aren't actively hiding from you. Once you've made contact, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Gather Information and Earn Income in the settlement.



Additionally,
you can immediately attempt a
Diplomacy
check to
Make an Impression
on members of your ancestry rather than needing to converse for 1 minute. You take a -5 penalty to the check. If you fail, you can engage in 1 minute of conversation and attempt a new check at the end of that time rather than accepting the failure or critical failure result. If you have the
Glad-Hand
feat, you don't take the penalty on your immediate Diplomacy check if the target is of your ancestry.
