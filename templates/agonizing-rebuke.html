Agonizing Rebuke


5 Points
Cultural Training; Intimidation
Requires: Expert in Intimidation

When you successfully Demoralize a foe, that foe takes 1d4 mental damage at the start of each of its turns as long as it remains frightened and continues to engage in combat with you. If you have master proficiency in Intimidation, the damage increases to 1d6, and if you have legendary proficiency, the damage increases to 1d8.
