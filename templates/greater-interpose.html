Greater Interpose


4 Points
Warrior; Armor
Prerequisite: Sacrifice Armor; Master in Warrior

You can use Sacrifice Armor to reduce any type of damage from an attack or effect requiring a Reflex save. Additionally, When you use Sacrifice Armor against a critical hit or a critical failure on a Reflex save that would otherwise cause you to take double damage, you take the normal amount of damage instead (and that amount is still reduced).
