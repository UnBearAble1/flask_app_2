Undead Bloodline Focus: Undeath's Blessing


3 Points
Religion; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Expert at Religion

You gain the focus spell, Undeath's Blessing, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Undeath's Blessing; Necromancy, Negative

1 Action ; somatic; Range touch; Targets 1 living creature touched; Saving Throw Will; Duration 1 minute

You instill within a creature the touch of the grave. For the duration, harm spells treat the creature as undead and heal spells treat the creature as living. In addition, harm spells gain a +4 status bonus to the Hit Points restored to the target. If the target wishes to avoid the spell, it can attempt a Will save to reduce the effects.

  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target heals half as much from heal and takes half as much damage from harm for 1 round.
  • Failure Effects as described in the text.



    Additionally, Necromantic energy flows through you. Gain 3 temporary hit points until the end of your next turn.
