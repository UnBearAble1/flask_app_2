Invidiak


Threat: 6

For most demons, possessing a creature requires a ritual, magic item, or condition, but for invidiaks, who lack a body, possession is akin to donning a suit of clothing. Known also as shadow demons, invidiaks are formed from envious mortal souls.

Recall Knowledge - Fiend (Religion)

CE, Medium, Demon, Fiend, Incorporeal, Controller
Perception +9; darkvision
Languages Abyssal, Common; telepathy 100 feet
Skills Acrobatics +10, Deception +11, Religion +6, Society +8, Stealth +10
Str -5, Dex +4, Con +3, Int +2, Wis +2, Cha +5
AC 18; Fort +7 , Ref +9 , Will +11; +1 to all saves vs. magic
HP 36; Immunities disease, poison, precision; Resistances all 5 (except force, ghost touch, or positive; double resistance vs. non-magical); Weaknesses cold iron 5, good 5
Speed fly 30 feet

Features:

  • Exorcism Vulnerability: If an invidiak attempts to possess a creature and the creature rolls a critical success on its saving throw to resist possession, or if an invidiak's possession of a creature is ended prematurely, such as via a successful dispel magic, the invidiak takes 6d6 mental damage and cannot use possession for 24 hours.
  • Favored Host: Each invidiak has a particular type of creature they prefer possessing, chosen from the following list: aberration, animal, beast, or any humanoid (such as dwarf)--in most cases, the favored type of target is human. When an invidiak uses possession against a favored host, the duration of possession increases to unlimited, and the effect lasts until dispelled or until the invidiak dismisses it. If you need to determine what type it is randomly, roll 1d6:


    • 1-2: Human
    • 3: Aberration
    • 4: Animal
    • 5: Beast
    • 6: Another humanoid type (whatever is most convenient for the situation)
  • Unbodied Possession: An invidiak leaves no body behind when using possession.

Actions:

  • Melee Single Action claw +8 (+4 / +0) (agile, evil, finesse, magical), Damage 2d4 cold plus 2d6 evil
  • Divine Innate Spells DC 21

    • Legendary: possession (range touch);
    • Master: dimension door, shadow blast;
    • Expert: darkness, dimension door (at will); fear
    • Cantrips:
      telekinetic projectile
  • Shadow Blend; Two Actions (divine, illusion) The invidiak Strides up to twice their speed. If the invidiak ends their movement in an area of dim light, they become hidden as long as they don't move and the lighting in the area they're in doesn't grow brighter than dim light.
