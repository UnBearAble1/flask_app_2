Frost Vial


Alchemical, Bomb, Cold, Consumable, Splash
Usage
held in 1 hand;
Bulk
L
Activate
Single Action,
Strike

The liquid reagents in this vial rapidly absorb heat when exposed to air. A frost vial deals the listed cold damage and cold splash damage. On a hit, the target takes a status penalty to its Speeds until the end of its next
turn.

TRAINED:
Cost: 5 gold
The bomb deals 2d6 cold damage and 2 cold splash damage, and the target takes a -5-foot penalty.

Cost: 20 gold
The bomb deals 3d6 cold damage and 3 cold splash damage, and the target takes a -10-foot penalty.

MASTER:

LEGENDARY:
