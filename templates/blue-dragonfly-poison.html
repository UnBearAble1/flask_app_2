Blue Dragonfly Poison


TRAINED
Alchemical, Consumable, Ingested, Poison
Price 7 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate Single Action Interact

Boggards brew a potent toxin made from blue dragonflies. Swampseers consume this mixture to awaken their divine powers, but the poison inspires crippling hallucinations in most other creatures.

Saving Throw DC 16 Fortitude; Onset 10 minutes; Maximum Duration 30 minutes;

  • Stage 1 dazzled (10 minutes);
  • Stage 2 dazzled and frightened 1 (10 minutes);
  • Stage 3 frightened 1 and confused 1 (1 minute)
