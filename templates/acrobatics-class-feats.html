Acrobatics Class Feats


BALANCE


  • Steady Balance (T) -1 pt: Improved success on balance, not flat-footed while balancing, and can use acrobatics to Grab an Edge.

COMBAT MOBILITY


  • Daring Act [T/T] -4 pts: Stride w/o triggering reactions from target, leave them flat-footed against your next strike


    • Daredevil's Gambit [E/E] -4 pts: Also enter target's space, getting +2 AC against them, they are flat-footed against all your attacks, and attacks against you may hit them
    • Daring Flourish [T/T] -3 pts: You get an attack as part of daring act
  • Furious Sprint [M/T] -3 pts: Stride five times your speed for 2 actions, or 8x for 3.
  • Light Step [M] -7 pts: Your strides and steps ignore difficult terrain
  • Swift Elusion [M] -5 pts: Reaction stride adjacent to attacking foe, or move them
  • Vexing Tumble [E/T] -4 pts: Stride half speed and possibly make foe flat-footed

DEFENSIVE MOVES


  • Acrobatic Dodge (E) -3 pts: Reaction to gain +1 to AC and step after a strike misses
  • Defensive Roll (L) -7 pts: Once every 10 min, take 1/2 dmg from an attack that would drop you to 0 hit points
  • Dodging Roll (M) -6 pts: Reaction to gain resistance 10 against area effect, then step
  • Freedom Domain: Unimpeded Stride (M) -4 pts: Gain a focus spell to escape magical restraints
  • Freedom Domain: Word fo Freedom
    (M) -3 pts: Gain a focus spell to suppress restrictive conditions on allies for 1 round

ESCAPE


  • Contortionist (T) -1 pt: Creatures you escape from are flat-footed against you

FALLING


  • Cat Fall (E) -1 pt: Treat falls as 20 feet shorter
  • Dancing Leaf (M) -4 pts: No damage from falls near walls, +5 jump distance
  • Masterful Cat Fall (M) -2 pts: Treat falls as 50 feet shorter
  • Legendary Cat Fall (L) -4 pts: Take no damage from falls, always land on feet
  • Impressive Landing (T) -2 pts: reaction to damage area you fall into

FLIGHT


  • Aerobatic Mastery (T) -1 pt: Get +2 to maneuver in flight, can combine two maneuvers

PRONE OR CRAWLING


  • Prone Fighter (L) -3 pts: Not flat footed while prone
  • Kip-Up (E) -2 pts: Stand up without triggering reactions
  • Nimble Crawl
    (T) -1 pts: crawl half your speed instead of 5 feet
  • Masterful Crawl (M) -2 pts: crawl speed equal to your land speed

SKILL SUBSTITUTION


  • Acrobatic Performer (M) -2 pts: Can use acrobatics to perform
  • Graceful Leaper (M) -3 pts: Can use acrobatics to high and long jump

SPECIAL TERRAIN


  • Cloud Walk (L) -7 pts: Stride across water, clouds, traps, other weight-based limiters
  • Feather Step (E) -4 pts: Step into difficult terrain
  • Shake it Up (T) -2 pts: Attempt to force foe to fall when both of you are balancing
  • Travel Domain: Agile Feet (M) -2 pts: Gain a focus spell to give speed bump and ignore difficult terrain
  • Travel Domain: Traveler's Transit (M) -7 pts: Gain a focus spell to get climb or swim speeds
  • Water Step (M) -3 pts: Stride across water

SQUEEZE


  • Quick Squeeze (T) -1 pt: Squeeze at 5 feet per round
  • Legendary Squeeze (L) -3 pts: Squeeze at full speed
  • Implausible Infiltration (L) -16 pts: Squeeze through solid walls and floors

TUMBLE THROUGH


  • Acrobat Dedication (T) -1 pt: Ignore difficult terrain when tumbling through on crit
  • Tumbling Opportunist (E)-1 pt: Try to trip foe you tumble through
  • Tumble Behind (E) -3 pts: If you tumble through, foe is flat footed
  • Tumbling Strike (E) -5 pts: An all around improved version of tumble-through + attack
