Personal Rain Cloud


TRAINED

Evocation, Water
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions ; somatic, verbal
Range 60 feet; Targets 1 creature
Saving Throw Reflex; Duration 1 minute

You conjure a 5-foot-wide rain cloud that follows the target wherever it goes. It stays roughly an arm's length overhead, unless it must drift lower to fit under a ceiling. The cloud rains constantly on the target, keeping it wet and dampening the ground in the wake of any movement. The rain extinguishes non-magical flames. The target gains fire resistance 4. Creatures with weakness to water take damage equal to their weakness at the end of each of their turns. Creatures can attempt a Reflex save to avoid the cloud.

Heightened:
The amount of fire resistance increases by 4.
