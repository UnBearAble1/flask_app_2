Ongoing Strategy


4 Points
Warrior; Strategem
Prerequisites: Devise a Strategem, Master in Warrior

You're constantly studying small aspects of everyone's movements, even if you don't have a stratagem in place ahead of time. On any Strike for which you didn't Devise a Stratagem, you still deal +3 Precision Damage so long as the weapon or unarmed attack you used is one that would have let you use your Intelligence modifier had you Devised a Stratagem.
