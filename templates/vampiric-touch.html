Vampiric Touch


EXPERT
Death, Necromancy, Negative
Traditions arcane, divine, occult
Two Actions ; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 living creature
Saving Throw basic Fortitude

Your touch leeches the lifeblood out of a target to empower yourself. You deal 6d10 negative damage to the target. You gain temporary Hit Points equal to half the negative damage the target takes (after applying resistances and the like). You lose any remaining temporary Hit Points after 1 minute. You cannot cast this spell again as long as you have any temporary hit points remaining from a previous casting.

Heightened: The damage increases by 3d10
