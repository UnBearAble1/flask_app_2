Slow


EXPERT
Transmutation
Traditions arcane, occult, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Saving Throw Fortitude; Duration 1 minute

You dilate the flow of time around the target, slowing its actions.


  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target is slowed 1 for 1 round.
  • Failure The target is slowed 1 for 1 minute. They can attempt a fortitude save at the end of every round after the first, ending the effect on a success.
  • Critical Failure The target is slowed 2 for 1 minute. They can attempt a fortitude save at the end of every round after the first, reducing the condition to slowed 1 on a success. At the end of each round afterward, they can attempt the same thing to remove the condition altogether.

Heightened (Master) You can target up to 10 creatures.
