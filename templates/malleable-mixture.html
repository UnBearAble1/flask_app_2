Malleable Mixture


Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir
Usage
held in 1 hand;
Bulk
L
Activate
Single Action,
Interact

Your bones, muscles, and organs become vastly softer and more pliable. You can fit into and through small or narrow spaces as though you were smaller than your normal size, and you gain an item bonus to your Fortitude and Reflex DCs against attempts to
Grapple,
Shove, or
Trip
you.

EXPERT:
Cost: 20 gold
You can fit into and through small or narrow spaces as though you were 1 size smaller than your normal size (minimum Small). The bonus to your DCs is +2, and the duration is 1 minute.

MASTER:
Cost: 50 gold
You can fit into and through small or narrow spaces as though you were 2 sizes smaller than your normal size (minimum Tiny). The bonus to your DCs is +3, and the duration is 10 minutes.
