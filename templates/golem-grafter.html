Golem Grafter


13 points
Alchemist; Golem Grafter
Prerequisites: Expert in Alchemist

Your flesh has been specially treated with the same arcane and alchemical processes used to toughen the skin of flesh golems. Increase your maximum Hit Points by 5. You gain resistance to physical damage (except adamantine) equal to your number of golem grafter feats.
