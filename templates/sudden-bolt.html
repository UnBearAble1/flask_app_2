Sudden Bolt


TRAINED

Uncommon, Electricity, Evocation
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 60 feet; Targets 1 creature or object
Saving Throw basic Reflex

Heightened: The damage increases by 2d12.
