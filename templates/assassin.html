Assassin


Threat 7

NE, Medium, Humanoid, Lurker
Perception +9
Languages Common
Skills Acrobatics +10, Athletics +5, Deception +9, Diplomacy +2, Intimidation +2, Medicine +6, Society +4, Stealth +11, Survival +4, Thievery +7, Underworld Lore +6
Str +3, Dex +5, Con +2, Int +2, Wis +2, Cha +0
AC 20; Fort +8, Ref +12, Will +10
HP 70
Speed: 30 feet

Features:

  • Swift Sneak:
    The assassin can move their full Speed when Sneaking.
  • Deny Advantage:
    The assassin isn't flat-footed to creatures of threat 8 or lower that are hidden, undetected, flanking, or using surprise attack.
  • Sneak Attack:
    The assassin deals an extra 2d6 precision damage to flat-footed creatures.
  • Surprise Attack:
    On the first round of combat, creatures that haven't acted yet are flat-footed to the assassin.
  • Scoundrel: When the assassin feints, on a success the target is flat-footed against their attacks until the end of their next turn.

Reactions:

  • Nimble Dodge


    • Trigger The assassin is targeted with a melee or ranged attack by an attacker they can see;
    • Effect The assassin gains a +2 circumstance bonus to AC and DR 5 against the triggering attack.
  • Ready Shot:


    • Trigger: The assassin has waited in ambush, and readied an action to fire at an unsuspecting target AND the target is in the square the assassin predicted.
    • Effect: The assassin make a strike with advantage.

Actions:

  • 1 Action: Melee rapier +11 [+6/+1] (deadly 1d8, disarm, finesse, magical), Damage 2d6+7 piercing
  • 1 Action: Ranged composite shortbow +11 [+6/+1] (deadly 2d10, magical, propulsive, range increment 60 feet, reload 0), Damage 2d6+7 piercing
  • 1 Action: Poison Weapon
    (manipulate) Requirements The assassin is wielding a piercing or slashing weapon and has a free hand; Effect The assassin applies a poison to the weapon.
  • 1 Action: Quick Draw
    The assassin Interacts to draw a weapon, then Strikes with that weapon.
  • 1 Action: Tumble Through: When the assassin tumbles through (acrobatics vs Reflex) they leave their foes flat footed, and don't provoke attacks of opportunity.

Known Poisons:

  • Hunting Spider Venom x3
  • Gorgon's Breath x 2
