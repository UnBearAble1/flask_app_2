Sap Life


3 points
spellcaster; Harm Spell
Prerequisites: Trained in spellcaster; Healing

You draw the life force out of your enemies to heal your own wounds. When you cast a harm spell and damage at least one living creature, you regain Hit Points equal to twice the tier of your harm spell. If you aren't a living creature, you gain no benefit from this feat.
