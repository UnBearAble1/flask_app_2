Noisemaker Snare


TRAINED
Uncommon, Clockwork, Consumable, Fire, Mechanical, Snare, Trap
Price 6 gp

When a creature enters this snare's square, it triggers an extremely loud clockwork device, which explodes with a bang that can be heard from 200 feet away and deals 2d6 fire damage. The creature must attempt a DC 16 Reflex save.


  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature takes half damage.
  • Failure The creature takes full damage and is deafened for 1 round.
  • Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and is deafened for 1 minute.
