Undeath Domain: Touch of Undeath


3 Points
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Religion

Your magic carries close ties to the undead. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Touch of Undeath; Necromancy, Negative

1 Action somatic; Range touch; Targets 1 living creature; Saving Throw Fortitude

You attack the target's life force with undeath, dealing 5d6 negative damage. The target must attempt a Fortitude save.

  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target takes half damage.
  • Failure The target takes full damage, and positive effects heal it only half as much as normal for 1 round.
  • Critical Failure The target takes double damage, and positive effects heal it only half as much as normal for 1 minute.
