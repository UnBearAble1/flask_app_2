Ravening Maw


MASTER
Evil, Healing, Necromancy
Traditions divine, occult, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 60 feet; Targets one living creature
Saving Throw Will; Duration 3 rounds

The target becomes overwhelmed with cannibalistic hunger and can restore its vitality by feasting on the flesh of the fallen. On its turn, the target can use 2 actions (these actions have the manipulate trait) to cannibalize an adjacent dying creature, or one that has died within the last minute. If the cannibalized creature is dying, it dies. If the cannibalized creature is an ally of the target, the target regains 5d8 HP; otherwise, the target regains 5d4 HP. Certain monsters (such as those whose blood is acidic or magical) might inflict additional effects on those who cannibalize them at the GM's discretion. Once cannibalized, a particular creature's body doesn't have enough meat to cannibalize again. During any round there is an applicable subject for cannibalization that the target is aware of in the area that the target can't or doesn't cannibalize, the target takes 5d8 mental damage.

Heightened: The amount of HP restored by cannibalizing a creature increases by 1d8 for eating an ally and 1d4 otherwise, and the mental damage increases by 2d8.
