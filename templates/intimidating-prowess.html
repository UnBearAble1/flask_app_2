Intimidating Prowess


1 Point
Intimidation; Both Coerce and Demoralize
Prerequisites Strength +3; expert in Intimidation

In situations where you can physically menace the target when you Coerce or Demoralize, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your Intimidation check and you ignore the penalty for not sharing a language. If your Strength score is +4 or higher and you are a master in Intimidation, this bonus increases to +2.
