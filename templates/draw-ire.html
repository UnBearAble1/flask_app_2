Draw Ire


TRAINED

Emotion, Enchantment, Mental
Traditions arcane, occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 120 feet; Targets 1 creature
Saving Throw Will; Duration 1 minute

You cause mental distress to a creature, goading it to strike back at you. You deal 3d8 mental damage to the creature and cause it to take a -1 status penalty to attack rolls and reduce its damage one dice category, against creatures other than you. The creature must attempt a Will saving throw.


  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target takes half damage and the penalty. The spell ends at the end of the target's next turn.
  • Failure The target takes full damage and the penalty.
  • Critical Failure The target takes double damage, and the status penalty is -2.

Heightened: The damage increases by 2d8.
