Vow


-1 to -3 points

MENTAL, LAWFUL
You have sworn an oath to do something, and aim to do it at all costs. The point value of this complication is based on how inconvenient this is for you. Here are some examples:


  • Minor Vow: [-1 pt] Silence during daylight hours; vegetarianism; chastity; use no edged weapons
  • Major Vow: [-2 pts] Keep silence at all times; never sleep indoors; own no more than your horse can carry.
  • Great Vow: [-3 pts] Never refuse any request for aid; always fight with the wrong hand; hunt a given foe until you destroy him; challenge every knight you meet to combat.

Note that if you could represent your Vow using another disadvantage, you only get points for one of the two disadvantages (your choice). No one may get points for Vow (Never kill) and Pacifism (Cannot Kill), etc.

If your Vow ends because you fulfill it or it had a built in time limit, you can either buy it off with points or take another vow of equal value.
