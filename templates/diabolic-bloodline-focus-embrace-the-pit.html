Diabolic Bloodline Focus: Embrace the Pit


4 Points
Religion; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Diabolic Bloodline Focus: Diabolic Edict

You gain the focus spell, Embrace the Pit, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Embrace the Pit; Evil; Morph; Transmutation

1 Action; verbal; Duration 1 minute

Devil horns grow from your skull, and your skin takes on features of the devil responsible for your diabolic bloodline. You gain resistance 5 to evil, fire, and poison, and resistance 1 to physical damage (except silver). You can take good damage, even if you aren't evil, and you gain weakness 5 to good damage.



Additionally, immediately upon casting this spell, until the end of your next turn, Blood Magic Hellfire s fills your tongue with lies. You gain a +1 status bonus to Deception checks
