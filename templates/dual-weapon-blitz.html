Dual Weapon Blitz


2 Points
Warrior; Two Weapon Fighting

2 Actions; Requirements You are wielding two one-handed melee weapons, each in a different hand; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior

You attack as you dash among foes. Stride up to your Speed. At any point during this movement, you can Strike once with each of the two required weapons. These Strikes can be against the same or different targets, as you see fit. Apply your multiple attack penalty to the Strikes normally.
