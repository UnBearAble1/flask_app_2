Summon Construct


TRAINED

Conjuration
Traditions arcane
Three Actions; material, somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

You conjure a construct to fight for you.
This works like summon animal, except you summon one of the creatures below, instead:


  • Animated Statue
  • Necrophidius
  • Soulbound Doll

Heightened (expert): You may instead summon one of the following:


  • Dig-Widget
  • Terra-Cotta Soldier
  • Scarecrow

Heightened (master): You may instead summon one of the following:


  • Giant Animated Statue
  • Levaloch
  • Tupilaq

Heightened (legendary): You may instead summon one of the following:


  • Animated Furnace
  • Swordkeeper
  • Spiral Centurion
