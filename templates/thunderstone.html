Thunderstone


Alchemical, Bomb, Sonic, Consumable, Splash
Usage
held in 1 hand;
Bulk
L
Activate
Single Action,
Strike

When this stone hits a creature or a hard surface, it explodes with a deafening bang. A thunderstone deals the listed sonic damage and sonic splash damage, and each creature within 10 feet of the space in which the stone exploded must succeed at a Fortitude saving throw with the listed DC or be deafened until the end of its next turn.

TRAINED:
Cost: 5 gold
The bomb deals 2d4 sonic damage and 2 sonic splash damage, and the DC is 16.

EXPERT:
Cost: 20 gold
The bomb deals 3d4 sonic damage and 3 sonic splash damage, and the DC is 18.

MASTER:
Cost: 50 gold
The bomb deals 4d4 sonic damage and 4 sonic splash damage, and the DC is 20.

LEGENDARY:
Cost: 85 gold
The bomb deals 5d4 sonic damage and 5 sonic splash damage, and the DC is 22.
