Untwisting Iron Buffer


10 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts
Prerequisites: Master in Warrior

You gain the Untwisting Iron Buffer ki spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Untwisting Iron Buffer; Abjuration

1 Action, verbal
(Special; This Ki power takes 2 focus points to cast)

You send currents of protective ki to the surface of your body that protect you from harm, at least until your enemies dissipate them. You gain 25 temporary Hit Points, which last for up to 3 rounds.
