Combat Flexibility


12 Points
Warrior; Feat Versatility
Prerequisites: Master in Warrior

When you make your daily preparations, you gain one feat that relies on a warrior skill no more than one tier one lower than yours, and that costs 10 points or less, that you don't already have but meet the prerequisites for. You can use that feat until your next daily preparations. You must meet all of the feat's other prerequisites.



Boost (+3 Points) Ultimate Flexibility: you can adapt to the battlefield's challenges by spending 1 hour to train. If you do, you can reselect the feats chosen with combat flexibility as if you had made your daily preparations. You can't trade out limited-use abilities that you've already used.
