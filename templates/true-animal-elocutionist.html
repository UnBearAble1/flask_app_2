True Animal Elocutionist


8 Points
Natural Abilities; Animal Kinsmanship
You can ask questions of, receive answers from, and use the Diplomacy skill with all animals. You also gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Make an Impression on these animals (which usually uses the Diplomacy Skill.) A broad group of this type (such as in the animal class elocutionist feat) also have an indifferent or friendly starting attitude toward you and give you time to make your case, though the others react to you like any other adventurer.
