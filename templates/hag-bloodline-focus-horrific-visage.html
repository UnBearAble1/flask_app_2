Hag Bloodline Focus: Horrific Visage


7 Points
occultism; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Hag Bloodline Focus: Jealous Hex, Master in occultism

You gain the focus spell, Horrific Visage, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Emotion, Fear, Illusion, Mental, Visual

2 Actions; somatic, verbal; Area 30-foot-radius emanation centered on you; Saving Throw Will

You briefly transform your features into the horrific visage of a hag, striking fear into your enemies. Foes in the area must attempt a Will save.


  • Success The foe is frightened 1.
  • Failure The foe is frightened 2.
  • Critical Failure The foe is frightened 3 and Fleeing 1.

At the start of each of your turns, the target can attempt another Will save, ending the effect on a success. Additionally, once you cast this spell, The first creature that deals damage to you before the end of your next turn takes 6 mental damage (basic Will save.)
