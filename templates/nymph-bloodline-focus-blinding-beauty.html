Nymph Bloodline Focus: Blinding Beauty


7 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Nymph Bloodline Focus: Nymph's Token

You gain the focus spell, Blinding Beauty, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Blinding Beauty; Emotion, Enchantment, Incapacitation,Visual

1 Action; somatic; Area 30-foot cone; Saving Throw Will

You channel the allure of your nymph bloodline into a terribly beautiful glance. Each enemy in the area must attempt a Will saving throw.

  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature is dazzled until the start of its next turn.
  • Failure The creature is blinded until the start of its next turn.
  • Critical Failure The creature is blinded for 1 minute.



    Additionally, when you cast this spell and until the end of your next turn, Nymph grace distracts your foes, imposing a -1 status penalty on one target's Will saves for 1 round.
