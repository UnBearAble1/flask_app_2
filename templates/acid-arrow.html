Acid Arrow


TRAINED

Acid, Attack, Evocation
Traditions: arcane, primal
Two Actions somatic, verbal
Range 120 feet; Targets 1 creature

You conjure an arrow of acid that continues corroding the target after it hits. Make a spell attack against the target. On a hit, you deal 6d6 acid damage plus 1d6 persistent acid damage. On a critical hit, double the initial damage, but not the persistent damage.

Heightened:
The initial damage increases by 3d6, and the persistent acid damage increases by 1d6.
