Ferocious Beasts


3 Points
Cultural Training; Animal Kinsmanship
Prerequisites Beast Tamer or animal companion; Ferocity

Animal companions or bonded animals you have gain the Ferocity feat, and they gain a reaction they can use only for Ferocity. If you have the Undying Ferocity ancestry feat, all animal companions or bonded animals you have also gain the benefits of that feat when using the Ferocity reaction.
