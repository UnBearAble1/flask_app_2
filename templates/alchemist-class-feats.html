Alchemist Class Feats


BOMBS


  • Artokus's Fire (M) -5 pts: Once per round, add damage to alchemist fire
  • Astonishing Explosive (E) -4 pts: Once per min, add fascination/stun to bombs
  • Bomb Maker (T) -3+ pts: Gain multiple benefits to crafting bombs
  • Debilitating Bomb (E) -5+ pts: Once per round, inflict conditions with bombs
  • Splash Control (E) -2+ pts: Expand your control of a bomb's splash area
  • Demolition Charge (E) -4 pts: Rig 1-4 bombs to explode on trigger
  • Exploitive Bomb (E) -3 pts: Once a round, make a bomb overcome resistances
  • Far Lobber (T) -3+ pts: Throw bombs farther, maybe more accurately
  • Mega Bomb (M) -8 pts: Once a minute, make a huge area bomb with no attack
  • Quick Bomber (T) -3 pts: Draw a bomb and throw it with one action
  • Smoke Bomb (T) -4 pts: Once a round cause bomb to create concealment
  • Sticky Bomb (M) -8 pts: Once a round, add splash damage to persistent damage

ELIXERS


  • Combine Elixirs (E) -5 pts: Once a round, mix two elixirs together you create
  • Extend Elixir (T) -5+ pts: Extend duration of some Elixirs

GOLEM GRAFTER


  • Golem Grafter (E) -13 pts: Gain +5 HP and +1 DR, and +2 HP per Golem Grafter feat, and +1 additional DR if you have all four Golem Grafter feats.


    • Accursed Clay Fist (E) -4 pts: Gain 2d8 dmg fist that gives penalty against curses
    • Iron Lung (E) -1 pt: Better success on inhaled poison save, and gain ability to breathe out poisons you've recently inhaled
    • Legs of Stone (E) -2 pts: Better resist shove and trip, gain shove options
    • Quicken Heartbeat (E) -4 pts: Action to reduce Slow or add Quickened

HEALING


  • Alchemical Healer (T) -6+ pts: Gain benefits around healing
  • Healing Bomb (M) -5 pts: Once a round, throw an elixir like a bomb to heal
  • Merciful Elixir (E) -6+ pts: Once a round, Elixer of Life can maybe heal conditions
  • Miracle Worker (L) -15 pts: Once a min, your high level elixir may resurrect
  • Supreme Surgical Prodigy (L) -15 pts: Your elixirs of life heal max hit points

META ALCHEMY


  • Alchemical Alacrity (M) -7 pts: You craft an extra item with quick alchemy
  • Alchemical Crafting Efficiency (M) -2 pts: Craft extra in downtime
  • Efficient Alchemy (M) -15 pts: You craft an extra item with advanced alchemy
  • Enduring Alchemy (E) -5 pts: Quick Alchemy items remain potent for a round
  • Persistent Alchemy (M) -10 pts: Can make an item's effect last a day
  • Unstable Concoction (E) -2 pts: Make a risky item that does 50% better

MUTAGEN


  • Elastic Mutagen (T) -2 pts: Quicksilver mutagens give you added benefits
  • Feral Mutagen (M) -5 pts: Bestial mutagens give you added benefits
  • Genius Mutagen (L) -15 pts: Cognitive mutagens give you great added benefits
  • Glib Mutagen (M) -3 pts: Silvertongue mutagens give you added benefits
  • Invincible Mutagen (E) -5 pts: Juggernaut mutagens give you added benefits
  • Mindblank Mutagen (M) -10 pts: Serene mutagens give you added benefits
  • Mutagenist (T) -6+ pts: Gain two mutagen formulas, once a day regain mutagen benefit
  • Mutagenic Discovery (E) 5 pts: Can use two mutagens simultaneously
  • Revivifying Mutagen (E) -8 pts: Can use Expert or higher tier mutagens to heal yourself

POISON


  • Chemical Contagion (M) -4 pts: Reaction to poison second target when 1st crit fails
  • Poison Weapon (T) -3+ pts: Can draw/apply poison with 1 action, get free poisons
  • Poison Coat (E) -4 pts: Reaction to poison a melee attacker with poisoned clothing
  • Poisoner's Twist (T) -3 pts: Action to deal 2d6 damage to previously poisoned target
  • Poisonous Deluge (L) -7 pts: You gain a focus spell that lets you spread 3 contact poisons over an area with an instantaneous effect
  • Shaped Contaminant (T) -2 pts: Can fill inhaled poisons in a line instead of cube
  • Sticky Poison (E) -3 pts: Flat check to not lose poison on strike
  • Subtle Delivery (T) -1 pt: Can use blowgun to deliver poison
  • Tenacious Toxins (T) -3 pts:
    Duration of your poisons increase by 1st stage interval amount
  • Toxicologist (T) -5+ pts: Gain numerous benefits when crafting poisons
  • Toxilogical Discovery (E) -5 pts: Can combine two injury poisons into one
