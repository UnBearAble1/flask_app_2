Long Lived


1 Point
Physiology; Age
Your people can live up to 750 years of age. In addition to the narrative benefits, you gain the following benefits as well:

  • When you critically fail on a Lore check (excluding all-encompassing lores such as Bardic lore) you fail instead. If you succeed, you critically succeed instead.
  • If you are untrained in a Recall Knowledge skill, treat it as +0 instead of -4, and you can attempt checks that require you to be trained.
