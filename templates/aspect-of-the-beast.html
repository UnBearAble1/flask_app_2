Aspect of the Beast


9 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Expert in Nature

You gain the Aspect of the Beast focus spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Aspect of the Beast; Morph; Transmutation

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Duration 5 minutes

You gain Low-light vision and imprecise scent 30 feet. You also gain speeds based on the type of animal you choose to take on the aspects of:

-- Ape Speed 25 feet, climb Speed 20 feet

-- Deer Speed 50 feet

-- Frog Speed 25 feet, swim Speed 25 feet

-- Shark swim Speed 35 feet (No land speed)

-- Snake Speed 20 feet, climb Speed 20 feet, swim Speed 20 feet
