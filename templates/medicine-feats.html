Medicine Feats


CRAFT ITEMS


  • Medical Researcher [T] -1 pts: Use medicine to craft healing items

FOCUS SPELLS


  • Plague Domain: Divine Plagues [M] -7 pts: Focus spell inflicts target within 15 feet with a condition-inflicting disease
  • Plague Domain: Foul Miasma [M] -3 pts: Focus spell possibly spreads diseases inflicting target into a 15 foot cloud
  • Soothing Mist [M] -6 pts: Focus spell heals 5d8, ends persistent damage, damages undead

INVESTIGATION


  • Forensic Acumen [T] -2 pts: Use medicine to investigate a body for clues

MOBILITY


  • Rapid Response [T] -2 pts: Reaction to stride with +10 toward fallen ally
  • Ward Medic [E] -5 pts: Treat wounds/disease on more than one target simultaneously

REMOVE CONDITIONS


  • Advanced Medicine [M] -4 pts: First aid can reduce frightened or sickened
  • Emergency Medical Assistance [M] -3 pts: 2 Actions to greatly help end persistent damage
  • Keep It Together! [M] -8 pts:
    2 actions attempt to end ally's fascinated, frightened, stunned, or stupefied conditions
  • Legendary Medic [L] -10 pts: 1 hour to attempt to cure blinded, deafened, doomed, or drained
  • Treat Condition [E] -4 pts: 2 Actions to attempt to end clumsy, enfeebled, or sickened

    • Holistic Care [E] -7 pts: Add frightened, stupefied, and stunned

TREAT DISEASE AND POISON


  • Inoculation [T] -1 pt: Those you treat against bonuses against same disease
  • Robust Recovery [E] -1 pt: When you treat disease/poison, target gets enhanced bonuses

ADMINISTER FIRST AID


  • Battle Medicine [T] -7 pts: You can treat wounds in combat


    • Paragon Battle Medicine [M] -10 pts: Your battle medicine can also reduce sickened, enfeebled or clumsy, possibly frightened, stunned, stupefied, or drained as well.
    • Doctor's Visitation [E] -3 pts: Stride and use Battle Medicine for 1 action, or first aid / treat condition for 2 actions
    • Superior Medic [E] -5 pts: Battle Medicine gives another 1d10 hp.
    • Godless Healing [T] -5 pts: You get 5 more HP, 6 hour immunity when Battle Medicine is used on you


      • Mortal Healing [T] -5 pts: When you treat wounds to creatures who haven't received divine healing, heal 1d8 extra
  • Continual Recovery [E] -4 pts: Administer First Aid immunity reduced to 12 hours.
  • Risky Surgery [T] -1 pt: Treat successes as crit successes, and failures as crit failures on perform surgery.
