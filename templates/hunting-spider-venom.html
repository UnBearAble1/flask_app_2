Hunting Spider Venom


EXPERT
Alchemical, Consumable, Injury, Poison
Price 20 gp
Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk L
Activate Two Actions Interact

This venom erodes its target's defenses, aiding the spider in securing prey.

Saving Throw DC 18 Fortitude; Maximum Duration 6 rounds;

  • Stage 1 1d8 poison damage and flat-footed (1 round);
  • Stage 2 1d10 poison damage, clumsy 1, and flat-footed (1 round);
  • Stage 3 1d12 poison damage, clumsy 2, and flat-footed (1 round)
