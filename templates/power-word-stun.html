Power Word Stun


LEGENDARY
Auditory, Enchantment, Mental
Traditions arcane
Single Action; verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Duration varies

You stun the target with an arcane word of power. Once targeted, the target is then temporarily immune for 10 minutes. The effect of the spell depends on the target's level.

Moderate or Less: The target is stunned for 1d6 rounds.
Serious: The target is stunned for 1 round.
Critical or Higher The target is stunned 1.
