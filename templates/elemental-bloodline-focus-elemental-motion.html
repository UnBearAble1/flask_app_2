Elemental Bloodline Focus: Elemental Motion


7 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Elemental Bloodline Focus: Elemental Toss; Master in Nature

You gain the focus spell, Elemental Motion, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Elemental Motion; Evocation

2 Actions; somatic, verbal; Duration 1 minute

You call upon your element to propel you, improving your Speed depending on your element. This spell has your element's trait.

  • Air or Fire: You gain a fly Speed equal to your Speed. You can only fly for the duration of the single action, and fall at the end of the action if you aren't on a solid surface.
  • Earth You gain a burrow Speed of 10 feet.
  • Water You gain a swim Speed equal to your Speed and can breathe underwater.
