Snare Hopping


3 Points
Crafting; Snares
Prerequisites Snare Crafting;

You can magically move your snares around. You gain the snare hopping warden spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Snare Hopping; Conjuration, Teleportation

2 Actions somatic, verbal

Range 50 feet; Targets one snare you built

You may not have placed your snare in the right place, but you can magically move it there. You teleport the target snare to another unoccupied location in range.
