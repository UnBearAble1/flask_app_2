Creation Domain: Artistic Flourish


4 Points
Crafting; Focus Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Crafting; Trained in Religion

You have divine abilities related to crafting and art. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Artistic Flourish; Transmutation

2 Actions; material, somatic; Range 15 feet; Targets 1 item or work of art that fits entirely within the range; Duration 10 minutes

You transform the target to make it match your artisanal and artistic vision. The item grants a +1 item bonus to attack rolls if it's a weapon or skill checks if it's a skill tool. The target is a beautiful and impressive piece for its new quality, but the effect is obviously temporary, so its monetary value doesn't change.



When you cast this spell, any previous artistic flourish you had cast ends.
