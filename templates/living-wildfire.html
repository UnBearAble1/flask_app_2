Living Wildfire


Threat: 5

Living wildfires appear as humanoids made of living fire.

Recall Knowledge - Elemental (Arcana, Nature)

N, Medium, Elemental, Fire, Artillery
Perception +10; darkvision, smoke vision

  • Smoke Vision The living wildfire ignores the concealed condition from smoke.
Languages Ignan
Skills Acrobatics +9
Str +3, Dex +4, Con +2, Int -2, Wis +3, Cha +0
AC 17; Fort +6, Ref +10, Will +8
HP 40; Immunities bleed, fire, paralyzed, poison, sleep; Weaknesses cold 5
Speed 25 feet

Features:

  • Explosion (fire) When the living wild fire dies, it explodes, dealing 3d6 fire damage to each creature in a 10-foot emanation (DC 19 basic Reflex save).

Actions:

  • Tendril: Melee Single Action; +8 (+4 / +0) (agile, finesse, reach 10 feet), Damage 2d6+5
  • Fire Mote: Ranged Single Action; +10 (+5 /+0) (range increment 60 feet), Damage 2d8+6 fire plus 2d4 persistent fire
