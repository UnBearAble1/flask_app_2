Dire Wolf


Threat 5

Much larger and more foul-tempered than their common cousins, dire wolves haunt primeval lands that accommodate their massive size and proportionately large hunting grounds and appetites. Orcs are fond of using dire wolves as mounts, finding their vicious tempers perfect for hunting and warfare.

Recall Knowledge - Animal (Nature)

N, Large, Animal, Skirmisher
Perception +9; low-light vision, scent (imprecise) 30 feet
Skills Acrobatics +8, Athletics +10, Stealth +8, Survival +8
Str +5, Dex +3, Con +4, Int -4, Wis +3, Cha -2
AC 19; Fort +8, Ref +10, Will +6
HP 60
Speed 35 feet

Features:
Pack Attack: The dire wolf's Strikes deal 1d6 extra damage to creatures within reach of at least two of the wolf's allies.

Actions:

  • Jaws; Melee Single Action; +10 [+5/+0] (reach 10 feet), Damage 2d8+5 piercing plus Knockdown or Grab
  • Worry; Single Action (attack) Requirements The dire wolf has a creature grabbed with its jaws. Effect The dire wolf fiercely shakes the grabbed creature with its teeth, dealing
    2d8+5 damage (DC 19 basic Fortitude save).
