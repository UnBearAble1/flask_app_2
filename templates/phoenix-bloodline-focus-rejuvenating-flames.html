Phoenix Bloodline Focus: Rejuvenating Flames


12 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Expert in Nature
You gain the focus spell, Rejuvenating Flames, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.


Rejuvenating Flames; Fire, Healing, Necromancy
2 Actions; somatic, verbal
Area 15-foot cone; Saving Throw basic Reflex
Special: This Focus Spell takes 2 Focus Points to Cast
You create a gout of flame that both heals and burns. You restore 5d4 HP to all allies in the area, and those allies gain a +1 status bonus to Fortitude saves for 1 minute. Enemies in the area take 5d4 fire damage with a basic Reflex save. (Note: If you are Master in Nature, you can make this a Master-Tier healing effect but the spell costs 3 focus points to cast.)



Additionally, when you cast this spell, The primal fire of life and death flows through you or one target. Choose to have either you or a target of the spell gain 2 temporary Hit Points
for 1 round, or to have a target of the spell take 2 fire damage.
(if the spell already deals initial fire damage, combine this with the spell's initial damage before determining weaknesses and resistances).
