Premonition of Avoidance


3 Points
Religion; Hazards
Prerequisites: Trained in Religion

Reaction; Trigger You attempt a saving throw against a hazard, but you haven't rolled yet.

Your deity grants you limited foresight, warning you of dangers an instant before they occur. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to the triggering saving throw.
