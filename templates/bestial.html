Bestial


-3 points

MENTAL
You think and react the way a wild animal would, lacking the standards normally associated with "Civilization." Your moral code, sense of propriety, concepts of territory and property and the like are all akin to those of a wild animal. You aren't out-of-control or completely chaotic, simply animalistic. You tend to ignore anyone who leaves you alone, and may display affection for people who treat you well or feed you. You also may respect dominance, but whether or not you respect ownership or human individuality is an open question.

You can understand languages, but not speak them, though anyone may communicate back and forth with you using abilities that allow them to speak with animals. The DM May greatly limit your ability to select any feat or class ability that requires civilized skills or training, such as alchemy, arcane or occult spellcasting, or the society skill.

Any attempt to behave in a civilized way can only be accomplished with a Self Control Check.
