Unstable Concoction


2 Points
Alchemist; Meta Alchemy
Prerequisite: Expert in Alchemist

You can create extraordinary--albeit volatile--alchemical wonders, though they have an unfortunate tendency to violently explode when you use them. When you use Quick Alchemy, you can create items that are 50% more efective (GM discretion in some cases.) Upon activating the item, attempt a DC 10 flat check. On a success, the item functions normally. On a failure, the item explodes, dealing 1d10 force damage per tier.
