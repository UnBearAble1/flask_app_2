Shape of the Dragon


20 Points
Arcana; Dragon Disciple
Prerequisites Dragon Disciple; Legendary in Arcana

You've discovered how to transform yourself into a dragon. Once per day, you can cast legendary-tier dragon form as an innate arcane spell, transforming into your chosen type of dragon. Any time you score a critical hit with an unarmed Strike gained from dragon form, you recharge the spell's breath weapon immediately.
