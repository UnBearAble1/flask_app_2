Imperial Bloodline


10 Points
Spellcaster; Magical Bloodine
Prerequisites: Trained in Spellcaster (Arcane)

One of your ancestors was a mortal who mastered magic. Such magical blood can remain latent for generations, but in you it manifested in full. Add the following spells to your repertoire, even if they aren't normally part of the arcane tradition(note you must still have the appropriate level of spellcaster proficiency to cast the spell):

  • cantrip: detect magic,
  • Trained: magic missile, dispel magic,
  • Expert: haste, dimension door,
  • Master: prying eye, disintegrate,
  • Legendary: prismatic spray, maze,

Whenever you cast a spell from the list of spells above, gain the following Blood Magic ability: A surge of ancestral memories grants you or one target a +1 status bonus to skill checks for 1 round.
