Lay on Hands


8 Points
Religion; Lay on Hands
Prerequisites: Expert in Religion

You gain the lay on hands devotion spell, and increase your focus point pool by 1.



Lay on Hands; Healing, Necromancy, Positive
Special: This Focus Spell takes 2 Focus Points to Cast


1 Action somatic

Range touch; Targets 1 willing living creature or 1 undead creature

Your hands become infused with positive energy, healing a living creature or damaging an undead creature with a touch. If you use lay on hands on a willing living target, you restore 20 Hit Points; if the target is one of your allies, they also gain a +2 status bonus to AC for 1 round. Against an undead target, you deal 20 damage and it must attempt a basic Fortitude save; if it fails, it also takes a -2 status penalty to AC for 1 round.

Special: If you are Master tier in Religion, you can cast this spell as a Master-Tier effect (increasing it's healing to 30). If you are Legendary tier, you can cast this as a Legendary-Tier effect (increasing it's healing to 40), but it costs 3 Focus Points.
If you use either of these options, you cannot use either again until you have completed 2d4 full rests.
