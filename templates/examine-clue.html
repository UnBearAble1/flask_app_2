Examine Clue


EXPLORATION, CONCENTRATION, SECRET
You take ten minutes to contemplate a clue, then make an Investigation check.


  • Critical Success
    The GM tells you how important the clue is: highly important, somewhat important, tangentially important, or not important. The GM also tells you one reason the clue is important, if it is.
  • Success
    As critical success, but the GM doesn't tell you a reason.
  • Failure
    Your results are inconclusive.
  • Critical Failure
    You misconstrue the information. As success, but the GM provides incorrect information.
