Create Artwork


EXPLORATION or DOWNTIME, CONCENTRATE
You either spend ten minutes preparing a piece of art (a sketch, a culinary delight, calligraphy, or the like) or spend a longer stretch of downtime working on it (typically for paintings, composing - but not performing - music, sculpture, etc). The default DC is usually based on
the Quality Spectrum, with DCs getting lower based on how much time you have put into the work. Creating Artwork rarely has an impact on its own, but it might influence the DCs of subsequent
Diplomacy
checks against those who enjoy it--or even change their attitudes--if the GM sees fit.

  • Critical Success
    Your artwork impresses the observers, and they're likely to share stories of your ability.
  • Success
    You prove yourself, and observers appreciate the quality of your artwork.
  • Failure
    Your artwork falls flat.
  • Critical Failure
    Something about your artwork really turns people away.

DC improvements based on time invested:

  • Up to 1 hour: Base DC
  • Up to 12 hours: DC is reduced by 1
  • Up to 48 hours: DC is reduced by 2
  • Up to 200 hours: DC is reduced by 3
  • Up to 500 hours: DC is reduced by 4
