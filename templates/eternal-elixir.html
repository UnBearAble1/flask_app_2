Eternal Elixir


8 Points
Alchemist; Elixers
Prerequisites Extend Elixir, Legendary in Alchemist

Your body readily accepts and retains minor changes. When you drink one of your lesser alchemical (or other trained-tier) items made with Quick or Advanced Alchemy, you can make the elixir's duration indefinite. If you later consume a different elixir and make it indefinite, the effect of the previous indefinite elixir ends.
