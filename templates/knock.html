Knock


TRAINED

Transmutation
Traditions arcane, occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 door, lock, or container; Duration 1 minute

You make the target easier to open. Knock grants a +4 status bonus to any creature that tries to open the target door, lock, or container with an Athletics or a Thievery check. You can attempt a Thievery check to open the target as part of casting knock, and you are +0 instead of -4 if you are untrained.
This may open the lock instantly, even if it would normally take longer to pick the lock

Knock counteracts lock.
