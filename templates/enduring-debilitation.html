Enduring Debilitation


5 Points
Warrior; Debilitating Strike
Free action; Usable once per minute;
Trigger You apply a debilitation to a creature; Prerequisites Debilitating Strike

You can make your debilitation last an exceptionally long time. The triggering debilitation lasts for 1 minute instead of until the end of your next turn. A creature may spend 1 action to attempt to shake off the effects of your enduring Debilitation - they must succeed on a Fortitude save to do so. They can attempt this each round.
