Lesson of Snow


3 points

Occultism; Hex Spell
Prerequisites: Master in occultism

You learn the Personal Blizzard hex. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Personal Blizzard; Conjuration, Hex

1 Action somatic; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature; Saving Throw Fortitude; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

You summon an isolated blizzard of obscuring, scouring ice to follow your target. The target attempts a Fortitude save.

  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target takes 1d6+2 cold damage, and the spell ends.
  • Failure The target takes 1d6+2 cold damage and 1d6+2 persistent cold damage. (The persistent damage automatically ends when the spell ends.) It is concealed to other creatures, and other creatures are concealed to it.
  • Critical Failure As failure, but both the cold damage and the persistent cold damage increase to 2d6+4
