Stealth and Hiding


These effects relate to avoiding the senses of others, and remaining unperceived.

Attack Without Notice


[Base: 16, Mod +8]
Activation: Instantaneous
Your next action must be a melee strike. Make a stealth check against the perception DC of each witness (individually, including the target). If you succeed, they do not realize you were the source of the attack.

  • Can be a ranged strike (Mod +10)
  • Can be any kind of attack (the attack effect may still be obvious, depending on the nature of the attack) (Base +4, mod +20)

Invisibility


[Base: 12, Mod +8]
Activation: Duration

You become invisible - Hostile actions end your invisibility.

  • Hostile actions do not end your invisibility. (Base +4)
  • While invisible, detection abilities that normally detect or reveal invisible creatures do not reveal you. Creature can still seek you, however. (Base +4, Mod +10)



More Easily Avoid Targeting While Hidden



[Base: 8]
Activation: Duration



You increase the Flat DC to hit you while Hidden by 2

Silenced


[Base 8, Mod +8]
Activation: Duration
You become Silenced: you make no sound, preventing creatures from noticing you using hearing. Because you generate no sound, you can't use sonic attacks, nor can you use actions with the auditory trait, including effects with verbal components.

Stealth Effects


Hide in Plain Sight
[Base: 12, Mod+8]

  • You can hide and sneak without needing cover or concealment.


Stealth at Full Speed

[Base: 8]
You can move full speed while sneaking
or avoiding notice



Special Sense Avoidance
[Base: 4]
Whenever you use the Avoid Notice, Hide, or Sneak actions, you are always considered to be taking precautions against a special sense, such as Tremor-sense, chosen at creation.

  • Against all special senses (Base: 12, Mod -4)




Suppress Sensory Indicator
[Base: 8]
You suppress one of your sensory indicators, such as scent, kinetic tremors, or life aura (chosen at creation), preventing creatures from passively noticing its presence via a specific sense other than sight or hearing, even if the creatures have the sense as a precise or imprecise scent. A creature attempting a Perception check to Seek with the special sense and other senses might notice the lack of the indicator.

Create a Diversion Effects


Failed Checks Aren't Recognized
[Mod: 2]
If you fail a Deception check to Create a Diversion, humanoid creatures aren't aware that you were trying to trick them unless you get a critical failure on your roll.





Extra Beneficial Create a Diversion
[Mod: 2]
Your hidden condition on a successful Create a Diversion roll lasts for 1 additional round, maybe longer depending on the diversion you create (as adjudicated by the GM)
