Envenomed Snare


MASTER
Uncommon, Consumable, Mechanical, Poison, Snare, Trap
Price 50 gp

This snare is coated with purple worm venom and tipped with needles, which deliver the venom to the first creature to enter the square. That creature takes 4d6 damage (DC 20 basic Reflex) and, on a failure, the creature is exposed to the purple worm venom.

Craft Requirements Supply 1 dose of purple worm.
