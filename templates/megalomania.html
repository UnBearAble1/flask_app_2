Megalomania


-6 points

MENTAL, LAWFUL
You believe you are destined for something greater, or are generally more powerful than you are. You believe you are are capable of doing anything you are at least trained in, at least as well as the best person at that thing. You cannot (no self-control check allowed) doubt yourself, back down from a challenge, or show any degree caution pertaining to any skill you are trained in, including any form of combat. You walk through any door you please, stroll casually down dungeon halls, and are brazen beyond belief.

You may attract follower with the Fanaticism complication, but ultimately nobody enjoys hearing you ramble on about how awesome you are. Young or particularly naive people may react to you positively, but others take -2 to reaction rolls.
