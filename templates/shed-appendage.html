Shed Appendage


1 Point
Physiology; Useful Bonus Appendage
Reaction; Requires Useful Bonus Appendage; Trigger You become grabbed.

You can shed your bonus appendage to escape. You cease being grabbed, then Stride without triggering any reactions from the creature that grabbed you. It takes 1 week for your appendage to fully grow back. Until it does, you can't use any of your Useful Bonus Appendage feats.
