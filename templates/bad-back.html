Bad Back


-3 or -6 Points

PHYSICAL
Whenever you roll an athletics check or you critically fail a fortitude or reflex saving throw, or when an attack roll or athletics check critically succeeds against you, you must also make an additional DC 9 flat check. On a failure, you throw out your back. What this means is based on the severity of your complication:

Mild: [-3 pts] The pain renders you stunned 1 either on this round, if applicable, or the next. Further, you are clumsy 1 until you rest for an hour or someone helps you reset your back (a trained DC medicine check.)
If you rolled a natural 1 on the flat check, you are stunned 1 until someone resets your back, and clumsy 2 instead of clumsy 1.

Severe: [-6 pts] The flat check is DC 13. On a failure, you are stunned until the end of your next round, and clumsy 3 and stupefied 2 until you get a full 8 hour rest or someone helps you reset your back (an expert DC medicine check.) If you critically fail the flat check, you take 2d10 damage, are stunned for 2 rounds, and are clumsy 3, sickened 1, and stupefied 3 until you get a full 8 hour rest or someone helps you reset your back.
