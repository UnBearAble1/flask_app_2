Sickening Counter


1 Point
Physiology; Inflict Conditions
Reaction; Usable once per day; Trigger: A creature you are aware of damages you with a melee Strike.

Your body does something nasty when attacked. The attacker must succeed at a Fortitude saving throw or become sickened 1 (sickened 2 on a critical failure). It can't recover from the sickened condition while it's adjacent to you.
