Travel Domain: Traveler's Transit


7 Points
Acrobatics; Battlefield Mobility
Prerequisites: Master in Acrobatics; Trained in Religion

You have power over movement and journeys.You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Traveler's Transit; Evocation

2 Actions (somatic, verbal); Duration 1 minute
