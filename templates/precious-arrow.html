Precious Arrow


7 Points
Warrior; Ranged Weapons
1 Action; Transmutation; Requires: Eldritch Archer; Master in Warrior

You enchant a piece of ammunition with the magical essence of a precious material, allowing you to leverage certain creatures' weaknesses. Choose cold iron or silver; if you shoot the arrow before the end of your turn, it counts as that material.
