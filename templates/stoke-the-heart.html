Stoke the Heart


6 Points
occultism; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in occultism

You learn the Stoke the Heart hex cantrip.



Stoke the Heart; Cantrip, Emotion, Enchantment ,Hex

1 Action verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

Intense fervor fills the target creature, empowering their blows. The target gains a +3 status bonus to damage rolls. Once this spell ends, the target becomes temporarily immune for 1 minute.
