Dandy


5 Points
Diplomacy; Community
Prerequisites: Trained in Diplomacy

You are a consummate student of dignity, etiquette, and fashion. You can spend at least one day of downtime to manipulate the course, tone, or content of a rumor to your benefit. The difficulty, degree of change, and final outcome of Influencing a Rumor is determined by the GM based on the size of the community, the relative perceptiveness of the inhabitants, and the agency of other rumormongers.
Typically the difficulty starts with at least "Trained" for a small village and increases to at least "Expert" for a town, at least "Master" for a city, and at least "Legendary" for a metropolis.
