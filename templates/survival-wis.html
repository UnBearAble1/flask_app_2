Survival (WIS)


You are adept at living in the wilderness, foraging for food and building shelter, and with training you discover the secrets of tracking and hiding your trail.

Untrained Actions


  • Subsist in the wild by foraging for food and building shelter.
  • Sense Direction: you can stay oriented in the wild.

Trained Actions


  • Cover Tracks: You cover your tracks, moving up to half your travel speed.
  • Track: You follow tracks, moving at up to half your travel speed.
