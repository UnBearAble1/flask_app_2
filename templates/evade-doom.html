Evade Doom


4 Points
Behavioral Trait, Resistance
When you would gain the doomed condition, you can attempt a DC 15 flat check. On a success, you don't gain the doomed condition. Additionally, when you have the
doomed
condition, the condition affects you as if its value was 1 lower than it actually is (doomed 1 has no effect, doomed 2 causes you to die at dying 3, and so on).
