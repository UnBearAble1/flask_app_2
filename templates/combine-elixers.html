Combine Elixers


5 Points
Alchemist; Elixers

Free Action; Additive 3;
Prerequisite: Expert in Alchemist; Frequency once per round; Trigger You use Quick Alchemy to craft an alchemical item that has the elixir trait

You add in a mixture that turns your single crafted elixir into a combination of that elixir and another that you know. You must succeed on both crafting rolls, each with the Additive penalty. If either check fails, you fail to make either. When this combination elixir is consumed, both the constituent elixirs take effect. For example, you can combine two elixirs of life to create a combined elixir that heals twice the normal amount, or you can combine a lesser darkvision elixir with a lesser eagle-eye elixir to both gain darkvision and find secret doors.
