Ranger's Masterful Companion


5 Points
Warrior; Hunt Prey, Companion
Prerequisites Ranger's Companion

Your animal companion assaults the prey even without your orders. During an encounter, even if you don't use the Command an Animal action, your animal companion can still use 1 action that round on your turn to Stride toward or Strike your prey.
