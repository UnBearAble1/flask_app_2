Perception Feats


COMBAT ABILITIES


  • All the Time In the World [L] -8 pts: For three actions, gain 9 mental actions instantaneously

HAZARDS


  • Hazard Finder [E] -4 pts: Roll even if not searching to find, and get +1 against hazards

INITIATIVE

Battlefield Surveyor [E] -3 pts: Get +2 to perception for initiative

INVESTIGATION


  • Bolera's Investigation [M] -7 pts: Possibly force a target to be unable to deliberately lie, as well as gain penalties to further deception
  • Lie Detector [E] -3 pts: Gain +1 to prevent lies, then +1 to follow up social check
  • Ongoing Investigation [E] -3 pts: Investigate while exploring at full speed / do another activity simultaneously
  • Plot the Future [E] -4 pts: Predict how likely something is, and what can influence that
  • Reconstruct the Scene [E] -4 pts: Get impression of a location and gain clues
  • Sense Alignment [M] -6 pts: Try to figure out a target's alignment
  • That's Odd [M] -5 pts: GM will tell you if something is odd or curious in a room

PURSUE A LEAD


  • Pursue a Lead [E] -5 pts: Designate person or item as a clue, get +1 to checks to investigate it. Can have two investigations going.


    • Clue In [E] -2 pts: Reaction to give Lead bonus to ally


      • Clue them All In [E] -1 pt: Clue In hits all allies in area


        • Lead Investigator [E] -2 pts: Brief allies to give them your lead bonus ongoing
    • Connect the Dots [M] -3 pts: Learn if / how two leads are connected
    • Detective's Readiness [E] -2 pts: Use lead bonus on saving throws
    • Interrogation [E] -1 pt: Can pursue a lead using a conversation
    • Just One More Thing [M] -7 pts: Reroll a check to influence someone, gaining extra bonus if they are a lead
    • Red Herring [E] -3 pts: Can tell if a lead is not useful
    • Solid Lead [E] -1 pt: You can switch back to an ended lead pursuit once a day
    • Suspect of Opportunity [E] -5 pts: Once an hour, switch to foe as lead as reaction
    • Underworld Investigator [E/T] -1 pt: Get lead bonus on thievery checks if investigating
    • Whodunnit [E] -3 pts: Once a day, get answer to two specific lead questions

SCOUT


  • Incredible Scout [M] -2 pts: When you scout, give allies +2 initiative

SEARCH


  • Expeditious Search [M] -2 pts: Search in half or less time
  • Observant Explorer [M] -6 pts: Check to notice stealthed creatures even when not searching
  • Thorough Search [E] -2 pts: Double search time to get +2 to seek checks

SENSES


  • Anticipate Ambush [E] -1 pts: Move at half speed to penalize enemy stealth initiative
  • Grave Sense [E/E] -5 pts: Vague sense to detect undead
  • Instinctive Strike [M] -5 pts: Use scent to strike hidden creatures for 2 actions
  • Sense Chaos [E] -3 pts: Vague sense to detect chaos
  • Sense Evil [M] -7 pts: Vague sense to detect evil
  • Sense Good [M] -4 pts: Vague sense to detect good
  • Sense the Unseen [L] -8 pts: Turn undetected creatures to hidden on failed seek
  • Soulsight [M/M] -8 pts: Detect spirits of living creatures, sentient undead, and haunts as an imprecise sense
  • Spiritual Sense [E/M] -7 pts: Can sense haunts without searching as well as creatures in the ethereal plane and more as an imprecise sense
  • Supertaster [M] -1 pt: Can learn if food/drink is poisoned with a taste, and get +2 to recall knowledge when tasting something
  • True Perception [L] -30 pts: You always have True Seeing active

SKILL SUBSTITUTION


  • Battle Assessment [M] -4 pts: Use deception to gather info on an enemy in combat
  • Edgewatch Detective [M] -4 pts: Use perception to Track

SPECIAL DEFENSES


  • Foresee Danger [E] -4 pts: Reaction to use perception DC instead of AC against attack
  • Predictable! [E] -4 pts: 1 action to try and gain AC bonuses against target
