Blight bombs contain volatile toxic chemicals that rot flesh. A blight bomb deals the listed poison damage,
persistent poison damage, and splash damage.

TRAINED:
Cost: 5 gold
The bomb deals 2d6 poison damage, 2d4 persistent poison damage, and 2 poison splash damage.

EXPERT:
Cost: 20 gold
The bomb deals 3d6 poison damage, 3d4 persistent poison damage, and 3 poison splash damage.

MASTER:
Cost: 50 gold
The bomb deals 4d6 poison damage, 4d4 persistent poison damage, and 4 poison splash damage.

LEGENDARY:
Cost: 85 gold
The bomb deals 5d6 poison damage, 5d4 persistent poison damage, and 5 poison splash damage.
