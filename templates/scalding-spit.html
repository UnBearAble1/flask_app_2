Scalding Spit


5 Points
Cultural Training; Elemental
Prerequisites Torcher

Your bodily fluids burn with surprising volatility, as if you ran on oil instead of blood. As long as you are taking persistent fire damage, you gain a boiling spit ranged unarmed attack with a range of 30 feet that deals 3d6 fire damage.
