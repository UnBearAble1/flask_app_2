Animal Allies


TRAINED

Conjuration
Traditions primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Area 5-foot emanation; Saving Throw basic Reflex

You summon tiny, ordinary animals from the environment, such as insects, birds, or fish, to quickly lash out at nearby foes. The animals swarm around the creatures in the area, dealing each of them 6d8 piercing damage with a basic Reflex save.
