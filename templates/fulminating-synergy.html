Fulminating Synergy


6 Points
Spellcaster; Halcyon Spells
Prerequisites: Dualistic Synergy, Master in Spellcaster

When you draw upon your spells' synergy, you create a physical manifestation of their magical resonance and invoke it to harm a foe. When you use Dualistic Synergy, choose a damage type: acid, cold, electricity, or fire. In addition to Dualistic Synergy's normal effect, a foe within 30 feet of you takes an amount of damage of the chosen type equal to 2x the spell's tier. If the spell already deals damage of the chosen type, combine the damage before applying weaknesses and resistances.
