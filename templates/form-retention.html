Form Retention


5 Points
spellcaster; Improved Spellcasting
Prerequisites: Master in spellcaster

You have trained your mind and body to tolerate polymorph magic for longer periods of time, so long as you prepare for the change appropriately. When you cast a polymorph spell you can cast the spell at one tier higher. This doesn't grant any of the normal benefits of heightening a spell, but increases the duration up to 10 minutes. For example, if you were casting animal form in an expert level slot with Form Retention, you would get the benefits of a trained-tier animal form that lasts for up to 10 minutes. If the spell can be Dismissed, that doesn't change.
