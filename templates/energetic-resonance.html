Energetic Resonance


4 Points
Spellcaster; Saves and resistances

Reaction; [Abjuration]; Trigger You would take acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic damage from a spell. Prerequisites: Expert in spellcaster

Your blood resonates with magical energy, mitigating the effects of harmful spells. Make a spellcasting roll of a tier higher than the spell targeting you, gaining all the costs, risks and consequences of casting a spell of that tier. If successful, you gain resistance to one of the triggering effect's damage types equal to five times the spell's tier.
