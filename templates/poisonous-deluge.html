Poisonous Deluge


7 points
Alchemist; Poison
Prerequisites: Legendary in Alchemy
You can cast the Poisonous Deluge Focus Spell, and gain 2 Focus Points.


3 Actions; Somatic;
Requirements You have at least three of the same contact poison.

Special: This focus spell costs 2 focus points to cast.
As part of casting this focus spell, you Interact to draw three vials of the same contact poison and throw them in the air, where they shatter and spread a poisonous deluge affecting a 20-foot burst within 60 feet. All creatures in the burst are automatically exposed to the poison, immediately bypassing any onset time, and must attempt a saving throw against that poison. The three vials must be identical, even if the poison can come in multiple types or different levels.
