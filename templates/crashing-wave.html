Crashing Wave


EXPERT
EvocationWater
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Area 30-foot cone; Saving Throw basic Reflex

You summon a crashing wave that sweeps away from you. You deal 8d6 bludgeoning damage to creatures in the area, and creatures are pushed back 10 feet on a failed save, or 20 on a critical failure. The water also extinguishes non-magical fires in the area.

Heightened: The damage increases by 4d6.
