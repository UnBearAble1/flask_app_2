Underworld Investigator


1 Point
Perception; Pursue a Lead
Prerequisites: Pursue a Lead, Trained in Underworld Lore

Your work as an investigator makes you familiar with the criminal element. You might be a criminal yourself, or you might have gained an understanding of how they operate from the outside. You gain your Pursue a Lead circumstance bonus to Thievery checks to investigate the subject of your lead (such as checks to Steal a clue from a suspect or Pick a Lock to open a safe with damning documents).
