Summon Axiom


MASTER
Conjuration
Traditions
divine
Three Actions; material, somatic, verbal
Range
30 feet;
Duration
sustained up to 1 minute

You conjure a lawful celestial, monitor, or fiend to fight on your behalf. You can't summon a creature unless its alignment is one of your deity's preferred alignments (or, if you don't have a deity, is within one step of your alignment). This works like summon animal, except you summon one of the creatures below, instead:


  • Axiomite (LN)
  • Erinyes (LE)
  • Legion Archon (LG)

Heightened (legendary): You may instead summon one of the following:


  • Shield Archon (LG)
  • Sacistran (LE)
  • Zelekhut (LN)
