Expert Rider


1 Points
Cultural Training, Animal Kinsmanship


Your people incorporate mounts deeply into their culture. You gain the following benefits:

  • a +1 circumstance bonus to Nature checks to use Command an Animal on a specific type of creature your people use as mounts (pick at creation, GM gets final say).
  • When you Command an Animal you're mounted on to take a move action (such as Stride), you automatically succeed instead of needing to attempt a check.
  • Any mount you've ridden for at least two days will automatically return to you upon your specified call (a whistle, horn, shout, etc) as long as it can hear you.
  • Any animal you're mounted on acts on your turn, like a minion.

    • If you Mount an animal in the middle of an encounter, it skips its next turn and then acts on your next turn.
  • If you have an animal companion and it is a mount, it becomes trained in Intimidation. If your animal companion uses Support while serving as your mount, it grants you the effects of the
    Aid
    reaction on your first Intimidation check to
    Demoralize
    on the same turn, even though it can't take reactions.
