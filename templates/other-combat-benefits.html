Other Combat Benefits


These feats modify elements of combat or warfare not listed elsewhere.

Combat Maneuver Against Larger Foes

You can attempt to Disarm, Grapple, Shove or Trip creatures larger than you with greater effect.

  • You can do on creatures three sizes larger than you (Mod: 2)
  • Your penalties to do so are
    reduced by 1 per category (Mod: 1)
  • You have no penalty to do so (Mod: 4)

Critical Specialization

(Mod: 4)


You gain the Critical Specialization effect of weapons / brawling

Nonvocal Demoralization



(Mod: 2)

You can Demoralize in a special fashion that loses the auditory trait and gains the visual trait, and you don't take a penalty when you attempt to Demoralize a creature that doesn't understand your language.

Predict Attack Result


Activation: Instantaneous
Default Range: 30 feet

(Base: 8, Mod +4)

Pick a creature, roll a d20. If you choose to attack the creature with a Strike, on your first strike against it this turn, you must use the result of the d20 you rolled.

  • Boost: Can include a combat maneuver or Strike (Mod +3)

Armor and Shield Effects



Improved Armor Use
[Mod: 4]
You unlock the armor traits of a base category one better than yours (so use hybrid if you are adept, or warrior if you are hybrid.) If you are warrior, the GM will add one additional trait to the armor for you to unlock.



Rest in Armor
You can rest in Armor.

  • In Light Armor (Mod: 4)
  • In Medium Armor (Mod: 10)
  • In Heavy Armor (Mod: 15)


Free Shield Hand
[Mod: 4]
Shield hand counts as free for interact actions

  • Can also hold an item (Mod +8)


Environmental Fighting Effects


Use bludgeoning/slashing melee underwater
[Mod: 1]
You don't take the usual penalties for using a bludgeoning or slashing melee weapon in water.


Use bludgeoning/slashing ranged underwater
[Mod: 1]
You can use ranged bludgeoning or slashing weapons underwater





Use any ranged weapons underwater

[Mod: 1]
Your ranged weapons underwater don't have halved ranged increments


Using ranged weapons in high wind
[Mod: 1]
You don't take penalties from using a ranged weapon in high wind

Improvised Weapon Effects


Using Improvised Weapons
[Mod: 1]
You don't take penalties for improvised weapons


Critical Specialization with Improvised Weapons
[Mod: 1]

Apply critical specialization effects to improvised weapons (GM picks best closest match)




Enhanced Improvised Weapon
[Base: 4]
Pick a certain type of non-weapon item. In your hands, you treat that item as a certain type of weapon (also chosen at creation.)


Nonlethal Attack Effects


Lethal Unarmed Attacks
[Mod: 1]
You can ignore the nonlethal trait with your unarmed attacks





Nonlethal Weapon Attacks
[Mod: 1]
You ignore the -2 to hit with nonlethal attacks using a lethal weapon
