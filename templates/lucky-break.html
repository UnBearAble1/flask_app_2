Lucky Break


2 Points
Natural Abilities; Luck
Prerequisites Lucky Defense

You catch yourself as you make a mistake. You can trigger Lucky Defense when you fail or critically fail on an Athletics or Acrobatics skill check, in addition to its normal trigger. When you do, you reroll the triggering skill check and use the better result. This still counts against Lucky Defense's frequency, as normal.
