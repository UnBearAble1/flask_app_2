Elemental Heart


3 Points
Natural Abilities; Elemental
2 Actions; Usable once per day

Choose one of the following damage types: acid, cold, electricity, or fire. Once chosen, this can't be changed. Energy bursts forth from your body. You deal 5d6 damage to all adjacent creatures. They get a basic Reflex save.



Boost: (+5 points) When you use Energy Emanation, you can create an emanation of 5 feet, 10 feet, or 15 feet. The damage increases to 7d6.
