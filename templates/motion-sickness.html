Motion Sickness


-2 points

PHYSICAL
When at sea or in another rocking, swaying or similarly moving vehicle, you are sickened 1. You can attempt a DC 12 flat check to expel your guts to clear the sickness just as with the normal sickened condition, but the effect only gives you a minute of reprieve.
You can only attempt this twice during a period of motion sickness.
