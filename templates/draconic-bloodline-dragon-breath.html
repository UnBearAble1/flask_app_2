Draconic Bloodline: Dragon Breath


6 Points
Arcana; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Draconic Bloodline Focus: Dragon Claws

You gain the focus spell, Dragon Breath, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Dragon Breath; Evocation;

2 Actions; somatic, verbal; Area 30-foot cone or 60-foot line originating from you; Saving Throw basic Reflex or Fortitude

You spew energy from your mouth, dealing 7d6 damage of the same element as your claws. Pick cone or line at the selection of this feat - it cannot be changed later.



Additionally, immediately upon casting this spell, until the end of your next turn, Draconic scales grow briefly on you, granting a +1 status bonus to AC for 1 round.
