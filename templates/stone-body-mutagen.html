Stone Body Mutagen


Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir, Mutagen, Polymorph
Usage
held in 1 hand;
Bulk
L
Activate
Single Action,
Interact

This mutagen calcifies your body making it as hard as stone.



Benefit
You gain resistance to physical damage (except bludgeoning) equal to the listed amount.



Drawback
Your Speeds are reduced by 10 feet (minimum 5 feet) and you take a -2 penalty to Reflex saves.

EXPERT:
Cost: 20 gold
You gain resistance 5 to physical damage (except bludgeoning) and the duration is 10 minutes.

MASTER:
Cost: 50 gold
You gain resistance 5 to physical damage (except bludgeoning) and the duration is 1 hour.

LEGENDARY:
Cost: 85 gold
You gain resistance 10 to physical damage (except bludgeoning) and the duration is 1 hour.
