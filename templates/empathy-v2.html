Empathy (V2)


Empathy measures your character's ability to understand the emotions and motivations of other sapient creatures, to read their body language, and to understand their core values and beliefs.

Untrained Actions


  • Sense Motive: You try to tell whether a creature's behavior is abnormal.
  • Discover Motivations: You watch or study an NPC to learn more about that NPC's preferences.
