Deadly Weapon Poison


9 Points
Alchemist; Poison
Prerequisites Improved Poison Weapon; Master in Alchemist

Your poisons are devastatingly potent. When you apply a simple poison with Poison Weapon, the poison deals 4d4 poison damage instead of 1d4 poison damage. When you apply an injury poison to your weapon, the DC to resist that poison increases by 2, to a maximum of your alchemist, warrior, deception or stealth DC.
