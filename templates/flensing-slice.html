Flensing Slice


4 Points
Warrior; Two Weapon Fighting

1 Action; Requires: Double Slice; Prerequisites: Master in Warrior

When you hit with both attacks with Double Slice, you flense the target, making it bleed and creating a weak spot. The target takes 2d8 persistent bleed damage. The target becomes flat-footed, and its resistances to any physical damage types are reduced by 5; these two effects last until the beginning of your next turn.
