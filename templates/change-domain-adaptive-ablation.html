Change Domain: Adaptive Ablation


3 Points
Arcana; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Arcana; Trained in Religion

You can restructure the physical and metaphysical. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Adaptive Ablation; Abjuration

Reaction; somatic; Duration 1 minute; Trigger: You take energy damage

You shift yourself to adapt to the mystical wavelengths of the energy damage, protecting yourself from further harm. You gain resistance 10 to the triggering energy type; you gain this resistance after you take the triggering damage, so it applies only to future damage you take during the duration.
