Elemental Reprisal


2 Points
Natural Abilities; Elemental
Reaction; Frequency once per day; Trigger A creature hits you with a melee Strike
You channel elemental energy into a creature that strikes you. Pick lightning, acid, fire, electricity, cold, or bludgeoning (earth trait).
The creature must attempt a Fortitude save. On a failure, the creature takes 2d6 persistent damage of your elemental type and is sickened 1 by the pain. On a critical failure, it instead takes 4d6 persistent
damage and is sickened 2.
