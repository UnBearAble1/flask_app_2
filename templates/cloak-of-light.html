Cloak of Light


MASTER
Healing, Light, Necromancy, Positive
Traditions divine, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Duration sustained for up to 5 rounds

You surround yourself in holy light that restores the living and rebuffs undead. You glow with bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light to the next 30 feet. Living creatures that begin their turn adjacent to you recover 2d6 Hit Points. Undead creatures that begin their turn adjacent to you take 8d6 positive damage.

Heightened (Legendary) The healing increases by 1d6, and the damage to undead increases by 4d6.
