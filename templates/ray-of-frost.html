Ray of Frost


Attack, Cantrip, Cold, Evocation
Traditions;
arcane, primal
Two Actions somatic, verbal
Range 120 feet; Targets 1 creature

You blast an icy ray. Make a spell attack roll. The ray deals cold damage equal to 5d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier.

  • Critical Success The target takes double damage and takes a -10-foot status penalty to its Speeds for 1 round.
  • Success The target takes normal damage.
