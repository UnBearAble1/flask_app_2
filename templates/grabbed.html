Grabbed


You're held in place by another creature, giving you the flat-footed and immobilized conditions. If you attempt a manipulate action while grabbed (such as casting a spell with a somatic, material or focus component), you must succeed at a DC 5 flat check or it is lost; roll the check after spending the action, but before any effects are applied.
