Claws


1 Points
Physiology; Claws
You gain a claw unarmed attack that deals 2d4 slashing damage. Your claws are in the brawling group and have the agile, finesse, and unarmed traits.



Boost (+1 Point) Powerful Claws: Your claw damage increases to 2d6.

Boost (+1 Point) Fae Killer: Your claws are considered cold iron

Boost (+1 Point) Lycanthrope Killer: Your claws are considered silver

Boost (+2 Points) Spiteful Rake: You go for the eyes. You gain the Spiteful Rake Action

  • 1 Action. You make a claw Strike. If you hit and deal damage, the target is
    dazzled
    for 1 round. On a critical hit, the target is instead
    blinded
    for 1 round and dazzled until restored to maximum hit points. Either way, once you use this attack on an enemy, they become immune for 24 hours as they grow wary of your tricks.
