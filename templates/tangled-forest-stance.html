Tangled Forest Stance


9 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts

1 Action; Stance; Requirements You are unarmored; Prerequisites: Powerful Fist

You extend your arms like gnarled branches to interfere with your foes' movements. You can make lashing branch unarmed attacks. These deal 2d8 slashing damage; are in the brawling group; and have the agile, finesse, nonlethal, and unarmed traits. While you're in Tangled Forest Stance and can act, every enemy in your reach that tries to move away from you must succeed at a Reflex save, Acrobatics check, or Athletics check against your Warrior DC or be immobilized for that action. If you prefer, you can allow the enemy to move.
