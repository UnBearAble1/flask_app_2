Legendary Sneak


16 Points
Stealth; Movement
Prerequisites Swift Sneak; legendary in Stealth

You can Hide and Sneak even without cover or being concealed on your turn. If you don't have cover or greater cover at the end of your turn, however, you will become observed. When you employ an exploration tactic other than Avoiding Notice, you also gain the benefits of Avoiding Notice unless you choose not to.
