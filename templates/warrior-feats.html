Warrior Feats


Because there are so many available feats for Warriors, allowing a rich and complex diversity between them, we have broken Warrior Feats down into a few categories for ease of your perusal:

General Warrior Feats:
These feats have a variety of different flavors and effects, but don't belong to any of the following categories.

Warrior Tactic Feats:
Tactics represent fighting methods so complicated you cannot benefit from more than one at a time. They generally will determine the entire way your character enters battle, and have many feats within each tactic. Rage, Panache, and Sneak Attack are examples.

Ki Arts Feats: In contemporary western fantasy game terms, you can think of these as 'monk feats.' They involve a great deal of unarmed styles and mystical combat abilities.

Weapon Style Feats: These feats are those that make sense for certain weapon types, such as sword-and-board, two-handed weapons, dueling, or archery.

Religious Warrior Feats: These are the feats of holy warriors, such as paladins, and often have prerequisites in the Religion skill as well.
