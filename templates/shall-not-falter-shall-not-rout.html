Shall not falter, shall not rout


10 Points
Religion/Warrior; Devotion Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Religion; Expert in Warrior

You gain the shall not falter, shall not rout focus spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Shall Not Falter, Shall Not Rout: Healing, Necromancy

1 Action verbal; Duration 1 minute

Calling out the fifth line of the Crimson Oath, you seal your most grievous injuries with a stroke of your blade. The first time each round that you Strike with your weapon and deal damage to a foe, you recover a 2d8 hit points (Expert Tier Healing). This healing can't raise your current HP above half of your maximum HP. When you receive healing from this ability, you can opt to infuse more power into it for by spending an action, increasing it to a Master tier effect and gaining an additional 2d8 healing. If you do so, the spell immediately ends, and you cannot use it again until you have completed 2d4 full rests.
