Phoenix Bloodline


13 Points
Spellcaster; Magical Bloodline
Prerequisites: Trained in Spellcaster (Primal)

You have been blessed by a phoenix, perhaps via some magical interaction with a similarly blessed individual. Add the following spells to your repertoire, even if they aren't normally part of the primal tradition (note you must still have the appropriate level of spellcaster proficiency to cast the spell):


  • cantrip: Detect Magic
  • Trained: burning hands and See Invisibility
  • Expert: fireball and remove curse
  • Master: Breath of Life and Disintegrate
  • Legendary: Contingency and Moment of Renewal

Whenever you cast a spell from the list of spells above, gain the following Blood Magic ability: The primal fire of life and death flows through you or one target. Choose to have either you or a target of the spell gain temporary Hit Points equal to 2 per tier of the spell for 1 round, or to have a target of the spell take fire damage equal to 2 per tier of the spell
(if the spell already deals initial fire damage, combine this with the spell's initial damage before determining weaknesses and resistances).
