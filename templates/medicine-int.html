Medicine (INT)


You can patch up wounds and help people recover from diseases and poisons.

Untrained Actions


  • Recall Knowledge about diseases, injuries, poisons, and other ailments. You can use this to perform forensic examinations if you spend 10 minutes (or more, as determined by the GM) checking for evidence such as wound patterns. This is most useful when determining how a body was injured or killed.
  • Stabilize Injuries: You perform first aid on an adjacent creature that is dying or bleeding.

Trained Actions


  • Treat Disease: You treat a patient to prevent the spread of disease.
  • Treat Poison: You treat a patient to prevent the spread of poison.
  • Administer First Aid: You treat a patient to try and recover hit points.
  • Perform Surgery: You attempt to remove a specific injury.
