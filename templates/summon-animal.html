Summon Animal


TRAINED

Conjuration
Traditions arcane, primal
Three Actions; material, somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

You conjure an animal to fight for you. You summon one of the creatures listed below. This creature gains the summoned trait. Heightening the spell grants you more powerful summoned animals.

This spell requires greater concentration than an ordinary sustained spell. While sustaining this spell, if you take any damage, you must make a flat check or have the spell disrupted.
The flat check equals the damage you took, up to a maximum of DC 18.


  • Ankhrav
  • Lion
  • Grizzly Bear
  • Narwhal
  • Piranah Swarm
  • Giant Wasp

Heightened (expert): You may instead summon one of the following:


  • Gryphon
  • Tiger
  • Rhinoceros
  • Emperor Cobra
  • Cave Bear
  • Dire Wolf
  • Giant Moray Eel

Heightened (master): You may instead summon one of the following:


  • Giant Mosquito
  • Owlbear
  • Giant Tarantula
  • Great White Shark
  • Elephant
  • Megalania
  • Blood Boar

Heightened (legendary): You may instead summon one of the following:


  • Quetzalcoatlus
  • Giant Squid
  • Deadly Mantis
  • MegaPrimatus
  • Aurumvorax
  • Bulette
  • Krooth
