Hard to Fool


9 Points
Behavioral Trait, Perception
You gain a +1 circumstance bonus on Perception checks against illusions. When you come within 10 feet of an illusion that can be disbelieved, the GM rolls a secret check for you to disbelieve it, even if you didn't spend an action to Interact with the illusion.
