Telekinetic Projectile


Attack, Cantrip, Evocation
Traditions: arcane, occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature

You hurl a loose, unattended object that is within range and that has 1 Bulk or less at the target. Make a spell attack roll against the target. If you hit, you deal bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage--as appropriate for the object you hurled--equal to 5d6 plus your spellcasting ability modifier. No specific traits or magic properties of the hurled item affect the attack or the damage.

  • Critical Success You deal double damage.
  • Success You deal full damage.
