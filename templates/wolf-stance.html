Wolf Stance


5 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts
1 Action; Stance; Requirements You are unarmored; Prerequisites: Powerful Fist

You enter the stance of a wolf, low to the ground with your hands held like fanged teeth. You can make wolf jaw unarmed attacks. These deal 2d8 piercing damage; are in the brawling group; and have the agile, backstabber, finesse, nonlethal, and unarmed traits. If you're flanking a target while in Wolf Stance, your wolf jaw unarmed attacks also gain the trip trait.
