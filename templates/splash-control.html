Splash Control


2 Points
Alchemist; Bombs
Prerequisites: Expert in Alchemist

When throwing an alchemical bomb with the splash trait, you can deal splash damage to only your primary target instead of the usual splash area.


Boost: (+3 Points) Expanded Splash: When you throw an alchemical bomb that has the splash trait it deals splash damage to every creature within 10 feet of the target.

Boost: (+5 Points) Greater Splash (requires Master in Alchemist, Expanded Splash): When you throw an alchemical bomb that has the splash trait it deals splash damage to every creature within 15 feet of the target.

Boost: (+2 Points) Directional Splash: You can lob bombs with great force and a precise trajectory to angle the splash in a cone spraying in a single direction. When throwing an alchemical bomb with the
splash
trait, instead of splashing all squares adjacent to the target, you can treat the target's space as the first affected square of a 15-foot cone directed away from you, potentially allowing you to avoid allies and splash deeper into enemy lines. If the target takes up more than a single square, the target's square closest to you is the first affected square of the cone. If you have the Greater Splash boost, this cone becomes a 20-foot cone.
