Wild Shape


20 Points
Nature; Wild Shape
Prerequisites: Expert in Nature

You are one with the wild, always changing and adapting to meet any challenge. You gain the wild shape order spell, which lets you transform into a variety of forms that you can expand with Order feats. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Wild Shape; Polymorph, Transmutation

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Duration 1 minute (or longer)

(Special: Duplicating Trained Polymorph spells cost 1 focus point, Expert 2 focus points, Master 3 focus points, and Legendary 3 focus points but also cannot be used again for a number of days equal to 15 - your total number of wild shape feats.)

You infuse yourself with primal essence and transform yourself into another form. You can polymorph into any form listed in the Pest Form or Animal Form spells, which last 10 minutes. All other wild shape forms last 1 minute. You can add more forms to your wild shape list with wild shape feats; your feat might grant you some or all of the forms from a given polymorph spell. When you transform into a form granted by a spell, you gain all the effects of the form you chose. You can always heighten your lower level shapes to the highest tier wild shape you know, though bear in mind the focus point cost listed above.
