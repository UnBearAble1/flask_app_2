Touch of Corruption


10 Points
Religion; Touch of Corruption
Prerequisites: Master in Religion

You gain the Touch of Coruption devotion spell. It costs 1 Focus Point to cast a focus spell. This feat grants a focus pool of 1 Focus Point



Touch of Coruption; Necromancy, Negative

1 Action somatic; Range touch; Targets 1 creature

You infuse the target with negative energy. If the target is living, this deals 5d6 negative damage (basic Fortitude save); on a failed save, the target also takes a -2 status penalty to AC for 1 round.



If the target would be healed by this effect (usually because they are undead); they regain 1d8+4 Hit Points.
