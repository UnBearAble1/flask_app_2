Armiger's Mobility


6 Points
Warrior; Armor
Prerequisites expert in Warrior

You've learned how best to move in heavy armor. When wearing heavy armor, if you meet the Strength requirement, instead of reducing the movement penalty by 5 feet, you reduce the penalty by 10 feet (typically reducing the penalty to 0). If you have the Iron Born feat ancestry feat, increase your Speed by 5 feet while wearing heavy armor.
