Delusions


-1 to -3 points

MENTAL, CHAOTIC
You believe something that is not actually true. The primary complication here is how your character will actually behave, the secondary being how others react to you for it. You have to role-play your belief at all times, though the point cost of this complication varies on the severity:

Minor [-1]
You can function more or less normally, as this is more of a quirk of your behavior. Maybe you believe that you are the rightful heir to a minor throne, that rabbits are plotting against you, or something similar. When people finally notice this delusion, you are -1 to reaction rolls with them.

Major: [-2]
This strongly affects your behavior, but still doesn't ruin your entire life. Maybe you think that you have abilities you don't have, like a perfect sense of direction, or maybe you believe that all guards are acting against you all the time.
People are more likely to take notice of your behavior, and you take a -3 to reaction rolls with them.

Severe: [-3]
This may keep you from functioning well in society. Your delusion may be dangerous, for example you may believe you actually ARE the king of the land, or that you are effectively un-killable. You may also believe things that could constantly get you in trouble, such as "You can take whatever you want from stores. It's a free country." You take
-6 to reaction rolls with people, though they usually pity you.

The same delusion could be rated at different levels depending on how far your character is willing to take it. For example, let us say you have the delusion "Everything colored purple is alive." Here are examples of the three levels of delusion with that same exact wording:

  • Minor: You won't discuss serious matters with purple things in the room.
  • Major: You picket the Capitol demanding Civil Rights For Purple Things.
  • Severe: You attack all purple things on sight.
