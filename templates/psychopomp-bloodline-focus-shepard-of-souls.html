Psychopomp Bloodline Focus: Shepard of Souls


6 Points
Religion; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Psychopomp Bloodline Focus: Spirit Veil; Master in Religion

You gain the focus spell, Shepard of Souls, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Shepard of Souls; Healing, Necromancy

Reaction; verbal; Trigger A living creature within 30 feet takes damage; Range 30 feet; Area 1 creature

You stay the hand of fate for one not yet destined to die, sacrificing some of your own vitality to heal them. You lose up to 15 Hit Points as a sacrifice, and the target reduces the damage by twice the number of Hit Points sacrificed. If this reduction exceeds the amount of damage, the target recovers Hit Points equal to the excess amount.
