Undead Bloodline Focus: Drain Life


8 Points
Religion; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Undead Bloodline Focus: Undeath's Blessing

You gain the focus spell, Drain Life, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Drain Life; Necromancy, Negative

1 Action; somatic; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 living creature; Saving Throw basic Fortitude

You close your hand and pull life energy from another creature into yourself. This deals 4d6+3 negative damage; the target must attempt a basic Fortitude save. You gain temporary Hit Points equal to the damage the target takes after resolving its save and applying your blood magic if applicable. If the target's Hit Points were lower than the damage you dealt, you gain temporary Hit Points equal to their remaining Hit Points instead. The temporary Hit Points last for 1 minute.

Blood Magic: Additionally, Necromantic Energy flows through the area. Either choose to gain 3 temporary hit points until the end of your next round, or deal 3 extra negative damage to a single target.
