Research Ritualized Casting


DOWNTIME

EXPLORATION

By spending 8 hours researching lore related to your region and your particular method of casting magic, you can attempt to find nearby areas and times ideal for casting a spell in a ritualized fashion. To do so, make an appropriate Recall Knowledge check using the skill associated with your spellcasting tradition (Arcana for Arcane, Occultism for Occult, Nature for Primal, and Religion for Divine.) The DC Is hard for the tier of the spell you are researching.


  • Critical Sucess: You find a location and time which is nearby (within a day) and ideal to your time frame (within 24 hours of when you are hoping for.) The location bonus is +3.
  • Succes: As critical success, except either your location is far (within 1d4+1 days) or the timing is bad (within 1d4+1 days of your ideal timing) and the location bonus is +2.
  • Failure: As success, except your location is far AND the timing is bad, and the location bonus is +1.
  • Critical Failure: You are unable to ascertain any useful information, and cannot try again for 2d4 days.
