Supersensitive


-3 points

SUPERNATURAL
You pick up a lot of psychic noise when around other sapient beings. This doesn't give you any useful sort of telepathy, but you do hear a sort of psychic "mumbling" that grows more intense the more people around you. If there is even one sapient being within 50 feet of you, you are clumsy 1 and stupefied 1. These each increase to 2 if there are 10 or more people, or 3 for 100 or more, or 4 for more than a thousand.

The advantage to this, however, is it counts as an imprecise sense for detecting sapient life - even through walls and other barriers!
