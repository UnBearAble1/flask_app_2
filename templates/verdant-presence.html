Verdant Presence


10 Points
Spellcaster; Plants

Reaction; Frequency once per hour; Trigger You take damage or cast a non-cantrip primal spell. Prerequisites: Master in Spellcaster (Primal)

The life-giving properties of the aeon orbs flourish within you. Small plants or fungi magically appear on all surfaces in a 60-foot emanation around you. This life withers after 1 day unless the environment can support it. You can choose one of the following options each time you use this feat.

  • You can cause any number of 5-foot squares in the area to be light undergrowth,
    provides lesser cover. Up to 8 of those squares can be difficult terrain.
  • You can cause a tree or giant mushroom to appear in any unoccupied location in the area; it occupies an entire 5-foot square and provides standard cover. The tree or mushroom has 5 Hardness and 50 Hit Points.

If the spell you cast to trigger this effect was master or legendary, the undergrowth bears edible fruit or vegetables, the tree bears edible fruit, or the mushrooms themselves are edible.
