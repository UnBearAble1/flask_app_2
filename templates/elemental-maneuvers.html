Elemental Maneuvers


6 Points
Natural Abilities; Elemental
1 Action; Concentrate, Manipulate, Primal, Transmutation, and an element type, or shadow, or force

You batter your foes with some form of energy, shadow or force. If the next action you take is an Athletics check to Disarm, Shove, or Trip, that action has a range of 15 feet. You don't receive any item bonuses to that check or any circumstance bonuses related to your physical body, but if you roll a critical failure on that Athletics check, you get a failure instead.
