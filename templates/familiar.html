Familiar


8 Points
Spellcasting; Familiar
Requires: Trained in Spellcasting

You make a pact with a creature that serves you and assists your spellcasting. You gain a familiar. If your spellcasting is Arcane, occultism or Divine, this familiar is an animal. If it is Primal, it can be an animal or a Leshy (Plant).
