Healing and Dying


These effects restore health, prevent death, or remove harmful conditions or effects.

Fast Healing

Activation: Instantaneous
[Positive, Healing]
Default Range: Touch, Linked Skill: Medicine
Default Duration: Varies (see below)

You gain Fast Healing. Though fast healing itself has a duration, this effect is not considered to have a duration, and is therefore incompatible with limits such as "sustained."

Degree of Healing:

  • Fast Healing 5 (Base: 8)
  • Fast Healing 10 (Base: 16)

Duration:

  • 1 round. Per Additional Round: Mod +4, to a maximum of Mod +40 at 10 additional rounds.

Modifications:

  • Your healing effect can heal you even if you have negative healing (Mod: +6)

Grant Temporary Un-life


[Base: 16]
Activation: Duration
Default Range: Touch, Linked Skill: Medicine
Default Target: 1 Dead Creature

You target a dead creature. It animates, retaining all of the abilities it had in life, but with temporary hit points equal to its normal hit point total. The creature maintains its original motivations and personality, and is in no way controlled by you. Once the temporary hit points have been reduced to 0, this effect ends and the creature cannot be animated once more. The maximum amount of time the creature can have been dead is 3/1d6 rounds (this can be increased with the duration boost), and the maximum duration the reanimation lasts is one minute, but each can be improved using boosts.

Hasten Effect Recovery

[Base: 12]
Activation: Instantaneous
Default Range: Touch

You experience hastened effect recovery. Any detrimental conditions/results of effect that would be gone after 24 hours end, though this doesn't shorten the duration of any active spells/effect affecting you. Note this doesn't count as a 'rest' so cannot help you recover from effects that require a rest.

Negative Healing

[Mod: 5]

You have the negative healing ability, which means you are harmed by positive damage and immune to negative effects as if you were undead. If a Negative effect specifically says it heals creatures with negative healing, it will heal you as well.

Persistent Damage Aid


You automatically are considered as having the requisite items needed to aid in a particular type of persistent damage recovery, chosen upon creation.

  • Common: Bleed, Fire, Acid, Negative, Lightning, Poison [Base: 8]
  • Less Common: Piercing, Slashing, Bludgeoning, Cold, Precision, Sonic, Mental, Alignment, Force [Base: 2]

Reduced First Aid Cooldown

Linked Skill: Medicine


When you use Administer First Aid, the window of time before you can use it again on a damaged creature is reduced. As per Administer First Aid, the maximum amount of healing is: "to a maximum of the amount of damage the creature has taken since the last time Administer First Aid was used on them within the last 24 hours." By selecting this feat, you reduce the time window down:

  • 12 Hours (Mod: 2)
  • 8 Hours
    (Base: 2)
  • 6 Hours (Base: 6)
  • 4 Hours (Base: 8)

Modifications:

  • If this effect only applies to you (though from any use of Administer First Aid, not just yours): (reduce cost by half)

Remove Wounds


Activation: Instantaneous
Default Range: Touch, Linked Skill: Medicine


You remove a wound from a target creature.

  • Remove Serious Wound (Base: 12)
  • Remove Grievous Wound (Base: 16)

Restore Hit Points



Activation: Instantaneous
[Positive, Healing], Linked Skill: Medicine
Default Range: Touch

You restore hit points to a target creature. The cost is based on the amount of hit points restored:

  • 1d8 hit points (Base 8)
  • 2d8 hit points (Base 8, Mod +4)
  • 4d8 hit points (Base 12)
  • 5d8 hit points (Base 12, Mod +4)
  • 6d8 hit points (Base 16)

Modifications:

  • Your healing effect heals creatures with negative healing (Mod +8)
  • You take all of the damage you heal (Mod -8)

Shift Hit Points


[Base: 12]
Activation: Instantaneous
Range: 30 feet
Default Area: You and 1 other creature within range

You can pool all of the current hit points between two willing creatures (this can be improved by increasing the area), then redistribute them as you wish. Distribute the Hit Points of all targets however you choose, except that no target can receive fewer than 1 Hit Point or more than their maximum Hit Points.

Dying and Recovery Check Effects


Reduce Dying Condition
[Base: 4]
Activation: instantaneous, Linked Skill: Medicine
Default Range: Touch
You reduce the dying condition of a target creature by 1.


Lessened Dying Condition
[Mod: 4]
Your dying condition starts at dying 1, instead of the number of wounds you have


Harder to Kill
[Mod: 4]
You Die at Dying 5, not Dying 4


Stay Standing
[Base: 12]
Activation: instantaneous
Default Action: Reaction
As a reaction to getting reduced to 0 hit points, you instead get dropped to 1 hp, but still gain a wound.




Improved Recovery Checks
Reduce your recovery check DCs by 1 (Mod: 2 per, maximum reduction of 5.)


Prevent Death
[Base: 12]
Activation: instantaneous, Linked Skill: Medicine
Default Range: 30 feet
Default Action: Reaction
As a reaction to a creature dying, you prevent that creature from dying.


No Body left Behind
[Mod: 1]

When you die, your body disappears with an aesthetic effect (turns to red mist, explodes into falcons, etc.) Choose at creation whether it leaves behind your belongings or not.
