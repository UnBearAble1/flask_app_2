Family Domain: Unity


4 Points
Diplomacy; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Diplomacy; Trained in Religion

You aid and protect your family and community more effectively. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Unity; Abjuration, Fortune

Reaction verbal; Trigger You and 1 or more allies within range are targeted by a spell or ability that allows a saving throw; Range 30 feet; Targets each ally targeted by the triggering spell

You put up a united defense. Each ally can use your saving throw modifier instead of its own against the triggering spell. Each ally decides individually which modifier to use.
