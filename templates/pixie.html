Pixie


Threat: 5

Insatiably curious, overly excitable, and just a bit puckish, pixies are wanderers and tricksters who use their pixie dust to create all sorts of whimsical situations, as well as to defend themselves. Other creatures often have trouble understanding a pixie's rapid, rambling way of speaking.

Recall Knowledge - Fey (Nature)

CN, Small, Fey, Sprite, Controller
Perception +8; low-light vision
Languages Common, Sylvan
Skills Acrobatics +10, Deception +8, Nature +6, Stealth +8
Str -1, Dex +5, Con +1, Int +3, Wis +2, Cha +3
AC 17; Fort +6 , Ref +8 , Will +10; +1 status to all saves vs. magic
HP 30; Weaknesses cold iron 5
Speed 15 feet, fly 45 feet

Actions:

  • Shortsword: Melee Single Action; +7 (+3 / -1)
    (agile, finesse, magical, versatile S), Damage 2d4+5 piercing
  • Longbow: Ranged Single Action; +8 (+3 / -2)
    (deadly 1d10, magical, range increment 100 feet, reload 0, volley 20 feet), Damage 2d8+4 piercing
  • Primal Innate Spells DC 20;


    • Master: invisibility (at will; self only); dispel magic;
    • Trained:
      entangle, faerie fire; illusory disguise;
    • Cantrips: dancing lights, detect magic, ghost sound, shield
  • Sprinkle Pixie Dust; Single Action (manipulate): The pixie sprinkles pixie dust onto one of its arrows. If the pixie hits a creature with that arrow before the pixie's next turn, the arrow inflicts one of the following special effects instead of dealing damage. Each effect depends on the target's DC 20 Will save. On a critical hit, the target treats its save result as one degree worse.

    • Charm (emotion, enchantment, incapacitation, mental) The target suffers the effects of a charm spell, except it doesn't gain a bonus to its save if the only hostile act was the pixie firing its bow, and the pixie can choose to direct the target's adoration toward another creature rather than itself.
    • Memory Loss (divination, mental) On a failed Will save, the target loses the last 5 minutes of its memory.
    • Sleep (enchantment, incapacitation, mental, sleep) The target suffers the effects of a expert-level sleep spell.
    • Subdual (enchantment, mental, nonlethal) The target takes 4d6 mental damage, depending on its basic Will save.
