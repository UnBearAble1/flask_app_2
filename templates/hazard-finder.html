Hazard Finder


4 Points
Perception; Hazards
Prerequisites: Expert in Perception

You have an intuitive ability to sense hazards. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Perception checks to find traps and hazards, to AC against their attacks, and to saves against their effects. Even if you aren't Searching, you get a check to find hazards that normally require you to be Searching. You still need to meet any other requirements to find the hazards.
