Leng Sting


LEGENDARY
Necromancy, Poison
Traditions arcane, occult
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Saving Throw Fortitude

You use the power of nightmares to magically replicate the venomous attack of the aberrant and cunning Leng spiders. You deal 4d6 piercing damage to the touched creature and afflict it with Leng spider venom. If the target takes piercing damage, they must attempt a Fortitude save.


  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target takes 4d6 poison damage.
  • Failure The target is afflicted with Leng spider venom at stage 1.
  • Critical Failure The target is afflicted with Leng spider venom at stage 2.

Leng Spider Venom (incapacitation, poison): Maximum Duration 6 rounds;
Stage 1 3d6 poison damage and drained 1 (1 round);
Stage 2 4d6 poison damage and drained 2 (1 round);
Stage 3 5d6 poison damage, drained 2, and confused (1 round)
