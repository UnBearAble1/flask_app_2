Antidote


Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir, Healing
Usage
held in 1 hand;
Bulk
L
Activate
Single Action,
Interact

An antidote protects you against toxins. Upon drinking an antidote, you gain an item bonus to Fortitude saving throws against poisons for 6 hours.

TRAINED:
Cost: 5 gold
You gain a +2 item bonus.

EXPERT:
Cost: 10 gold
You gain a +3 item bonus.

MASTER:
Cost: 20 gold
You gain a +4 item bonus.

LEGENDARY:
Cost: 50 gold
You gain a +4 item bonus, and when you drink the antidote, you can immediately attempt a saving throw against one poison of master tier or lower affecting you. If you succeed, the poison is neutralized.
