Improvised Pummel


1 Point
Warrior; Improvised Weapons
Requirements You are wielding an improvised weapon; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior, Improvised Weapon Fighter

You make a Strike with your wielded improvised weapon. You gain a +1 item bonus to the attack roll. If the attack is a critical hit, in addition to the effect of the critical hit, the improvised weapon breaks. If the item has an especially high hardness, or if it's an artifact, cursed item, or other item that's difficult to break or destroy, the item doesn't break and the attack is a hit instead of a critical hit.
