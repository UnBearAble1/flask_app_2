Frightful Presence (Monster)


(aura, emotion, fear, mental) A Non allied creature that first enters the area must attempt a Will save. Regardless of the result of the saving throw, the creature is temporarily immune to this monster's Frightful Presence for 1 minute.

  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected by the presence.
  • Success The creature is frightened 1.
  • Failure The creature is frightened 2.
  • Critical Failure The creature is frightened 4.
