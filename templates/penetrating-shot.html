Penetrating Shot


5 Points
Warrior; Hunt Prey, Ranged

2 Actions; Open ; Requirements You are wielding a ranged weapon; Prerequisites: Hunt Prey; Master in Warrior

You shoot clear through an intervening creature to hit your prey. Choose a target that is giving lesser cover to your hunted prey. Make a single ranged Strike with the required weapon against the chosen target and your hunted prey. This attack ignores any lesser cover the chosen target provides your hunted prey. Roll damage and hit location only once, and apply it to each creature you hit. A Penetrating Shot counts as two attacks for your multiple attack penalty.
