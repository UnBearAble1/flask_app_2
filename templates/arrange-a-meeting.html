Arrange a Meeting


EXPLORATION
You reach out to your contacts, do research, or hit the streets, businesses, and gathering places in an attempt to arrange a meeting with an important person. The GM determines the DC of the check
and the amount of time it takes (typically 2 hours, but sometimes more), along with any benefit you might be able to gain by spending coin on bribes, drinks, or gifts. It may be impossible to reach a certain individual without first meeting with one or more others first and convincing them. Others may be impossible to reach at all.

  • Critical Success You arrange a meeting.
  • Success: You arrange a meeting, likely with strings attached.
  • Critical Failure You draw the wrong sorts of attention, or get locked out of certain circles.
