Airburst


Air; Evocation
Traditions arcane, primal
One Action; verbal
Area 10-foot emanation; Targets all creatures and unattended objects in the area

A blast of wind wildly pushes everything nearby. Unattended objects of 1 Bulk or less are pushed 10 feet away from you. Large or smaller creatures must attempt a Fortitude save.


  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature takes a -2 status penalty to checks made during its reactions until the end of your turn.
  • Failure As success, and the creature is pushed 10 feet away from you.
  • Critical Failure The creature is pushed 20 feet away from you and can't use reactions until the end of your turn.

Heightened (Expert) Increase the area to a 20-foot emanation and increase the distance objects and creatures are pushed to 15 feet (or 30 on a critical failure)
