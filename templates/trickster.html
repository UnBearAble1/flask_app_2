Trickster


-3 points

MENTAL, CHAOTIC
You are compelled to prank truly dangerous foes. Each day, make a self-control check. On a failure, you must seek out a skilled warrior, a dangerous monster, or a whole group of reasonably competent opponents and play a prank on them. It's the danger you are addicted to, so you must do something that has real risk to it. If you succeed on your self-control check, you get a cumulative -1 each day you succeed until you finally fail.
