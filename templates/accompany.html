Accompany


3 Points
Performance; Teamwork
Prerequisites: Expert in Performance

Reaction; Concentrate, Manipulation; Trigger An ally within 30 feet Casts a Spell.

You use your performances to supplement an ally's spellcasting, providing magical energy for their spells. You have two options:

If the triggering ally is attempting to cast a Focus Spell, attempt a Performance check, using a Master DC (Moderate), and spend the same number of Focus Points the caster would have to cast the spell. If you succeed at the Performance check, your ally doesn't need to spend the focus points.

If the triggering ally is attempting to cast a Spell using Spellcasting, you attempt to channel the spell energy for them. Make a spellcasting check of the same DC the caster would have, but using your total Performance skill modifier (with a maximum value equal to the spellcaster's Spellcaster bonus.) You take all of the results of the Spellcasting roll, including any miscast surges and accumulated strain.
