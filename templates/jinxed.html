Jinxed


3 Points
Natural Abilities; Luck
2 actions; Usable once per day; Misfortune

You can curse another creature with clumsiness. This curse has a range of 30 feet, and you must be able to see your target. The target gets a Will saving throw to resist this jinx.

  • Success The target is unaffected and is temporarily immune for 24 hours.
  • Failure The target is clumsy 1 for 1 minute.
  • Critical Failure The target is clumsy 2 for 1 minute.
