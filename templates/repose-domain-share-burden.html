Repose Domain: Share Burden


6 Points
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Religion

You ease mental burdens. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Share Burden; Abjuration, Mental

Reaction; somatic; Trigger An ally within 30 feet is about to attempt a saving throw against a harmful emotion effect, and you would not otherwise have to save against the effect.

You shoulder some of your ally's pain. Both you and your ally use the outcome one degree of success better than the result of your ally's saving throw, even if the triggering effect usually affects only one target.
