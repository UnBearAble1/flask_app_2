Protection Domain: Protector's Sacrifice


3 Points
Religion; Domain Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Religion

You ward yourself and others. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Protector's Sacrifice; Abjuration

Reaction somatic; Trigger An ally within 30 feet takes damage; Range 30 feet

You protect your ally by suffering in their stead. Reduce the damage the triggering ally would take by 15. You redirect this damage to yourself, but your immunities, weaknesses, resistances and so on do not apply.



You aren't subject to any conditions or other effects of whatever damaged your ally (such as poison from a venomous bite). Your ally is still subject to those effects even if you redirect all of the triggering damage to yourself.
