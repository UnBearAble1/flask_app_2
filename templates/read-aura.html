Read Aura


Cantrip, Detection, Divination
Traditions arcane, divine, occult, primal
Cast: 1 minute (somatic, verbal)
Range 30 feet; Targets up to 10 objects

You focus on the target object(s), opening your mind to perceive magical auras. When the casting is complete, you know whether that item is magical, and if it is, you learn the school of magic.

If your read aura would foil an illusion by revealing it is illusion magic, the GM will make a secret spellcasting check for you vs the effect. On a failure, you know it is magical, but misread the school of magic. On a critical failure, you don't detect the magic at all. However, items that have an illusion aura but aren't deceptive in appearance (such as an invisibility potion) typically are read normally.
