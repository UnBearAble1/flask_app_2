Shadow Bloodline Focus: Steal Shadow


5 Points
occultism; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Shadow Bloodline Focus: Dim the LIght

You gain the focus spell, Steal Shadow, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Steal Shadow

Necromancy, Shadow

2 Actions; somatic, verbal; Range 60 feet; Targets 1 creature; Saving Throw Fortitude; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

You steal a creature's shadow, leaving it weakened and vulnerable. The target must attempt a Fortitude save.

  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target is enfeebled 1 for 1 round.
  • Failure The target takes 4d4 negative damage and is enfeebled 1 for the spell's duration as you tear its shadow away, collecting it in your hand. On subsequent rounds, when you Sustain the Spell, you can throttle the shadow to deal 4d4 negative damage to the target (basic Fortitude save). Until the spell ends, the target doesn't cast a shadow.
  • Critical Failure As failure, but the target is enfeebled 2.



    Additionally, Shadows grow deeper around you, imposing a -1 status penalty to Perception for 1 round to your target.
