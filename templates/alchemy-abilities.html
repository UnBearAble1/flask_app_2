Alchemy Abilities


These abilities are centered around improving your ability to use Alchemy.

Enhanced Alchemy Rolls



You can make quick alchemy rolls with certain bonuses:



  • +1 to the Quick Alchemy Roll (60 points, Tier C)
  • +2 to the Quick Alchemy Roll (120 points, Tier B)
  • Rolled with Advantage (200 points, Tier A)
  • Turn a critical failure to a failure (40 points, Tier C)

Extra Advanced Alchemy Uses



Once, when you use Advanced Alchemy during your daily preparations, you create two items instead of one. (35 points, Tier C)



  • Per additional use per daily preparation: (+25 points, +1 Tier every 3 uses)

Modifications:



  • Limited to your maximum tier -1 or lower items (-10%)
  • Limited to your maximum tier -2 or lower items (-25%)
  • Limited to your maximum tier -3 or lower items (-50%, Tier Mod -1)
  • Limited to either Elixirs, Bombs, or Ignitions, specified when you select this (-30%)
  • Limited to either Poisons or Tools, specified when you select this (-60%)

Quick Alchemy Potency


Items you craft with Quick Alchemy remain potent for one additional round (Tier C, 30 points)
