Unhealing


-4 or -6 points

PHYSICAL
You cannot recover hit points through rest or natural healing. No use of the medicine skill works on you. There are two levels of this complication:


  • Partial [-4 points]:
    You can heal naturally if a rare condition is met, such as when bathing in blood or in a wild magic zone.
  • Full [-6 points] You can never heal naturally.
