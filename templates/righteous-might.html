Righteous Might


MASTER
Polymorph, Transmutation
Traditions divine
Cast Two Actions somatic, verbal; Requirements You have a deity.
Duration 1 minute

You focus all your divine energy to arm yourself with powerful divine armaments granted by your deity. While this spell is active, you cannot cast spells. You can Dismiss the spell.

You gain the following modifications and abilities:

  • AC: 21 (unless your AC was already better)
  • 15 temporary Hit Points.
  • Speed 40 feet.
  • Resistance 3 against physical damage.
  • Darkvision.
  • A special attack with a righteous armament version of your favored weapon, which is the only attack you can use. The attack is +11 to hit, and deals 2d8+5 points of damage of the normal type for the weapon, as well as +1d8 alignment damage (based on your deity.)
  • Athletics of +12 (unless yours is higher)
