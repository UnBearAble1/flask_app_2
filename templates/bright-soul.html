Bright Soul


1 Points
Physiology; Elemental
The fire inside you asserts itself as beaming, radiant light. Your body is naturally luminescent, glowing with the effects of a primal light cantrip. The light is involuntary and constant; if counteracted, it returns in 1d4 rounds. You suffer a -2 circumstance penalty on Stealth checks to Hide or Sneak (and may be unable to hide or sneak at all in darkness) and gain a +2 circumstance bonus on saves against light effects and effects that inflict the blinded or dazzled conditions.
