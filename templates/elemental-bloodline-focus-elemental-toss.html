Elemental Bloodline Focus: Elemental Toss


4 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Expert in Nature

You gain the focus spell, Elemental Toss, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Elemental Toss; Attack, Evocation;

1 Action; somatic; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature

With a flick of your wrist, you fling a chunk of your elemental matter at your foe. Make a spell attack roll, dealing 5d8+3 bludgeoning damage (or fire damage if your element is fire) on a success, and double damage on a critical success. This spell has your element's trait.
