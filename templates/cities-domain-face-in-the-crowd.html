Cities Domain: Face in the Crowd


3 Points
Society; Focus Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Society; Trained in Religion

You have powers over urban environments and denizens. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Face in the Crowd; Illusion, Visual

1 Action; somatic; Duration 1 minute; Range: 10 feet; Targets: You and up to 10 willing creatures.

While in a crowd of roughly similar creatures, your appearance becomes bland and nondescript. You gain a +2 status bonus to Deception and Stealth checks to go unnoticed among the crowd, and you ignore difficult terrain caused by the crowd.
