Nymph Bloodline Focus: Establishing Ward


10 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Nymph Bloodline Focus: Blinding Beauty; Master in Nature

You gain the focus spell, Establishing Ward, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Establishing Ward; Abjuration

2 Actions; somatic, verbal; Area 30-foot burst; Duration 1 minute

You bind a part of your essence to your surroundings, creating a warded area that grants you power within it, but drains you outside of it. While within the area, you can make nature's wrath Strikes by commanding plants, wind, rocks, and other natural features to attack enemies. These are melee Strikes that use your Nature skill as an attack modifier, can target any creature within the ward, and deal bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage equal to 3d6 plus your wisdom modifier.



Upon Casting the Spell, you can immediately make a single nature's wrath Strike. You are quickened while inside the ward, and can use the additional action only to make nature's wrath Strikes. You are slowed 1 while outside the area.



Additionally, when you cast this spell and until the end of your next turn, Nymph grace distracts your foes, imposing a -1 status penalty on one target's Will saves for 1 round.
