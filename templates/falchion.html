This weapon is a heavier, two-handed version of the curved-bladed scimitar. It is weighted toward the blade's end, making it a powerful slashing weapon.

Forceful:
This weapon becomes more dangerous as you build momentum. When you attack with it more than once on your turn, the second attack gains a +2 circumstance bonus to damage, and each subsequent attack gains a circumstance +4 bonus to damage

Sweep:
This weapon makes wide sweeping or spinning attacks, making it easier to attack multiple enemies. When you attack with this weapon, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your attack roll if you already attempted to attack a different target this turn using this weapon.
