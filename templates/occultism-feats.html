  • Disturbing Knowledge [M] -6 pts: Confuse or frighten 1 or more targets

    • Focused Locks: [E] -4 pts: Your hair has reach, and you can get a bonus attack with it after you cast a focus spell
  • Root Magic [T] -2 pts: Create an item that gives bonuses to first save vs spell/haunt
  • Scholarly Defense [M] -4 pts: Gain +4 DR against aberrations and oozes

Soul Domain

Void Domain

SPIRIT HUNTER


  • Spirit Hunter [T] -1 pt: You become trained in Spirit or Haunt Lore
  • Spectral Strike [E] -3 pts: 1x a day, you can make weapons ghost touch for 10 mins
  • Grave's Voice [E*] -5 pts: Use spirit/haunt lore as diplomacy with spirits
  • Investigate Haunting [E*] -3 pts: Always get to recall knowledge at +2 about incorporeal undead or haunts in the area
  • Peer Beyond [M*] -4 pts: Get +2 saves against incorporeal undead and haunts, also instantly gain insight as to how to permanently put them to rest

*Spirit or Haunt Lore requirement
