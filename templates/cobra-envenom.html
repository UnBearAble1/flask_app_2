Cobra Envenom


4 Points
Warrior; Ki Arts

1 Action; Frequency once per minute; Requirements You are in Cobra Stance; Prerequisites: Cobra Stance

You lash out with devious intent and the power to envenom your foe. Make a cobra fang Strike. Your reach with this Strike is 5 feet greater than normal. If this Strike hits, the target takes 2d4 persistent poison damage. Special: If you have this feat, the circumstance bonus to Fortitude saves and Fortitude DC granted by Cobra Stance increases from +1 to +2.
