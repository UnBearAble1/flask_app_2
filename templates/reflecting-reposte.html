Reflecting Reposte


5 Points
Warrior; Variable

Reaction; Requirements You have a shield raised or are benefiting from Dueling Parry or Twin Parry; Trigger An opponent casts a spell and you critically succeed at a saving throw against it; Prerequisites Dueling Reposte, Mirror Shield, or Twin Reposte; Legendary in Warrior

Use your attack roll modifier with your shield or parrying weapon for your counteract check. If you counteract the spell, it is reflected upon the caster. If the spell targeted individual creatures, it targets the caster only. If the spell targeted an area centered on a point, the new area is centered on the caster. If the spell was a cone or line, the cone or line emanates from you toward the caster. If the spell has a save DC, it uses your class DC, and if it requires a spell attack roll, use the same attack roll modifier as the counteract check.
