Family Domain: Soothing Words


4 Points
Diplomacy; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Master in Diplomacy; Trained in Religion

You aid and protect your family and community more effectively. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Soothing Words; Emotion, Enchantment, Mental

1 Action; verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 ally; Duration 1 round

You attempt to calm the target by uttering soothing words in a calm and even tone. The target gains a +2 status bonus to Will saving throws. This bonus increases to +3 against emotion effects. In addition, when you Cast this Spell, you can attempt to counteract one emotion effect on the target.
