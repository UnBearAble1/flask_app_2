Remove Greater Curse


LEGENDARY
Rare, Healing, Necromancy
Cast 24 hours; Cost rare minerals worth 1000 gp; Secondary Casters 2
Primary Check Religion or Occultism (Legendary); Secondary Checks Arcana, Nature, Occultism, or Religion
Range 10 feet; Target(s) 1 greater or similar curse

This powerful ritual can attempt to remove a curse or similar effect that normally can only be removed by a deific or artifact effect. Exactly what can be removed is determined by the GM, and each individual effect requires its own special components (for example, curing a creature of vampirism may take the heart of the vampire who turned them).


  • Critical Success: You remove the effect entirely
  • Success: As critical success, but the process is exhausting to the effected creature. They are clumsy 2, drained 2, and stupefied 2 for 1 week.
  • Failure: You fail to remove the effect.
  • Critical Failure: You fail to remove the effect and all casters must immediately make a fortitude or willpower saving throw (as appropriate to the curse) against the ritual's DC or contract the curse or condition themselves.
