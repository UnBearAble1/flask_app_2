Nymph Bloodline


10 Points
Spellcaster; Magical Bloodine
Prerequisites: Trained in Spellcaster (Primal)

One of your ancestors was inspired by a nymph, or perhaps was a nymph themself, and now the beauty of nature lives in you. Add the following spells to your repertoire, even if they aren't normally part of the primal tradition (note you must still have the appropriate level of spellcaster proficiency to cast the spell):

  • cantrip: tanglefoot,
  • Trained: charm, calm emotions,
  • Expert: animal vision, vital beacon,
  • Master: crushing despair, repulsion,
  • Legendary: unfettered pack, moment of renewal,

Whenever you cast a spell from the list of spells above, gain the following Blood Magic ability: Nymph grace accentuates your movements and distracts your foes, either granting you a +1 status bonus to Diplomacy checks for 1 round or imposing a -1 status penalty on one target's Will saves for 1 round.
