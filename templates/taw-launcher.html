Designed by conrasu warriors, this complex device is a crossbow and fires small wooden bullets known as taws. A system of blades within the launcher can rapidly reshape a taw as it's loaded, allowing the launcher to fire taws of different shapes, such as flechettes.

Deadly d10:
On a critical hit, the weapon adds a weapon damage die of the listed size. Roll this after doubling the weapon's damage.

Modular (B, P, or S):
The weapon has multiple configurations that you can switch between using an Interact action. Typically, switching between configurations of a modular weapon allows it to deal different types of damage (listed in the trait, such as "modular B, P, or S"), though it's possible for a modular weapon's description to list more complicated configurations.

Uncommon:
Something of uncommon rarity requires special training or comes from a particular culture or part of the world.
