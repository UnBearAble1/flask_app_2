Cauldron


2 Points
Crafting; Craft Specific Items
Prerequisites: Expert in Crafting

You can use the Craft activity to create non-mutagen alchemical elixirs and drugs. You can brew a great deal of magic within your cauldron. When you Craft a batch of elixirs or drugs, you can make up to six in a batch instead of the usual four per batch.
