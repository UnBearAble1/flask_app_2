Radiant Burst


2 Points
Natural Abilities; Elemental
2 Actions; Usable once per day; Fire, Incapacitation, Light, Visual

Your skin glows with intensity. Creatures within 10 feet who can see you must succeed at a Fortitude save


  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature is dazzled for 1 round.

  • Failure The creature is blinded for 1 round and dazzled for 4 rounds.
  • Critical Failure The creature is blinded for 4 rounds and dazzled for 10 minutes.
