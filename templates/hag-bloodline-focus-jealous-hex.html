Hag Bloodline Focus: Jealous Hex


2 Point
occultism; Focus Spell
Prerequisites: Expert in occultism

You gain the focus spell, Jealous Hex, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Curse, Necromancy

1 Action; verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature; Saving Throw Will; Duration up to 1 minute

You draw forth a hag's innate jealousy to deny a target its greatest attribute. The target is cursed with an adverse condition depending on its highest ability modifier: Strength (enfeebled); Dexterity (clumsy); Constitution (Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma (stupefied). On a tie, the creature decides which of the conditions associated with the tied ability modifiers to take. The target must attempt a Will save.

  • Success The target is unaffected.
  • Failure The condition's value is 1.
  • Critical Failure The condition's value is 2.

At the start of each of your turns, the target can attempt another Will save, ending the effect on a success. Additionally, once you cast this spell, The first creature that deals damage to you before the end of your next turn takes 6 mental damage (basic Will save.)
