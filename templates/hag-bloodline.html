Hag Bloodline


11 Points
Spellcaster; Magical Bloodine
Prerequisites: Trained in Spellcaster (occult)

A hag long ago cursed your family, or you are a descendant of a hag or changeling, and their accursed corruption infests your blood and soul. Add the following spells to your repertoire, even if they aren't normally part of the occult tradition(note you must still have the appropriate level of spellcaster proficiency to cast the spell):

  • cantrip: daze,
  • Trained: illusory disguise, touch of idiocy,
  • Expert: blindness, outcast's curse,
  • Master: mariner's curse, baleful polymorph,
  • Legendary: warp mind, spiritual epidemic,

Whenever you cast a spell from the list of spells above, gain the following Blood Magic ability: Spiteful curses punish your foes. The first creature that deals damage to you before the end of your next turn takes 4 mental damage per spell tier and must attempt a basic Will save.
