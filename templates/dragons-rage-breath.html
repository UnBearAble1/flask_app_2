Dragon's Rage Breath


7 Points
Warrior; Rage

Two Actions; Requires: You haven't used this ability since you last Raged; Concentrate, Evocation, Instinct, Rage; Prerequisites: dragon instinct and Master in Warrior

You breathe deeply and exhale powerful energy in a 30-foot cone or 60-foot line, dealing 10d6 damage. The area and damage type match those of your dragon (If you used this ability in the last hour, the area and the damage are halved (15-foot cone or 30-foot line; 5d6 damage). Each creature in the area must attempt a basic Reflex save.
