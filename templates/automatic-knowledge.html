Automatic Knowledge


2 Points
Knowledge Skills; Recall Knowledge
Prerequisites expert in a Knowledge Skill

You know basic facts off the top of your head. Choose a skill you're an expert in that has the Recall Knowledge action.
You can use the Recall Knowledge action with that skill as a free action once per round. If you do, you must take 10 (instead of rolling), only adding your raw proficiency bonus with no other modifiers (including attribute bonuses.)


Special: You can select this feat multiple times, choosing a different skill each time. You can use Automatic Knowledge with any skills you have chosen, but you can still use Automatic Knowledge only once per round.
