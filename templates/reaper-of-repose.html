Reaper of Repose


2 Points
Religion/Warrior; Undead Hunter
Prerequisites: Expert in Religion; Master in Warrior

Your skill and fervor allow you to pierce undead defenses and exploit their weaknesses. When you make an attack using a weapon and hit an undead creature, you ignore 5 points of the creature's resistances, and the undead's weaknesses are 2 higher against your attack.
