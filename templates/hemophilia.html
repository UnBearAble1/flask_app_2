Hemophilia


-6 points

PHYSICAL
You bleed a lot when your skin is punctured. Anytime you take bludgeoning, slashing or piercing damage you take an additional 1d6 persistent bleed. On a critical hit, you take an additional 2d6 persistent bleed. Checks to end persistent bleed damage are 2 points more difficult, so a traditional flat DC15 check would be DC17, and with assistance, DC12 instead of DC10.

You cannot take any abilities that give you resistance toward or any benefit against persistent bleed damage.
