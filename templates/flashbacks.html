Flashbacks


-1 to -4 points

MENTAL
You sometimes experience flashbacks while under stress. These flashbacks are hallucinations, participatory memories, or similar phenomena. When the DM feels you are in a stressful situation, they will make a DC 16 flat check. On a success, you will experience a flashback. If you fail a self-control check for another stress based disadvantage, you will experience these flashbacks in tandem!

Flashback's point value is based on the severity:

  • Mild [-1 pt]: Duration 1d4 rounds. You realize you are experiencing hallucinations, but are clumsy 2 during this time.
  • Severe: [-2 pts] Duration is 1d4 minutes. The hallucinations seem real, making you clumsy 2 and dazzled during this time.
  • Crippling: [-4 pts] Duration is 2d6 minutes. As Severe, but the flashback seems completely, 100% real, and can be potentially fatal, as you are receiving no input from the real world.
