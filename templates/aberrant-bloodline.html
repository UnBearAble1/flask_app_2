Aberrant Bloodline


10 Points
Spellcaster; Magical Bloodine
Prerequisites: Trained in Spellcaster (Occult)

Something speaks to you from beyond the stars or below the earth. Ancient and unknowable, this alien influence presses against your mind. Add the following spells to your repertoire, even if they aren't normally part of the Occult tradition (note you must still have the appropriate level of spellcaster proficiency to cast the spell):

  • cantrip: daze,
  • Trained: spider sting and touch of idiocy,
  • Expert: vampiric touch and confusion
  • Master: black tentacles and feeblemind
  • Legendary: warp mind and uncontrollable dance

Whenever you cast a spell from the list of spells above, gain the following Blood Magic ability:



Aberrant whispers:
shield one target's mind or your own, granting a +2 status bonus to Will saving throws for 1 round.
