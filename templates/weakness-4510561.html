Weakness


-1 to -10 points

SUPERNATURAL
You take damage merely from being in the presence of a certain thing.
You take this damage immediately, then in the interval shown. This damage cannot be resisted or mitigated in any way. The more quickly you take damage, the more the value of this complication:

  • Rapid: [4 pts] 5d6 damage per minute
  • Moderate: [2 pts] 5d6 damage per 5 minutes
  • Slow: [1 pt] 5d6 damage per 10 minutes

You also gain a multiplier based on how rare the substance is:

  • Rare (e.g., exotic minerals): x 1/2
  • Occasional: (e.g exposed blood, airborne pollen): x1.
  • Common (e.g., smoke, nearby magic, horses, loud noises): x2.
  • Very Common (e.g., sunlight, living plants): x3

Special: You can't take more than two weaknesses.
