Wall of Radiance


EXPERT
Evocation, Light
Traditions arcane, primal
Three Actions; material, somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Duration 1 minute

You form a wall of brilliant light that obscures creatures. You create a thin wall of light in a straight line up to 60 feet long and 10 feet high. You must create the wall in an unbroken open space so its edges don't pass through any creatures or objects, or the spell is lost. The wall causes bright light within 50 feet, and dim light 50 feet beyond that. Creatures are concealed to other creatures on the opposite side of the wall from them.The wall stands vertically. If you wish, the wall can be of a shorter length or height. Creatures adjacent to the wall are dazzled. Creatures that move through the wall take 6d6 fire damage (basic Fortitude save) as the concentrated light burns them.
