Tin Cobra


EXPERT
Uncommon, Clockwork, Consumable, Mechanical, Poison, Snare, Trap
Price 18 gp

This clockwork cobra activates when a creature enters its square, at which point it lashes out and spits venom, dealing 3d6 poison damage. The target must attempt a DC 18 Fortitude save.


  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature takes half damage.
  • Failure The creature takes full damage and is sickened 1.
  • Critical Failure The creature takes double damage is sickened 2.
