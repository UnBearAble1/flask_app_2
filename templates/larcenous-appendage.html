Larcenous Appendage


1 Point
Physiology; Useful Bonus Appendage
Prerequisites Useful Bonus Appendage

Your Appendage's chaotic and independent nature causes it to perform mischief even when you don't intend it, and it can somehow steal things it shouldn't be able to handle. You can use it to Steal an object, though it still can't hold onto it at the end of your action. If you have a hand free, you can transfer the stolen object to your hand. Otherwise, your appendage surreptitiously drops it on the ground in your space, where you can grab it later. Observers other than your target who haven't witnessed you stealing something with it are usually distracted, meaning the GM will usually decrease their Perception DCs, as normal for Stealing something.
