Blightburn Blast


LEGENDARY
Disease, Evocation
Traditions arcane, occult, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 15-foot cone
Saving Throw basic Fortitude

You channel a blast of powerful blightburn radiation from your hands. You deal anywhere from 1d6 to 12d6 poison damage to creatures in the area (you choose the damage amount). Creatures who fail their save are afflicted with stage 1 of blightburn sickness (on a critical failure, the creature moves immediately to stage 2).

Blightburn Sickness; Onset 1d4 days; The sickened condition cannot be reduced until the drained condition is removed
Stage 1 drained 1 (1 day);
Stage 2 drained 1 and sickened 1 (1 day);
Stage 3 drained 2 and sickened 2 (1 week);
Stage 4 drained 3 and sickened 3 (1 month);
Stage 5 increase drained condition by 1 (1 year)
