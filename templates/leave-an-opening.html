Leave an Opening


2 Points
Warrior; Teamwork
Prerequisite: Master in Warrior

When you hit hard enough, you leave an opening so your ally can jump in on the action. Whenever you critically hit a flat-footed opponent with a melee attack and deal damage, the target triggers any reactions from your allies that would trigger
if the enemy had used a manipulate action.
