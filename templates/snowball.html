Snowball


TRAINED

Attack, Cold, Evocation, Water
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature

You throw a magically propelled and chilled ball of dense snow. The target takes 6d8 cold damage and potentially other effects, depending on the result of your spell attack roll.


  • Critical Success The target takes double damage and a -10-foot status penalty to its Speeds for 1 round.
  • Success The target takes full damage and a -5-foot status penalty to its Speeds for 1 round.
  • Failure No effect.

Heightened : The damage increases by 3d8.
