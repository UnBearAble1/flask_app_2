Join Pasts


Uncommon, Cantrip, Divination, Mental
Traditions: occult
One Action somatic
Range touch; Targets Up to 6 willing creatures; Duration 1 round

With touches to the targets' foreheads, you bring them into mental communion. The targets can share thoughts and experiences, but not words, allowing creatures to have "experienced" the situation as if they were there (though not conferring any bonuses, conditions, or other effects from having lived through the situation, just the memories).
When one target attempts to Recall Knowledge, the other can Aid the first target's skill check, using any Lore skill (even if that Lore wouldn't normally apply) without having made any preparations to Aid.
