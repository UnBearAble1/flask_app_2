Temporary Potions


7 Points
Crafting; Craft Specific Items
Prerequisites Cauldron

During your daily preparations, you can create a batch of two temporary non mutagen alchemical elixirs or drugs using a formula you know (you begin play with 3 formulas of trained-tier). These items follow the normal rules to Craft them, except for the time they take, with some additional restrictions. They must both be the same type of elixir or drug, and they may only be trained-tier items. Any items you create this way become inert bottles of liquid the next time you make your daily preparations, and any remaining effects of the temporary items end. A temporary item has no value. If you have master proficiency in your tradition's spell DCs, you can create a batch of three temporary elixirs or drugs during your daily preparations, and if you have legendary proficiency, you can create a batch of four.
