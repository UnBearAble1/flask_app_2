Summon Instrument


Cantrip, Conjuration
Traditions: divine, occult
Three Actions material, somatic, verbal
Duration: 1 hour

You materialize a handheld musical instrument in your grasp. The instrument is virtuoso, but it plays only for you. The instrument vanishes when the spell ends. If you cast summon instrument again, any instrument you previously summoned disappears.
