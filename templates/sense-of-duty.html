Sense of Duty


-1 to -4 points

RELATIONSHIPS, LAWFUL
You feel an overwhelming sense of duty toward a particular class of people. You won't abandon them when they are in trouble, betray them, let them suffer or go hungry, nor act against them in any way. Unlike Duty, which is imposed on you, this is how you genuinely feel.

The value of this complication is based on the size of the group you feel the sense of duty toward.

  • Small Group (e.g., your close friends, adventuring companions, or squad): [-1 pt]
  • Large Group (e.g., a nation or religion, or everyone you know personally): [-2 pts]
  • Entire Race (all humanity, all elves, etc.): [-3 pts]
  • Every Living Being: [-4 pts]

You cannot claim points for a Sense of Duty toward Dependents, the point costs of these traits already take such a bond into account.

You can take a Sense of Duty toward adventuring companions. If you do, you must share equipment with and render aid to the other members of your adventuring party, and go along with majority decisions. The DM might make this mandatory in games where the party needs to get along.
