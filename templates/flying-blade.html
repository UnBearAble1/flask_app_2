Flying Blade


3 Points
Warrior; Panache
Prerequisites: Panache

You've learned to apply your flashy techniques to thrown weapons as easily as melee attacks. When you have panache, you apply your bonus precision damage on ranged Strikes you make with a thrown weapon within that weapon's first range increment. The thrown weapon must be an agile or finesse weapon. This also allows you to make a thrown weapon ranged Strike for Confident Finisher and any other finisher that includes a Strike that can benefit from your precise strike.
