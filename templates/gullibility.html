Gullibility


-2 points

MENTAL
You believe lies more easily than others. You take -4 to Sense Motive checks, and even if you (the player) are sure something is a lie, your character must still make a self-control roll or believe the lie! Particularly well crafted lies, or lies about things you are unfamiliar with, give you a -6 to the self control roll.
