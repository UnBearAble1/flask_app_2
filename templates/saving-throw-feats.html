Saving Throw Feats


FORTITUDE


  • Acquired Tolerance [E] -3 pts: Advantage on poison saves (one type a day)
  • Diehard [E] -2 pts: You die at Dying 5 instead of 4

    • Numb to Death [E] -4 pts: Once a day, gain benefits of healed while dying
  • Divine Health [M] -2 pts: Get +1 saves against disease and improved saves
  • Fast Recovery [E] -5 pts: Twice the HP from resting, improved healing against disease and poison, and faster healing of drained condition
  • Inner Strength [E] -5 pts: Reduce enfeebled condition by 1 for an action
  • Lifeblood's Call
    [E] -1 pt: You get a damage bonus = twice your injuries/doomed levels
  • Necromantic Resistance [M] -7 pts: Resist negative 5 and +1 saves against necromancy

    • Necromantic Resilience [M] -3 pts: Improved success on necromantic effects
  • Resist Poison [M] -5 pts: Gain 5 points of poison resistance
  • Immutable Body [M] -3 pts: Get +2 to saves against poisons and diseases
  • Toughness [E] -4 pts: Get +5 max HP and bonus to recovery checks

REFLEX


  • Swift River [M] -8 pts: End penalties to speed/immobilized/slowed at the end of your round
  • Cartwheel Dodge [E] -3 pts: Step 10 feet after a successful reflex save

WILLPOWER


  • Ardent Nature [E] -4 pts: Gain +1 and new roll against attempts to control your actions
  • Aura of Courage [M] -8 pts: Auto reduce frightened by 1, end of turn reduce for allies
  • Bravery [E] -8 pts: Auto reduce frightened by 1, improved success on fear saves


    • Resounding Bravery [E] -3 pts: Gain bonus to save and temp HP when critically saving
  • Cognitive Loophole [E] -4 pts: Reaction to ignore a mental effect for one round
  • Conceited Mindset [E] -1 pt: Get +2 to saves against mental effects, improved success but risk worsened failures
  • Determination [M] -8 pts: Once a day, end a condition or check to end a spell impacting you
  • Enlightened Presence [M] -5 pts: You and nearby allies gain +2 against mental effects.
  • Pure Clarity [M] -8 pts: Once a minute reroll a save against mental effect with +2.


    • Projected Clarity [L] -4 pts: Allies gain the benefits of your pure clarity
  • Shake it Off [E] -4 pts: Reduce frightened and sickened with an action
  • Unshakeable Idealism [E] -4 pts: Get +1 on checks against fear/despair, can be stunned instead of fleeing
  • Ward Mind [M] -4 pts: Ignore a mental effect for a round as a reaction at end of round
