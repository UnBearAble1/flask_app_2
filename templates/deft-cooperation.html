Deft Cooperation


1 Point
Professional; Teamwork
Prerequisites: Expert in Professional

When you successfully Aid a skill check, if you attempt the exact same skill check (not just using the same skill, but using it for the same purpose, for instance a check to Climb the same wall) on your next turn, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your skill check. This improves to +2 if you critically succeeded on your aid.
