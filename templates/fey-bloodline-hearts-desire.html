Fey Bloodline: Heart's Desire


4 Points
Arcana; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Fey Bloodline Focus: Genie's Veil

You gain the focus spell, Heart's Desire, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Emotion, Illusion, Mental

2 Actions; somatic, verbal; Range 60 feet; Targets 2 creatures; Saving Throw Will; Duration varies

You surround your target with wondrous illusions of their greatest desires, which distract them from reality. The target must attempt a Will save.


  • Critical Success The target disbelieves the illusion and is unaffected.
  • Success For 1 round, the target is stupefied 2 and takes a -2 status penalty to any checks it makes to determine the effects of reactions.
  • Failure For 1 minute, the target is stupefied 2 and can't use reactions.
  • Critical Failure As failure, and all spaces are difficult terrain for the target.



    Additionally, your spellcasting warps reality and distracts your foes.. Until the end of your next turn, you gain a +1 status bonus to Deception checks or a target takes a -1 status penalty to Perception for 1 round.
