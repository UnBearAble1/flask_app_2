Magic Sense


5 points
Arcana; Senses
Prerequisites: Master in Arcana

[Detection, Divination]

You have a literal sixth sense for ambient magic in your vicinity. You can sense the presence of magic auras as though you were always using a trained-tier detect magic spell. This detects magic in your field of vision only. When you Seek, you gain the benefits of an expert tier detect magic spell on things you see (in addition to the normal benefits of Seeking). You can turn this sense off and on with a free action at the start or the end of your turn. Special This feat has the trait corresponding to the tradition of spells you cast (arcane, divine, natural, or occult).
