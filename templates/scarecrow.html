Scarecrow


3 Points
Professional; Liberator
Prerequisites Liberator; Expert in Warrior

You fight fiercely to protect your crop. When you hit a creature that is adjacent to two or more members of your crop with a weapon or unarmed attack, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to your damage roll.. When you hit a creature who has a member of your crop grabbed, that member can attempt to Escape using a reaction.
