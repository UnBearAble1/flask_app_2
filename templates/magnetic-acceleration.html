Magnetic Acceleration


EXPERT
Attack, Evocation
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 120 feet; Targets 1 creature or object

You magnetize a small metal object, such as a nail or coin, and launch it away from you at massive speed. Make a ranged spell attack roll; if the target is wearing metal armor or is made of metal, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your attack roll with magnetic acceleration. On a hit, the target takes 5d6 bludgeoning damage and 4d6 piercing damage, or double damage on a critical hit.

Heightened : The bludgeoning and piercing damage each increase by 2d6.
