Susceptible


-1 to -4 points

PHYSICAL
You are particularly weak against some type of contaminant. If you are exposed to even trace amounts, amounts not harmful to others, you must make a saving throw as if you were fully exposed to it in the fashion it normally effects others. Further, you treat your results against your weakness as one category worse (critical success is downgraded to success, for example.) The value of this complication is based on how broad your weakness is:

  • Very Common (e.g., any Disease, any Poison) [-4 pts]
  • Common (e.g., any weaponized disease, any injury Poison): [-3 pts]
  • Occasional (e.g., Intestinal Disease, Ingested Poison): [-1 pt]
