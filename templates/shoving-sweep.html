Shoving Sweep


1 Point
Warrior; Two Handed Weapon
Reaction; Requirements You're wielding a melee weapon in two hands; Trigger A creature within your reach leaves a square during a move action its using; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior and Athletics

You swing your weapon at your foe, rebuffing them back. You attempt to Shove the triggering creature, ignoring the requirement that you have a hand free. The creature continues its movement after the Shove.
