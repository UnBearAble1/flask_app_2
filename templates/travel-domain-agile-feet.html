Travel Domain: Agile Feet


2 Points
Acrobatics; Battlefield Mobility
Prerequisites: Master in Acrobatics; Trained in Religion

You have power over movement and journeys. You gain the following Focus Spell, and increase your Focus Point Pool by 1.



Agile Feet; Transmutation

1 Action,
somatic; Duration until the end of the current turn

The blessings of your god make your feet faster and your movements more fluid. You gain a +5-foot status bonus to your Speed and ignore difficult terrain. As part of casting agile feet, you can Stride or Step; you can instead Burrow, Climb, Fly, or Swim if you have the appropriate Speed.
