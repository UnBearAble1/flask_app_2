Uncommon

25 gp

worn clothing;

Experimental clothing is custom tailored in radical, avant-garde styles by independent dressmakers, utilizing expensive or eccentric materials and unconventional patterns. At the GM's discretion, wearing experimental clothing may impart a +1 item bonus or -1 item penalty on checks to
Make An Impression, depending on the target's fashion sense.
