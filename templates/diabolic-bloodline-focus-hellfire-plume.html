Diabolic Bloodline Focus: Hellfire Plume


5 Points
Religion; Bloodline Spells
Prerequisites: Diabolic Bloodline Focus: Embrace the Pit; Master in Religion

You gain the focus spell, Diabolic Edict, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Hellfire Plume; Evil, Evocation, Fire

2 Actions; somatic, verbal; Range 60 feet; Area 10-foot radius, 60-foot-tall cylinder, Saving Throw basic Reflex

You call forth a plume of hellfire that erupts from below, dealing 4d6+3 fire damage and 4d6 evil damage.
