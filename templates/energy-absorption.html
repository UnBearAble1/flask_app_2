Energy Absorption


5 Points
Arcana; School Spells
Prerequisites: Master in Arcana ; Protective Ward

You gain the energy absorption school spell, and you increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.



Energy Absorption; Abjuration, Wizard

Reaction verbal; Trigger An effect would deal acid, cold, electricity, or fire damage to you.

You gain resistance 20 to acid, cold, electricity, or fire damage from the triggering effect (one type of your choice). The resistance applies only to the triggering effect's initial damage.
