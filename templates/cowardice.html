Cowardice


-5 points

MENTAL
When you have to risk danger, make a self control check or refuse to do so unless genuinely threatened with greater danger. If you must risk death, roll at -4. You are also -2 on all checks versus fear, and -4 against any attempt at intimidation. Take a -2 to reaction rolls with anyone who values courage if they learn of your cowardice.
