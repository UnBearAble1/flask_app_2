Sacrifice Armor


5 Points
Warrior; Armor
Prerequisite: Expert in Warrior

Reaction; Trigger You are hit by a Strike that deals physical damage; Usable once per 10 minutes.

Reduce the damage you take by 20 (plus up to 10 more depending upon how powerful or magical your armor currently is, as determined by the GM) Your armor becomes broken, reducing its Hit Points to its Broken Threshold. If your armor was already damaged, it is destroyed instead.

Boost (+4 points)
Greater Interpose (requires: Master in Warrior) You can use Sacrifice Armor to reduce any type of damage from an attack or effect requiring a Reflex save. Additionally, When you use Sacrifice Armor against a critical hit or a critical failure on a Reflex save that would otherwise cause you to take double damage, you take the normal amount of damage instead (and that amount is still reduced).
