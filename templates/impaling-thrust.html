Impaling Thrust


6 Points
Warrior; Grappling

2 Actions; Requirements You are wielding a melee weapon that deals piercing damage; Prerequisites: Master in Warrior

You impale your enemy and hold them in place with your weapon. Make a melee Strike with the required weapon. If the Strike hits and deals damage, your target is grabbed until they successfully Escape, you attack with the required weapon, or you Release the required weapon, whichever comes first. When the target is no longer grabbed, they take 2 persistent bleed damage.
