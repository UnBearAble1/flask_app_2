Elemental Bloodline Focus: Elemental Blast


7 Points
Nature; Focus Spell
Prerequisites:Elemental Bloodline Focus: Elemental Motion

You gain the focus spell, Elemental Blast, listed below. You also increase your focus pool by 1.



Elemental Blast; Evocation

2 Actions somatic, verbal; Range 30 feet (burst only); Area 10-foot-radius burst, 30-foot cone, or 60-foot line; Saving Throw basic Reflex
*Special: This Focus Spell costs 2 focus points to cast

You gather elemental energy and blast your foes in one of the various listed shapes of your choosing, dealing 8d6+3 bludgeoning damage (or fire damage if your element is fire). This spell has your element's trait.
