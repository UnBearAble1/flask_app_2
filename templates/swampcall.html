Swampcall


TRAINED

Uncommon, Earth, Transmutation
Traditions primal
Two Actions ; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Area 10-foot burst
Duration 1 minute

You call upon the spirits of the soil to twist and churn, transforming the terrain in the targeted area into a sodden mess. The area becomes difficult terrain. Creatures in the area when you Cast this Spell must attempt a Reflex saving throw.


  • Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Failure The creature sinks partially into the mud. The creature takes a -10-foot circumstance penalty to its Speeds (except for its swim Speed, if any) and becomes flat-footed. These effects last until the creature leaves the area or until the end of its next turn, whichever comes first.
  • Critical Failure As failure, but the penalty to Speeds (except Swim speed) is -15 feet.

Heightened (expert) The range increases to 60 feet, and the area increases to a 20-foot burst.
