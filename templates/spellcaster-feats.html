Spellcaster Feats


COUNTERSPELL


  • Absorb Spell [E] -2 pts:
    Crit on a save against a spell, store it and cast it.
  • Counterspell [T] -1+ pts: If you know a spell, reaction lets you use spellcasting to counteract a spell

EXTRA SPELLS


  • Drain Bonded Item [T] -6+ pts: Once a day, cast an already cast spell with advantage
  • Physical Evolution [M] -7+ pts: Cast a battleform spell for free
  • Scroll Savant [E/E] -12 pts: Create 2 lower level scrolls per day
  • Second Chance Enchantment [M] -10 pts: If creature crit saves against your enchantment, you can cast again next round with no roll on different target

FAMILIAR


  • Familiar [T] -8 pts: You gain a familiar


    • Baba Yaga's Patronage [T] -2 pts: Your familiar is an item, not animal
    • Enhanced Familiar [E] -5+ pts: Familiar has extra abilities
    • Familiar Conduit [E] -6 pts: Use familiar as point of origin for a spell
    • Familiar's Eyes [M] -12 pts: Use your familiar's senses instead
    • Familiar's Language [T] -4 pts: Speak with animals of your familiar's type
    • Familiar Mascot [T] -3 pts: An ally can benefit from your familiar's ability
    • Improved Familiar [T] -6 pts: Special familiar requires 2 less abilities for you
    • Improved Familiar Attunement [T] -2 pts: Use familiar like bonded item
    • Leshy Familiar Secrets [T] -2 pts: Your leshy familiar gets some new ability options
    • Mask Familiar [T] -1 pt: Your familiar is literally your mask.
    • Mutable Familiar [T] -2 pts: You can modify some aspects of your familiar in 10 minutes

HALCYON SPELLS


  • Halcyon Speaker [E] -3 pts: You can turn arcane spell to primal, or vice versa


    • Cascade Bearer's Spellcasting [E] -3 pts: Add occultism / divine to speaker types
    • Dualistic Synergy [E] -4 pts: Metamagic: If arcane, free recall knowledge check with +1, if Primal, temp HP, if Halcyon get both


      • Fulminating Synergy [M] -6 pts: Add some elemental damage to Dualistic Synergy

        • Synergistic Spell [M/L/M] -12 pts: 1x day, cast two halcyon spells simultaneously
      • Shared Synergy [E] -3 pts: Give benefit to an ally within 30 feet
    • Persistent Creation [M] -3 pts: Cast creation spell at will


      • Charged Creation [M] -3 pts: Use creation as originator for line or cone spells

HARM SPELL


  • Command Undead [E] -3 pts: Your Harm spell can control undead


    • Improved Command Undead [E] -3 pts: Extend duration of control
  • Harming Hands [T] -3 pts: Your Harm spell does d10s instead of d8s
  • Necrotic Infusion [M] -5 pts: Use harm to heal undead, and it gets bonus negative dmg
  • Sap Life
    [T] -3 pts: Damage someone with Harm, gain some hit points for it
  • Vile Desecration [E] -2 pts: Deal evil damage with Harm to
    celestials

HEAL SPELL


  • Communal Healing [T] -2 pts: Heal another, gain a few hit points yourself


    • Improved Communal Healing [T] -1 pt: Use it on another person
  • Cremate Undead [T] -3 pts: Heal against undead deals persistent fire damage
  • Denier of Destruction [T] -4 pts: Cast Heal as reaction but reduces dmg to ally
  • Healing Hands [E] -5 pts: Heal spell heals d10s instead of d8s

    • Magic Hands [E] -7 pts: Your Treat Wounds heals an additional d8
  • Heroic Recovery [E] -4 pts: Give a target of your Heal various bonuses
  • Holy Castigation [E] -3 pts: Your Heal spell damages fiends as if undead


    • Castigating Weapon [E] -4 pts: If you do, your strikes do extra good dmg to them too
  • Radiant Infusion [E] -4 pts: Heal also adds positive damage to target's attacks
  • Ressurectionist [M] -4 pts: Restored a creature from death? give it fast healing 5
  • Turn Undead [E] -3 pts: Heal spells against undead may cause them to flee

HARM OR HEAL SPELLS


  • Cast Down [E] -4 pts: Deal damage with Harm/Heal, knock creature prone
  • Channel Smite [E/E] -5 pts: 2 Actions to strike and add Harm/Heal as damage
  • Defensive Recovery [E] -5 pts: Restore HP with Harm/Heal, give +2 to AC
  • Directed Channel [E] -4 pts: Area Harm/Heal can now be a cone instead
  • Ebb and Flow [E] -3 pts: Possibly Heal/Harm additional creature that normally isn't
  • Echoing Channel [E] -6 pts: Damage a second creature for less with Heal/Harm
  • Fast channel [E] -5 pts: Get 3 action version of Heal/harm with 2 actions
  • Martyr [T] -3 pts: Restore more HP with Harm/Heal by taking damage yourself
  • Metamagic Channel [M] -4 pts: 1x 10 Min, Use a metamagic feat on Harm/Heal for free
  • Remediate [E] -6 pts: Use a three action Harm/Heal to attempt to counteract effects
  • Selective Energy [M] -10 pts: Remove some people from Harm/Heal area effect

IMPROVED SPELLCASTING


  • Blood Component [E] -8 pts: Take damage to remove various components form casting
  • Effortless Concentration [M] -3 pts: 1x a minute, sustain a spell as a free action
  • Eschew Materials [E] -3 pts: No material components required, but one hand free is
  • Form Retention [M] -5 pts: Cast a polymorph spell at higher tier to extend duration
  • Spell Penetration [E] -3 pts: Ignore 1 point of a creature's spell resistance
  • Steady Spellcasting [T] -1 pt: Get DC 15 flat check to avoid spell disruption


    • Ward Casting [T] -2 pts: Decrease DC to 10 with a reaction
  • Shift Spell [M] -10 pts: Move an active spell's area of effect for an action.

MAGICAL BLOODLINES

These feats give you a cantrip and 2 spells per tier added to your repertoire, though you must be able to cast them. They require you have a specific tradition, and grant a blood magic effect that occurs when you cast spells granted from the feat.


  • Aberrant Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Occult. Effect: get/give +2 to will saves for 1 round
  • Angelic Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Divine. Effect: get/give +1 to all saves for 1 round
  • Demonic Bloodline [T] -14 pts: Divine. Effect: get/give -1 to AC or +1 to intimidation for 1 round
  • Diabolic Bloodline [T] -14 pts: Divine. Effect: get/give fire damage or +1 to deception for 1 round
  • Draconic Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Arcane. Effect: get/give +1 to AC for 1 round
  • Elemental Bloodline [T] -13 pts: Primal. Effect: get/give elemental damage or +1 to intimidation checks for 1 round
  • Fey Bloodline [T] -14 pts: Primal. Effect: get/give concealment for 1 round
  • Genie Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Arcane. Effect: get/give +1 to deception or -1 to perception for 1 round.
  • Hag Bloodline [T] -11 pts: Occult. Effect: creatures who hit you for 1 round take mental damage
  • Imperial Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Arcane. Effect: get/give +1 to skill checks for 1 round
  • Nymph Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Primal. Effect: get/give +1 to Diplomacy checks or -1 to Will saves for 1 round
  • Phoenix Bloodline [T] -13 pts: Primal. Effect: get/give 2 temp hp/tier, or 2 fire damage/tier for 1 round.


    • Ruby Resurrection [L] -16 pts: Once a day, when reduced to 0 (or killed) gain 30 hit points and cast a master-tier fireball on your location that doesn't hurt you
  • Psychopomp Bloodline [T] - 10 pts: Divine. Effect: them get/give negative/positive damage or +2 to fortitude saves for 1 round
  • Shadow Bloodline [T] - 10 pts: Occult. Effect:get/give +1 to stealth or -1 to perception checks
  • Undead Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Occult. Effect: get/give temp HP or deal negative damage

  • Ancestral Blood Magic [T] -3 pts: Gain bloodline effect when casting ancestry spells

  • Anoint Ally [T] -3 pts: For 1 minute, can give ally your bloodline effect instead of self
  • Bloodline Metamorphosis [M] -8 pts: Can swap out 1 spell a day in your repertoire for any of the same level and tradition
  • Bloodline Mutation [L] -15 pts: Gain form benefits from bloodline, including better nightvision and a movement type
  • Crossblooded Evolution [M] -8 pts: Can learn a spell not from your tradition

    • Greater Crossblooded Evolution [L] -8 pts: Learn two more
  • Entreat with Forbearers [T] -3 pts: Get some bonuses against bloodline creatures
  • Occult Evolution [T] -7 pts: Once a day, temporarily add mental spell to your repertoire
  • Tenacious Blood Magic [M] -6 pts: Your blood magic effect lasts a minute
