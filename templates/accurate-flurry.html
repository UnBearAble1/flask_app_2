Accurate Flurry


3 Points
Warrior; Two Weapon Fighting

Requirements You are wielding two melee weapons, each in a different hand. Usable once per minute; Prerequisites Impossible Flurry

You refine the precision of your unbelievable storm of blows. When you perform an Impossible Flurry, your first attack with each weapon instead takes the multiple attack penalty as though you had already made one attack this turn. All subsequent attacks take the maximum multiple attack penalty, as described in Impossible Flurry.
