Shatter


TRAINED

Evocation, Sonic
Traditions occult, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 unattended object

A high-frequency sonic attack shatters a nearby object. You deal 2d10 sonic damage to the object, ignoring up to 4 points of the object's hardness.

Heightened: The damage increases by 2d10, and the Hardness the spell ignores increases by 4.
