Swift Banishment


5 Points
Spellcaster; Martial Combat
Reaction; Trigger You critically hit a creature that is not on its home plane; You are able to cast the Banishment spell. Prerequisites: You can cast and have in your repertoire the Banishment spell.

The force of your blow sends your victim back to its home plane. Roll to cast the Banishment spell, your target is the creature you struck.
