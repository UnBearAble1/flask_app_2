Climbing Bolt Eldritch Arrow


6 Points
Warrior; Ranged Weapons
Free Action; You can only use one Eldritch Arrow per round; Prerequisites Eldritch Archer; Master in Warrior
You transform a non-magical arrow or bolt into a Climbing Bolt Eldritch Arrow, which you can then fire with a later action. You must shoot the ammunition before the end of your turn or the magic dissipates. The DC is your tradition skill DC (occultism, arcane, nature, or religion) or spellcasting DC.



Climbing Bolt: The shaft of this bolt is wrapped with fine twine. When the bolt strikes a solid surface, the twine unwinds and enlarges into a 50-foot-long rope, securely fastened to the surface the bolt struck. The rope can be pulled free with an Interact action and a successful DC Athletics check. (This arrow lasts for 10 minutes before dissolving, and only one can exist at a time.)
