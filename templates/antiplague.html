Antiplague


Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir, Healing
Usage
held in 1 hand;
Bulk
L
Activate
Single Action,
Interact

Antiplague can fortify the body's defenses against diseases. Upon drinking an antiplague, you gain an item bonus to Fortitude saving throws against diseases for 24 hours; this applies to your daily save against a disease's progression.

TRAINED:
Cost: 5 gold
You gain a +2 item bonus.

EXPERT:
Cost: 10 gold
You gain a +3 item bonus.

MASTER:
Cost: 20 gold
You gain a +4 item bonus.

LEGENDARY:
Cost: 100 gold
You gain a +4 item bonus, and when you drink the antidote, you can immediately attempt a saving throw against one disease of master tier or lower affecting you. If you succeed, you are cured of the disease.
