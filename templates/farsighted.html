Farsighted


-2 points

PHYSICAL
It takes you triple the amount of time to read. You are -4 to perception checks to detect things within 3 feet of you, you are -4 to manual tasks that require seeing up close, and all creatures within 3 feet are concealed to you (usually only applies if they occupy your square).

If you can wear special glasses to eliminate this complication while wearing them, it is only worth 1 point instead of 2.
