Spider Sting


TRAINED

Necromancy, Poison
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Saving Throw Fortitude

You magically duplicate a spider's venomous sting, stabbing at an enemy and possibly affecting them with the spider's weakening venom. Make a spell attack roll. On a hit, you deal 4d4 piercing damage to the touched creature and afflict the creature with spider venom. If the target takes piercing damage, they must attempt a Fortitude save.


  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target takes 2d4 poison damage.
  • Failure The target is afflicted with spider venom at stage 1.
  • Critical Failure The target is afflicted with spider venom at stage 2.

Spider Venom (poison); Maximum Duration 4 rounds.

  • Stage 1 2d4 poison damage and enfeebled 1 (1 round);
  • Stage 2 1d4 poison damage and enfeebled 2 (1 round).
