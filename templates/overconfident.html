Overconfident


-3 points

MENTAL
You believe you are simply more capable and powerful than you actually are. You must make a self-control roll any time the DM feels you show an unreasonable degree of caution. If you fail, you must go ahead as though you were able to handle the situation! You cannot be cautious.

You take -2 on reaction rolls from experienced people, though +2 from young or naive ones.

This is a smaller scale version of megalomania. You can't have both.
