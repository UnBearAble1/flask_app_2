Powerful Sneak


4 Points
Warrior; Sneak Attack
Prerequisites: Sneak Attack; Master in Warrior

You have learned to exploit your enemies' lowered defenses. When you critically succeed at a Strike using your Strength modifier on the attack roll and you would deal sneak attack damage, you can change the additional damage from sneak attack into ordinary damage of the same type as your Strike, rather than precision damage.
