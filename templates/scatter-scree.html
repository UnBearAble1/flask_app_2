Scatter Scree


Cantrip, Earth, Evocation
Traditions arcane, primal
Two Actions;
somatic, verbal
Range 30 feet; Area two contiguous 5-foot cubes
Saving Throw basic Reflex; Duration 1 minute

You evoke a jumble of rocks in the area. The scattering rocks deal bludgeoning damage equal to 5d4 plus your spellcasting ability modifier to creatures in the area, with a basic Reflex save. The ground in the area becomes difficult terrain for the duration. A creature can Interact to clear a square of this scree.

If you cast this spell again, any previous scatter scree you have cast ends.
