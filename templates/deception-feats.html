Deception Feats


AVOID MAGICAL DETECTION


  • Blank Slate (L) -8 pts: Avoid all non legendary magical detection, revelation, scrying
  • Hidden Magic (E) -3 pts: Hide the magical auras of your gear with deception check
  • Slippery Secrets (M) -8 pts: Can roll to avoid magical uncovering of your secrets
  • Spy's Countermeasures (L) -5 pts: Detect incoming divination attempts and create false results on a successful save

CONVERSATION


  • Discreet Inquiry (E) -1 pt: Hide the true subject of your gather information attempts
  • Doublespeak (M) -3 pts: Disguise true speech meaning with allies from listeners
  • Secret Speech (T) -1 pt: You gain a secret language and can secretly pass messages
  • Distracting Flattery (E) -3 pts: You can try to cover an ally's social blunders
  • Sow Rumor (E) -4 pts: Spread rumors about a target throughout a community

CREATE A DIVERSION


  • Lengthy Diversion [T] -2 pts: Can remain hidden if crit succeed on create a diversion

DISGUISE


  • Analyze Idiolect (E) -3 pts: Interact for 10 minutes and gain +4 to impersonate them
  • Backup Disguise (E) -3 pts: Prep a backup disguise to change into in <1 round
  • Beneath Notice (M) -3 pts: Impersonate a nondescript crowd member swiftly
  • Charlatan (T) -2 pts: Pretend your are casting spells when using magic items
  • Lion Blade Spy (E) -3 pts: Gain Espionage Lore, no penalties on some disguises
  • Quick Disguise (E) -4 pts: Disguise and Impersonate much more quickly

FOCUS SPELL


  • Trickery Domain: Sudden Shift (M) -2 pts: Focus spell to step and become concealed as a reaction
  • Trickery Domain: Trickster's Twin (M) -8 pts: Focus spell creates an illusory double of you that mimics your movement

ILLUSIONS


  • Convincing Illusion (E) -4 pts: Reaction to try to re-convince someone of your illusion

LIES


  • Confabulator (E) -2 pts: Reduce bonus target gets when you repeatedly lie/create diversions

SKILL SUBSTITUTION


  • Charming Liar (T) -3 pts: You can use Lie to Make an Impression
  • Fabricated Connections (M) -4 pts: Use deception to make impression, request, and more
  • Lie to Me (T) -1 pt: If engaged in conversation, use deception to detect lies
  • Reveal Machinations
    (L) -12 pts: Reveal you were part of an NPC's life all along, imposing frightened 2 or 3, and letting you giving your information about them

SPECIAL DEFENSES


  • Bravo's Determination (L) -12 pts: Deception check to avoid getting dropped to 0 HP.
  • Flicker (M) -5 pts: Gain concealment until your next turn for an action.

SPELL-LIKE ABILITY


  • Innocent Butterfly (L) -8 pts: Cast Aura of the Unremarkable once a day as innate
  • Perfect Distraction (L) -8 pts: Leave an animated decoy in your place as per Mislead spell

    • Reactive Distraction (L) -5 pts: As a reaction, leave your decoy to take a hit for you

VIGILANTE


  • Vigilante (E) -3 pts: You have two separate identities with maybe two different alignments

    • Minion Guise (E) -2 pts: Familiar/Animal Companion/Minion also changes identities
    • Quick Change (M) -2 pts: You shift between identities much more quickly
    • Subjective Truth (M) -4 pts: Defeat detect lies if not a lie for your current identity
    • Many Guises (L) -7 pts: Can change to a generic nondescript identity as well
