Stubborn Persistence


4 Points
Behavioral Trait, Resistance
When you would become fatigued, attempt a DC 15 flat check. On a success, you aren't fatigued. If the fatigued condition has an underlying cause that you don't address, such as lack of rest, you must attempt the check again at an interval determined by the GM until you fail the flat check or address the underlying cause.
