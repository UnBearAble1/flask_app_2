Slip the Grasp


3 points
Physiology; Escape
1 Action; Requirements You are grabbed, immobilized, or restrained.

Attempt a check to Escape. If you succeed, you can Stride, Step, or make a Strike with a melee unarmed attack targeting the creature you Escaped from. If you fail, you instead attempt to Escape a second time. You increase your multiple attack penalty as appropriate for the actions you took, but only after completing both actions.
