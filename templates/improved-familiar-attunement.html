Improved Familiar Attunement


2 Points
Spellcaster; Familiar
Requires: Familiar, Drain Bonded Item

Your connection with your familiar alters your drain bonded item feature so that you store your magical energy in your familiar, rather than an item you own; you also gain the Drain Familiar free action instead of Drain Bonded Item. Drain Familiar can be used any time an ability would allow you to use Drain Bonded Item and functions identically, except that you draw magic from your familiar instead of an item.
