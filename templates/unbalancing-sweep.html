Unbalancing Sweep


4 Points
Warrior; Crowd Fighting

2 Actions; Flourish, Open; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior

You make a great sweep about yourself, knocking creatures off their feet and away from you. Choose up to three adjacent foes and choose whether to Shove or Trip all three of them. Whichever of the two options you choose, roll a separate Athletics check against each foe, performing the same action against each foe.
