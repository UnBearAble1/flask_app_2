Tyrant's Exalt


3 Points
Religion/Warrior; Champion's Reaction
Requires: Iron Command

When you use Iron Command, each enemy within 15 feet of you other than the triggering creature must also either drop prone or take 3 mental damage, and the effects they take can't be adjusted by anything that changes your Iron Command. For instance, the Iron Repercussions feat couldn't turn the damage into persistent mental damage for creatures other than the triggering creature.
