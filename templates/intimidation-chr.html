Intimidation (CHR)


You bend others to your will using threats.

Untrained Actions


  • Coerce: You attempt to bully a creature into doing what you want.
  • Demoralize: You can shake an enemy's resolve.
  • Persuade: Intimidation is one of the three approaches when you attempt to persuade someone of a truth or to taking your side.
