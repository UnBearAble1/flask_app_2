New Cantrips


ARCANE



  • Elemental Shield (abj) Reaction, V, 1 creature;


    • Trigger: A creature you can see takes fire, cold, electricity, or acid damage.
    • Effect: You shield a creature from elemental damage. The creature gains resistance 5 against the triggering damage.
  • Kinetic Shield (abj) Reaction, V, 1 creature;


    • Trigger: A creature you can see takes force, negative, positive, or necrotic damage.
    • Effect: You harden a creature's body against certain types of damage. The creature gains resistance 5 against the triggering damage.
  • Sight Warding (abj) 3 actions, M/V/S, 10 feet, sustained up to 1 minute


    • You ward a 5 x 5, 2 dimensional piece of reality from sight. This pane can be set anywhere within range, angled in any fashion, but remains static once the spell is cast. The pane is completely intangible. It completely prevents vision from detecting anything on the other side, but nothing else. The warded pane appears to be an incongruous 'hole' in vision, and is easily spottable from a distance.
  • Soften Fall (abj) 2 actions, V/S, 10 feet, 1 creature, 1 minute:


    • You cushion the air beneath a creature, greatly softening falls. For the duration, the creature treats falls at 50 feet shorter.
  • Ward Opening (abj) 10 min, M/V/S, touch, 8 hours:


    • You ward a single door, window, or object that can be opened such as a book or container to alert you if it is opened. Functions as alarm spell, but limited to the objects listed.
  • Waterproofing (abj) 2 actions, V/S, touch, 24 hours, 1 or more objects of up to 8 bulk.


    • You prevent an object from becoming wet. The duration is divided evenly among objects you have the
      protected in this way. Any object larger than 3 bulk counts as two items for duration.
