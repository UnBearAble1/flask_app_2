Quaking Stomp


15 Points
Warrior; Rage

2 Actions; Usable Once Per Week; Manipulate, Rage; Prerequisites: Rage; Legendary in Warrior

You stomp the ground with such force that it creates a minor earthquake, with the effects of the earthquake spell, centered on you. You are also caught in the effects of the earthquake, and must immediately attempt any relevant saving throws.
