MASTER
Illusion, Shadow
Traditions arcane, occult
Reaction; verbal
Trigger A spell or magical effect deals damage
Range 60 feet; Targets the triggering spell

Exchanging material energy with that of the Shadow Plane, you transform the triggering spell into a partially illusory version of itself. Attempt to counteract the target spell. If the attempt is successful, any creatures that would be damaged by the spell instead take only half as much damage, but the spell otherwise works as normal. Treat shadow siphon's counteract tier as 1 higher for this attempt, even if that would put it above legendary
