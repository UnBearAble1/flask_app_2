10 Points
Spellcaster; Magical Bloodine
Prerequisites: Trained in Spellcaster (occult)
Whether due to a velstrac's manipulations or a planar jaunt gone horribly wrong, your bloodline was infused with a vein of shadow.. Add the following spells to your repertoire, even if they aren't normally part of the occult tradition (note you must still have the appropriate level of spellcaster proficiency to cast the spell):
  • cantrip: chill touch,
  • Trained: grim tendrils, darkness,
  • Expert: chilling darkness, phantasmal killer,
  • Master: shadow siphon, collective transposition,
  • Legendary: duplicate foe, disappearance,
Blood Magic: Shadows grow deeper around you or one target, either granting a +1 status bonus to Stealth or imposing a –1 status penalty to Perception for 1 round.
