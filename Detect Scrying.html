EXPERT
Detection, Divination
Traditions arcane, occult
Two Actions ; somatic, verbal
Area 30-foot emanation; Duration 1 hour

By tapping into trace divinatory auras, you detect the presence of scrying effects in the area. If detect scrying is higher tier than a scrying effect, you gain a glimpse of the scrying creature and learn its approximate distance and direction.

Heightened (Master) The duration is until the next time you make your daily preparations.
