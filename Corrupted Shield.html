3 Points
Religion/Warrior; Shields
Prerequisites: Shield Ally ; Shield Block;
Negative, Divine, Necromancy
Your shield ally is more than just a spirit of protection— it's a conduit for deadly divine magic. When you use your shield ally to Shield Block a melee unarmed attack or a melee weapon Strike from an adjacent creature, the attacker takes 2d6 evil or negative damage (your choice). If the attack or Strike breaks or destroys your shield, double this additional damage.
