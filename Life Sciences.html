You are educated in the scientific study of living things, from the smallest organisms to the largest biological systems.

  • Craft Drug, Poison, Food or Medicine: You can craft drugs, poisons, food or medicinal items using the crafting rules. 
  • Recall Knowledge: About bioengineering, biology, botany, ecology, genetics, xenobiology, zoology, and other fields of biological science, as well as aberrations, animals, humanoids, monstrous humanoids, oozes, and plant creatures.
