3 Points
Arcana; School Spells
Prerequisites: Expert in Arcana
You gain the augment summoning school spell, and you increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Augment Summoning; Conjuration
1 Action verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature you summoned
You augment the abilities of a summoned creature. The target gains a +1 status bonus to all checks (this also applies to the creature's DCs, including its AC) for the duration of its summoning, up to 1 minute.
