Threat: 4

A living thunderclap is a humanoid-shaped storm cloud that cracks and booms with thunder.

Recall Knowledge - Elemental (Arcana, Nature)

N, Medium, Air, Elemental, Artillery 
Perception +9; darkvision
Languages Auran
Skills Acrobatics +8, Stealth +8
Str +3, Dex +4, Con +2, Int -3, Wis +1, Cha +0
AC 18; Fort +6, Ref +8, Will +5
HP 32; Immunities bleed, paralyzed, poison, sleep, sonic
Speed fly 50 feet; swiftness

Features: 
Swiftness The living thunderclap doesn't trigger reactions when it moves.

Actions
  • Gust: Melee Single Action; +7 (+3 / -1) (agile), Damage 2d6+4 bludgeoning plus Push 5 feet
  • Lightning Bolt; Ranged Single Action; +9 (+4 / -1) (electricity, range increment 50 feet), Damage 2d12 electricity
  • Thunderbolt; Two Actions (electricity, evocation, primal, sonic) The living thunderclap emits a bolt of lightning that crashes with deafening thunder. The living thunderclap makes a lightning bolt Strike that deals 1d12 electricity damage. If it hits, the target and any creatures within a 15-foot emanation around the target take 2d6 sonic damage and must attempt a DC 18 basic Fortitude save. Any creature that fails its save is also deafened for 1d4 rounds.
