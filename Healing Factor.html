Physiology; Healing and HP

Your people are quick healers. You gain the following benefits: 
  • When you get a full rest, you recover twice the amount of hit points.
    • In addition, you reduce the severity of your drained and doomed condition by 2 when you rest for a night instead of by 1.
  • When anyone uses the Medicine skill to Administer First Aid, you can eat a snack to add your Constitution modifier to the hit points healed.
