10 Points
Natural Abilities; Elemental
Frequency: once per day
You can spend 1 minute in focused preparation, then enter a fire your size or larger to instantly teleport to any fire that is your size or larger up to 5 miles away. You take no damage from entering or exiting fires when using this ability. Once you enter the fire, you instantly know the rough locations of other large fires within range and can exit from the original fire, if you prefer. You can't carry extradimensional spaces or any other creatures with you when you teleport; if you attempt to do so, the activity fails.
