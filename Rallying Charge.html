4 Points
Warrior; Teamwork
2 Actions; Open, Visual ; Prerequisites: Expert in Warrior
Your fearless charge into battle reinvigorates your allies to carry on the fight. You Stride up to your Speed and make a melee Strike. If you strode 10 feet or more and your Strike hits and damages an enemy, each ally within 60 feet who saw you hit gains temporary Hit Points equal to your Charisma modifier. These temporary Hit Points last until the start of your next turn.
