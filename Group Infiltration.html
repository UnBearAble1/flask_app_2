4 Points
Professional; Liberator
Prerequisites  Expert in Professional ; Expert in Stealth [Fortune]
You can guide your allies to move without notice. When you and any of your allies within 60 feet are Avoiding Notice, you can all Follow the Expert, and you can pick which member of your group is the expert. You and those allies can roll a single Stealth check, using the lowest modifier, instead of rolling separately. You also roll twice and take the better result. This doesn’t apply for initiative rolls. This is a fortune effect.
