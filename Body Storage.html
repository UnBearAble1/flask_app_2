1 Points
Physiology; Exploration
You can store 1 Bulk of objects within your body (cheek pouches, hollow in your torso, or the like). Stowing an action in this way takes an action, but once per round you can do so as a free action. The DC of checks to Steal objects from inside you increases by 4. Additionally, if you store only one object within your body, you can draw it effortlessly into your hand as part of another action to use the object. Drawing the item grants this other action the manipulate trait.

Boost: (+8 points) You squirrel away useful objects and seem to retrieve them at just the right time. You gain the Prescient Consumable and Prescient Planner feats, except that you always withdraw the objects from your body, and it only takes one action to do so
