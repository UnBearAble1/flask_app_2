Cantrip, Evocation
Traditions: arcane, divine, occult, primal
Two Actions somatic, verbal
Range 10 feet; Targets 1 object (cook, lift, or tidy only); Duration sustained

The simplest magic does your bidding. You can perform simple magical effects for as long as you Sustain the Spell. Each time you Sustain the Spell, you can choose one of a few options.

  • Cook: Cool, warm, or flavor 1 pound of nonliving material.
  • Lift: Slowly lift an unattended object of light Bulk or less 1 foot off the ground.
  • Make: Create a temporary object of negligible Bulk, made of congealed magical substance. The object looks crude and artificial and is extremely fragile—it can't be used as a tool, weapon, or spell component.
  • Clean: Remove filth from an object of light Bulk or less. You can affect an object of 1 Bulk with 10 rounds of concentration, and a larger object with 1 minute per Bulk. The filth falls to the ground near the object. 
  • Soil:  Lift trace amounts of filth from the ground beneath or adjacent to an object to soil an object of light Bulk or less. You can affect an object of 1 Bulk with 10 rounds of concentration, and a larger object with 1 minute per Bulk. 

Prestidigitation can't deal damage or cause adverse conditions. Any actual change to an object (beyond what is noted above) persists only as long as you Sustain the Spell.
