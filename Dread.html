-1 to -10 points

SUPERNATURAL
You have a supernatural aversion to a particular substance that requires you to keep a certain distance from it. If you are forced to be within proximity of your dreaded substance, you can do absolutely nothing except flee until you are outside the effective area. You automatically sense the presence of the substance when you get within the radius of it. You don’t know exactly where it is, but you know its direction, and are compelled to go in the opposite direction. 

Distance: 
  • You cannot go within 5 feet of the substance [-2 pts] 
  • You cannot go within 15 feet of the substance [-3 pts] 
  • You cannot go within 30 feet of the substance [-4 pts] 

Rarity of Substance: 
  • Rare (e.g. exotic materials or gems, strange magic types) [x ½] 
  • Occasional (e.g., air-borne pollen, more common gems or materials, schools of magic): [x1]
  • Common (e.g., smoke, nearby magic, horses, loud noises): [x2]
  • Very Common (e.g., sunlight, living plants): [x3]
