Price 15 gp; Damage 2d4 B; Bulk 1
Hands 1
Category Simple
Group Club; Traits Shove, Two-Hand 2d8

This reinforced lute is suitable both for use as a handheld musical instrument and for bashing heads should a crowd turn sour. Its strings are finely braided wires that run along its sturdy metal neck.

Shove: You can use this weapon to Shove with the Athletics skill even if you don't have a free hand. This uses the weapon's reach (if different from your own) and adds the weapon's item bonus to attack rolls as an item bonus to the Athletics check. If you critically fail a check to Shove using the weapon, you can drop the weapon to take the effects of a failure instead of a critical failure.

Two-Hand 1d8: This weapon can be wielded with two hands. Doing so changes its weapon damage die to the indicated value.
