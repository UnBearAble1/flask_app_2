3 Points
Professional; Special Defenses
Prerequisites: Master in Professional
Reaction; Trigger You attempt a saving throw, but you haven't rolled yet.
When danger calls, you have a strange knack for coming out on top. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to the triggering save.

Boost (+5 Points) Incredibly Charmed: (Usable once per hour) You always seem to bounce back from the worst. When you use Charmed Life, roll the save twice (including the +2 circumstance bonus from Charmed Life) and use the better result.
