LEGENDARY
Evocation, Fire, Light, Positive
Traditions divine, primal
Two Actions ; somatic, verbal
Range 500 feet; Area 60-foot burst
Saving Throw Reflex

A powerful globe of searing sunlight explodes in the area, dealing 5d10 fire damage to all creatures in the area, plus 8d6 additional positive damage to undead creatures. Each creature in the area must attempt a Reflex save.

  • Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
  • Success The creature takes half damage.
  • Failure The creature takes full damage.
  • Critical Failure The creature takes full damage and becomes blinded permanently.

If the globe overlaps with an area of magical darkness, sunburst attempts to counteract the darkness effect.
