Alchemical, Consumable, Elixir, Mutagen, Polymorph
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L 
Activate Single Action, Interact

Your skin grows tough scales like a drake, your eyesight become sharp and your pupils slitted, and your limbs grow wiry and quick, but your mind and reflexes become slow.

Benefit You gain the listed bonus to AC, a Dexterity cap of +2 (as usual, use your lowest Dexterity cap if you have more than one), and an item bonus to Perception checks. You also gain the Final Surge action.
  • Final Surge 
    • Activate Single Action, 
    • Effect You Stride twice. The drakeheart mutagen's duration ends.
Drawback You take a –1 penalty to Will saves, Reflex saves, and all skill checks to Recall Knowledge.
