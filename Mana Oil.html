MASTER
Alchemical, Consumable, Olfactory
Price 35 gp
Hands 2; Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate Single Action Interact

You can spread this rainbow-hued metallic oil onto one melee weapon, one thrown weapon, or 10 pieces of ammunition. Mana Oil spoils quickly, so once you open a vial, you must use it all at once, rather than saving it. For the next hour, the weapon or ammunition counts as magical for any physical damage it deals.
