6 Points
Warrior; Rage
2 Actions; Attack, Concentrate, Rage; Prerequisites superstition instinct; Master in Warrior
You draw upon your superstitious fury to destroy a spell. Make a Strike with a melee weapon or an unarmed attack against a creature, object, or a spell manifestation (such as the wall created by wall of fire or the guardian from spiritual guardian). If your Strike hits, you can attempt to counteract a single spell or magical effect on the target. You use the result of your attack roll for the counteract check. Whether or not you succeed at your Strike, the target becomes temporarily immune to your Sunder Spell for 24 hours.
