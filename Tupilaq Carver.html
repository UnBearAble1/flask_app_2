2 Points
Cultural Training; Spellcasting
Prerequisites Trained in Spellcaster, Primal or Divine
Add the summon construct spell to your spell repertoire. The constructs you summon have a distinct ivory scrimshaw appearance, and if you include a drop of blood, lock of hair, or other portion of a creature’s body as part of the spell’s material component, the summoned construct gains a +4 status bonus to Perception checks to sense or locate that creature.
