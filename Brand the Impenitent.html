TRAINED

Abjuration, Curse
Traditions divine
Two Actions somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Saving Throw Fortitude; Duration varies

You brand the target with an ethereal copy of your deity’s religious symbol. This brand can’t be hidden, but it is visible only to followers of your faith, who see it as clearly as a lit torch. Followers of your faith ignore the target’s concealed condition, if any, and the target gains a –1 status penalty to AC against attacks by followers of your faith, as well as weakness 1 against their attacks. If the target is a follower of your deity in good standing, the spell fails.

  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The spell lasts for 1 round.
  • Failure The spell lasts for 1 minute.
  • Critical Failure The spell has an unlimited duration.
