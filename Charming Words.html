4 Points
Arcana; School Spells
Prerequisites: Expert in Arcana
You gain the charming words school spell, and you increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Charming Words; Auditory, Enchantment, Incapacitation, Linguistic, Mental
1 Action verbal; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature; Saving Throw Will; Duration until the start of your next turn
You whisper enchanting words to deflect your foe's ire. The target must attempt a Will save.
  • Critical Success The target is unaffected.
  • Success The target takes a –1 circumstance penalty to attack rolls and damage rolls against you.
  • Failure The target can't use hostile actions against you.
  • Critical Failure The target is stunned 1 and can't use hostile actions against you.
