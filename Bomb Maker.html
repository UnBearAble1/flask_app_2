3 Points
Alchemist; Bombs
Prerequisite: Trained in Alchemist
You specialize in explosions and other violent alchemical reactions. You know two formulas for alchemical bombs for each tier of bomb you can make,  in addition to your other formulas. 

Boost (+2 Points) Expert Bomb Maker (requires Expert Alchemist): When using quick alchemy to make trained-tier bombs, you don't automatically critical fail on a natural one. 

Boost (+4 Points) Master Bomb Maker (requires Master Alchemist, Expert Bomb Maker Boost): When using advanced alchemy to make bombs during your daily preparations, you craft an extra bomb for each bomb you make.
