TRAINED

Plant, Transmutation
Traditions primal
Two Actions ; somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 non-magical club or staff you hold
Duration 1 minute

The target grows vines and leaves, brimming with primal energy. The target becomes a magical item, gaining a +1 item bonus to attack rolls and increasing the damage dice type by a step. Additionally, as long as you are on your home plane, attacks you make with the target against aberrations, extraplanar creatures, and undead increase the number of weapon damage dice to three.
