1 Point
Natural Abilities; Elemental
1 Action; Usable once per day
Range: 30 feet. Targets: One creature holding an item.
You make the item unbearable to hold. The creature makes a Fortitude Save. 
  • Success: The creature is unaffected 
  • Failure: The creature can either drop the item or take 4d6 fire damage to the hand. 
  • Critical Failure: The creature drops the item AND takes 4d6 fire damage to the hand. 

Boost: (+3 points) You can use this ability once per 10 minutes.
