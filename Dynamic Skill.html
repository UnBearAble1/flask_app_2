3 Points
Natural Abilities; Knowledge
During your daily preparations, you can gain the trained proficiency rank in one skill of your choice. This proficiency lasts until you prepare again. Since this proficiency is temporary, you can’t use it as a prerequisite for a skill increase or a permanent character option like a feat.

Boost: (+4 points) Expert Dynamic Skill: When you choose a skill in which to become trained with Dynamic Skill, you can also choose a skill in which you are already trained and become an expert in that skill. This lasts until your Dynamic Skill expires.

Boost: (+2 points) True Dynamic Skill: During your daily preparations, you can choose to permanently retrain some of your existing skills. The only options you can retrain into, however, are whatever the skills you chose the previous day for Dynamic Skill or Expert Dynamic skill (if you have that boost) were.

Boost: (+2 points) Universal Dynamic Skill: Once per day, for a single action, you can change the skills you selected with Dynamic Skill and Expert Dynamic Skill.
