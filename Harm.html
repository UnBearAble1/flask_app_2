EXPERT

Necromancy, Negative
Traditions divine
One Action to Three Actions
Range varies; Targets 1 living creature or 1 willing undead creature

You channel negative energy to heal the undead or damage the living. If the target is a willing undead creature, you restore 1d8 Hit Points. If the target is living, you deal 5d8 negative damage to it, and it gets a basic Fortitude save. The number of actions you spend when Casting this Spell determines its targets, range, area, and other parameters.

  • Single Action (somatic) The spell has a range of touch.
  • Two Actions (somatic, verbal) The spell has a range of 30 feet. If you're healing an undead creature, increase the Hit Points restored by 8.
  • Three Actions (material, somatic, verbal) You disperse positive energy in a 30-foot emanation. This targets all living and undead creatures in the burst.

Heightened: The amount of healing increases by 1d8, or 2d8 for the 2-action version, and the amount of damage to the living increases by 2d8.
