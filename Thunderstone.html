Alchemical, Bomb, Sonic, Consumable, Splash
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L 
Activate Single Action, Strike

When this stone hits a creature or a hard surface, it explodes with a deafening bang. A thunderstone deals the listed sonic damage and sonic splash damage, and each creature within 10 feet of the space in which the stone exploded must succeed at a Fortitude saving throw with the listed DC or be deafened until the end of its next turn.
