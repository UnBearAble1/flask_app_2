MENTAL
You respond poorly to excessive stimulation. When in a strange place, or when there’s a lot of stuff going on, you have to make a self control roll or simply freeze up. Out of combat, this means that you double the time to complete tasks if you fail your roll as you have to pause and gather your bearings. In combat, this means you will be stunned in the first round of combat as you have to adjust to what is happening.
