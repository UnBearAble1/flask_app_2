2 Points
Natural Abilities; Elemental
1 Action; Usable Once Per Day; Concentrate (plus choose an element or shadow)
You shroud yourself in obscuring elemental material. You become concealed for 5 rounds. You can't use this concealment to Hide or Sneak, as normal for concealment that makes your position obvious.

Boost: (+1 point) When you use The Shroud, you can spend one additional action to disperse it as a 10-foot-radius cloud centered on you instead of as haze; the activity gains the concentrate trait. All creatures within the cloud become concealed, and all creatures outside the cloud become concealed to creatures within it.

Boost: (+2 points) You can use The Shroud one additional time per day
