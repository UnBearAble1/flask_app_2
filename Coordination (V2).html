Coordination measures your ability to perform tasks requiring dexterity and grace. When you use the Escape basic action, you can use your Acrobatics modifier instead of your unarmed attack modifier.

Untrained Actions

  • Balance:  You move across a narrow surface or uneven ground, attempting an Acrobatics check against its Balance DC
  • Trip: You try to knock a creature to the ground.
  • Tumble Through: You can try to move through the space of one enemy.

Trained Actions

  • Disarm: You try to knock something out of a creature’s grasp.
  • Maneuver in Flight: You try a difficult maneuver while flying.
  • Squeeze: You contort yourself to squeeze through a space so small you can barely fit through.
