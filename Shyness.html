-1 to -3 points


MENTAL
You are incredibly uncomfortable around strangers.  As with all complications, you are expected to roleplay this character trait! The value of this complication is based on the severity: 
  • Mild: [-1 pt] You are uneasy with strangers, especially particularly assertive ones or ones you find attractive.  You have -1 on all social skills around them. 
  • Severe: [-2 pts] You are very uncomfortable around strangers, and tend to be quiet even among friends. -2 on social skills.  
  • Crippling: [-3 pts]  You avoid strangers whenever possible. You may become trained in social skills at all,  and you treat all rolls at them as one category worse. Further, your shyness causes most people to have -1 reaction rolls with you.
