SUPERNATURAL, CHAOTIC
You have must feed on something only attainable through social interaction with other sapient beings. This could be blood, life force, mana, sex, or whatever else you workout with the DM. If this is your only source of sustenance, make sure to pick up restricted diet as well. 

Whenever you have an opportunity to indulge, you'll have to make a self-control roll or do so. Make this check at -2 if someone deliberately tempts you, or the item is visible in large quantities around you. If you fail the check, you must feed, regardless of the consequences. Make a second self-control roll to stop feeding once you have had your fill. If you fail, you go into frenzy and overindulge, which could kill your victim.
