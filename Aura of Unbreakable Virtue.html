10 Points
Religion/Warrior; Holy Power
Prerequisites: Any Champion's Oath
1 Action; Usable Once Per Day; Duration 1 Minute.
You are a paragon of all that is good, your overwhelming aura causing evildoers to cower. All evil creatures within 15 feet of you take a –1 status penalty to checks and DCs that target you or your allies. This penalty is –2 against creatures that are the favored foe from one of your Champion's Oath.
