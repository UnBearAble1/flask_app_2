MAGICAL BLOODLINES

These feats give you a cantrip and 2 spells per tier added to your repertoire, though you must be able to cast them. They require you have a specific tradition, and grant a blood magic effect that occurs when you cast spells granted from the feat.
  • Aberrant Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Occult. Effect: get/give +2 to will saves for 1 round 
  • Angelic Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Divine. Effect: get/give +1 to all saves for 1 round
  • Demonic Bloodline [T] -14 pts: Divine. Effect: get/give -1 to AC or +1 to intimidation for 1 round 
  • Diabolic Bloodline [T] -14 pts: Divine. Effect: get/give fire damage or +1 to deception for 1 round
  • Draconic Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Arcane. Effect: get/give +1 to AC for 1 round
  • Elemental Bloodline [T] -13 pts: Primal. Effect: get/give elemental damage or +1 to intimidation checks for 1 round
  • Fey Bloodline [T] -14 pts: Primal. Effect: get/give concealment for 1 round 
  • Genie Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Arcane. Effect: get/give +1 to deception or -1 to perception for 1 round. 
  • Hag Bloodline [T] -11 pts: Occult. Effect: creatures who hit you for 1 round take mental damage 
  • Imperial Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Arcane. Effect: get/give +1 to skill checks for 1 round 
  • Nymph Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Primal. Effect: get/give +1 to Diplomacy checks or -1 to Will saves for 1 round 
  • Phoenix Bloodline [T] -13 pts: Primal. Effect: get/give 2 temp hp/tier, or 2 fire damage/tier for 1 round. 
    • Ruby Resurrection [L] -16 pts: Once a day, when reduced to 0 (or killed) gain 30 hit points and cast a master-tier fireball on your location that doesn't hurt you 
  • Psychopomp Bloodline [T] - 10 pts: Divine. Effect: them get/give negative/positive damage or +2 to fortitude saves for 1 round
  • Shadow Bloodline [T] - 10 pts: Occult. Effect:get/give +1 to stealth or -1 to perception checks 
  • Undead Bloodline [T] -10 pts: Occult. Effect: get/give temp HP or deal negative damage
