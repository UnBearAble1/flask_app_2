18 Points
Physiology; Senses
You can see in darkness and dim light just as well as you can see in bright light, though your vision in darkness is in black and white.

Boost (+4 points) Defy the Darkness: You gain greater darkvision, enabling you to see through magical darkness even if it normally hampers darkvision (such as the darkness created by a heightened darkness spell). You can't cast spells with the darkness trait, use item activations with the darkness trait, or use any other ability with the darkness trait.
