These effects benefit you when interacting with hazards.

For each of the ones outlined below, you'll first need to select the scope of your effect: 
  • All Hazards (Default)  
  • Traps Only (Mod -4) 
  • Haunts Only (Base -4)

Analyze Hazard


[Base: 8] 
Linked Skill: Thievery 
Activation: Instantaneous 
Default Range: 30 feet.

You learn the Hazard's effects.

Other Hazard Effects


Re-Arm Hazard
[Base 8, Mod -2]
Linked Skill: Thievery
Activation: Instantaneous, Default Range: Touch
You can re-arm a disarmed hazard that otherwise couldn’t be.

Don't Trigger Movement Based Hazards
[Base: 12, Mod +8]
Linked Skill: Thievery
You don't trigger movement based hazards with movement 


Stop Hazard
[Base: 12, Mod +8]
Linked Skill: Thievery
Activation: Instantaneous, Default Range: 10 feet
Default Action: Reaction
You prevent a triggered trap from being triggered, or you delay the activation until the start or end of your next turn (your choice)
