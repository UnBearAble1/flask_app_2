3 Points
Physiology; Bite Attack
Reaction; Prerequisites: Bite Attack*; Trigger A foe gives you the grabbed or restrained condition using a part of its body.
Whenever someone grabs onto you, you instinctively bite down hard. Sometimes that makes them let go, and sometimes it just makes them angrier, but either way, it’s both satisfying and tasty. Make a Bite attack against the triggering foe. On a critical hit, you are no longer grabbed. This Strike doesn’t count toward your multiple attack penalty, and your multiple attack penalty doesn’t apply to this Strike.

*At the DM's discretion, any other feat or ability that allows you to attack with a bite will work for the prerequisite.
