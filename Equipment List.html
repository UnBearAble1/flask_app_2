Armor

You can find the full rules for armor here.

Guide for the following table: 
  • AC: Your AC Bonus for wearing the armor 
  • DR: The damage resistance you gain against all physical attacks 
  • PEN: The penalty you take against all acrobatics and stealth checks if you are not proficient in the armor.  If there is a number in parenthesis, that is a penalty to reflex saves. In order to get rid of this penalty, you must take the Mighty Bulwark feat.
