Religion; Litany Spells
Prerequisites: Expert in Religion
You undercut your enemy's vain moralism, opening their heart to evil. You can cast the following spell, and Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

Litany of Depravity; Evil, Evocation, Litany
1 Action; verbal ; Range 30 feet; Targets 1 good creature ; Duration 1 round
Your litany calls into question the morals your foe claims to hold dear. The target gains weakness 7 to evil, then becomes temporarily immune to all your litanies for 1 minute.
