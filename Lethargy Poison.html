7 Points
Crafting; Craft Specific Items
Prerequisites: trained in Crafting
During your daily preparations, you can prepare a dose of lethargy poison from ordinary materials in a wilderness or urban area. If you're an expert in Crafting, you can instead craft a dose of stupor poison. The save DC for this poison is equal to your crafting DC. Only you can use these poisons, and they expire the next time you make your daily preparations. The GM might decide that the area you're in is too barren to provide the materials you need for this feat.
